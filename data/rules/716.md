The roots @deva[हस्] 'to laugh at,' @deva[भाष्] 'to kill,' @deva[पृच्] 'to go on,' or 'ask,' @deva[यत्] 'to strive,' @deva[जय्], @deva[भक्] and @deva[शक्] take the affix @deva[य]; @deva[हस्यं] 'what is fit to be laughed at,' @deva[भाष्यं] 'what ought to be killed;' &c.
