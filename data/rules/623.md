## Non-Conjugational Tenses of Desideratives

### (a) Perfect

The Perfect is formed by adding @deva[आम्] to the base and appending the Perfect forms of the auxiliary verbs @deva[अस्], @deva[भू], and @deva[कृ] (see @ref[490], @ref[526]).

### (b) Aorist

In the Aorist the Desiderative Bases take the terminations of the 5th Variety.

### (c) Benedictive

In the Benedictive the Parasm. terminations are added without @deva[इ] and the Atm. ones with the intermediate @deva[इ].

### (d) Remaining Tenses

The remaining tenses have no peculiarities.
