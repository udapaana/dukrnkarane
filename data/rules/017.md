The numerical figures in Sanskrit are:
    @deva[१, २, ३, ४, ५, ६, ७, ८, ९, ०]
    1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 0.

These are combined to express larger figures precisely in the same way as in English; @deva[१२५] 125, @deva[५४०] 540, &c.
