The Infinitive is formed by the affix @deva[तुम्] with the same effect as the @deva[त्] of the Future; e. g.

| Roots | | Infinitive | Roots | | Infinitive |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| @deva[इ] | to go | @deva[एतुम्] | @deva[ग्रन्थ्] | to compose | @deva[ग्रन्थितुम्] |
| @deva[वद्] | to grow | @deva[वक्तुम्] | @deva[पच्] | to cook | @deva[पक्तुम्] |
| @deva[दा] | to give | @deva[दातुम्] | @deva[वच्] | to cut | @deva[वक्तुम्] or @deva[वच्तुम्] |
| @deva[नी] | to carry | @deva[नेतुम्] | @deva[गुह्] | to cover | @deva[गुहितुम्] or @deva[गूढुम्] |
| @deva[कृ] | to do | @deva[कर्तुम्] | @deva[सह्] | to bear | @deva[सहितुम्] or @deva[सोढुम्] |
| @deva[भू] | to be | @deva[भवितुम्] | @deva[चुर्] | to steal | @deva[चोरयितुम्] |
| @deva[यू] | to shake | @deva[यवितुम्] or @deva[यौतुम्] | @deva[बुध्] | Cau. | @deva[बोधयितुम्] |
| @deva[वृ] | to choose | @deva[वरितुम्] or @deva[वरीतुम्] | | Desi. | @deva[बुबुधिषितुम्] |
| @deva[गै] | to sing | @deva[गातुम्] | | Ātm. Freq. | @deva[बोबुध्यितुम्] |
| @deva[गम्] | to go | @deva[गन्तुम्] | &c. | &c. | &c. |
