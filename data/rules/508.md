## Veṭ Roots with Mandatory इ and Optional Forms

The roots @deva[स्तृह्], @deva[धू] and @deva[धू] admit @deva[इ] necessarily before consonantal terminations except @deva[व]; @deva[स्तृह्]—@deva[सस्तृहिव] 1 dual, @deva[सस्तृहिथ], @deva[सस्तृहोथ] 2 sing.; @deva[धुयाविथ], @deva[धुषोथ] 2 sing. of @deva[धू], &c.

[This rule contains extensive paradigm tables for many @deva[वेट्] (@[Veṭ]) roots with optional forms, including @deva[तञ्ज्], @deva[त्रश्], @deva[भञ्ज्], @deva[मृज्], @deva[अञ्ज्], @deva[स्निह्], @deva[स्यन्द्], @deva[रन्ध्], @deva[सिध्], @deva[कॢप्], @deva[तृप्], @deva[त्रप्], @deva[क्षम्], @deva[क्षिप्], @deva[अनू], @deva[क्षिश्र्], @deva[नश्], @deva[अक्ष्], @deva[कुश्], @deva[गाह्], @deva[गुह्], @deva[तुह्], @deva[तूह्], @deva[दुह्], @deva[स्तुह्], @deva[मुह्], @deva[वह्], @deva[स्निह्], and @deva[स्नेह्].]

@note[type=note]{@deva[रन्ध्] and @deva[जन्हु] insert a nasal when their final is followed by a vowel. @deva[रन्ध्], however, does not do it in the Aorist or when it takes @deva[इ], except in the Perf.}

@note[type=note]{Roots ending in @deva[म्] change to @deva[न्] when followed by @deva[म्] or @deva[व्].}

@note[type=note]{The final @deva[ह्] of the roots @deva[दुह्], @deva[मुह्], @deva[स्निह्], and @deva[स्तुह्] is changed to @deva[घ्] or @deva[ढ्] when followed by any consonant except a nasal or a semi-vowel or by nothing.}

@note[type=note]{When @deva[द्] or @deva[ढ्] is dropped, the preceding @deva[अ], @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] is lengthened.}
