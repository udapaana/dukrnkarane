P. P. Participles have sometimes the sense of neuter abstract nouns; as @deva[वचितं] 'speech', @deva[शयितं] 'sleep', @deva[हसितं] 'laughing,' similarly @deva[स्थितं], @deva[गतं], etc.; See Bhaṭṭ. VII. 125.
