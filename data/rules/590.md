## Three Types of Passive Verbs

There are three kinds of Passive Verbs:

1. **The Passive properly so called** (@deva[कर्मणि प्र॰]), as @deva[राघेण क्रयं दीयते]

2. **An Impersonal Passive** (@deva[भावे प्र॰]), as @deva[गच्यते] 'it is gone'

3. **A Reflexive** (@deva[आत्मनेपदी प्र॰]), as @deva[ओदनं पच्यते] 'rice is cooked'
