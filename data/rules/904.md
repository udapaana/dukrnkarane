Besides showing simple futurity participle expresses intention or purpose; @deva[अनुगमिष्यत् मुनितनयाम्] wishing to follow the daughter of the sage; @deva[दास्यत्] wishing to give, @deva[वनचरान् दमयिष्यन्] intending exercising, as it were, to tame the wild beasts.
