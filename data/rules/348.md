The words @deva[दाराः] 'a wife', @deva[अक्षताः] 'uninjured rice', @deva[लाजाः] 'fried rice', @deva[असवः] 'life (the vital airs in the body)' and @deva[गृहः] 'a house', which are always used in the plural.
