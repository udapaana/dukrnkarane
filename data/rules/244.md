All the rules given above for the change of the finals of the Tat. compounds do not hold good in the case of the Neg. Tat.;[^1] e. g. @deva[न राजा अराजा] one not a king; @deva[न सखा: असखा], &c.

**(a)** But in the case of the word @deva[पथिन्] the final @deva[इन्] is optionally dropped and @deva[अ] added; @deva[अपथं] or @deva[अपथ्या:] 'absence of a road'. @deva[पथिन्] when so modified in a Tat. is neu.;[^2] (but @deva[अपथ्य: देश:]).
