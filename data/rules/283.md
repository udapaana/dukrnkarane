@deva[अ] is added to the finals of the following—[^1]

**(a)** @deva[वर्चस्] preceded by @deva[ब्रह्मन्] or @deva[हस्तिन्]; @deva[ब्रह्मवर्चसम्] 'the divine Majesty of Bramhan or the glory of a Brâhmana'; 'pre-eminence or sanctity arising from sacred knowledge'; @deva[हस्तिवर्चसम्] 'the splendour or magnificence of an elephant'.

**(b)** @deva[तमस्] preceded by @deva[अव], @deva[सु] and @deva[अन्ध]; e. g. @deva[अवतमं तमः अवतमसम्] 'slight darkness,' @deva[संततं तमः संतमसम्] 'great or continuous darkness; @deva[अन्धं तमः अन्धतमसम्] 'pitchy darkness.'

**(c)** @deva[रहस्] preceded by @deva[एक], @deva[अप] or @deva[तप्]; @deva[एकगतं रहः एकरहसम्] 'secret, solitary'; @deva[अपगतं रहः अपरहसम्] 'a little secret,' or 'desolate'; @deva[तप्तं रहः तपरहसम्] 'a hot secluded place.'

**(d)** @deva[उरस्] in the Loc. and preceded by @deva[प्रति]; @deva[उरसि प्रति यद् उरसम्] 'against the breast.'

**(e)** @deva[गौ] preceded by @deva[अनु], when length is implied; @deva[अनुगव यावत्] 'a vehicle measuring as much as a bull in length.'
