## Insertion of व, रि, or रु in Parasmaipada Frequentatives

@deva[व] or the syllable @deva[रि] or @deva[रु] is inserted between the @deva[अ] of the reduplicative syllable and the radical consonant of roots ending in short @deva[ऋ] or having it for their penultimate.

Similarly @deva[रि], @deva[रु] or @deva[ल] or @deva[लु] is inserted in the case of @deva[कृत्]:

@deva[लृत्]—@deva[ल्लृत्]—@deva[वलृद्] or @deva[वरिलृद्] or @deva[वरूलृद्] or @deva[ललृ]—@deva[लु]

@deva[छु]—@deva[चोछु], or @deva[चारिछु] or @deva[चारूछु]
