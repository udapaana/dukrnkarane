## Augment व् in Causals of Roots Ending in आ

Roots ending in @deva[आ] (@deva[ओ], @deva[औ] or @deva[ऐ] changeable to @deva[आ]; see @ref[459]) and the roots @deva[ऋ] 'to go,' @deva[हृ] 'to be ashamed,' @deva[हु] 'to go,' (9 cl.), 'to flow' (4. cl. Â.) and @deva[प्लु] 'to choose, to go,' insert the augment @deva[व्] before @deva[अय्] and their vowel takes Guṇa substitute.

**Examples:**
- @deva[दा], @deva[धा], @deva[हा]—@deva[दापयति]
- @deva[हृ]—@deva[हेपयति]
- @deva[हु], &c.—@deva[ह्लावयति]
- @deva[ऋ]—@deva[अर्पयति]
- @deva[प्लु]—@deva[प्लावयति]
- @deva[प्लु]—@deva[प्लोपयति]
