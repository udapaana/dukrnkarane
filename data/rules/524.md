## Samprasāraṇa in Reduplicative Syllable of व्यथ्

The root @deva[व्यथ्] takes Samprasāraṇa in the reduplicative syllable in the Perfect; @deva[विव्यथे] 1 & 3 sing., @deva[विव्यथिषे] 2 sing.
