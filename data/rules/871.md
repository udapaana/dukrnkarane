## Ablative/Locative for Interval of Time or Space

Words expressive of the interval of time or space are used with the Ablative or the Locative; @deva[अद्य भुक्त्वा द्विकात् द्विके द्वाहात् भोक्ता] having dined to-day he will dine again after two days; @deva[समस्थितम् शुनत द्विकात्] or @deva[द्वयोजनम्] frozen standing here as a river a mark two miles distant.[^1]
