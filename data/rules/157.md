The pronominal adverbs that are most commonly met with are derived from the pronouns @deva[तद्], @deva[एतद्], @deva[यद्], @deva[इदम्], @deva[किम्], and @deva[सर्व] and the pronominal adjectives @deva[पूर्व्], @deva[पर] &c, by means of such affixes as @deva[तस्], @deva[त्र], [^1]@deva[ह], @deva[क] &c. added in the sense of the Ab. or the Loc., @deva[दा], [^2]@deva[दानीम्], @deva[हि] &c. showing time, [^3]@deva[तात्] showing point of time, place or direction, @deva[आ], @deva[आत्], @deva[आदि] &c. expressing direction, @deva[था], @deva[थ][^4] &c. expressing way or manner &c.; e. g.

@deva[तद्]...........................@deva[तदा], then; @deva[तदानीम्], at that time; @deva[तर्हि] then, therefore; @deva[तथा], so, @deva[तत्र], there; @deva[ततः], thence, thereupon, therefore, &c.

@deva[इदम्].........................@deva[इदानीम्], now; @deva[इत्थम्], thus; @deva[अत्र], here; @deva[अतः], therefore; @deva[इतः], from this, hence; @deva[अधुना], now; @deva[इह], here.

@deva[एतद्]..........................@deva[एतर्हि], now; @deva[इत्थम्] thus; @deva[अतः] hence, therefore; @deva[अत्र] here.

@deva[यद्]...........................@deva[यर्हि], when; @deva[यदा] when; @deva[यथा] as; @deva[यत्र] where; @deva[यतः], whence,, since, because.

@deva[किम्]..........................@deva[कर्हि], when; @deva[कदा], when; @deva[कथम्], how; @deva[कुत्र], where; @deva[क्व], where; @deva[कुतः], where, whence; @deva[कुतः], whence, how.

@deva[सर्व्]..........................@deva[सर्वदा], always; @deva[सदा], always; @deva[सर्वतः], everywhere, on all sides; @deva[सर्वत्र] everywhere, in all places.

@deva[पर]...........................@deva[परतः] further on, beyond &c.

@deva[पूर्व्]..........................@deva[पुरः], @deva[पुरस्तात्] before, in front, &c.

@deva[अधर].........................@deva[अधः], @deva[अधस्तात्] or @deva[अधरस्तात्], @deva[अधरतः], @deva[अधरात्] down, below.

@deva[अवर]..........................@deva[अवः], @deva[अवस्तात्] or @deva[अवरस्तात्], @deva[अवरतः], behind, below, downwards.

@deva[अपर]..........................@deva[पश्चात्], from behind, afterwards, westward, &c.

@deva[दक्षिण].......................@deva[दक्षिणा], @deva[दक्षिणात्], @deva[दक्षिणाहि] to or in the south, on the right side.

@deva[उत्तर].........................@deva[उत्तरा] @deva[उत्तरात्], @deva[उत्तराहि] to or in the north, &c.
