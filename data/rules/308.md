**(a)** A prātipadika ending in @deva[कर], except @deva[धक्कर], @deva[तक्कर], @deva[स्किकर] and @deva[बहुकर]; @deva[भ]. @deva[सर], preceded by @deva[यत्]; @deva[अमर]: @deva[अमे] and @deva[तुद्]; and @deva[खर] preceded by @deva[सेवा]. @deva[द्वाप] and words expressive of place; the words @deva[नद], @deva[चोर], @deva[देव], @deva[भाइ], @deva[भर]. @deva[व्छय], and @deva[नह्], nominal bases ending in @deva[ण्य], nominal and verbal derivatives formed by the affix @deva[अ] causing Vṛddhi or Guṇa, such as @deva[ओढनः], @deva[ओसन:], @deva[कुम्भकारः], @deva[भावहारः], @deva[पाठ्यः], @deva[ताइम:], &c. and words ending in the Tad. affixes @deva[इयस्], @deva[दुह], @deva[माच] and @deva[इक] (with some exceptions) and in the kṛit affix @deva[स्वर] form their feminine in @deva[ई] when they are not used adjectively;[^1] e. g. @deva[भोगकरी] conducing to pleasures; @deva[एककरी] &c.; @deva[पतिसी], @deva[सिनसी], &c.; @deva[अविसरी], &c.; @deva[सेनाखरी], @deva[कुरुखरी], a woman of the Kuru country; @deva[मत्स्यखरी], &c.; @deva[नदी], @deva[देवी], @deva[सटी], &c.; @deva[सौपर्णी], @deva[वैनतेयी], &c.; @deva[ऐन्द्री], @deva[औरसी], &c.; @deva[कुम्भकारी], @deva[नगरकारी], &c.; @deva[उरुद्यसी-दुही-माचां] measuring or reaching as far as the knee; &c. @deva[आशिथकी], @deva[छागणकी], &c.; @deva[पाटसी], @deva[ताइसी], @deva[स्वरी], evanescent, an unchaste woman &c., @deva[गल्वरी] &c., but @deva[स्किकरा]. @deva[बहुकुरारा नगरी].

**(b)** Also words ending in the Tad. affixes @deva[न], @deva[स्न], @deva[इक्]. @deva[य] (causing Vṛiddhi) and the words @deva[नदुन] and @deva[नठुन];[^2] @deva[धोणी]. @deva[गोत्नी] fit for a man; @deva[शाकोकी], @deva[तरुणी], @deva[नठुनी], &c. also words ending in the kṛit. affix @deva[अन] causing a nasal to be inserted before it; @deva[आह्वेमकरणी].[^3]

**(c)** Words indicative of the stages of life except the last take the fem. @deva[ई];[^4] @deva[कुमारी], @deva[किशोरी], @deva[बक्री], @deva[बष्टी], @deva[बिरण्टी] (both meaning a 'a young woman'); but @deva[वृद्धा]. @deva[स्थविरा] &c. as these imply old age; @deva[कन्या] is an exception.

**(d)** **Obs**—The words @deva[केवल], @deva[मामक], @deva[भागधेय] @deva[धाण], @deva[अमर], @deva[समान], @deva[आर्येष्ठ], @deva[छन्दोगाल] and @deva[भेषज] form their fem. in @deva[ई] when they are used as names ( or in the Veda ), @deva[केवली], @deva[मामकी], @deva[समानी], @deva[आर्येष्ठी] &c., but @deva[केवला उमाना], &c. when they are not names.[^5]

**(e)** The words @deva[नर्तक], @deva[खनक], @deva[रञ्जक] and @deva[रनक], those derived by adding the Krit affixes @deva[आक], and @deva[अ] ( added to certain roots ) and the words included in the @deva[गोरादिगण] form their fem. in @deva[ई];[^6] @deva[नर्तकी], @deva[रञ्जकी] &c. @deva[छुट्टकी] one who divides or cuts; @deva[लुण्टाकी] robbing or stealing, @deva[भाङ्गी], &c.; @deva[गोरी], @deva[मञ्जरी], @deva[चुङ्गी], @deva[हरिणी], @deva[मातामही], @deva[पितामही] &c.; @deva[सुन्दर] has @deva[सुन्दरा] and @deva[सुन्दरी].
