## Vowel Shortening Before Vṛddhi in Causals

Roots ending in @deva[अय्], except @deva[अय्] 'to go' &c., @deva[कथ्] 'to love,' @deva[वस्] 'to eat,' @deva[दृश्] when it means 'to see' and @deva[यस्] when it does not mean 'to eat,' and the roots[^1] marked with an indicatory @deva[ङ्] (e. g., @deva[वण्], &c.), shorten their vowel before taking its Vṛddhi substitute.

**Examples:**
- @deva[पन्]—@deva[पानयति]
- @deva[कथ्]—@deva[कामयति]
- @deva[वद्]—@deva[वन्दयति]
- @deva[जन्]—@deva[जन्वयति]
- @deva[क्रय्]—@deva[क्रयापयति]
- @deva[दुह्]—@deva[दुःपयति]
- @deva[आक्]—@deva[आकपयति]
- @deva[जा]—@deva[जंपयति], &c.

**Exceptions:**
- @deva[इम्]—@deva[इमायति]
- @deva[चक्ष्]—@deva[चक्षयति]
- @deva[शुश्] 'to see'—@deva[शुशयति], but @deva[इमयति] in other cases
- @deva[पम्]—@deva[पामयति], &c.
- @deva[यश्] 'to eat'—@deva[यशयति]

### (c) Optional Lengthening

The roots @deva[पत्], @deva[नम्], @deva[वद्], @deva[ग्रन्थ्], @deva[रुद्], and @deva[इर्], lengthen their vowel optionally when not preceded by a preposition.

**Examples:**
- @deva[नमयति], @deva[नामयति]
- with preposition: @deva[प्रणमयति], &c.
