Bases ending in @deva[वत्] or @deva[ईवत्]—

These are the perfect participles derived from roots with the affix @deva[वत्] to which the intermediate @deva[इ] is prefixed in some cases. They have a @deva[न्] prefixed to their final @deva[त्] in the first five inflections, the penultimate @deva[अ] being lengthened before it. In the Nom. sing. m. the @deva[त्] is dropped and the Voc. sing. ends in @deva[वन्]. @deva[इ] is substituted for @deva[अ], before which the preceding short @deva[इ], if any, is dropped, before the vowel terminations beginning with those of the Acc. plu. and before the @deva[ई] of the Nom. Voc. and Acc. dual of the neuter nouns. The final @deva[त्] of a root is changed to @deva[द्] before @deva[वत्] reappears before this @deva[इ]; final @deva[त्] is changed to @deva[द्] before the consonantal terminations and in the Nom. Voc. and Acc. sing of the neuter.
