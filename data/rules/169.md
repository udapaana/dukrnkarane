**(a)** @deva[सकृत्] once, @deva[द्विः] twice, @deva[त्रिः] thrice, @deva[चतुः] four times; from @deva[पञ्चन्] onwards adverbs of frequency are formed by adding @deva[कृत्वः] before which final @deva[न्] is dropped e. g. @deva[पञ्चकृत्वः] five times, @deva[सप्तकृत्वः] seven times, &c.

**(b)** @deva[एकधा][^1] or @deva[ऐकध्यम्] in one way or part, @deva[द्विधा] or @deva[द्वेधा] or @deva[द्वैधं] in two ways or parts, @deva[त्रिधा] or @deva[त्रेधा] or @deva[त्रैध्यम्] in three ways, &c.; @deva[चतुर्धा] in four ways, @deva[षोढा] or @deva[षड्धा] in six ways, @deva[सप्तधा], @deva[अष्टधा], &c.

**(c)** @deva[एकशः] one by one; @deva[द्विशः] two and two, by twos; so @deva[त्रिशः] @deva[शतशः] &c.
