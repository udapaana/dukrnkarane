## Dative with छंद् (Promise)

@deva[छंद्] with @deva[प्रति] and @deva[आ] meaning 'to promise,' governs the Dative of the person to whom a promise is made after solicitation @deva[विप्राय गां प्रतिजानोति आप्यानोति वा] (Sid. Kau.)[^1]
