A prātipodika ending in short @deva[क] or @deva[द] forms its feminine by the addition of @deva[ई];[^1] @deva[कर्क-कर्की], @deva[दण्डयु-दण्डयुनी], @deva[स्वनी], @deva[मञ्जनी], @deva[परिदेवयु-परिदेवनी]; &c.

**N. B.** Words like @deva[स्वसु], @deva[ननान्दु], @deva[विधु], @deva[हरितु], @deva[स्वश्रु] &c. being themselves feminine do not take this termination.

**(a)** @deva[युवन्] forms its feminine by the addition of @deva[ति] before which the final @deva[न्] is dropped; @deva[युवतिः]

---

[^1]: @deva[क्रयेभ्यो ङीप्] । Pāṇ. IV. 1. 5.
