Before the affix @deva[ई]—[^1]

**(a)** nouns ending in consonants assume that form which they take before the termination of Inst. sing.; @deva[प्रत्यच्-श्-प्रतीची], @deva[राजन्—राज्ञी], @deva[भवत्—भवती], @deva[श्वन्—शुनी], @deva[अर्यमन्—आर्येणी], @deva[विद्वस्—विदुषी], &c. There are some exceptions, @deva[अर्हन्—अर्हती] &c.

**(b)** the preceding @deva[अ] or @deva[ई] is dropped; as @deva[गोर—गोरी आटी श—दूत्ती], @deva[पाम्वती]. &c.

**(c)** if a Prâtipadika end in @deva[य], part of a Taddhita affix, that @deva[य] is dropped @deva[गार्ग्य + ई = गार्ग्याः] 'the daughter of Garga.' &c.

**(d)** the final @deva[य] of the words @deva[सूर्य], @deva[तिष्य] the constellation of stars called @deva[पुष्य], @deva[अगस्त्य] and @deva[मत्स्य] is dropped;[^2] as @deva[सौरी], @deva[मत्सी], &c.

**(e)** in the case of the participles of the present and the future @deva[न] is inserted, as in the Nom. dual of the neu. (vide @ref[311]. a. b.); for instances, see @ref[236].

[^1]: @deva[हलन्तच्छपिदस्य] । Pān VI. 4. 150. For the meaning of the term Prâtipadika vide @ref[52].

[^2]: @deva[सूर्यतिष्यागस्त्यमत्स्यानां य उपधायाः] । Pan VI. 4. 149.
