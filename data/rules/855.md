## Genitive with Words Denoting Frequency of Time

Words having the senses of @deva[छन्दः] i. e. denoting frequency of time, such as @deva[द्विः], @deva[त्रिः], @deva[पुनः], &c. govern the Genitive of the time in the sense of the Locative; @deva[पञ्चकृत्वः भोजनम्] taking food five times a day; @deva[द्विरहो शतकम्], &c.[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. II. 3. 64
