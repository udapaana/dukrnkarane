## Particles Used with Certain Words

The following particles are used with certain words:

### @deva[अह]

@deva[अद्भुतम्] 'a wonder'

### @deva[का]

@deva[कापुरुष] 'a bad man', @deva[कौष्णम्] 'luke-warm', @deva[काजलम्] 'a little water'

### @deva[कु]

@deva[कुकृत्यम्] 'a bad deed'

### @deva[चन] / @deva[चित्]

@deva[किञ्चित्], @deva[कश्चित्], @deva[कञ्चन], @deva[किञ्चन], &c.

### @deva[न]

is generally changed to @deva[अ] or @deva[अन्] when prefixed to words beginning with a consonant or a vowel respectively. This particle has six different senses:[^1]

1. **@deva[सादृश्य]** 'likeness' or 'resemblance'; as @deva[अब्राह्मण] 'one not a Brāhmaṇa, but resembling a Brāhmaṇa' (wearing the sacred thread, &c.) i.e. 'a Kṣatriya or a Vaiśya'
2. **@deva[अभाव]** 'absence or negation'; @deva[अज्ञान] 'the absence of knowledge'
3. **@deva[अन्यत्व]** 'difference from'; as @deva[अयं अपट] 'this is something different from a cloth' i.e. 'a jar' or so
4. **@deva[अल्पता]** 'littleness' or 'smallness'; as @deva[अनुदरा कन्या] 'a girl having a slender waist'
5. **@deva[अप्राशस्त्य]** 'badness, unfitness', &c.; @deva[अकार्यम्] 'something unfit to be done', @deva[अकाल] 'an improper time, not a favourable opportunity'
6. **@deva[विरोध]** or 'opposition'; @deva[अनीति] 'non-morality; immorality', @deva[असुर] 'opposed to a god' i.e. 'a demon'

### @deva[म]

is generally used as an expletive. Used with a form of the present tense of a verb, it gives it the sense of the past tense; as @deva[भवति स्म] i.e. @deva[अभवत्]. When used with the particle @deva[मा] it has an intensive force; as @deva[मा स्म शोकं मनः कुर्यात्], &c.

### @deva[स्विद्]

is added to @deva[कि] and other indeclinables and asks a question or implies a doubt; @deva[किन्विद्], @deva[अहोस्विद्], &c.

### @deva[स्वी]

is used with @deva[कृ] and its derivatives like a preposition in the sense of 'acceptance'; @deva[स्वीकार], @deva[स्वीकृतम्], &c.

[^1]: These are given in the following couplet: @deva[तद्सादृश्यभावश्च तदन्यत्वं तदल्पता । अप्राशस्त्यं विरोधश्च नञार्थाः षट् प्रकीर्तिताः]
