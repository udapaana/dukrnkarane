A Bah. ending in @deva[पाद्] (substituted for @deva[पाद] ) optionally forms its fem. in @deva[ई] before which it is changed to @deva[पद्];[^1] @deva[व्याघ्रस्य इव पादौ यस्याः सा व्याघ्रपाद्-पदी], @deva[सिंहपाद्-पदी] and in @deva[आ] when @deva[पाद्] means 'a foot of a Vedic verse'; @deva[द्विपदा छन्द्], @deva[एकपदा] &c.

**(a)** But if @deva[पाद्] be preceded by one of the words @deva[कुम्भ], @deva[शाल], @deva[तूण], @deva[हंस], @deva[काक], @deva[शकुन्], @deva[सूकर]. &c. the fem. is necessarily formed in @deva[ई]. @deva[पाद्] changing into @deva[पद्] as before; @deva[कुम्भपदी] 'a woman whose feet are as big as a pitcher.'

**(b)** In other cases the fem. of pratipadikas ending in @deva[पाद्] is formed by adding @deva[आ]; @deva[हरिपादा], @deva[अजपादा], &c.

[^1]: @deva[पादोऽन्यतरस्याम्] । @deva[वाङिच] । Pāṇ. IV.1. 8, 9.
