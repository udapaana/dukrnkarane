## Adjective Agreement with Substantives

An adjective, participial or qualitative, must agree with the substantive it qualifies in gender, number and case; @deva[रूपवान् नरः] a handsome man; @deva[रूपवती स्त्री] a beautiful woman; @deva[गतदर्शनं च] a great calamity; @deva[एते मनोराः गतिं पत्तीति]; @deva[गच्छन्तो नरः], &c.

But numeral adjectives of fixed gender and number remain unchanged:—@deva[शतं ब्राह्मणाः] a hundred Brāhmaṇas; @deva[शतं स्त्रियः] a hundred women, @deva[विंशतिः बालकानि] twenty children.
