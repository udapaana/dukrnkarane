## Special Cases: इ and ऊह् in Benedictive

@deva[इ] 'to go'—@deva[ईयासम्]; but shortens its @deva[ई] after a preposition, @deva[सनियासम्]; Ātm· @deva[एषीय].

@deva[ऊह्] also, when joined with prepositions, shortens its @deva[ऊ] before weak terminations beginning with @deva[य]; @deva[संहुयासम्].
