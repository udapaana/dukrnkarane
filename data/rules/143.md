Correlatives are formed by the affix @deva[वत्] added to @deva[यद्], @deva[तद्] and @deva[एतद्], and @deva[यत्] added to @deva[इदम्] and @deva[किम्] in the sense of as much as; and @deva[दृश] or @deva[दृश्] in the sense of 'similar to'; @deva[तद्], @deva[एतद्] and @deva[यह] become @deva[ता], @deva[एता] and @deva[या] respectively before these, and @deva[इदम्] and @deva[किम्] @deva[इ] and @deva[कि] before @deva[वत्] and @deva[ई] and @deva[की] before @deva[दृश] or @deva[दृश्]; e. g. @deva[तावत्] (@deva[तत्परिमाणमस्य]), @deva[इयत्] (@deva[इदं परिमाणमस्य]), @deva[तादृश] like that, @deva[ईदृश], like this, @deva[कियत्] how much, &c.
