The roots @deva[भू] and @deva[गै] take the affix @deva[यत्]; @deva[स्था] takes @deva[अनीय]; and @deva[वद्], @deva[वृ] and @deva[पच्] take @deva[घ्यत्], in an active sense; @deva[अतुवोर्थो भव्यः] (also @deva[भव्यभूतनं]); @deva[गायतु गेयः] 'one who sings' (also @deva[गेयं रामे अनेन]); @deva[वाच्यः] 'one who speaks' @deva[उत्], @deva[स्थानीयः] 'one who stands for,' @deva[स्थाय], @deva[वर्यः बधित्ययोः], @deva[पाच्यः] 'food.'

**(3) By means of केलिम् (एलिम्).**
