Decline all nouns ending in @deva[अ] mas. and neu. similarly.

**(a)** The Loc. sing. of words ending in @deva[अह्] (substituted for @deva[अहन्] a day, at the end of a Tat. comp.) is optionally formed like that of nouns ending in @deva[न्]; e. g. @deva[स्वह्] has @deva[स्वहे], @deva[स्वहि] or @deva[स्वहनि]. So @deva[त्यहे], @deva[त्यहि] or @deva[त्यहनि] &c. See p. 73.
