The neuter forms @deva[भविष्यत्] and @deva[भाव्यम्] are used impersonally in the sense of 'being,' 'not being' this can be on all probability i. e. ' the noun denoting the agent being put in the Instrumental case: @deva[अत्र कस्यापि कारणेन भविष्यत्] there must be some cause; @deva[नद्यां सन्नादानुरूपं प्रभावेन भविष्यत्] or @deva[भाव्यम्] in all probability his strength must be corresponding to his sound; @deva[आरूढो भूतपुरस्य पुरस्सरैः भाव्यम्] (in all likelihood) be seated in the carriage, &c.
