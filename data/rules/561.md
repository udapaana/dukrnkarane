## Anit Roots Taking the Seventh Form

Anit roots ending in @deva[घ्र], @deva[इ], @deva[स्], and @deva[द्] and having @deva[इ], @deva[उ], @deva[ऊ] or @deva[ऋ] for their penultimate take this Form. @deva[गृह्] is an exception; it therefore takes the Fourth Variety.
