The roots @deva[शक्नु] and @deva[स्था] are generally used with present participles to show the continuity of the action denoted by them; @deva[पशून् वधं कुर्वन् आसीत्] used to kill (always kept on killing) animals; @deva[तं प्रतीक्षन्तं तस्थे] remained waiting for him.
