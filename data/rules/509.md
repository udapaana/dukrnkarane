## Nasal-Dropping Roots in Perfect

The roots @deva[श्रन्थु], @deva[मन्थु], @deva[रम्भु] and @deva[स्वञ्ज्] drop their nasal optionally before the terminations of the Perfect. @deva[श्रन्थु], @deva[मन्थु] and @deva[रम्भु] obey @ref[500], even before the strong terminations when their nasal is dropped.
