Other derivatives from the numerals:—

**(a)** by the Taddhita affix @deva[क] which is added to numerals ending in @deva[शत्] and @deva[ति] e. g. @deva[पञ्चक:] bought for five (rupees &c.) @deva[चत्वारिंशक:] bought for forty; @deva[विंशतिक:] bought for twenty (coins, &c.)

**(b)** by means of the Tad. affix @deva[तय] in the sense of "consisting of parts" or "collection"; e.g. @deva[चतुष्टय] (@deva[चतुष्टयी] fem.) consisting of four parts or a collection of four; so @deva[पञ्चतय] (fem. @deva[यी]); @deva[तय] is optionally changed to @deva[अय] after @deva[द्वि] and @deva[त्रि] e. g. @deva[द्वय] or @deva[द्वितय] (@deva[-यी] fem.) twofold, consisting of two parts, or a pair; @deva[त्रय] or @deva[त्रितय] (@deva[-यी] fem.) threefold or consisting of three parts, a collection of three, a triad.

**(c)** by means of @deva[क] and @deva[अय] e. g. @deva[षट्] a collection of six, @deva[पञ्चक्] collection of five, @deva[दशक्] a collection of ten, a decade, &c.
