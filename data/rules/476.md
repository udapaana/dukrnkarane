@deva[त] is inserted before the ending consonant of the root @deva[मस्] before which @deva[स] is dropped, and after the vowel of @deva[नश्], @deva[पुरु] and @deva[दिवु] followed by any consonant except a nasal or a semi-vowel: @deva[मग्धासि] &c.; @deva[नत्तासि], @deva[मंस्तासि]. The @deva[स] of @deva[मस्] is changed to @deva[य] when not dropped.
