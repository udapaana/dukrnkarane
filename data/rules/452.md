@deva[दा] and @deva[धा] drop their @deva[आ] after reduplication before weak terminations; @deva[दध्] becomes @deva[घद्] before @deva[स्], @deva[ध्व], @deva[त] and @deva[थ]. The Par. Imperative 2 per. singulars are @deva[देहि] and @deva[धेहि] respectively.
