## Insertion of री in Ātmanepada Frequentatives with Penultimate ऋ

When a root contains a penultimate @deva[ऋ] (@deva[ऋ]) original or brought in by Samprasāraṇa, the syllable @deva[री] is inserted between the @deva[अ] of the reduplicative syllable (which remains short against @ref[629] b) and the radical consonant:

@deva[पृट्]—@deva[पूर्यंय] = @deva[पुपूर्यंयते]

@deva[भञ्ज्]—@deva[भुञ्जय] = @deva[पुञ्जयते]

@deva[पृञ्]—@deva[परीयते]

@deva[मृड्] = @deva[मरीयते]
