In the ninth class @deva[न] is inserted between the root and the terminations. @deva[न] becomes @deva[ण] before the weak terminations beginning with a vowel, and @deva[नी] before the weak terminations beginning with a consonant.[^1]

[^1]: @deva[क्र्यादिभ्यो न:] । Pāṇ. III. 1. 81.
