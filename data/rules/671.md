## Extended Use of आन for Habit and Capacity

The termination @deva[आन] may be added to any root in the sense of 'in the habit of' or 'to show a particular standard of age, or capacity to do a thing':

### Examples

@deva[भोगं भुञ्जानः] 'one habituated to enjoy pleasures'

@deva[सत्यं विद्यानः] 'of a sufficient age, i.e. young enough to learn an art'

@deva[संयुयं विज्ञानं] 'able to kill the enemy', etc.
