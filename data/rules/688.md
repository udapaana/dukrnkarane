@deva[न][^1] is substituted for @deva[त्] when it immediately follows a final @deva[इ] or @deva[ऋ]; this final @deva[इ] is also changed to @deva[ई]; @deva[शि]—@deva[शीन], @deva[ऋ]—@deva[ऋण]; @deva[पुल्]—@deva[पूर्ण] (See @ref[693]).

**Exceptions:—**

(a) @deva[शीग्] has @deva[शीतं] when it means 'a part, a portion,' @deva[शीनं] in other senses.

(b) @deva[विङ्] 6. P. A. takes @deva[त्] in the sense of 'fit for enjoyment or famous,' @deva[विक्तः] 'wealth, property,' @deva[विकः] 'famous'; @deva[विनः] in other cases.

(c) @deva[भृङ्], @deva[पूर्] and @deva[चुस्छु]; @deva[भृतः], @deva[पूर्णः] 'filled,' (also @deva[पूतः] when it means the same as @deva[अत्]), @deva[चुस्छुः].

[^1]: @deva[इदुपधावो न्पनादौ नः पुशिप्य च हि] । @deva[परयोप्यस्तोऽसत्त्वानुपेत्य च] । @deva[स्यादियूस्] । Pāṇ. VIII. 2. 42–44.
