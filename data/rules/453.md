@deva[भी] optionally shortens its vowel before consonantal weak terminations.

(a) @deva[मा] and @deva[हा] 'to go' assume the forms @deva[मिम्] and @deva[जिह्] before vowel terminations and @deva[मिमो] and @deva[जिहो] before consonantal terminations.
