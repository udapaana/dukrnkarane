[^1]The present participle is used to denote the manner in which an action is done or the cause or object of an action; @deva[यवना अञ्चितं भुञ्जते] the Yavanas dine by lying down; @deva[हरिं पश्यन् मुच्यते] a man is absolved by (means of his) seeing Hari; similarly @deva[दर्शयन्], @deva[सर्वस्य शतम्] (Mb.)
