## Special Causal Forms with व् Augment

### (a) Roots Changing Final Vowel to आ

@deva[भू] 'to throw,' @deva[पीड्] 'to destroy,' @deva[जि] 'to conquer,' and @deva[क्री] 'to buy,' also insert @deva[व्], but after changing their final vowel to @deva[आ].

**Examples:**
- @deva[भावयति]
- @deva[पीडापयति]
- @deva[जापयति]
- @deva[क्रापयति]

### (b) Vowel Shortening Before व्

The roots @deva[प्लु], @deva[भू] or @deva[पू] 'to cook' and @deva[भा] (mit.), shorten their vowel necessarily, and @deva[प्लु] and @deva[स्तु] optionally, before the inserted @deva[व्] when not preceded by prepositions.

**Examples:**
- @deva[प्लुपयति], &c.
- @deva[पूपयति], @deva[भापयति]
- @deva[दापयति], @deva[प्लुपयति], @deva[रम्भूपयति], @deva[वर्पयाति], @deva[स्वापयति]

**With prepositions:**
- @deva[प्रदापयति], @deva[प्रम्भूपयति] only
