An Anusvāra followed by any consonant except @deva[म्], @deva[य], @deva[र्] or @deva[ह्] is changed to the nasal of the class to which the following letter belongs, necessarily when in the middle, and optionally when at the end of a word[^1]; e.g. @deva[अङ्क्] + @deva[इतः] = @deva[अं] + @deva[क्] + @deva[इतः] (by the preceding rule and then) @deva[अङ्कितः] marked (by this); so @deva[अर्च्] + @deva[इतः] = @deva[अर्चितः] honoured; @deva[छण्डितः] obstructed; @deva[शान्तः] calmed; @deva[युक्तितः] woven; @deva[त्वम्] + @deva[करोषि] = @deva[त्वं करोषि] or @deva[त्वङ्करोषि] thou doest; similarly @deva[संयन्ता] or @deva[सय्यन्ता] a restrainer; @deva[संवत्सरः] or @deva[सव्वत्सरः] a year; @deva[यं लोकम्] or @deva[यल्लोकम्] to which people.

---

[^1]: @deva[अनुस्वारस्य ययि परसवर्णः। वा पदान्तस्य] — Pāṇ. VIII. 4. 58.
