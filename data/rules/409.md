**(a)** The penultimate nasal of a root is dropped before @deva[न] &c. as @deva[युनक्ति], @deva[युञ्जत], @deva[युञ्जन्ति] &c. from @deva[युज्] 'to arrange, put together.'

**(b)** In the case of roots ending in a consonant, the imperative second person singular termination is @deva[आन] instead of @deva[हि]; as @deva[सुनान] from @deva[सु] 'to press out juice'.
