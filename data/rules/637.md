## Conjugation of Parasmaipada Frequentatives

### Conjugational Tenses

In the Conjugational Tenses the Parasm. Frequentative is conjugated like a root of the second class.

### General Tenses

In the General Tenses, it is conjugated like a primitive root, Ātm. base taking, however, Ātm. terminations (see @ref[485], @ref[486]).
