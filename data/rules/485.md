## Optional इ before Ārdhadhātuka with स

The roots @deva[कृत्], @deva[वृत्], @deva[गृध्], @deva[वृध्] and @deva[वृश्] take @deva[इ] optionally when followed by an Ārdhadhātuka (Non-conjugational termination) beginning with @deva[स] except in the Aorist; @deva[कृत्]—@deva[कर्तिष्याम], @deva[कर्त्स्याम]; @deva[अकर्तिष्यम्], @deva[अकर्त्स्याम] Cond. &c.
