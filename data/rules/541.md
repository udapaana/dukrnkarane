## Root Changes in Second Aorist

The roots @deva[अस्], @deva[स्था], @deva[पद्], @deva[वच्], @deva[भास्], @deva[स्वि] and @deva[दृश्] are changed to @deva[अर्], @deva[स्त], @deva[पत्], @deva[वच्], @deva[शिष्], @deva[श्व्], and @deva[दर्श्], respectively; as @deva[अवचत्], @deva[अशिषत्], &c. 3rd sing.
