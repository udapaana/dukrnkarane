## Dative with स्तुष्, गुह्, स्था, मा

The roots @deva[स्तुष्] 'to praise,' @deva[गुह्] 'to hide,' @deva[स्था] 'to stand,' and @deva[मा] 'to swear,' govern the Dative of the person to whom some object or feeling is to be conveyed, @deva[गोर्नों स्यसुयद् कृष्णं श्रुयरुते-नूनं-कृष्णं-स्तरीयु] 'a cowherd; else Kṛṣṇa instilled courage into him or apparently conceals her feeling from him or waits for him or swears before him;' (Vide Bhaṭṭi. VII. 73. 74,); but @deva[रानानं स्तुयते मन्त्रो] 'a minister praises his king.'[^1]
