The root @deva[वह्] not denoting an instrument for carrying, and @deva[ऋ] in the sense of 'a master or a Vaishya' take @deva[य]; @deva[वह्यं] 'a carriage or a vehicle;' but @deva[वाह्यः] (@deva[वह्+एयत्]) 'what can be borne,' @deva[आर्यः] 'a master or a Vaishya;' but @deva[आर्येः] (@deva[ऋ+एयत्]) 'fit to be approached or adored.'
