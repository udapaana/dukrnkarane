The @deva[न] is not changed to @deva[अ] or @deva[अन्] in the following cases[^1]:—@deva[नभाः] 'that which does not shine,' 'a cloud;' @deva[नपात्](pres. p.) 'one who does not protect;' @deva[नवेद्यः] 'one who does not know,' @deva[नासत्यौ] [ @deva[न सत्यौ असत्यौ न असत्यौ नासत्यौ][^2] ] the two Aświns, the physicians of the gods. @deva[नहुषिः] [ @deva[न हृष्यतीति]: ] name of a demon slain by Indra; @deva[नकुलः] [ @deva[न कुलस्य], for the mongoose is supposed to belong to no particular species of animals ]; @deva[नख] [ @deva[न खनस्य] that which leaves no cavity, or does not go to heaven being burnt down with the dead body ]; @deva[नपुंसकम्] [ @deva[न स्त्री पुमान्] ]; @deva[नक्षत्रम्] [ @deva[न क्षरतीति] ] that which does not move from its proper place, hence a star; @deva[नक्रः] [ @deva[न क्रामतीति] that which does not go far off from water ]; @deva[नाकः] [ @deva[न कं अकं न अकं आदिपत्] ]; @deva[नञ्] when it does not qualify an animal retains its @deva[न] optionally as @deva[नजः] or @deva[अजः] 'a mountain or a tree,' but @deva[अजः गृष्टः] 'a Sudra that cannot move.'

**N. B.** It should be noted that some of these are Bahuvrihi compounds.
