## Dative with भूष् and प्रच्छ् (Fortune Inquiry)

The roots @deva[भूष्] and @deva[प्रच्छ्] both meaning 'to determine the good or bad fortune of,' govern the Dative of the person with reference to whom the inquiry is made; @deva[कुलाय राष्यति भषते वा । पृच्छो गोपः] (Sid. Kau.)[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. I. 4. 38 and Sid. Kau. commentary
