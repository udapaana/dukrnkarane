## Expressing Desire by Adding Root to Root

The notion of 'desiring' is expressed by adding a root to a root:

### Examples

'to eat salt': @deva[लवणप्यति] = @deva[लवण] + @deva[यु], @deva[यु] meaning 'to take' or 'to desire'

Similarly:

@deva[पुष्पयति]

@deva[लवणगृह्यति] = @deva[लवण] + @deva[गृह्]

@deva[सुकर्षति], etc.
