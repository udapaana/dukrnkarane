## Guṇa Substitution for Final ऋ in Benedictive

Final @deva[ऋ] preceded by a conjunct consonant and the root @deva[ऋ] are changed to Guṇa under the same circumstances.

**Examples:**
- @deva[स्तृ]—@deva[स्तर्यात्]
- @deva[ऋ]—@deva[अर्यात्]
