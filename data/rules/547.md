## Formation of Third Aorist: Reduplication and Vowel Changes

(a) The root is first reduplicated and then the augment @deva[अ] and the terminations are added as in the 2nd Variety.

(b) Final @deva[इ] and @deva[उ] are changed to @deva[इय्] and @deva[उव्] before @deva[अ] and final @deva[आ] is dropped.
