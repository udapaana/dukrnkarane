## Alphabetical Arrangement and Nominal Verb Examples

In the following pages roots have been arranged according to their initial letters. It will be convenient to follow an alphabetical arrangement in stating the order in which they would be given in this chapter. This chapter will follow the following sequence:

### Examples of Nominal Verbs

| Form | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| @deva[छेदा]—@deva[छेदायति] | he shines |
| @deva[रेखा]—@deva[रेखायति] | he approaches |
| @deva[उषप्]—@deva[उषपायति] | it dawns |
| @deva[मही]—@deva[महीयते] | he is adored, etc. |
| @deva[मेधा]—@deva[मेधायति] | he is quick in understanding |
| @deva[तिरस्]—@deva[तिरस्यति] | he disappears |
| @deva[सुख्]—@deva[सुख्यति] | feels happy |
| @deva[अशुद्]—@deva[अशुद्यति] | he becomes healthy |
| @deva[दुख्]—@deva[दुख्यति] | feels unhappy |
| @deva[उरस्]—@deva[उरस्यति] | he becomes powerful |
| @deva[पयस्]—@deva[पयस्यति] | it spreads |
