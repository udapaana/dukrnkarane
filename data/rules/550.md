## Reduplication Rules for Roots Ending in उ

Roots ending in @deva[उ], short or long, substitute @deva[ञ] (changeable to @deva[इ] like @deva[इ]) for @deva[इ] in the reduplicative syllable, provided it be not (immediately) followed by a labial consonant or a semivowel or @deva[प्], followed by @deva[अ] or @deva[आ]: @deva[उ]-@deva[अस्पुचवत्], @deva[उ]-@deva[अप्चचवत्], @deva[उ]-@deva[अशुछुवत्], @deva[उ]-@deva[अवुवुवत्], &c. N. B. @deva[स्पुर्]-@deva[अस्पुस्पुरत्], @deva[उ]-@deva[अञ्जौप्रवत्]; @deva[स्पुर्]-@deva[स्पुर्द्] @deva[उ] 'to hasten' @deva[अत्रौञ्जत्र्]-@deva[अत्रौञ्जत्] or @deva[अत्रञ्जत्], @deva[उ]-@deva[अञ्जैव्स्मार्] @deva[अर्द्र्]-@deva[अर्द्रिजत्] or @deva[अर्द्रजत्], &c.

(d) The roots @deva[इ], @deva[छु] @deva[उ], @deva[इ] 'to go' @deva[इ] 'to swim' and @deva[क्षिप्] optionally preserve @deva[छ] or @deva[अ] @deva[अचिमत्] or @deva[अममत्], @deva[आविच्छत्] or @deva[अविछत्], @deva[आविमत्त्] or @deva[अयुषत्], @deva[आविवेत्] or @deva[अवेत्], @deva[अविक्षिपत्] or @deva[अक्षिपत्], @deva[आविघ्रप्त्] @deva[अयत्रपत्] or @deva[अयुत्रप्त्].
