## Substitution of पी for मे in Perfect and Frequentative

@deva[पी] is substituted for @deva[मे] 'to grow fat' in the Perfect and in the Frequentative; @deva[पिप्ये] 1 sing., @deva[पिपिष्ध]-@deva[ढ्] 2 pl.
