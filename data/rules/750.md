Guṇa is optionally substituted for the penultimate @deva[इ] and @deva[उ], roots beginning with any consonant and ending in any except @deva[ऋ] or @deva[व] when @deva[क्त्वा] has @deva[इ] prefixed to it; @deva[क्षुध्]—@deva[क्षुधित्वा] or @deva[क्षोधित्वा]; @deva[रुज्]—@deva[रुजित्वा] or @deva[रोजित्वा]; also @deva[रुप्त्वा]; @deva[क्षुध्]—6. P. @deva[क्षुधित्वा] or @deva[क्षोधित्वा]; @deva[स्पृश्]—@deva[स्पृशित्वा] or @deva[स्प्रशित्वा]; @deva[पिष्]—@deva[पिषित्वा], @deva[पेषित्वा] and @deva[पिष्ट्वा], or @deva[तुप्], &c., but @deva[विद्]—@deva[वेदित्वा], @deva[वित्त्वा].
