## Pada Selection in Desideratives

Roots in their Desiderative forms take the same terminations, Parasmai or Ātmane, that they do in the primitive. The roots @deva[वृ], @deva[मृ], @deva[हु] and @deva[टनु] take Ātm. terminations in the Desiderative.
