## Seventh Aorist: Optional Dropping of स/स्य in Ātmanepada

The roots @deva[दुह्], @deva[दिह्], @deva[लिह्], and @deva[यघुह्] when Ātmanepadi drop the initial @deva[स] or @deva[स्य] of the terminations of the 1st dual, 3rd sing. and 2nd sing and pl. optionally.

### PARADIGMS

|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[अदिक्षम्] | @deva[अदिक्षाव] | @deva[अदिक्षाम] |
| 2. | @deva[अदिक्षः] | @deva[अदिक्षतम्] | @deva[अदिक्षत] |
| 3. | @deva[अदिक्षत्] | @deva[अदिक्षताम्] | @deva[अदिक्षन्] |
