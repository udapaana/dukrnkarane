**Obs.** If the ending @deva[त्] of a word be followed by @deva[श], @deva[त्] is optionally inserted between them[^1] e.g. @deva[सत्] + @deva[शम्भुः] = @deva[सच्छम्भुः] and @deva[सत्त्शम्भुः] Śambhu existing for all times; and @deva[सच्छम्भुः] = @deva[सच्छम्भुः] and @deva[सत्त्छम्भुः] (by @ref[35]); then by @ref[28] (a) @deva[त्] being changed to @deva[ञ्] (and @deva[त्] to @deva[च्]) @deva[सञ्छम्भुः], @deva[सञ्च्छम्भुः] and @deva[सञ्त्छम्भुः] and finally by dropping @deva[त्] optionally (by @ref[20] a.) @deva[सञ्छम्भुः], @deva[सञ्च्छम्भुः] and @deva[सञ्छम्भुः].

---

[^1]: @deva[तोः शि]
