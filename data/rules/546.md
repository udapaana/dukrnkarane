## Roots Taking Third Aorist Variety

Roots of the tenth class, causals, some derivatives, the root @deva[कम्] and the roots @deva[भी], @deva[हु], and @deva[घू] when expressing fear or gent, take this Variety necessarily, and the roots @deva[घे] and @deva[चि] take it optionally.
