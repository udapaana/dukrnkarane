## णयच् for 'Becoming What It Was Not Before'

The suffix @deva[य] (@deva[णयच्]) is added to a few nouns such as @deva[बहु] 'much', @deva[भूरि] 'slow', @deva[भूतिधं] 'learned', @deva[यशस्] 'famous', @deva[उन्मनस्] 'agitated', etc. in the sense of 'becoming what it was not before', or 'what it was not like before':

### Examples

@deva[बहुः भवति]—@deva[बहुलायते] 'what was not much now becomes much'

@deva[उन्मनायति] 'he (who was not agitated before) becomes agitated'

Similarly @deva[यशसायते], etc.
