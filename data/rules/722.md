@deva[हन्] takes the affix @deva[यत्] optionally, before which @deva[वध्] is substituted for it; @deva[हन्+य=वध्यः]='what ought to be killed,' it optionally takes @deva[एयत्], in which case @deva[घात्] is substituted for it; @deva[घात्यः].
