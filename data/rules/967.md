@deva[तथा] 'likewise' often supplies the place of @deva[च]; @deva[रामः लक्ष्मणश्च] Rāma and Lakshmana; @deva[अङ्गस्त्रविष्टा च प्रत्युत्पन्नमतिरेव] both Aṅgastravishtā and Pratyutpannamatī; @deva[तथा हि] means 'for instance, to be more plain;' @deva[तथा च] 'likewise;' both are often used introducing quotations.
