The ordinals are declined regularly except:—

@deva[प्रथम] (see @ref[156]) and @deva[द्वितीय] and @deva[तृतीय] which are optionally declined like pronouns in the Dat. Ab. Gen. and Loc. sing ; e. g. @deva[द्वितीयस्मै-द्वितीयाय], &c. @deva[द्वितीययाः:-द्वितीयस्याः:] &c.
