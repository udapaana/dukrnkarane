Sometimes the terminations @deva[तर] and @deva[तम] are added to the comp. and super. in @deva[ईयस्] and @deva[इष्ठ] to intensify the meaning; as @deva[पापीयस्तर], @deva[पापीयस्तम]; @deva[श्रेष्ठतर], @deva[श्रेष्ठतम].
