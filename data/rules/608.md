## Roots with Two Causal Forms

The roots @deva[युज्], @deva[विच्छ्], @deva[भुज्], @deva[पुष्], @deva[पन्], and @deva[कुद्] have two forms in the causal.

**Examples:**
- @deva[युज्]—@deva[युज्यति-ते], @deva[योजापयति-ते]
- @deva[विच्छ्]—@deva[विच्छयति-ते], @deva[विच्छाययति-ते], &c.
