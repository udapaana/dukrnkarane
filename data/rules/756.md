(a), @deva[च्युत्] and @deva[छुद्] do not take Samprasāraṇa, @deva[प्रच्युत्य]; @deva[उच्छुत्य] 'having become old,' @deva[उप्छुत्य] 'having covered,' but @deva[ऋ] with @deva[परि] or @deva[सम्], takes Samprasāraṇa optionally, @deva[परिस्युत्य] or @deva[परित्वर्य]; @deva[संस्युत्य] or @deva[संत्वर्य].
