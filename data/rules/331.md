A Bah. ending in @deva[नख] and @deva[मुख] and used as proper names forms fem. in @deva[आ];[^1] @deva[सुवर्णनखा], @deva[गौरमुखा]; but @deva[ताम्रमुखी] a girl having a ruddy face.

---

[^1]: @deva[नखमुखात्संज्ञायाम्] । Pāṇ. IV. 1. 58.
