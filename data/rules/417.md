(I) The second person sing. termination of the Parasm. Imperative is @deva[हि] when the base ends in any consonant except a nasal or a semivowel; also in the case of the root @deva[यज्] 3. P. 'to sacrifice.'[^1]

[^1]: @deva[दुङालभौ हेचिः] । Pāṇ. VI. 4. 101
