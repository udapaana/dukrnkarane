**(a)** After final @deva[न्] the @deva[सु] of the loc. is changed to @deva[षु].

**(b)** @deva[र्] (optionally changeable to @deva[र्]) may be optionally inserted between @deva[न्] and @deva[सु].

@deva[कमल्] m. f. neu. naming the goddess of wealth or a lotus (@deva[कमले कमलौ वा आवसताम्:—वा—पौ—वा])—

Mas. and Fem.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. | @deva[कमल्][^1] | @deva[कमलौ] | @deva[कमलः] |
| A. | @deva[कमलम्] | ,, | ,, |
| I. | @deva[कमला] | @deva[कमलभ्याम्] | @deva[कमलभिः] |
| D. | @deva[कमले] | ,, | @deva[कमलेभ्यः] |
| Ab. | @deva[कमलः] | ,, | ,, |
| G. | ,, | @deva[कमलोः] | @deva[कमलाम्] |
| L. | @deva[कमलि] | ,, | @deva[कमलषु] |

Decline similarly @deva[सुगण्], @deva[सुगणन्], m. f. one who counts well, @deva[द्वार्] f. a door and other words ending in @deva[र्] or @deva[न्]. The loc. plu. of @deva[सुगण्] is @deva[सुगण्षु-पटसु,-पट्षु]. The Nom. sing. of @deva[द्वार्] is @deva[द्वाः].

Neu.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. A. | @deva[कमल्] | @deva[कमली] | @deva[कमलि] |

The rest like the mas.

Decline similarly @deva[वृक्षु], @deva[वाक्] and other words ending in @deva[णु], @deva[र्] or @deva[क्]. e. g.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. A. | @deva[वाः] | @deva[वारी] | @deva[वारि] |
| I. | @deva[वारा] | @deva[वांभ्याम्] | @deva[वारिभिः] |
| L. | @deva[वारि] | @deva[वारोः] | @deva[वारिषु] |
