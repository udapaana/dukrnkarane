## Terminations of First Aorist Variety

The terminations of the First Variety are the same as those of the Imperf., except that of third person plural, which is @deva[उस्]—

| Person | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| 1. | @deva[अम्] | @deva[व] | @deva[म] |
| 2. | @deva[स्] | @deva[तम्] | @deva[त] |
| 3. | @deva[त्] | @deva[ताम्] | @deva[उस्] |
