## Substitution of गा and इ in Specific Roots

In the case of @deva[इ] with @deva[अधि], @deva[गा] is optionally substituted for @deva[इ] in the Conditional and the Aorist. @deva[इ] is substituted for the final vowel of the verbs @deva[च्या] 3 P. Â. 1 P., @deva[प्रा], @deva[दो], @deva[ते], @deva[धे], @deva[मा], @deva[स्था], @deva[भा] (substituted for @deva[इ] 2 P. and @deva[स्था] with @deva[अधि],) @deva[म्ला], @deva[मा], and @deva[को] before a consonantal weak termination. All terminations added to @deva[मा] substituted for @deva[इ] are weak.
