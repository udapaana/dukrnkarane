There are no articles in Sanskrit corresponding to the English Definite and Indefinite articles. The words @deva[कश्चित्] and @deva[एक], however, are often used in the sense of 'a certain' and the numeral @deva[एकः], i. e. as equivalent to 'one;' @deva[एकस्मिन् नरे] a certain man; @deva[एकः पान्थः] a traveller, @deva[स राजा] the king, &c.
