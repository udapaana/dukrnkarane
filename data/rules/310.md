The following eleven prātipadikas form their feminine in @deva[ई] under the circumstances mentioned—[^1]@deva[जानपद] when it qualifies @deva[वृत्ति] or 'maintenance', @deva[कुण्ड] when it means 'a vessel' or 'a man of a mixed class', @deva[गोण] 'a full sack', @deva[स्थल] 'natural site', @deva[भाज] 'boiled,' @deva[नाग] 'a huge elephant', and @deva[काल] 'a particular colour, @deva[नील] when it does not refer to a garment ( or rather refers to an animal ) or means 'indigo', @deva[कुश] when it means 'something made of iron. @deva[काषुक] 'desirous of enjoyment,' and @deva[कबर] 'a knot of hair; as @deva[जानपदी [वृत्ति:]], '@deva[जानपदी बुद्धि]'; @deva[कुण्डी अपात्रे] [a vessel | @deva[कुण्डश्वेत्या], (one that burns). @deva[गोणी आमये चेत्], @deva[गोणा अन्या] i.e. 'an empty sack;' @deva[स्थली अकृत्रिमा चेत्], @deva[स्थला अन्या] i.e. ground artificially prepared; @deva[भाजी भणा] (rice-gruel) @deva[चेत्], @deva[भाजा अन्या]; @deva[नागी स्थूला चेत्], @deva[नागा अन्या], @deva[काली वर्णश्चेत्], @deva[काला अन्या]; i.e. 'if it be a proper name;' @deva[नीली अजाड्यादुवै [ओषधिविशेषो गोपी]] @deva[चेत्], @deva[नीला अन्या नीलया रक्ता अश्री इत्यर्थे नीली] or @deva[नीला] (when a name), @deva[कुश्ची अयोविकारश्चेत्] but @deva[कुश्चा] 'a wooden peg'; @deva[काषुकी] 'a woman desirous of enjoyment,' @deva[काषुका] 'one anxious to meet her lover,' @deva[कबरी] 'a braid of hair,' but @deva[कबरा] variegated; @deva[श्योण] has @deva[श्योर्णी-णा].[^2]
