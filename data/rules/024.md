@deva[ए], @deva[ओ], @deva[ऐ] and @deva[औ], when followed by a vowel, are changed to @deva[अय्], @deva[अव्], @deva[आय्], and @deva[आव्] respectively[^1]; e.g. @deva[हरे] + @deva[ए] = @deva[हरय] for Hari; @deva[विष्णो] + @deva[ए] = @deva[विष्णव] for Viṣṇu; @deva[नै] + @deva[अकः] = @deva[नायकः] A leader; @deva[पौ] + @deva[अकः] = @deva[पावकः] the purifier i.e. fire.

**(a)** @deva[य] or @deva[व] at the end of a word and preceded by @deva[अ] or @deva[आ] is optionally dropped when followed by a vowel or a soft consonant[^2]; e.g. @deva[हरे] + @deva[एहि] = @deva[हरयेहि] or @deva[हर एहि]; @deva[विष्णो] + @deva[इह] = @deva[विष्णविह] or @deva[विष्णु इह]; @deva[श्रिये] + @deva[उत्कः] = @deva[श्रियायुत्कः] or @deva[श्रिया उत्कः] intent on getting money; @deva[गुरो] + @deva[उत्कः] = @deva[गुरावुत्कः] or @deva[गुरा उत्कः] longing to see the preceptor.

**Note:** Two vowels brought together by the disappearance of an intervening consonant or visarga do not coalesce.

**(b)** @deva[ओ] and @deva[औ], when followed by a termination beginning with @deva[य], become @deva[अव्] and @deva[आव्] respectively[^3]; e.g. @deva[गो] + @deva[य] = @deva[गव्यम्] produced from a cow as milk, ghee &c. @deva[नौ] + @deva[य] = @deva[नाव्यम्] accessible by a boat, navigable.

**N.B.** This rule holds good, in the case of roots, only when the @deva[ओ] or @deva[औ] is brought in by virtue of a termination;[^4] e.g. @deva[छो] + @deva[य] = @deva[छो] + @deva[य] = @deva[छव्यम्] fit to be lopped off. @deva[अवछिपत्] + @deva[य] = @deva[अवछिपौ] + @deva[य] = @deva[अवछिपाव्यम्] which ought necessarily to be cut off.

**(c)** The @deva[ओ] of the word @deva[गो] is also changed to @deva[अव्] when followed by the word @deva[यूति] (in the Veda and) in ordinary language when it means a particular measure of length[^5]; e.g. @deva[गव्यूतिः] four miles.

**(d)** The @deva[य] of the roots @deva[क्षि] and @deva[शी] is changed to @deva[अय्] when followed by the @deva[य] of the potential participle indicating capability[^6] e.g. @deva[क्षि] + @deva[य] = @deva[क्षै] + @deva[य] = @deva[क्षय्य] what can be reduced; so @deva[शय्य] what can be conquered; but @deva[क्षेयं], @deva[शेयं], @deva[योग्यं], @deva[हेयं] (what is fit to be removed but may not be so) @deva[वाच्यं], @deva[जेयं] (what ought to be curbed but which it may not be always possible to curb as) @deva[मनः].
