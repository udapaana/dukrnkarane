@deva[श] preceded by a word ending in any of the first four letters of a class and followed by a vowel, a semi-vowel, a nasal or @deva[ह्], is optionally changed to @deva[छ्],[^1] e.g. @deva[तद्] + @deva[शिवः] = @deva[तद्] + @deva[छिवः]; and @deva[तद्] + @deva[छिवः]. and then @deva[तत्] + @deva[छिवः], and @deva[तत्] + @deva[छिवः] by the above, and finally @deva[तच्छिवः] and @deva[तच्छिवः] that Śiva (see a, @ref[28].). Similarly @deva[तच्छ्लोकेन], @deva[तच्छ्लोकेन]; but @deva[वाक्श्रोतृति] speech falters (as @deva[श] is not followed by any of the letters mentioned in the rule).

[^1]: @deva[श्चुना श्चुः] — Pāṇ. VIII. 4. 63.
