The feminine of nouns denoting 'a male' is formed by adding @deva[ई] when the wife of that male is meant;[^1] @deva[गोपस्य स्त्री गोपी]; @deva[शूद्री] (sometimes @deva[शूद्राणी] also) 'wife of a Sudra'.

**(a)** but not of nouns ending in @deva[पालक]; as @deva[गोपालिका] 'the wife of a cowherd' (but @deva[गोपाल] has @deva[गोपाली]); @deva[अश्वपालिका] 'the wife of a horse-groom'.

**(b)** @deva[सूर्य] has @deva[सूर्या] 'the divine wife of Surya;' but @deva[सौरी] i.e. @deva[कुन्ती] who was a mortal.

[^1]: @deva[स्त्रियोगादाख्यायाम्] । Pāṇ. IV. 1. 48. @deva[पालकान्ताच्] । Vārtika. @deva[सूर्योदेवतायां चाप् वाच्य:] । Vārtika.
