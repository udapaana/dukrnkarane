## Exceptional Forms with Double Meanings

### अदन्

@deva[अदन्] has:
- @deva[अदनायति] 'he wishes to eat'
- @deva[अदनीयति] 'he wishes to possess food'

### उदक

@deva[उदक] has:
- @deva[उदनुयति] 'he wishes to drink water'
- @deva[उदकीयति] 'he wishes to possess water'

### धन्

@deva[धन्] has:
- @deva[धनायति] 'he wishes to acquire money'
- @deva[धनीयति] 'he wishes to be wealthy'
