## Final ऋ and ई Changes before Weak Terminations

The final @deva[ऋ], short or long, is changed to @deva[अर] before a vowel weak termination and @deva[ई] to @deva[इय] or @deva[य] as it is preceded by a conjunct consonant or not; as @deva[ऋ]→@deva[अर]→@deva[इरुव] ! dual; @deva[तितिर्]+@deva[इरे]=@deva[तितिरिरे], @deva[लिभिर्]+@deva[इरे]=@deva[लिभिरिरे], &c.
