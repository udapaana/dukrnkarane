**(a)** All the numerals from 20-99, monosyllabic words in @deva[ई] and such as are formed by the affix @deva[ता]; @deva[विंशतिः], @deva[श्रीः]; @deva[नम्रता] &c.

**(b)** The synonyms of @deva[भूमि], @deva[सरित्], @deva[लता] and @deva[वनिता] and these words themselves.

**Exceptions:**—@deva[स्रोतस्] n. and @deva[वाहस्] n. both meaning a 'river'.
