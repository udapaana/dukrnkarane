## Optional Nasal Dropping in गम् and यम्

@deva[गम्], and @deva[यम्] with @deva[उप्] 'to marry,' optionally drop their nasal, when they take the Ātm. terminations. @deva[यम्] when it means 'to give out' (as the faults of others) necessarily drops its nasal
