## Verb Ellipsis After Prepositions in Compounds

When used in a compound the verb may be omitted after the prepositions @deva[अति], @deva[अधि], @deva[अनु], @deva[अप], @deva[अव], @deva[अभि], @deva[उप], @deva[परि] and @deva[प्रति]; @deva[अतिक्रान्तो मालां] = @deva[अतिमाल], &c. Vide @ref[232].
