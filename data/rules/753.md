Roots of the 8th class ending in a nasal, except @deva[यम्] and the roots @deva[मन्], @deva[यन्] and @deva[हन्], drop their nasal necessarily; @deva[तन्], @deva[तंब्], @deva[यम्] and @deva[रम्] do it optionally; @deva[तन्] with @deva[वि]—@deva[वितत्य]; @deva[भ्रंश्] with @deva[प्रति]—@deva[प्रतिभ्रंश्य]; @deva[तंब्] with @deva[वि]—@deva[वितंब्य] or @deva[वितत्य]; @deva[रम्] with @deva[वि]—@deva[विरम्य] or @deva[विरत्य]; @deva[मन्] with @deva[प्र]—@deva[प्रणम्य] or @deva[प्रणत्य], &c.
