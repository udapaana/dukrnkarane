@deva[पीयः] has @deva[पूतः] when it is used with 'one's own limbs.' @deva[पूतं क्षौमं], and @deva[पीयः] or @deva[पूनः] in other cases, as @deva[पीयः] or @deva[पूनः रथः]; when it is preceded by a preposition, @deva[इ] is not substituted, and the vowel; @deva[प्रस्यातः]; but when it is used with @deva[अङ्गै] or @deva[ज्चृपतु] with the preposition @deva[आ], @deva[इ] is necessarily substituted; @deva[आपीतः अस्यः]; @deva[भूयौनं ज्चृषै].
