The roots @deva[यज्], @deva[नी] and @deva[जि], preceded by @deva[वि], take this affix, when they are connected with the words @deva[मुञ्ज], @deva[कट्यक] and @deva[उदुक] respectively, @deva[विषण्णो मुञ्जः] 'The Munja grass to be prepared for weaving into a rope' (@deva[मुञ्जानृत्युर्णाय शोधनीयस्य इत्यर्थः] Sid. Kau.); @deva[निवेय: कट्यकः] 'the girdle ought to be destroyed;' @deva[विश्वेञ्चुत् वृषेः] 'The plough to be pulled with great force,' @deva[चलेन कट्यक्षः]; in other cases these take the affix @deva[यत्]; @deva[विजेयः], @deva[नीतव्यः], @deva[जेयः].

[^1]: @deva[राजसूयमुषयक्षत्र-पद्यो-सूयगुयक्षुद्यस्यङ्कः]। Pāṇ. III. I. 114. Vide Sid. Kau. on the same.

[^2]: Cf. @deva[निजं जनुष्योसपाति हृवानो], &c. Rig. III. 59. 1.
