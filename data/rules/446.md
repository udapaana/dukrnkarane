A radical aspirate (2nd or 4th letter of a class) is changed to its corresponding unaspirate in the reduplicative syllable; as @deva[चित्]=@deva[चिचित्], @deva[धूह्]=@deva[दुधूह्], @deva[भुज्]=@deva[बुभुज्], &c.
