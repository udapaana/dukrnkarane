## Vowel Changes in Fourth Aorist

(a) In the Parasmaipada the radical vowel takes its Vṛddhi substitute; @deva[कृ]-@deva[अकार्षीत्]; @deva[इ]-@deva[ऐषीत्]; @deva[मञ्ज]-@deva[अमाञ्जीत्], &c.

(b) In the Ātmanepada, Guṇa is substituted for final @deva[इ] or @deva[उ], short or long; final @deva[ऋ] and the penultimate vowel remain unchanged; final @deva[ऋ] is changed to @deva[ऋर्] or @deva[ऊर्] in accordance with @ref[594]; @deva[चि]-@deva[अचेष्ट], @deva[नी]-@deva[अनेष्ट], @deva[च्यु]-@deva[अच्योष्ट], @deva[क्]-@deva[अकोष्ट], @deva[छ्] see below, @deva[गिर्]-@deva[अगरिष्ट], @deva[धूर्]-@deva[अधोरीष्ट], @deva[हूर्]-@deva[अहोरीष्ट].

(c) The Penultimate @deva[ऋ] of Anit roots is optionally changed to @deva[ऋर्] or @deva[ऊर्] @deva[सञ्जाघर्सुर्] or @deva[सञ्जाघोरुत्].
