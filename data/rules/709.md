The Participle of the Simple Future, Active as well as Passive, is formed from the third person singular form of the Second Future of a root. The Parasm. Ptc. is formed simply by dropping the final @deva[इ], and the Atm. and the Passive one by substituting @deva[मान] for the final @deva[इ]; e. g.

**Participles.**

| Roots | Par. | Atm. | Passive |
|---|---|---|---|
| @deva[दा]— | @deva[द्रास्यत्] | @deva[द्रास्यमाणः] | @deva[द्रास्यमाणः] or @deva[द्रायिष्यमाणः] |
| @deva[भू]— | @deva[भविष्यत्] | @deva[भविष्यमाणः] | @deva[भविष्यमाणः] or @deva[भाविष्यमाणः] |
