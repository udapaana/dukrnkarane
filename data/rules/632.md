## Conjugation of Ātmanepada Frequentative Base

### Conjugational Tenses

In the Conjugational Tenses the Ātm. Freq. Base is conjugated like the Conjugational base of the 4th class in Ātmanepada.

### General Tenses

In the General Tenses, and in all the Tenses of the Passive, the base loses its final @deva[य] when the base + @deva[अ] is preceded by a vowel and drops the @deva[य] itself when it is preceded by a consonant.

### Perfect

As regards the Perfect, the Freq. Base takes the Periphrastic Perfect.

### Aorist

In the Aorist the Ātm. terminations of the 5th form are added.

### Remaining Tenses

In the remaining Tenses the Ātm. terminations with the intermediate @deva[इ] prefixed to them are added as usual.

### Passive

The Passive is also formed like the Passive of derived verbs.
