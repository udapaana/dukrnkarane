## Ablative with Concealment

When concealment is to be had recourse to, that whose sight one desires to avoid is put in the Ablative case; @deva[मातुः निहितः कृष्णः] 'Kṛṣṇa conceals himself from his mother.'[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. I. 4. 28
