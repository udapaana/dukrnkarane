Roots of the Tenth class and Causals preserve their @deva[अय] before @deva[य]; it being separated by a short penultimate vowel if not, it is dropped; @deva[चोरय]—@deva[परिचोर्य]; @deva[बोधय]—@deva[परिबोध्य]; @deva[कृ]—cau. @deva[विकार्य]; @deva[नी] cau. with @deva[आ]—@deva[आनाय], &c.; but @deva[गृण्]—@deva[विगृण्य], @deva[प्रकृप्य], @deva[प्रकल्प्य]; @deva[प्रबोभिद्य] 'having again and again caused to be broken.'
