[^1]The roots @deva[विश्], @deva[युज्], @deva[पद्] and @deva[स्कन्द्] are used in their gerundive forms in @deva[अम्] in the sense of 'complete occupation or pervasion or repetition' with nouns which if not compounded would have stood in the Acc.; @deva[गेहगृहगृहविशयति] @deva[गेहं गेहयुजमभुयम्। स्कन्दमुगयति] so @deva[गेहयुजमुयुर्य], @deva[गेहयुमुनाम्], @deva[गेहयुस्कन्दम्], &c.
