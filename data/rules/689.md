Roots ending in @deva[ऋ][^1] (@deva[ऋ], @deva[लृ] and @deva[ओ] changeable to @deva[आ]) and beginning with a conjunct consonant containing a semi-vowel also substitute @deva[न्] for @deva[त्]; @deva[ह्रु] 'to run, to sleep,' @deva[प्राणः]; @deva[म्लै] 'to fade,' @deva[म्लानः]; @deva[स्रु]—@deva[स्त्राणः] 'collected to a mass,' &c.

**Exceptions:—**@deva[ग्रै] 'to name,' &c., @deva[स्वै] 'to contemplate,' @deva[ग्राणः] and @deva[प्लै]; @deva[स्वितः], @deva[स्वानः], @deva[ग्रातः] and @deva[प्रातः].

[^1]: @deva[पूर्वरुषाद्रुषुतैः] । Pāṇ. VIII. 2. 19.
