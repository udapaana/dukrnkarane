The roots @deva[वच्] implying reproach, @deva[पण्] meaning 'to transact business,' and @deva[वृ] 9. Atm. not denoting limit or restraint, take the affix @deva[य]; @deva[इ] @deva[य] is prefixed to @deva[वच्] if it hints reproachingly or demnation; but @deva[अनुप] (@deva[अन्+वच्+क्यप्] i. e. @deva[य]) @deva[गुरोः] 'the name of a preceptor ought not to be uttered (out of respect),' @deva[पण्यः गौः] 'a cow is saleable;' but @deva[पाण्यः] (@deva[पण्+एयत्] i. e. @deva[य]) @deva[ब्राह्मणः] 'a Brāhmaṇa deserving praise;' @deva[वर्यः] (that can be chosen or sought after)' a title of @deva[विष्णु]. @deva[शतेन वरा] 'hundred men (i. e. any one) can seek the hand of a girl;' but @deva[वार्या] (@deva[वृ+क्यप्] i. e. @deva[य]) @deva[कन्या] 'to be married by a particular man.'
