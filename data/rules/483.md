## Admission of इ by गम्, हन् and Aniṭ Roots

@deva[गम्] P. @deva[हन्] and @deva[अनिट्] (@[Aniṭ]) roots ending in @deva[अ] admit @deva[इ] in the Second Future and the Conditional; @deva[गम्] P. (also @deva[गा] substituted for @deva[इ] 'to go' and @deva[अध्यै] with @deva[अधि] 'to remember') also admits it in the Desiderative. @deva[गमिष्याम], @deva[हनिष्याम], @deva[करिष्याम], &c. Sec. Fut. 1 sing. @deva[अगमिष्यम्], @deva[अकरिष्यम्], @deva[अहनिष्यम्], &c. Cond. 1 sing.
