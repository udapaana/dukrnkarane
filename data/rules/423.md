@deva[अन्] P. 'to breathe,' @deva[जश्] 'to eat,' @deva[रुद्] P. 'to weep,' @deva[भ्रस्] P. 'to sigh' and @deva[स्वप्] P. 'to sleep,' insert the augment @deva[इ] between their final and the terminations beginning with any consonant except @deva[य]; in the case of 2 and 3 sing. Imperf. they insert @deva[ई] or @deva[आ]; e. g.

@deva[अन्] P. 'to breathe.'

**Present.**

| | S. | D. | P. |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[अनिमि] | @deva[अनिवः] | @deva[अनिमः] |
| 2. | @deva[अनिषि] | @deva[अनिथः] | @deva[अनिथ] |
| 3. | @deva[अनिति] | @deva[अनितः] | @deva[अनन्ति] |

**Imperfect.**

| | S. | D. | P. |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[आनम्] | @deva[आनिव] | @deva[आनिम] |
| 2. | @deva[आनीः]—@deva[आनः] | @deva[आनितम्] | @deva[आनित] |
| 3. | @deva[आनीत्]—@deva[आनत्] | @deva[आनिताम्] | @deva[आनन्] |

**Imperative.**

| | S. | D. | P. |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[अनानि] | @deva[अनाव] | @deva[अनाम] |
| 2. | @deva[अनिहि] | @deva[अनितम्] | @deva[अनित] |
| 3. | @deva[अनितु] | @deva[अनिताम्] | @deva[अनन्तु] |

**Potential.**

| | S. | D. | P. |
