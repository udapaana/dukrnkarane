## Roots ज्ञा, स्था, विश् with अधि Govern Accusative of Location

The roots @deva[ज्ञा], @deva[स्था] and @deva[विश्], with @deva[अधि], govern the Accusative of the place where the action takes place; @deva[अग्रशिलां अविष्टः] reclining at a stone-slab; @deva[अग्रं इन्द्रासनं अर्धं अविविशुः] occupied half of Indra's seat; @deva[अध्यास्त सदंधुषु अयोध्यां] lived in Ayodhyā delightful in all seasons.
