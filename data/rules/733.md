The roots @deva[कृ] and @deva[दृ] take both @deva[क्यप्] and @deva[एयत्]; @deva[कृत्यः], @deva[कार्यः]; @deva[दृत्यः]; @deva[दार्यः] 'what ought to be chosen, best,' &c.
