(a) @deva[ऋण][^1] when it means 'to incur debt' substitutes @deva[न्]; @deva[ऋणः] 'debt;' but @deva[ऋतः], 'gone.'

(b) @deva[क्षि], when the P. P. Participle has an active sense, substitutes @deva[न] and lengthens its @deva[इ]; @deva[क्षीणः], reduced, emaciated; but when a curse is implied, or pity expressed, the change is optional; @deva[क्षीणश्चायं] or @deva[क्षितश्चायं भिक्षुः] i. e. 'die;' @deva[क्षीणः] or @deva[क्षितः भिक्षुः सत्येतरे] 'oh, the poor ascetic is reduced thin;' but @deva[अन्यः क्षितः ऋतः].

(c) @deva[द्युत्][^2] takes @deva[न] when it does not mean 'to gamble;' @deva[द्युनः] 'a sport in which nothing is staked,' but @deva[द्युतः] 'gambling.'

(d) @deva[शा][^2] with @deva[निस्] takes @deva[न] except when it has @deva[शाकः] for its subject; @deva[निर्शानोऽग्निः] 'the fire is extinguished;' @deva[निर्शानः ऋषिः] 'the sage is absolved;' but @deva[निर्शितः शाकः]

(e) @deva[स्पृश्] takes @deva[न] when the notion of touch is not present; @deva[संस्पृतः वृश्चिकः] 'a scorpion contracted through cold;' when it means 'to be oppressed by or cold' @deva[स्पृष्टोऽयमुकेन अयं मृदानुकूल (की) स्पृष्ट-कृतः], but @deva[अग्निना स्पृष्टः] and not @deva[अग्नीन] as the idea of touch is present and also when preceded by @deva[परि]; @deva[परिस्पृष्टः युधि], &c.; with @deva[अनि] and @deva[अव] the change is optional; @deva[अनिस्पृषः] or @deva[अनिस्पृग्नः युधि], @deva[अवस्पृष्ठ] or @deva[अवस्पृनः व्यूहस्पृशः]; but @deva[समवस्पानः]

[^1]: @deva[ऋणुमाङ्घयुमात्रशुवै] । Pāṇ. VIII. 2. 60.

[^2]: @deva[दिवितोऽक्तिजिर्मीमेतु; निशानोऽन्यैः] । Pāṇ. VIII. 2. 49–50.
