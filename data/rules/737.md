(a) The roots @deva[युज्], @deva[याच्], @deva[रुच्], @deva[प्रथ्], @deva[स्पृश्], and @deva[एच्] do not change their @deva[ऋ] or @deva[व] to @deva[वृ] or @deva[यु] before @deva[घ्यत्]; @deva[योज्यं] @deva[याच्यं]; @deva[रोच्यं], @deva[प्रथ्यंयुम्] (@deva[प्रत्यशीतव्यः]), @deva[ज्ञेय्यं], @deva[स्पश्यं], @deva[गाच्यं].

(b) @deva[वच्] does not change its @deva[चु] to @deva[ऋ] before @deva[घ्यत्] when the meaning is 'what ought to be spoken out, speech,' @deva[वाच्यं]; but @deva[वाक्यं]; 'a sentence.'

(c) @deva[पञ्च्] when it means 'to go' does not change @deva[चु] its to @deva[ऋ]; @deva[पञ्च्यं]; when it means 'to bend' the @deva[चु] is changed to @deva[ऋ]; @deva[पञ्कुवं शरीरं].

(d) @deva[युज्] when it is preceded by @deva[प्र] and @deva[वि], takes the affix @deva[एयत्] in the sense of 'what is possible or capable of;' and does not change its @deva[ऋ] to @deva[यु]; @deva[प्रयुङ्कुम् शक्यं प्रयोज्यं]; @deva[निवौङुकुम् शक्यः निवौज्यः]; @deva[शङुयः]!

(e) @deva[भुज्] has @deva[भोजं] meaning 'food,' and @deva[भोज्यं] 'what is fit to be enjoyed.'
