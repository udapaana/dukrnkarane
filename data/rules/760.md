Desiderative bases add @deva[य] immediately and Freq. bases add it after dropping their @deva[य], when it is preceded by a consonant and dropping the final @deva[अ] when it is preceded by a vowel; @deva[बुध्]—@deva[परिबुबुधिष्य]; Fre. @deva[परिबोबुध्य]; @deva[भू] Fre. @deva[परिबोभूय] &c.
