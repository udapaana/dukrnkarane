## Locative with उप (exceeding) and आ (master of)

The prepositions @deva[उप] in the sense of 'exceeding' and @deva[आ] in that of 'the master of' govern the Locative; @deva[उपपर्याये हरेणाः] the merits of Hari exceed a pardruha; @deva[आपि मेदिन्याम् रामः] or @deva[आपि रायने पुः] Rāma is the lord of the earth; in other senses these prepositions are used with the Accusative, for which see @ref[806].[^1]
