The roots contained in the first nine classes and a few of the tenth are primitive roots, while almost all roots of the tenth class, the Causals, Desideratives, Frequentatives, Denominatives, and the roots @[gup], @[dhūp], @[vicch], @[paṇ], @[pan], @[ṛt], and @[kam], are comprised under the head of Derivative roots.
