@deva[गै] is substituted for @deva[अञ्च्] 1 P. 'to go' necessarily before any non-conjugational termination, and optionally before such as begin with any consonant except @deva[य]; @deva[एता], @deva[अञ्चिता]; @deva[एताम], @deva[अञ्चियाम] &c.
