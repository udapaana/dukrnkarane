## Substitution of गा for इ in Aorist

@deva[गा] is substituted for @deva[इ] in the Aorist, @deva[अगाम्], @deva[अगाव], @deva[अगाम] 1st per.; @deva[इ] with @deva[आङ्] 'to remember' @deva[अध्यगाम्], @deva[अध्यगाव], @deva[अध्यगाम्], &c.
