@deva[अप्] f. water, is declined in the plural; it lengthens its @deva[अ] in the N. and changes its @deva[प्] to @deva[द्] before consonantal terminations. @deva[आपः-आपः-आप्भिः-आद्भ्यः-आद्भ्यः-आपाम्-आप्सु].
