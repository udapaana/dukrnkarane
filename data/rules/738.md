Roots ending in @deva[ऋ], short or long, take the affix @deva[एलिम्] in the sense of 'what ought or must necessarily be done;' @deva[कृ]—@deva[कर्तुं] 'what must necessarily be cut off,' @deva[पाक्तुं] 'what must necessarily be cooked,' @deva[दा] to be with @deva[आ]—@deva[आत्तुं]; @deva[प्र] 'to mix,' @deva[यात्], &c.

(a) The roots @deva[वच्], @deva[प्रथ्], @deva[द्युत्], @deva[भृत्] and @deva[वद्] also do the same, @deva[वाच्यं] 'what must necessarily be on;' @deva[प्रथ्युत्] 'what must be spoken of distinctly;' @deva[द्युत्युत्], @deva[भात्युत्], @deva[वात्युत्].
