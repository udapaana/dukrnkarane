In the case of roots ending in @deva[आ], the terminations of the third person plural of the Imperfect is optionally @deva[उस्].

---

## Paradigms.

@deva[या] P. 'to go.'

**Present.**

| | S. | D. | P. |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[यामि] | @deva[यावः] | @deva[यामः] |
| 2. | @deva[यासि] | @deva[याथः] | @deva[याथ] |
| 3. | @deva[याति] | @deva[यातः] | @deva[यान्ति] |

**Imperfect.**

| | S. | D. | P. |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[अयाम्] | @deva[अयाव] | @deva[अयाम] |
| 2. | @deva[अयाः] | @deva[अयातम्] | @deva[अयात] |
| 3. | @deva[अयात्] | @deva[अयाताम्] | @deva[अयान्], @deva[अयुः] |

**Imperative.**

| | S. | D. | P. |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[यानि] | @deva[याव] | @deva[याम] |
| 2. | @deva[याहि] | @deva[यातम्] | @deva[यात] |
| 3. | @deva[यातु] | @deva[याताम्] | @deva[यान्तु] |

**Potential.**

| | S. | D. | P. |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[यायाम्] | @deva[यायाव] | @deva[यायाम] |
| 2. | @deva[यायाः] | @deva[यायातम्] | @deva[यायात] |
| 3. | @deva[यायात्] | @deva[यायाताम्] | @deva[यायुः] |

Conjugate similarly @deva[स्था] P. 'to tell,' @deva[दा] P. 'to cut,' @deva[पा] P. 'to protect,' @deva[धा] P. 'to fill,' @deva[ख्या] P. 'to eat,' @deva[क्षा] P. 'to fly,' @deva[म्ना] P. 'to shine,' @deva[मा] 'to measure,' @deva[रा] 'to give,' @deva[ला] 'to give or take,' @deva[वा] 'to blow,' @deva[भा] 'to cook' and @deva[हा] 'to bathe'.
