**Obs.** **(a)** The words @deva[सुसखि] a good friend (@deva[शोभनः सखा]), @deva[अतिसखि] a close friend (@deva[अतिशयितः सखा]), @deva[परमसखि] a great friend, or one having a great friend (@deva[परमः सखा परम्], @deva[परमः सखा यस्य]), are declined like @deva[सखि] in the N. V. and A. and like @deva[हरि] in other cases; but @deva[अतिसखि] (@deva[सखीमतिक्रान्तः] one who has abandoned a female companion) is declined like @deva[हरि]. **N. B.** @deva[सखी] fem. is declined like @deva[नदी].
