The following seven roots form their bases like the Desiderative in the senses indicated. These are:—@deva[किण्] to administer medicine, to treat as a patient, @deva[चिकीर्षति]; @deva[तिकित्] to censure, @deva[जुगुप्साते]; @deva[तिज्] to bear, to forgive, @deva[तितिक्षति]; @deva[विद्] to abhor, to act loathsomely, @deva[विविक्षति]; @deva[दीष्] to make straight, @deva[दीदिषति]-@deva[ते]; @deva[मान्] to reason, to think, @deva[मीमांसते]; @deva[शिष्] to sharpen, @deva[शीषाषति]-@deva[ते]. But @deva[किण्] to desire @deva[कैकषति]; to dwell, @deva[किकीषति]; @deva[दीष्] to cut @deva[दीदिषति]-@deva[ते] &c.
