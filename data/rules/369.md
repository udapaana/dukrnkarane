## Other Words Used Prepositionally (Gati)

There are several other words, also styled Gati by Sanskrit grammarians, used prepositionally. These are prefixed to certain roots only. We give the more important of them below.

### (a) @deva[अच्छ]

'towards' is prefixed to @deva[वद्] and to roots implying motion[^1]; @deva[अच्छगम्य] = @deva[गत्वा] 'going near', @deva[अच्छपतत्] 'flying towards', @deva[अच्छूद्य] 'having spoken to'.

### (b) Words Prefixed to @deva[कृ]

**(1)** @deva[अन्वाञ्च्] 'giving strength to the weak', @deva[अलम्] in the sense of 'decorating', @deva[ऊर्ती], @deva[उरती], @deva[उत्ती] all implying 'assent, acceptance or promise', @deva[खात्] and similar imitative sounds, @deva[असत्] and @deva[सत्] in the sense of 'dishonouring or honouring', @deva[प्रास्त] denoting 'fastening', &c. are prefixed to @deva[कृ]; @deva[अन्वाञ्चकरण], @deva[ऊर्तीकरण], @deva[असत्कार], @deva[खात्कृत्य], @deva[खाङ्कृत्य], @deva[प्रास्तकरण], &c.

**(2)** @deva[नमस्], @deva[भागु], @deva[निष्पा], @deva[वशो], @deva[साक्षात्] and some other words may be optionally prefixed to @deva[कृ] or remain separate; @deva[नमस्कार], @deva[वशोकृ] or @deva[वशो कृ] 'to bring under subjection', @deva[साक्षात् कृ] or @deva[साक्षात्कृ] 'to make manifest', &c.

### (c) @deva[अन्तर्]

is prefixed to roots meaning 'to go', @deva[धा], @deva[भू], and similar roots; @deva[अन्तरित्य] 'having disappeared', @deva[अन्तर्धान] 'disappearance', @deva[अन्तर्भूत], &c.

### (d) @deva[अस्तम्]

is prefixed to roots implying motion; @deva[अस्तमय] 'setting', @deva[अस्तंगत] 'set', @deva[अस्तंनी] 'to cause to set, to lead to destruction', &c.

### (e) @deva[आविस्], @deva[प्रादुस्], and @deva[तिरस्]

@deva[आविस्] and @deva[प्रादुस्] are prefixed to @deva[कृ], @deva[अस्] and @deva[भू] and @deva[तिरस्] to @deva[भू], @deva[धा] and similar roots, and optionally to @deva[कृ]; @deva[आविष्करण], @deva[आविर्भवन] 'manifestation', @deva[प्रादुर्—आविर्भूत] 'manifested', &c.; @deva[तिरोभूय] 'vanishing out of sight', @deva[तिरोधान] 'disappearance', &c.

### (f) @deva[पुरस्]

is prefixed to @deva[कृ], @deva[भू], @deva[गम्], &c.; @deva[पुरस्कृत] 'placed before, headed by', @deva[पुरोगत] 'gone in the front', &c.

[^1]: @deva[अच्छगतेर्यदेषु] Pāṇ. I.4.69.
