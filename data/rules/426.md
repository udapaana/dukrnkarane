The @deva[इ] of the root @deva[इ] P.[^1] 'to go' is changed to @deva[य] before a weak vowel termination; @deva[एति] @deva[इतः] @deva[यन्ति] 3 pers. Pre.; @deva[आयम्] @deva[ऐव] @deva[ऐम] 1st Pers. Imperf, @deva[ऐः] 2 sing. Imperf.; @deva[अयानि], @deva[इहि]; @deva[एतु] P. 2, 3 sings. Imp. @deva[यन्तु] 3 pl. Imp.

@deva[इ] with @deva[अधि] Â.[^2] 'to study.' &c. is regularly conjugated; as

[^1]: @deva[इ] P. 'to go' is conjugated regularly like @deva[वा]—@deva[एति] @deva[इतः] @deva[इयन्ति] 3rd Pers. Pre.; @deva[इहि] 2 sing. Imp. @deva[इयन्तु] 3 plural Imp.
[^2]: @deva[इ] with @deva[अधि] P. 'to remember' should be conjugated like @deva[इ]. @deva[अधियन्ति] 3rd pl. Pre. The line of Bhaṭṭi '@deva[सतीतयोरधीयन्]' III. 18 &c. is quoted in support of this opinion. @deva[क्त्विच्छाधेषाङ्क्षाधिकारामक्लेश्यातदेशयाङ्क] । @deva[त्त्वंये यङ] । Sid. Kau.
