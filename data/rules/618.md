## Radical Vowel Changes Before स् in Desideratives

The radical vowels undergo the following changes before @deva[स्]—

### (a) Strong and Weak Forms

The @deva[स्] with @deva[इ] is strong and without @deva[इ] weak.

The usual rule as to Guṇa substitute holds good in the one case and does not in the other.

**Examples:**
- @deva[दुह्]—@deva[विदूहिष्], @deva[विदुह्]
- @deva[इ]—@deva[दिदीषिष्], &c.

### (b) Lengthening and ऋ Changes

The final @deva[इ] and @deva[उ] and the penultimate @deva[अ] of @deva[गह्] and @deva[गह्] (substituted for @deva[इ] 2 Par. 'to go,' and for @deva[इ] with @deva[आधि] 'to remember' or 'to study') are lengthened and final @deva[ऋ], short or long, changed to @deva[ई]; or to @deva[ऊ] after labials or @deva[व] when the @deva[स्] is unaugmented.

**Examples:**
- @deva[जि]—@deva[जीजिष्]
- @deva[इ]—@deva[दूदूष्]
- @deva[कृ]—@deva[चिकीर्ष्]
- @deva[तृ]—@deva[तितीर्ष्]
- @deva[इ]—@deva[जीगिष्]
- @deva[इ]—@deva[इईष्], &c.

### (c) Exceptions

The roots @deva[दुह्], @deva[तुर्] and @deva[मुष्] do not change their vowel to Guṇa; and the roots @deva[मृज्], @deva[स्मर्] and @deva[भृज्] take Samprasāraṇa.

**Examples:**
- @deva[दुदूहिष्]
- @deva[मीवर्तिष्]
- @deva[ममर्जिष्]
- @deva[जिमुर्क्ष्]
- @deva[डुमुर्क्ष्]
- @deva[जुमुच्छिष्]

### (d) Optional Guṇa or इ Prefixing

Roots with an initial consonant, and having @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] short for their penultimate, and ending in any consonant, except @deva[ष्] or @deva[क्], change their vowel to Guṇa optionally or @deva[इ] is prefixed to @deva[स्].

**Examples:**
- @deva[शुन्]—@deva[दिशुनिष्], @deva[दिशोनिष्]
- @deva[द्रुह्]—@deva[दुद्रोहिष्] or @deva[दुद्रोहिष्] &c.
