When the final consonant of a word is followed by the initial letter @deva[छ्], @deva[च्] is inserted;[^1] e.g. @deva[तत्] + @deva[छात्रः] = @deva[तच्छात्रः] that scholar.

---

[^1]: @deva[श्छोऽट्] — Pāṇ. VIII. 4. 63.
