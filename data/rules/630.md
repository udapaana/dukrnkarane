## Nasal Insertion in Ātmanepada Frequentatives

When a root ends in a nasal preceded by @deva[अ] and the nasal is not dropped, @deva[व] (changeable to an Anusvāra or the nasal of the class to which the letter following it belongs) is inserted between the reduplicative @deva[अ] and the first radical consonant; the @deva[अ] of the reduplicative syllable thus remains unchanged (against @ref[629] b.).

### Examples

@deva[गम्]—@deva[गम्य]: @deva[नयम्यते] or @deva[नङ्गम्यते]

@deva[जन्]—@deva[जन्य]: @deva[वंजन्यते] or @deva[जञ्जन्यते]

But when @deva[जन्] = @deva[ज्ञातुं], the Ā. Freq. base is @deva[जाजाय], 3rd sing. @deva[जाजायते]

### (a) Extended Application

The same rule applies to the roots @deva[चर्], @deva[फण्], @deva[गुप्], @deva[जप्], @deva[वंद्], @deva[दंश्], @deva[मञ्ज्] and @deva[पद्].

@deva[चर्] and @deva[फण्] after inserting @deva[व्] change the @deva[अ] of the following syllable to @deva[अ]:

@deva[चर्]—@deva[चर्य]—@deva[वचर्य] = @deva[वचर्यते] or @deva[अचर्यते]; or @deva[चञ्चूर्यते] by @ref[694]

@deva[फण्] = @deva[फल्य] = @deva[वफञ्चयते] or @deva[पेफुल्यते] or @deva[पफूल्यते]

@deva[वंद्] = @deva[वद्]—@deva[वद्य] = @deva[ववद्यते] or @deva[ववन्द्वयते]

@deva[वुग्]—@deva[वञ्गयते] or @deva[वङ्गायते]
