@deva[दा] 'to give,' and @deva[धा], form their P. P. Ptc. as @deva[दत्तः]; the @deva[त्] of @deva[दत्तः] is dropped after a preposition ending in a vowel; @deva[प्र]—@deva[प्रदत्तः]; but in this latter case the preceding @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] is lengthened; @deva[नीदत्तः], @deva[दत्तः], &c.; @deva[त्] may optionally be retained also; @deva[अवदत्तः], @deva[अवदत्], @deva[प्रदत्तः], &c.
