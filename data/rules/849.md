## Genitive/Ablative with दूर, निकट and Synonyms

The words @deva[दूर] and @deva[निकट] and their synonyms govern either the Genitive or the Ablative; @deva[ग्रामात् ग्रामस्य वा वनं दूरं निकटं समीपं वा] the forest is distant from or near the village.[^1]

---

[^1]: Vārtikas (Sid. Kau.)
