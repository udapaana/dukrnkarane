## Genitive with वाञु (to wish) in Benedictions

@deva[वाञु] meaning 'to wish' governs the Genitive when the idea of a benediction is implied; @deva[धैर्यस्य वाञ्छति] wish to have patience; @deva[धत्तस्य मानस्य] desires to have wealth. So @deva[सर्पिषः मानयस्य].[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. II. 3. 55
