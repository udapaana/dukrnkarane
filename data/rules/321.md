A prātipadika ending in @deva[उ] not preceded by @deva[य] and denoting mankind forms its feminine in @deva[ऊ]:;[^1] e.g. @deva[कुरुः] 'a woman of the Kuru country'; but @deva[अध्वर्युः] 'the wife of an Adhvaryu'; also when it does not denote animal-kind; as @deva[अक्षाहुः]. @deva[कुक्कुटुः]; except in the case of @deva[रज्जु] and @deva[हनु];[^2] @deva[रज्जूः]; @deva[हनूः].

**(a)** **Obs.** A prātipadika ending in @deva[बाहु] and used as a proper name, and the word @deva[पञ्चगु] also form their feminine in @deva[ऊ];[^3] as @deva[भद्रबाहुः] 'a woman of that name'; but @deva[वृत्तबाहुः] 'a woman having well-rounded arms'; @deva[पञ्चगूः] !

**(b)** @deva[कङ्] and @deva[कमण्डलुः] if used as proper names take @deva[ई]; @deva[कङ्गी]: 'a woman of that name,' @deva[कमण्डल्वी]:; but @deva[कमण्डलुः कुः] in other cases.
