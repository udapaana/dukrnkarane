## Benedictive Mood Terminations

The Parasmaipada terminations of this mood may be obtained by prefixing @deva[यास्] to those of the Imperative after dropping its final @deva[स्] before @deva[स्त्] and @deva[त्] of the 2nd and 3rd per. sings.; and the Ātmanepada ones by prefixing @deva[स्] to the terminations of the Ātm. Potential and also to the @deva[त्] and @deva[थ्] occurring in them. These, therefore, are:—

**Parasmaipada:**

| | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[यासम्] | @deva[यास्व] | @deva[यास्म] |
| 2. | @deva[याः] | @deva[यास्तम्] | @deva[यास्त] |
| 3. | @deva[यात्] | @deva[यास्ताम्] | @deva[यासुः] |

**Ātmanepada:**

| | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[सीय] | @deva[सीवहि] | @deva[सीमहि] |
| 2. | @deva[सीष्ठाः] | @deva[सीयाथाम्] | @deva[सीध्वम्] |
| 3. | @deva[सीष्ट] | @deva[सीयाताम्] | @deva[सीरन्] |

(a) Parasmaipadā.
