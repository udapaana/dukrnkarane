## Guṇa Substitution for Final Vowels in Perfect

@deva[गुण] (@[Guṇa]) is substituted for the final vowel of roots ending in @deva[ऋ] preceded by a conjunct consonant, and in long @deva[ऋ] and of the roots @deva[ऋ], @deva[चक्ष्] and @deva[जाग्]; as @deva[तस्तरिव] 1 dual of @deva[स्तृ], &c. @deva[ऐकृव] 1 dual of @deva[कृ]; @deva[ववक्षिव] 2 sing. of @deva[वक्ष्]; @deva[रुरुव] 1st dual of @deva[रु]; @deva[सुषुप्व], @deva[शुश्रुविव] 1 dual of @deva[स्वप्] &c. weak terminations, and shorten their vowel when they do not; as @deva[शशरिव], @deva[शुश्रुविव] 1 dual of @deva[शृ], &c.
