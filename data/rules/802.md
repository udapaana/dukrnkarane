## Root विश् with अभि and आ: Accusative and Locative

@deva[विश्] with @deva[अभि] governs the Accusative; @deva[अभिनिविशते सुभगं मार्गं] he pursues the path of goodness; @deva[सुभगा सा गणिका यस्यां भवन्मनः अभिनिविशते] happy is that harlot girl on whom you have fixed your mind (see Bhaṭṭi. VIII, 80,).

Rarely this governs the Loc. @deva[अभिनिविशते पापं] fondly resorts to sin. @deva[विश्] with a preposition governs the Acc. but with @deva[आ] meaning 'to sit' takes the Loc. @deva[अत्र आसनं आविशत] sit on this seat.
