## Intransitive Verbs with Accusative of Space and Time

Intransitive roots govern the Accusative nouns denoting space or country, or duration of time and distance; @deva[कुरुक्षेत्रं वसति] he stays in the country of the Kurus; @deva[तत्र कतिपयं दिवसं अवसत्] there he dwelt for some days, @deva[गोदोहिकां] he sits down till a cow is milked; @deva[कोशं प्रतिष्ठे] he walks for a Koṣa, @deva[कोशं कुटिला नदी] the course of the river is winding for a Koṣa; but sometimes the Locative may be used; @deva[दीर्घकोशे स्थितः नरः] the hill is situated at a part of a Koṣa.[^1]
