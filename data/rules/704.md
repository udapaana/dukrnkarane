The affix @deva[त्][^1] added to roots having the sense of 'staying,' or 'going' shows the place where the action usually takes place; @deva[अत्र मुकुन्दस्य आसितं] 'this is the place where Mukunda to sit;' @deva[इदं गतं रामस्य] 'this is the path by which the husband of Rāmā used to pass;' @deva[अत्रुहौ एतत् अस्य] 'this is the place where Anaṅta took his food,' &c.

[^1]: @deva[अधिकरणे च क्तोऽयुद्धिस्मृत्यसौत्कारुयृत्त:] । Pāṇ. III. 4. 48.
