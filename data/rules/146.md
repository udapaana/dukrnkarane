Possessive pronouns are formed by means of the secondary affix @deva[ईय] added to @deva[तद्], @deva[एतद्], @deva[अस्मद्], and @deva[युष्मद्]; and @deva[अ] and @deva[ईन] added to @deva[अस्मद्], and @deva[युष्मद्], changed respectively to @deva[मामक्] and @deva[तावक्] in the sing. and @deva[आस्माक्] and @deva[यौष्माक्] in the plu. before these; e. g.

@deva[अस्मद्]

Mas.

| Sing. | Plural |
|---|---|
| @deva[मदीय] 'my, mine' | @deva[अस्मदीय] 'our, ours' |
| @deva[मामक] " | @deva[आस्माक] " |
| @deva[मामकीन] " | @deva[आस्माकीन] " |

Fem.

| Sing. | Plural |
|---|---|
| @deva[मदीया] 'my, mine' | @deva[अस्मदीया] 'our, ours' |
| @deva[मामिका] " | @deva[आस्माकी] " |
| @deva[मामकीना] " | @deva[आस्माकीना] " |

@deva[युष्मद्]

mas.

| Sing. | Plural |
|---|---|
| @deva[त्वदीय] 'thy, thine.' | @deva[युष्मदीय] 'your, yours.' |
| @deva[तावक] " | @deva[यौष्माक] " |
| @deva[तावकीन] " | @deva[यौष्माकीन] " |

fem.

| Sing. | Plural |
|---|---|
| @deva[त्वदीया] 'thy, thine.' | @deva[युष्मदीया] 'your, yours.' |
| @deva[तावकी] " | @deva[यौष्माकी] " |
| @deva[तावकीना] " | @deva[यौष्माकीना] " |

| @deva[तद्] | @deva[एतद्] |
|---|---|
| mas. @deva[तदीय] | mas. @deva[एतदीय] |
| fem. @deva[तदीया] | fem. @deva[एतदीया] |

**N. B.** These are declined like nouns ending in @deva[अ], @deva[आ] or @deva[ई] regularly. @deva[स्व] is a possessive pronoun by itself.
