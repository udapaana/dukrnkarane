## Aorist Passive Formation Rules

### (a) First Variety Formation

The Passive of the Aorist of roots belonging to the first variety is formed by dropping @deva[त्] and the @deva[आ] and appending the Ātmanepada terminations to the base.

**Examples:**
- @deva[स्था]—@deva[अधायि]
- @deva[कृ]—@deva[अकारि]
- @deva[धा]—@deva[अधायि]
- @deva[पच्]—@deva[अपचि]
- @deva[दिश्]—@deva[अदेशि]
- @deva[क्षिण्]—@deva[अक्षेशि], &c.

### (b) Varieties Taking Fourth, Fifth or Seventh Form

Roots belonging to the first, second, third and sixth Varieties take the fourth, fifth or seventh Variety in the Passive, in accordance with the general rules.

**Examples:**
- @deva[स्था]—@deva[अस्थायि] 1 sing.
- @deva[स्था]—@deva[अस्थायि]
- @deva[इ]—@deva[अषाटि]
- @deva[इ]—@deva[अगायि]
- @deva[चि]—@deva[अचायि]
- @deva[चर्]—@deva[अचेषि]
- @deva[नश्]—@deva[अनशि], &c.

### (c) Third Person Singular with इ

The third per. sing. of the Aorist Passive of all roots is formed by adding @deva[इ]—

#### (1) Guṇa Substitution Rules

Before this @deva[इ] the penultimate (prosodially) short vowel takes its Guṇa substitute and penultimate @deva[अ] except that of @deva[वद्] and of Seṭ roots ending in @deva[अल्] except @deva[वद्] with @deva[आ], @deva[कृष्], and @deva[स्तृ], and of the short vowel retain their Guṇa.

**Examples:**
- @deva[छिद्]—@deva[अच्छेदि]
- @deva[छुर्]—@deva[अच्छोदि]
- @deva[निन्द्]—@deva[अनिन्दि]
- @deva[तुर्]—@deva[अतारि]
- @deva[छर्]—@deva[अकारि]
- @deva[सृ]—@deva[अस्रारि]
- @deva[पस्]—@deva[अपारि]
- @deva[वद्]—@deva[अनेक्षि]
- @deva[गम्]—@deva[अगमि]
- @deva[वद्]—@deva[अवदि]
- @deva[वद्] with @deva[आ]—@deva[आवेदि]
- @deva[कृष्]—@deva[अकारि]
- @deva[शी]—@deva[अशायि]
- @deva[स्तु]—@deva[अस्तारि]
- @deva[लू]—@deva[अलारि]
- @deva[कृ] or @deva[कृ]—@deva[अकारि]

#### (2) Roots Ending in आ

Roots ending in @deva[आ] original or substituted (i. e. of roots in @deva[आ], @deva[औ], @deva[ऐ]) first @deva[य्] before this @deva[इ].

**Examples:**
- @deva[दा]—@deva[अदायि]
- @deva[घे]—@deva[अधायि]
- @deva[धे]—@deva[अधायि]
- @deva[शो]—@deva[अशायि], &c.

#### (3) Nasal Insertion

@deva[स्तम्भ्], @deva[जन्य्] and @deva[स्तज्] insert a nasal before their final consonant, so that their penultimate @deva[अ] cannot take Guṇa or Vṛddhi substitute.

**Examples:**
- @deva[अस्तभ्ङ्क्ष्]
- @deva[अजङ्क्ष्]
- @deva[अस्तञ्ज्]

#### (4) क्रम् Rules

@deva[क्रम्] without a preposition does the same optionally and with a preposition necessarily.

**Examples:**
- @deva[अक्राम], @deva[अचक्रमि]
- with @deva[आ]—@deva[अक्राम]

#### (5) Special Forms

@deva[भृज्] 'to break' forms @deva[अभार्जि] or @deva[अभ्रष्टि]; @deva[शाच्]—@deva[शाचयि] and @deva[अवाचयि] in the sense of 'to observe (10 Â.).'

#### (6) यज् and ज्या

@deva[यज्] takes Vṛddhi; @deva[ज्या] lengthens its vowel; @deva[अयाचि], @deva[अयाचि].

#### (7) इ Forms

@deva[इ] 'to go' has @deva[अगामि]; with @deva[अधि] Â., @deva[अध्यगामि] or @deva[अध्यषामि].

### (d) Roots at § 461

The roots at @ref[461] will have two forms.

**Examples:**
- @deva[युज्]—@deva[अयोपायि], @deva[अयोपायि]
- @deva[विच्छ]—@deva[अविच्छायि], @deva[आविच्छायि] &c.
- @deva[कृद्]—@deva[आतारि] or @deva[आतारिषि] 3 sing.

### (e) Optional Forms

@ref[596] (b) holds good in the Passive Aorist except in the 3rd sing.; the optional forms must be made up by appending the Âtmanepada terminations of the 5th Variety as those of the Aorist optionally take @deva[इ].

**Examples:**
- @deva[दा]—@deva[अदायि], @deva[अदायिषि]
- @deva[नी]—@deva[अनेषि], @deva[अनायिषि]
- @deva[कृ]—@deva[अकारि], @deva[अकारयिषि]
- @deva[हृ]—@deva[अहारि], @deva[अहारयिषि]; @deva[अहारयिषि] 1 sing.
- @deva[यह्]—@deva[अयहायि], @deva[अयाहायिषि] 1 sing. &c.
