The following compounds are always what they are laid down to be ( though they do not conform to the proper rules ); viz.

**(a)** Samāhāra Dwandwas:—@deva[गावश्च अजावश्च गवाजम्]; @deva[पुत्राश्च पौत्राश्च पुत्रपौत्रम्]; @deva[वृकपोत्रम्]; so @deva[स्त्रीकुमारम्], @deva[वडवगर्दभम्] camels and asses, @deva[वडवशशम्] camels and rabbits, @deva[मोक्षपथम्], @deva[दर्भशरम्] holy grass and reeds, @deva[दर्भकुलम्] grass and soft grass or shrubs, @deva[द्राक्षीदाक्षम्] &c.

**(b)** Itaretara Dwandwas:- @deva[दधिपयसी] curds and milk, @deva[कुशकाशिकी] holy sticks and grass, @deva[सर्पिर्मधुनी], @deva[मधुसर्पिषी] honey and ghee, @deva[शुक्रकृष्णे], @deva[अध्ययनतपसी], @deva[आतपसाने], @deva[उतलूतले], @deva[ऋक्सामे] (@deva[ऋक्+सामन्]) Rik and the Sāman verses, @deva[वाङ्मनसे] (@deva[वाक्+मनस्]) speech and mind (the @deva[न्] of @deva[सामन्] dropped and @deva[अ] added to @deva[मनस्] irregularly by Pān. V. 4. 77)
