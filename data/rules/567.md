## Dropping of स् in Fourth Aorist Terminations

After a short vowel, and after a consonant except a nasal or semi-vowel, the @deva[स्] of terminations beginning with @deva[स्त्] and @deva[स्थ] is dropped; @deva[इ]-@deva[ऐषीत्] 3 sing; @deva[इ]-@deva[ऐषाथाः] 2 sing; @deva[तिष्]-@deva[अतैषीत्थाः] @deva[अतिष्ट]; @deva[छर्]-@deva[अचरट्] 3rd sing. &c.
