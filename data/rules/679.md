## Formation of Past Passive Participle

The Past Passive Participle is formed by the addition of the affix @deva[त्] to the root.

### Examples

| Root | Participle | Meaning |
|------|------------|---------|
| @deva[स्ना] | @deva[स्नात] | bathed |
| @deva[जि] | @deva[जित] | conquered |
| @deva[नी] | @deva[नीत] | carried |
| @deva[श्रु] | @deva[श्रुत] | heard |
| @deva[भू] | @deva[भूत] | become |
| @deva[हृ] | @deva[हृत] | taken away |
| @deva[त्यज्] | @deva[त्यक्त] | abandoned |
| @deva[चिन्त्] | @deva[चिन्त्] | thought or meditated upon, etc. |
