[^1] With the particle @deva[पुरा] not joined with @deva[स्म], the Aorist, the Imperfect, the Perfect or the Present may be used; @deva[पुरात्राणां पुरा प्रवासिन्यः आसन्] (Sid. Kau.) here formerly dwelt pupils. But when @deva[स्म] is used with @deva[पुरा] the present alone can be used; @deva[पुरासि स्म पुरा] be formerly sacrificed.

[^1]: @deva[पुरे लुङ्लिटौ चादवैदे। पुरादूरात्मैच्छन्दसं परे लुट् च स्मस्यान्वं मा भूत् न तु स्मान्वस्य।] Sid. Kau.
