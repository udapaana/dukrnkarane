When the particle @deva[म] in combination with @deva[स्म] is used with the Imperfect in the sense of the Imperative, the augment @deva[अ] is dropped; @deva[म स्म भवः म स्म करोतु], @deva[म स्म विवदितुं प्रार्थयेत].

---

## PERFECT.
