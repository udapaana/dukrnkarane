The following words are derived from the root @deva[यह्] with the affix @deva[क्यप्]—@deva[अवयह्यः]; @deva[प्रयह्यः], @deva[पदे] 'two technical terms in grammar;' @deva[पयुक्तः] 'birds restrained from free motion, such as parrots, &c.' @deva[प्रवायुरेव्येन परोहत्राक्ता उपक्षये]। Sid. Kau. @deva[ग्रामयुद्धे सेना] 'an army stationed outside a village;' @deva[आर्युवाहास्ते आयुयुद्धे: तिर्व्यामुद्धीव उत्यर्थः] (Sid. Kau.) 'siding with the noble.' See Rāg. II. 43.
