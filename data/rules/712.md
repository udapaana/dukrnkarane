The affixes @deva[तव्य] or @deva[अनीय][^1] are added to roots or derivative bases in the sense of 'must be fit to be,' &c. Before these the ending vowel and the penultimate short of a root take their Guṇa substitute. Before @deva[तव्य] Seṭ roots take @deva[इ], Anit roots do not, and Veṭ roots take it optionally. Before @deva[अनीय] penultimate @deva[ऋ] is always changed to @deva[अर्] and not to @deva[ऋ] (as it sometimes does); e. g.

| Roots | | Pot. Ptc. |
|---|---|---|
| @deva[दा]— | @deva[दातव्यः], @deva[दानीयः] | what must or ought to be given. |
| @deva[चि]— | @deva[चेतव्यः], @deva[चयनीयः] | what must or ought to be searched into, &c. |
| @deva[नी]— | @deva[नेतव्यः], @deva[नयनीयः] | „ „ carried. |
| @deva[श्रु]— | @deva[श्रोतव्यः], @deva[श्रवणीयः] | „ „ heard. |
| @deva[भू]— | @deva[भवितव्यः], @deva[भवनीयः] | „ „ been. |
| @deva[कृ]— | @deva[कर्तव्यः], @deva[करणीयः] | „ „ done. |
| @deva[बुध्]— | @deva[बोद्धितव्यः], @deva[बोधनीयः], @deva[बोद्धव्यः] | „ „ known. |
| @deva[मुच्]— | @deva[मोक्तव्यः], @deva[मोचनीयः] | „ „ released. |
| @deva[मृज्]—[^2] | @deva[मार्ष्टव्यः], @deva[मार्जनीयः] | „ „ wiped. |
| @deva[सृज्]— | @deva[स्रष्टव्यः], @deva[सर्जनीयः] | „ „ created. |
| @deva[भृज्]— | @deva[भर्ष्टव्यः], @deva[भर्जनीयः], @deva[भृष्टव्यः], @deva[भर्जनीयः] | „ „ fried. |
| @deva[भिद्]— | @deva[भेत्तव्यः], @deva[भेदनीयः] | „ „ split. |
| @deva[निन्द्]— | @deva[निन्दितव्यः], @deva[निन्दनीयः]; | „ „ censured. |
| @deva[गुह्]—[^3] | @deva[गोहितव्यः], @deva[गूहनीयः], @deva[गुहितव्यः] | „ „ concealed. |

[^1]: @deva[तव्यस्त्रैयंथनीय्यै:] । Pāṇ. III. 1. 96. @deva[क्तेऽन्यतरस्याम् उपसंश्यानं] । Vārtika.

[^2]: The @deva[ऋ] of @deva[मृज्] is changed to @deva[आर्] against § 712.

[^3]: The @deva[उ] of @deva[गुह्] is lengthened instead of being Guṇated before a strong termination beginning with a vowel.
