This gerund or the one in @deva[क्त्वा] is used with the words @deva[अलम्], @deva[पुरस्] and @deva[पश्चात्], used as prepositions, although the notion of repetition is not present in these compounds; @deva[अलमनुशासित्वा स्त्रियम्] 'having taken to goes out;' so @deva[पश्चाद्गत्वा] or @deva[पश्चा गमम् भुङ्क्ते] or @deva[पश्चा बुद्धे].
