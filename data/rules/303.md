The @deva[न] of @deva[वाहन] is changed to @deva[ण] when preceded by a word denoting an object that can be borne; @deva[पुष्पवाहणम्]; but @deva[इन्द्रवाहनम्] (@deva[इन्द्रस्वामिके वाहनमित्यर्थः]: Sid. Kau.).
