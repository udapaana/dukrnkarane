The words @deva[अध्यास्] 'a kind of priest' and @deva[पुरोडाश्] 'sacrificial food' change their final to @deva[स्] before consonantal terminations the preceding @deva[आ] becoming @deva[अ]:—

@deva[अव्याज्].

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. | @deva[अव्याः] | @deva[अव्याजौ] | @deva[अव्याजः] |
| A. | @deva[अव्याजम्] | ,, | ,, |
| I. | @deva[अव्याजा] | @deva[अव्योभ्याम्] | @deva[अव्योभिः] |
| L. | @deva[अव्याजि] | @deva[अव्याजोः] | @deva[अव्यक्ष] |

@deva[पुरोडाज्].

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. | @deva[पुरोडाः] | @deva[पुरोडाशौ] | @deva[पुरोडाशः] |
| A. | @deva[पुरोडाशम्] | ,, | ,, |
| I. | @deva[पुरोडाशा] | @deva[पुरोडोभ्याम्] | @deva[पुरोडोभिः] |
| L. | @deva[पुरोडाशि] | @deva[पुरोडाशोः] | @deva[पुरोडक्ष] |

Bases derived from the root @deva[अञ्च्] 'to go' to worship.
