@deva[अहन्] n. meaning 'a day' changes its final @deva[न्] (which becomes visarga finally to visarga) when followed by consonantal terminations or by nothing; in other cases it is declined like @deva[नामन्].

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. A. | @deva[अहः] | @deva[अह्नी], @deva[अहनी] | @deva[अहानि] |
| I. | @deva[अह्ना] | @deva[अह्नोभ्याम्] | @deva[अह्नोभि] |
| D. | @deva[अह्ने] | ,, | @deva[अह्नेभ्य] |
| Ab. | @deva[अह्नः] | ,, | ,, |
| Ab. | @deva[पच्युः] | @deva[पच्यभ्याम्] | @deva[पच्यभ्यः] |
| G. | @deva[पच्यः] | @deva[पच्योः] | @deva[पच्याम्] |
| L. | @deva[पच्यि] | ,, | @deva[पच्यषु] |

Decline similarly @deva[सच्यिन्] and @deva[केदिषिन्]; the latter word, however, does not insert the @deva[य्] in the first five inflections; e. g. Nom. @deva[सच्या] @deva[सच्यानौ] @deva[सच्यानः]; @deva[केदिषाः-षाणौ-षाणः]; Acc. @deva[सच्यानम्] @deva[सच्यानौ-सच्यः]; @deva[केदिषाणम्-केदिषाणौ-केदिषः]
