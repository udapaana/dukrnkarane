## Augment इ in Second Future and Conditional

@deva[इ] is to be prefixed, or not, or optionally to the terminations given above, according as the root is @deva[सेट्] (@[Seṭ]) or @deva[अनिट्] (@[Aniṭ]) or @deva[वेट्] (@[Veṭ]). Before the terminations the final vowel and the penultimate vowel take their @deva[गुण] (@[Guṇa]) substitute.
