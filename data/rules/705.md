(a) The P. P. Participles of roots implying motion, of intransitive roots, and of the roots @deva[शी], @deva[छुत्], @deva[स्था], @deva[आस्], @deva[द्युल्] 'to dwell,' @deva[जन्], @deva[सुप्], and @deva[जु] 4. cl. have an active sense; @deva[गतोऽहं मद्रापुरं] 'I went to Madras;' @deva[स्थगनः भवः] 'the boy has become languid,' @deva[क्षीणा धृता: ह्रारिः] 'Hari embraced Lakshmi;' @deva[शेषोऽग्रिशयात्रि:] 'slept on Sesha;' @deva[वैकुण्ठे-मविष्टितः] 'dwelt in Vaikuṇṭha;' @deva[शिनुषुदुवसितः] 'served Shiva,' @deva[हरिदिनेऽद्य उपोषितः] 'observed a fast on the day sacred to Hari,' @deva[रामजुञु जातः] 'was born after Rāma;' @deva[गङ्गासुस्यतः] sat on Garuḍa; @deva[विश्चैतनुधीर्ह्रातः] 'grown after this,' &c.
