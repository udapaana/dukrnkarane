## Roots Preserving Vowel in Reduplicative Syllable

The roots given under @ref[400] preserve their vowel unchanged; i. e. do not substitute @deva[इ] in the reduplicative syllable, &c. @deva[कृट्]—@deva[अचक्रयत्], @deva[वृ]—@deva[अवर्त्रत्], @deva[शद्]—@deva[आशाशत्त्], @deva[रद्]—@deva[अरर्त्रत्], @deva[पद्]—@deva[अपस्पत्], @deva[प्छुट्]—@deva[अपप्छुटत्]. @deva[क्षुद्]—@deva[अचक्षुत्त्].
