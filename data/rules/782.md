## Verb Agreement with Subject in Number and Person

The verb must agree with its subject in number and person; @deva[आसीद् राजा नलो नाम] there was a king, Nala by name; @deva[अहं गच्छामि] I go; @deva[ब्राह्मणौ भुञ्जतः] two Brāhmaṇas go, &c.
