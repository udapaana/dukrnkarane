## Ablative/Accusative with स्तोक, अल्प, कष्ट, कतिपय

The words @deva[स्तोकं] 'a little' @deva[अल्प] 'a little,' @deva[कष्ट] 'difficulty,' and @deva[कतिपय] 'some' when used in an adverbial sense with verbs, are used in the Ablative with a sense of 'beginning;' @deva[कतिपयात् मुद] let off with little; similarly @deva[अल्पात् अलप्यच्युच्युः कष्टात् कयष्टो छत्तं] do with difficulty; @deva[कतिपयात् कतिपययु मायु]. but @deva[स्तोकेन विषेण हतः] killed with a little poison; when used adverbially they take the Accusative also; @deva[स्तोकं गच्छति] goes a little.
