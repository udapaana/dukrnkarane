@deva[पूर्व] 'prior, eastern', @deva[अपर] 'posterior, western', @deva[दक्षिण] 'southern', @deva[उत्तर] 'superior, northern, subsequent', @deva[अपर] 'other', and @deva[अधर] 'inferior, lower,' when they denote relation in space, time or person with reference to a particular thing or point of time &c. and are not a name, are pronouns[^1] and are then declined like @deva[सर्व]; but they are optionally declined like @deva[राम] in the Nom. plu. and Ab. and Loc. sing. when mas. e. g. Nom. @deva[पूर्वे पूर्वौ पूर्वे] or @deva[पूर्वाः] Ab. @deva[पूर्वात्-पूर्वेस्मात् पूर्वेभ्याम् पूर्वेभ्यः]; Loc. @deva[पूर्वे-पूर्वेस्मिन्]; &c., but @deva[दक्षिणाः गायकाः] (skilful musicians) only, and not @deva[दक्षिणे], for @deva[दक्षिण] does not show any relation here; so @deva[उत्तराः कुरवः] and not @deva[उत्तरे], as @deva[उत्तराः] is a name here.
