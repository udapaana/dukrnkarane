## Roots Ending in आ in Perfect

Roots ending in @deva[आ] take @deva[औ] instead of @deva[आ] in the 1st and 3rd per. sing. and drop their final @deva[आ] before vowel weak terminations, and before such as take the augment @deva[इ]; as @deva[ददौ], @deva[दद्विव], @deva[दद्विम] 1st Per. @deva[ददौ], @deva[दद्व] 2. Per. sing. of @deva[दा].
