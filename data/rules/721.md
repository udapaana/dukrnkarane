@deva[जृ] takes this affix with the negative particle prefixed to it and forms @deva[अजर्यं] 'what cannot grow old;' but this must qualify @deva[संगतं] 'friendship;' cf. @deva[तेन संगतमेण रामयोरयं कथं जरति]। Bhaṭṭi. VI. 53. In @deva[अत्रैरर्जर्यं जरसंबोध्यमानेन नित्यसंगत्वात्] Aṅg. XVIII. 7, the word @deva[अजर्यं] can be construed as an adverb; but if does not qualify @deva[संगतं], it will take the affix @deva[य]; as @deva[अजरिता कन्या].
