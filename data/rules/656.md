## Formation with क्यष्य (य) Affix

The affix @deva[य] is added to @deva[लोहित] and some other words and to words ending in the affix @deva[आ] (@deva[शट्]) and the nominal base thus formed is conjugated in the Parasmaipada and Ātmanepada:

### Examples

@deva[लोहित]—@deva[लोहितायति-ते] 'becomes red'

@deva[पटपटायते] 'utters the sound Paṭpaṭ'

---

### (f) By means of the affix इङ् (किङ् and गाङ्) and conjugated in Parasm. and Ātm.
