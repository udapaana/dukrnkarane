## Formation of Second Aorist Variety

In this Variety @deva[अ] is added on to the root and then the terminations of the Imperf. of the first group of conjugational classes are added; viz.

**Parasmaipada:**

| Person | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| 1. | @deva[अम्] | @deva[व] | @deva[म] |
| 2. | @deva[स्] | @deva[तम्] | @deva[त] |
| 3. | @deva[त्] | @deva[ताम्] | @deva[अन्] |

**Ātmanepada:**

| Person | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| 1. | @deva[इ] | @deva[वहि] | @deva[महि] |
| 2. | @deva[थाः] | @deva[इथाम्] | @deva[ध्वम्] |
| 3. | @deva[त] | @deva[इताम्] | @deva[अन्त] |
