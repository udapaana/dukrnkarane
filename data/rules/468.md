**Terminations:—**[^1]

| | Parasm. | | | Ātm. | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| | S. | D. | P. | S. | D. | P. |
| 1. | @deva[तास्मि] | @deva[तास्वः] | @deva[तास्मः] | @deva[ताहे] | @deva[तास्वहे] | @deva[तास्महे] |
| 2. | @deva[तासि] | @deva[तास्यः] | @deva[तास्थ] | @deva[तासे] | @deva[तासाथे] | @deva[तास्ध्वे] |
| 3. | @deva[ता] | @deva[तारौ] | @deva[तारः] | @deva[ता] | @deva[तारौ] | @deva[तारः] |

[^1]: The forms of the First Future may be derived by adding to the Nom. sing. of the noun of agency derived with the affix @deva[तृ] the forms of the Present Tense of the root @deva[अस्] 'to be' in the 1st and 2nd persons. The forms of the Nom. are the forms of the 3rd pers.
