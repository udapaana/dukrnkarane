## Genitive/Locative with Direction Words

Words showing distance and direction are used with the Genitive; @deva[देवदत्तस्य उत्तरं] in the north or to the north of Devadatta; these are sometimes used in the Locative also. The town Pāṭaliputra is to the north or near the town of Bihār. [OCR quality severely degraded in this rule][^1]
