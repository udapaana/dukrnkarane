## Vowel Substitution in दा, धा, स्था Roots

@deva[दा], @deva[धा], and roots assuming the forms of @deva[दा] and @deva[धा] (Vide @ref[459]) and @deva[स्था] substitute @deva[इ] for their final vowel in the Ātm. This @deva[इ] does not take its Guṇa substitute. In the Parasm. these roots take the first Variety (vide @ref[532]).
