**Terminations of the Conditional:—**[^1]

| | Parasm. | | | Ātmane | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| | S. | D. | P. | S. | D. | P. |
| 1. | @deva[स्यम्] | @deva[स्याव] | @deva[स्याम] | @deva[स्ये] | @deva[स्यावहि] | @deva[स्यामहि] |
| 2. | @deva[स्यः] | @deva[स्यतम्] | @deva[स्यत] | @deva[स्यथाः] | @deva[स्येथाम्] | @deva[स्यध्वम्] |
| 3. | @deva[स्यत्] | @deva[स्यताम्] | @deva[स्यन्] | @deva[स्यत] | @deva[स्येताम्] | @deva[स्यन्त] |

[^1]: These are obtained similarly by adding the terminations of the Imperfect to @deva[स्य] the usual rules of Sandhi being observed.
