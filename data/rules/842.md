## Ablative with Debt as Cause

When a word expressive of 'a debt,' is merely intended to be stated as a cause, it is put in the Ablative case; @deva[शतात् ऋणात्] a thing mortgaged for a hundred rupees, @deva[ऋणात् बद्धः] tied down as it were by the debt he owes.
