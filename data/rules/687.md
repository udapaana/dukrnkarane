(a) @deva[अञ्च्] in the sense of 'to worship' takes @deva[इ]; @deva[अञ्चित] (b) @deva[गम्] 'bent' not in the sense of worship @deva[गुप्तः] with @deva[सम्]—@deva[समूढः] (@deva[समुङ्गिरे शबरीति पदै]).

(b) @deva[युज्] and @deva[शुष्] reject @deva[इ] when they express the idea of 'immoderation' or 'rudeness;' @deva[युष्टः] 'rude,' @deva[विषृष्टः] 'ill-mannered;' but @deva[युजितः] 'overpowered, trifled with,' &c., @deva[विश्रुषित] 'tormented or ill-treated.'
