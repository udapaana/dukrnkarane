**Terminations of the Second Future:—**[^1]

| | Parasm. | | | Ātmane | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| | S. | D. | P. | S. | D. | P. |
| 1. | @deva[स्यामि] | @deva[स्यावः] | @deva[स्यामः] | @deva[स्ये] | @deva[स्यावहे] | @deva[स्यामहे] |
| 2. | @deva[स्यसि] | @deva[स्यथः] | @deva[स्यथ] | @deva[स्यसे] | @deva[स्येथे] | @deva[स्यध्वे] |
| 3. | @deva[स्यति] | @deva[स्यतः] | @deva[स्यन्ति] | @deva[स्यते] | @deva[स्येते] | @deva[स्यन्ते] |

[^1]: These terminations, it will be seen, are obtained by adding to the Present tense @deva[स्य] or @deva[स्य] the suffixes mentioned before which @deva[ष्] and @deva[य] are dropped before a vowel.
