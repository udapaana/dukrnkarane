## Dative/Genitive with मन् (Think, Esteem)

The indirect object of the root @deva[मन्] i.e. 'to think' or 'to esteem' (the Dative or even the Gen. may be used in this sense); one says @deva[पुत्राय पुत्रस्य वा मन्यते] one considers him a son.[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. II. 3. 17
