The Gerund of the roots @deva[हन्] and @deva[विद्][^1] is compounded with their objects and expresse totality; @deva[कन्याविदं वरयति] 'chooses as many girls as he sees, i. e. all of them,' @deva[ब्राह्मणघातं भोजयति], @deva[यं यं ब्राह्मणं जानाति स्मरति विन्दति वा तं सर्वे भोजयति इत्यर्थः] (Sid. Kau.) 'he feeds every Brāhmaṇa that he knows or comes across or remembers i. e. all of them.'

(a) [^2]The gerunds of @deva[विद्] 'to get' and @deva[जीव्] 'to live' are joined with @deva[यावद्] in the sam3 sense; @deva[यावद्वेदं खादति] 'eats what he gets.'

(b) [^3]With the words @deva[पनस्] and @deva[उदर] the gerund of @deva[पूर्] is used; @deva[पनस्पूरं स्थूलति]; @deva[उदर्पूरं खादति] 'eats so as to fill his belly.'
