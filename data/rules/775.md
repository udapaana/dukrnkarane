(a) [^1]The gerunds of @deva[अद्] and @deva[युज्] are used with nouns denoting a period of time intervening between the repetition or performance of the action; @deva[च्यवहरघातं] or @deva[च्यवहरभवतं गां पाययति] 'he makes the cows drink once in every two days to intervene, i. e. every third day' @deva[अद् पायित्वा च्यवभुषूहुत्रकुरुम्य पुनः पाययतीत्यर्थः।] (Sid. Kau.); similarly @deva[च्यहत्रियुर्य] or @deva[च्यहतुररु].

(b)[^2] Similarly the gerunds of @deva[विद्] with @deva[आ], and @deva[गह्] are used with @deva[नाम्न्] and in the sense of the Acc.; @deva[नाम्यच्छेदुमाच्चुयथः]; @deva[नामग्राहं मामाहते], &c.

(c) The gerund of @deva[भू] may optionally be used after @deva[तूष्णीं] and @deva[अन्यत्र]; @deva[तूष्णीभूतम्]—@deva[भूत्वा]—@deva[भावं]; @deva[अन्यत्रभूतम्], @deva[अन्यत्रभूत्वा], @deva[अन्यत्रभावं].

---

**(c) The Infinitive.**
