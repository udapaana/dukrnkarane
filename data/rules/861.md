## Genitive/Dative with Benediction Words

The words @deva[आयुष्मन्], @deva[मङ्गल], @deva[भङ्ग], @deva[कुशल], @deva[इष्ट], @deva[अयः] and @deva[हित] and words having the same sense, when used in a sentence containing a benediction, govern the Dative or the Genitive case; @deva[आयुष्मन् चिरञ्जीवितं कृष्णाय कृष्णस्य वा यथाट्] (Sid. Kau.); may Kṛṣṇa live long; similarly @deva[मङ्गल], @deva[भङ्ग], @deva[कुशल], @deva[निरायष्मन्], @deva[इष्ट], @deva[हो], @deva[अयःयोजनम्]. @deva[हित. पुत्रनं वा यथाट्] (Sid. Kau.).[^1]
