## Formation of Passive Base with य

### (a) Addition of य

@deva[य] is added to the root, which is weak i. e. no Guṇa or Vṛddhi substitute takes place before it.

**Examples:**
- @deva[नी]—@deva[नीय]
- @deva[चित्]—@deva[चिद्य]

### (b) Changes Before य

Before @deva[य] roots undergo the same changes as they do before the Âtmanepadi terminations of the Benedictive.

**Examples:**
- @deva[चि]—@deva[चीय]
- @deva[कृ]—@deva[क्रिय]
- @deva[स्तृ]—@deva[स्तर्य]
- @deva[ऋ]—@deva[अर्य]
- @deva[कृ]—@deva[कार्य]
- @deva[पु]—@deva[पूय]
- @deva[वच्]—@deva[वच्य] (but @deva[नित्य]—@deva[निर्य])
- @deva[वच्]—@deva[उच्य]
- @deva[यह्]—@deva[यह्य], &c.

### (c) Final आ Changed to ई

The final @deva[आ] (original or substituted) of @deva[दा] 'to give,' @deva[दे], @deva[दो], @deva[धा], @deva[मा], @deva[ह्लादा], @deva[मा], @deva[घे], @deva[प्रा] 'to drink,' @deva[पा] and @deva[हा] 'to abandon' is changed to @deva[ई] in other cases it remains unchanged.

**Examples:**
- @deva[दा] and @deva[हा]—@deva[दीय]
- @deva[घे]—@deva[घीय]
- @deva[हा]—@deva[ह्रीय]

@note[type=exception]{@deva[दा] 'to cut, to purify'—@deva[द्याय], @deva[हा]—@deva[ज्ञाय], @deva[पा]—@deva[यायात्].}
