The following words drop their final vowel or the final consonant with the preceding vowel and take @deva[अ] when at the end of a Bah. compound:—

**(a)** @deva[अक्षि] and @deva[सक्थि] 'both meaning parts of the body';[^1] @deva[जलजवत् अक्षिणी यस्य स जलजाक्षः] 'one whose eyes are like lotuses'; @deva[दीर्घे सक्थिनी यस्य स दीर्घसक्थः] 'one having long thighs', 'spindlelegged'; @deva[कमले इव अक्षिणी यस्याः सा कमलाक्षी] (@deva[स्त्री]); but @deva[दीर्घसक्थिय शकटम्] 'a cart with long poles'; @deva[रखलासा वेणुपात्री] 'a bamboo stick with big eyelike holes'; (where @deva[अ] is added by @ref[282] (c). and hence the feminine ends in @deva[आ]; see N. B. For @deva[सक्थिय] see also (e) below.

**N. B.** The fem. of Bah. compounds ending in @deva[अम्] is formed by adding @deva[आ] when it qualifies an inanimate object.

**(b)** @deva[अङ्गुलि], when the compound is an attribute of @deva[द्राख्];[^2] @deva[पञ्च अङ्गुलयो यस्य तत्पञ्चाङ्गुलं दारु] (@deva[अङ्गुलिसङ्ख्यावयवं यान्यादिविशेषणकाष्ठम्]); but @deva[पञ्चाङ्गुलिः हस्तः] the hand having five fingers.

**(c)** The words @deva[मूर्धन्] preceded by @deva[द्वि] or @deva[त्रि], @deva[लोमन्] preceded by @deva[अन्त्] and @deva[बहि], and @deva[नेतृ] used in apposition to the name of a constellation of stars;[^3] @deva[द्वौ मूर्धनौ यस्य स द्विमूर्धः] two-headed; @deva[त्रिमूर्धः] but @deva[द्वामूर्धनी]; @deva[अन्तर्लोमः]; @deva[बहिर्लोमः], @deva[मृगो नेता यासां ताः मृगनेत्रा रात्रयः] 'nights of which the Mṛiga constellation is the leader' i.e. 'whose position in the heavens marks their advance,' so @deva[पुष्यनेत्राः] &c.

**(d)** Feminine words ending in a termination showing 'a complement of,' and the word @deva[प्रमाणी];[^4] e. g. @deva[कल्याणी पञ्चमी यासा रात्रीणी ताः कल्याणीपञ्चमा रात्रयः]; 'nights whose complement is an auspicious fifth night.' @deva[स्त्री प्रमाणी यस्य असौ स्त्रीप्रमाणः] 'one whose authority is a woman.'

**(e)** @deva[हलि] and @deva[सक्थिय] optionally become @deva[हल] and @deva[सक्थय] when preceded by @deva[अ], @deva[इस] or @deva[ह];[^5] @deva[अहलः-लिः] having no plough; @deva[असक्थयः-द्विऊः] 'one who has no thighs,' @deva[दुःसक्थयः द्विऊः] 'one having deformed thighs'; @deva[द्वसक्थय:-द्विऊः]; &c. @deva[शक्ति] is another reading for @deva[सक्थि] in the sūtra; so @deva[अशक्ः-क्तिः]; &c.

**(f)** @deva[प्रजा] and @deva[मेधा] under similar circumstances drop their @deva[आ] and take @deva[अस्];[^6] as @deva[अविद्यमाना प्रजा यस्य अस्तो अप्रजाः] having no progeny, @deva[दुष्टा प्रजा यस्य स दुष्प्रजाः] having bad progeny, @deva[शोभना मेधा यस्य स सुमेधाः] of good intellect; similarly @deva[दुर्मेधाः], @deva[अमेधाः].
