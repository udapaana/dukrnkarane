**Obs.** **(a)** @deva[औडुलोमि] a descendant of Uduloman (@deva[उदुलोमः अपत्यं पुमान्]) is declined like @deva[हरि] in the sing. and dual and like @deva[ब्राह्म] in the plural, the original word @deva[उदुलोम] being substituted;[^1]

N. @deva[औडुलोमिः औडुलोमी], @deva[उदुलोमाः] &c.

**(b)** Words of similar derivation (see Pāṇ.- II. 4. 62. 63. 65. 66 and IV. 1. 105) also substitute their originals in the plural, e.g. @deva[गार्ग्यस्य अपत्यं गार्ग्यः] N. @deva[गार्ग्यः गार्ग्यौ गार्गाः] &c.

---

[^1]: From @deva[उदुलोमन्] name of a sage, + @deva[इञ्] (@deva[इ]) added @deva[अपत्यार्थं] by '@deva[प्राग्दिव्यप्र]' Pāṇ. IV. 1. 96, the final @deva[न्] being dropped (Pāṇ. VI. 4. 144).
