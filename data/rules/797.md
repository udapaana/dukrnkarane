## Accusative Case Denotes the Object

The Accusative denotes the object i.e. the person or thing upon whom or which the effect of an action takes place; @deva[हरिं सेवते] he worships Hari; @deva[ग्रामं गच्छति दूर्वां स्पृशति] going to a village he touches grass.[^1]
