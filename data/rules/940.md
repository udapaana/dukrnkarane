When the word @deva[नु] is not used with roots meaning 'to remember,' such as @deva[स्मृ], @deva[लुङ्], &c, the Second Future is used in the sense of the Imperfect; @deva[स्मरिष्यति लुङ् गोकुले वस्यावः] Kṛishṇa, do you remember that we dwelt in Gokula?
