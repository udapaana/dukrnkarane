## Final Vowel Elision in Causals

@deva[दीपी], @deva[वेपी] and @deva[दरिद्रा] drop their final vowel before @deva[अय्].

**Examples:**
- @deva[दीपयति-ते]
- @deva[वेपयति-ते]
- @deva[दरिद्रयति-ते]
