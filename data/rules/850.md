## Genitive with Verbs of Incorrect Knowledge, Memory, Control, Compassion

The root @deva[ज्ञा] meaning 'to have an incorrect knowledge of' governs the Genitive; @deva[तेलं सर्पिषः जानीते] supposed oil to be ghee; but @deva[सर्पिः जानोति].

(a) Verbs implying 'to think of, to remember,' such as @deva[स्मृ], @deva[ईश्] with @deva[अधि], 'to be master of,' and 'to have compassion upon' such as @deva[दया], &c., govern the Genitive as to the object; @deva[वाणीपतेः स्मरति] (Meg. 50) dost thou remember the lord? @deva[रामस्य दयमानः] (Bhaṭṭi. VIII. 119) taking pity on Rāma.

(b) @deva[कृ] meaning 'to impart additional properties' governs the Genitive; @deva[काष्ठं स्वरूपस्य भोः] 'fuel imparts heat to water.'
