Words ending in @deva[इस्] and @deva[उस्]; in @deva[मन्], and @deva[अस्], and having two vowels in them; in @deva[त्र] and such as have @deva[ल] for their penultimate; @deva[वर्पिस्] 'liquid ghee', @deva[ज्योतिस्], @deva[धनुस्], @deva[चर्मन्], @deva[वर्मन्] 'an armour', @deva[पक्ष्मन्], @deva[मनस्], @deva[यत्र], @deva[छत्र], &c., @deva[कूल], @deva[कुल], @deva[स्थल], &c.

**Exceptions:**—

**(a)** @deva[छदिस्] fem. 'the roof of a carriage or house' and @deva[सीमन्] fem. 'a boundary'.

**(b)** @deva[पुत्र], @deva[अमित्र] (@deva[न मित्रम्]), @deva[शात्र] 'pupil', @deva[सूत्र], @deva[मन्त्र], @deva[मुत्र] 'name of a demon' and @deva[उड्डु] all mas., @deva[याग्रा], @deva[माग्रा], @deva[भग्रा] 'a smith's bellows', @deva[दुग्धा], @deva[बद्रा] 'a leather strap', all fem.

**(c)** and @deva[तूल], @deva[उपल], @deva[ताल], @deva[कोशल] 'a granary or store house for corn', @deva[तरल] 'the middle gem of a necklace', @deva[करम्बल], @deva[वेश्वल] 'a Brāhmaṇa who attends on an idol', and @deva[त्रुवल], which are all mas.
