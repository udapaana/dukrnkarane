(a) @deva[भू] when it has a Subanta prefixed to it and is without a preposition takes the affix @deva[क्यप्] impersonally; @deva[ब्रह्मणि भावः ब्रह्मभूयं] 'identity with Brahman.' When no Subanta is used prepositionally with it, it takes the affix @deva[यत्]; @deva[भव्यः] or @deva[भूयम्].

(b) @deva[वह्] under similar circumstances takes either @deva[क्यप्] or @deva[एयत्] in a passive sense in the Veda, namely: @deva[वाह्यं] or @deva[वहयेत्] 'expounding the Veda;' (@deva[वहतेवेदः तस्य वेदित्] Sid. Kau.).
