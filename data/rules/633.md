## Paradigms of Ātmanepada Frequentatives

The 3rd singular of @deva[बोबुध्] the Ātm. Freq. Base of the root @deva[बुध्] and @deva[देदीय] that of the root @deva[द्या]:

### Conjugational Tenses

| Tense | Active (@deva[बोबुध्]) | Active (@deva[देदीय]) | Passive (@deva[बोबुध्]) | Passive (@deva[देदीय]) |
|-------|------|------|---------|---------|
| Present | @deva[बोबुध्यते] | @deva[देदीयते] | @deva[बोबुध्यते] | @deva[देदीय्यते] |
| Imperfect | @deva[अबोबुध्यत] | @deva[अदेदीयत] | @deva[अबोबुध्यत] | @deva[अदेदीय्यत] |
| Imperative | @deva[बोबुध्यताम्] | @deva[देदीयताम्] | @deva[बोबुध्यताम्] | @deva[देदीय्यताम्] |
| Potential | @deva[बोबुध्येत] | @deva[देदीयेत] | @deva[बोबुध्येत] | @deva[देदीय्येत] |

### General Tenses

| Tense | Active (@deva[बोबुध्]) | Active (@deva[देदीय]) | Passive |
|-------|------|------|---------|
| Perfect | @deva[बोबोधाञ्चके], etc. | @deva[देदीयाञ्चके], etc. | Like the Active |
| Aorist | @deva[अबोबोधिष] | @deva[अदेदीयिष] | @deva[अबोबोधि], @deva[अदेदीयि] |
| F. Future | @deva[बोबोधिता] | @deva[देदीयिता] | Like the Active |
| Sec. Future | @deva[बोबोधिष्यते] | @deva[देदीयिष्यते] | " |
| Conditional | @deva[अबोबोधिष्यत] | @deva[अदेदीयिष्यत] | " |
| Benedictive | @deva[बोबोधिषीष्ट] | @deva[देदीयिषीष्ट] | " |

**Note**: Irregular Ātm. Freq. Bases will be given under @ref[639].

---

**PARASMAIPADA FREQUENTATIVES**

The Parasm. Frequentative is peculiar to the Veda. Its forms are very rarely to be met with in Classical composition.
