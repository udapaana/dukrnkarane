Bases in @deva[यस्] or @deva[ईयस्]

Comparatives in @deva[यस्] are declined like bases in @deva[वत्] in the first five inflections and the Voc. sing. and like nouns in @deva[अस्] in other respects; e. g.

@deva[श्रेयस्] (comparative of @deva[प्रशस्य]) more praiseworthy.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. | @deva[श्रेयान्] | @deva[श्रेयांसौ] | @deva[श्रेयांसः] |
| V. | @deva[श्रेयन्] | ,, | ,, |
| A. | @deva[श्रेयांसम्] | ,, | @deva[श्रेयसः] |
| I. | @deva[श्रेयसा] | @deva[श्रेयोभ्याम्] | @deva[श्रेयोभिः] |

The rest like @deva[वम्मत्]. Decline all comparatives in @deva[ईयस्] such as @deva[गरीयस्], @deva[लघीयस्], @deva[प्रथीयस्], &c. similarly.

Neu.

| | N. V. A. |
|---|---|
| @deva[श्रेयः] | @deva[श्रेयसी] | @deva[श्रेयांसि] |

The rest like @deva[मनस्]. Decline n. nouns in @deva[ईयस्] similarly.
