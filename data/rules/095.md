**(a)** @deva[च्] is changed to @deva[क्] when at the end of a pada or when followed by a consonant except a nasal or a semi-vowel.

**(b)** In the case of roots beginning with @deva[च्] it is changed to @deva[य्] under the same circumstances;

**(c)** when forming part of the roots @deva[चृ], @deva[धृ], @deva[दृज्] and @deva[सिच्], it is optionally changed to @deva[य्] i. e. to @deva[क्] or @deva[य्];

**(d)** and in the case of the root @deva[वह्] it is changed to @deva[य्] under the above-mentioned circumstances.[^1]

**(e)** The @deva[च्] of @deva[उष्णिच्ह्] f. a kind of metre is changed to @deva[क्] when followed by a hard consonant or by nothing, and to @deva[ग्] before a soft consonant (Pāṇ. III. 2, 59).

---

[^1]: @deva[च्] is changed to @deva[क्] when at the end of a pada or when followed by a consonant except a nasal or a semi-vowel.
