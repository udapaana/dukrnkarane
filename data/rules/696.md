(a) Roots ending in a nasal lengthen their penultimate vowel before a weak termination beginning with any consonant except a nasal or a semivowel, or before the affix @deva[किन्] @deva[चर्]—@deva[चारिन्], @deva[तर्]—@deva[तारिन्], &c.

(b) Anit roots ending in a nasal, the root @deva[वन्] 1. P. and the eight roots of the 8th class—@deva[तन्] and others (see p. 355) drop their nasal before a weak termination beginning with any consonant except a nasal or a semivowel:—

| Roots | | P. P. Ptc. | Roots | | P. P. Ptc. |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| @deva[मन्] | 'to think', &c. | @deva[मतः] | @deva[नम्] | 'to bow' | @deva[नतः] |
| @deva[ङृह्] | 'to strike,' | @deva[हतः] | @deva[यम्] | 'to restrain' | @deva[यतः] |
| @deva[एघ्] | 'to sport' | @deva[हतः] | @deva[वन्] | 1. P. 'to sound, to serve,' &c. | @deva[वतः] |
| @deva[गच्] | 'to go' | @deva[गतः] | @deva[युष्] | 'to shine' | @deva[युतः] |
| @deva[तन्] | | @deva[ततः] | @deva[वुच्] | 'to graze' | @deva[वतः] |
| @deva[क्षुण्] | | @deva[क्षुतः] | @deva[नुद्] | 'to beg' | @deva[नतः] |
| @deva[सम्प्] | | @deva[सप्तः] | | | |
