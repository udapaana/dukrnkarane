The roots given at @ref[414] and @deva[ज्ञा] substitute @deva[न्] for @deva[त्].

| Roots | | P. P. Ptc. | Roots | | P. P. Ptc. |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| @deva[री] | to flow, to move | @deva[रीणः] | @deva[जू] | to grow old | @deva[जीर्णः] |
| @deva[ली] | to melt, etc. | @deva[लीनः] | @deva[दृ] | to tear | @deva[दीर्णः] |
| @deva[श्ली] | to go, to hold | @deva[श्लीनः] | @deva[नी] | to lead | @deva[नीर्णः] |
| @deva[श्ली] | to go, to move | @deva[श्लीनः] | @deva[पू] | to fill, to gratify | @deva[पूर्णः] |
| @deva[धू] | to shake | @deva[धूनः] | @deva[भृ] | to bear, to nourish | @deva[भूर्णः] |
| @deva[नू] | to destroy | @deva[नूनः] | @deva[मृ] | to hurt, to kill | @deva[मूर्णः] |
| @deva[लू] | to cut | @deva[लूनः] | @deva[वृ] | to choose | @deva[वूर्णः] |
| @deva[इ] | to go | @deva[ईणः] | @deva[श्रि] | to tear asunder | @deva[शीर्णः] |
| @deva[कू] | to scatter | @deva[कीर्णः] | @deva[स्तृ] | to scatter | @deva[स्तीर्णः] |
| @deva[गृ] | to utter a sound, to praise, etc. | @deva[गीर्णः] | @deva[ज्ञा] | to grow old | @deva[ज्ञीणः] |
