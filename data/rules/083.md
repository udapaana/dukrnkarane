The word @deva[क्रोष्टृ] a jackal, is declined like a word ending in @deva[ऋ] necessary in the first five inflections and optionally before the vowel terminations beginning with the Inst. sing. except that of the Gen. plu.[^1] e. g.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. | @deva[क्रोष्टा] | @deva[क्रोष्टारौ] | @deva[क्रोष्टारः] |
| V. | @deva[क्रोष्टः] | ,, | ,, |
| A. | @deva[क्रोष्टारम्] | ,, | @deva[क्रोष्टॄन्] |
| I. | @deva[क्रोष्ट्रा-त्रुणा] | @deva[क्रोष्टृभ्याम्] | @deva[क्रोष्टृभिः] |
| D. | @deva[क्रोष्टे-त्रे] | ,, | @deva[क्रोष्टृभ्यः] |
| Ab. | @deva[क्रोष्टुः-क्रोष्टोः] | ,, | ,, |
| G. | ,, | @deva[क्रोष्टोः-त्रोः] | @deva[क्रोष्टृणाम्] |
| L. | @deva[क्रोष्टरि-त्रौ] | ,, | @deva[क्रोष्टृषु] |

**(a)** @deva[क्रोष्टृ] becomes @deva[क्रोष्ट्रृ] in the fem. also (by @deva[स्त्रियाम् च] Pāṇ. VIII. 1. 96.) which with the fem. term. @deva[ई] becomes @deva[क्रोष्ट्री] and is declined like @deva[नदी].

**Obs.**—The word @deva[प्रियक्रोष्टृ] neu. is declined like @deva[मधु] and optionally like @deva[क्रोष्टृ] mas. before the vowel terminations from the I. sing.; e. g. D. sing. @deva[प्रियक्रोष्टुणे-त्रे-त्रुणे].
