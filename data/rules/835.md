## Ablative with परि+जि (Disgust)

In the case of @deva[जि] with @deva[परि] the thing unbearable is put in the Ablative and the context alone shows the acceptable or difficult; @deva[तां पराज्यते] (Bhaṭṭi. VIII. 71,) 'who got disgusted with the love (of Rāvaṇa)'; but @deva[वाचात् पराजयते].[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. I. 4. 26
