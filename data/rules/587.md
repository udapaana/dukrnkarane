## Ātmanepada Benedictive Terminations and Paradigms

The Ātm. terminations, are strong. Before these radical vowel takes its Guṇa substitute, but when the intermediate @deva[इ] is not prefixed to the terminations, the final @deva[ऋ] remains unchanged and @deva[ऋ] is changed to @deva[ऋर्], or to @deva[उर्] if a labial or @deva[व्] precedes.

**Examples:**
- @deva[चि]—@deva[चेषीष्ट]
- @deva[धु]—@deva[धोषीष्ट]
- @deva[लृ]—@deva[लिरिषीष्ट]
- @deva[स्तृ]—@deva[स्तरिषीष्ट], or @deva[स्तीरिषीष्ट]
- @deva[वृ]—@deva[वुरिषीष्ट] or @deva[वूरिषीष्ट], &c.
