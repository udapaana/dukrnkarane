If a Bah. end in @deva[ऊधस्] it takes the suffix @deva[ई] in the fem. before which @deva[न्] is substituted for the final @deva[अस्];[^1] @deva[पीन ऊधः यस्याः सा पीनोधनी] having a large udder; @deva[कुण्डोधनी] (see Rag. I. 84.); also when it is preceded by a numeral or an indeclinable; @deva[अद् बृहती], @deva[अत्यती] having an udder exceedingly large; but @deva[ऊघः अतिकान्ता अत्यघा].

**(a)** Similarly a Bah. ending in @deva[द्वायन्], and @deva[हायन्] denoting age, and having a numeral for its first member takes @deva[ई] in the fem.; @deva[द्विहायनी], @deva[त्रिहायनी] 'a girl two years old;', @deva[त्रिहायणी], &c; but @deva[द्विहायना शाला] a building built two years since.

**N. B.**—the @deva[य] of @deva[हायन्] is changed to @deva[ण] after @deva[द्वि] and @deva[बहुर्] only when it takes @deva[ई]; @deva[बहुहैयणी बाला]; but @deva[द्विहायना], @deva[बहुहैयना शाला].
