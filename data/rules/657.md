## Formation with इङ् Affixes (किङ् and गाङ्)

Verbal bases are formed from the nouns given below by the addition of the affixes @deva[इङ्] (@deva[किङ्]), and @deva[इङ्] (@deva[गाङ्]) with various senses.

Those derived by means of @deva[किङ्] are conjugated in the Ātmanepada and those by @deva[गाङ्] in the Parasmaipada.

### Examples

@deva[पुच्छ] with @deva[उद्], @deva[देव], and @deva[धट्]:

@deva[उपुच्छङ्गते] 'raises the tail'

@deva[उद्भुच्छङ्गति] 'he raises the load'

@deva[धटे] 'collects together the vessels'

@deva[सुरेश्वरते विष्णु] 'drinks surā cooked in pots'

@deva[कनीषकति घर्] 'causes to sprinkle', etc.
