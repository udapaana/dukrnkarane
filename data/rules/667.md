## Formation of Present Parasmaipada Participle

The participle of the present Parasmaipada is formed by the addition of the affix @deva[अत्] to that form of a root, primitive or derivative, which it assumes before the third person plural termination of the Present Tense. If the base ends in @deva[अ], the @deva[अ] is dropped.

### Examples

| Root (Class) | 3rd Pl. Present | Participle | Meaning |
|--------------|-----------------|------------|---------|
| @deva[भू] (1) | @deva[भव] + @deva[अन्ति] | @deva[भव] + @deva[अत्] = @deva[भवत्] | being, standing |
| @deva[तुद्] (1) | @deva[तुद] + @deva[अन्ति] | @deva[तुद] + @deva[अत्] = @deva[तुद्वत्] | being, standing |
| @deva[द्विष्] (2) | @deva[द्विष्] + @deva[अन्ति] | @deva[द्विष्] + @deva[अत्] = @deva[द्विषत्] | hating |
| @deva[अद्] (2) | — | @deva[अद्यत्] | eating |
| @deva[या] (2) | — | @deva[यात्] | going |
| @deva[हु] (3) | — | @deva[जुहुवत्] | sacrificing |
| @deva[दिव्] (4) | — | @deva[दीव्यत्] | playing |
| @deva[सु] (5) | — | @deva[सुन्वत्] | extracting |
| @deva[तुद्] (6) | — | @deva[तुद्यत्] | giving pain |
| @deva[रुज्] (7) | — | @deva[रुज्यत्] | preventing |
| @deva[कृ] (8) | — | @deva[कुर्वत्] | doing |
| @deva[तन्] (8) | — | @deva[तन्वत्] | stretching, etc. |
| @deva[क्री] (9) | — | @deva[क्रीणत्] | buying |
| @deva[चुर्] (10) | — | @deva[चुर्णत्] | stealing, etc. |
| @deva[चोर्] (10) | — | @deva[चोरयत्] | stealing |

### Derived Forms

@deva[बोध्] cau. of @deva[कृ]: @deva[बोधयत्] 'causing to know'

@deva[बुबोधिष्] desid. of @deva[बुध्]: @deva[बुबोधिषत्] 'desiring to know'

@deva[दित्स्] desid. of @deva[दा]: @deva[दिदासत्] 'desiring to give'

@deva[चेक्षिप्] freq. of @deva[क्षिप्]: @deva[चेक्षिपत्] 'throwing again and again', etc.

### (a) Optional वस् Form

The form @deva[अत्] is optionally changed to @deva[वस्] when added to the root @deva[विद्]: @deva[विदत्] or @deva[विद्वस्] 'knowing'

### (b) Agent Sense

The affix @deva[अत्] when added to @deva[षिष्] and @deva[सु] 'to extract Soma juice in a sacrifice' has the sense of an agent: @deva[शत्रुवत्] m. 'an enemy', @deva[पवं सनं सुन्वन्तो वृत्रायत: यः] 'all who sacrifice in a sacrifice'

### (c) Fitness, Respect

When affixed to @deva[अर्ह्] it has the sense of 'fitness, respect': @deva[अर्हत्] 'one deserving respect, respectable'

### (d) Ease vs. Difficulty

@deva[आधि] 2 P. and @deva[आधि] cau. take the affix @deva[अत्] when the idea of difficulty is not present: @deva[अधीयत्] 'studying without experiencing any difficulty', @deva[परयावत्] 'looking or hearing with ease'; but @deva[कुञ्चुण अधीते], @deva[छ्वायः पूर्तिति]
