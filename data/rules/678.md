## Periphrastic Perfect Participles

The participles of the Periphrastic Perfect Parasmaipada and Ātmanepada are formed by the addition of the Perfect Participal forms of the auxiliary verbs @deva[कृ], @deva[अस्] or @deva[भू] to the base in @deva[आम्].

### Examples

@deva[दयालासिवस्], @deva[ञ्चत्रिषिवस्]

@deva[गण्]—@deva[गणयामासिवस्], @deva[गणयामचक्रुवस्], etc.
