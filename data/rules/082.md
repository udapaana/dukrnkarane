Nouns expressive of relationship like @deva[पितृ] m. a father, @deva[मातृ] f. a mother, @deva[देवृ] m. a husband's brother, &c. with the exception of @deva[नप्तृ] a grandson, @deva[भर्तृ] a husband and @deva[स्वसृ] a sister, as also the words @deva[स्तोतृ] one who praises (Un. II, 92), @deva[नृ] a man (Un.II. 98) and @deva[सप्तेतृ] a charioteer, do not change their @deva[ऋ] to @deva[आर्] in the N. dual and plural and Acc. sing. and dual; e. g.

| | @deva[पितृ] | | | | @deva[मातृ] | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| N. | @deva[पिता] | @deva[पितरौ] | @deva[पितरः] | N. | @deva[माता] | @deva[मातरौ] | @deva[मातरः] |
| V. | @deva[पितः](@deva[र्]) | ,, | ,, | V. | @deva[मातः](@deva[र्]) | ,, | ,, |
| A. | @deva[पितरम्] | ,, | @deva[पितॄन्] | A. | @deva[मातरम्] | ,, | @deva[मातॄः] |

The rest like @deva[धातृ]. The rest like @deva[स्वसृ].

Decline similarly @deva[भ्रातृ]. Decline similarly @deva[यातृ] a hus-
@deva[जामातृ], @deva[देवृ], @deva[स्तोतृ], @deva[सप्तेतृ] and... band's brother's wife, @deva[दुहितृ] a
@deva[नृ]. The Gen. plu. of @deva[नृ] is daughter, and @deva[ननान्दृ] or @deva[ननन्दृ]
@deva[नृणाम्] or @deva[नूणाम्][^1] a husband's sister.
