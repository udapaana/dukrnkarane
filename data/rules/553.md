## Samprasāraṇa in Third Aorist

The roots @deva[हे] and @deva[स्पुघ्] can take Samprasāraṇa, @deva[चि] does it optionally; @deva[हे] @deva[उ]-@deva[हाक्]-@deva[हाइ] or @deva[हृघ्], then @deva[चुहुद्], @deva[चुहात्] by @ref[550]; then @deva[अचुहात्], @deva[अचुहूत्]; @deva[स्पुघ्]—@deva[स्पृभ्द्]—@deva[स्पाइ]-@deva[चुस्पुघ्]—@deva[चुस्पुघ्]; @deva[अचुस्पुघ्त्], @deva[अचुस्पुघम्] 1st sing.
