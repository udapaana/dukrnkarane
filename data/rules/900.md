Many past passive participles are used actively in which case they may govern the Accusative case, like a Perfect tense active; @deva[आरूढशैलम्] (Rag. VI. 77.) which had ascended the mountains; similarly @deva[अतिक्रमप्रसूतः सर्वितम्]; @deva[आगतोदयपौरुषम्] crossed i. e. got over the calamity; @deva[अवारूढस्य यमुनायां तीरम्] arrived to the bank of the Yamunā, &c.
