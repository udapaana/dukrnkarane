A radical @deva[इ] preceding or following a vowel is sometimes changed to @deva[ऊ] before @deva[त्] or @deva[न्]; when it is preceded by @deva[य] it is dropped; @deva[इङ्]—@deva[इतः], @deva[ऊतः]; @deva[पुरीत्], @deva[पुङ्]—@deva[पूर्णः], @deva[नीरु]—@deva[लीणः], @deva[पु]—@deva[पूतः], @deva[द्युतः] (See @ref[693], c.).
