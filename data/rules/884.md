The pronoun @deva[तद्] has often the sense of 'well known, renowned,' &c. @deva[तौ पार्वतीपरमेश्वरौ] those (well-known) Pārvatī and Parameśvara; @deva[तान्यस्मै वनस्थलानि] those well-known forest sites.

(a) When repeated, this pronoun has the sense of 'various, several;' @deva[तेषु तेषु रमणीयेषु स्थानेषु] in those various highly delightful spots; @deva[छूटन्ति तैस्तैः यत्नैः] notwithstanding several efforts–@deva[कृत्येतैः स्तवतियत्नैः] &c (Bhag. VII. 20).
