## Formation of Nominal Verbs Expressing Wish (क्यच्)

When the sense of 'wish' is to be expressed, a nominal verb may be derived from any Subanta or nominal stem by affixing @deva[य] to it. The derivative verbal base derived in this manner is conjugated in the Parasmaipada only.
