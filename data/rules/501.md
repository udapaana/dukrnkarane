## Insertion of त after Reduplicative Syllable

@deva[त] is inserted after the reduplicative syllable in the case of roots beginning with @deva[अ] and ending in a conjunct consonant, in that of @deva[अह्] 'to pervade' and @deva[चक्ष्] 'to go'; consequently @deva[अ] after @deva[त] is changed to @deva[आ]; @deva[अञ्ज्] @deva[अनज्]+@deva[अ]=@deva[आनज्]+@deva[अ]+@deva[अ]=@deva[आनञ्ज], similarly @deva[आनन्द] 1 sing. of @deva[अन्द्], @deva[आनर्द] of @deva[अर्द्], &c.
