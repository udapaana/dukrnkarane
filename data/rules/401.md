Some roots of the 10th class exclusively take the Ātmanepada i. e. even when the fruit of the action expressed by them does not accrue to the agent. These are:—@deva[अर्थ्] to request, to desire, @deva[कुह्] to astonish, to deceive, @deva[चिन्त्] to be conscious of, to think, @deva[दंश्] to bite, @deva[दृश्] (or @deva[दर्श्] according to some) to see, to bite, @deva[दुह्] or @deva[दुह्] to accumulate, @deva[तन्त्र्] to support a family, @deva[मन्त्र्] to counsel secretly, @deva[मूष्] to search, to hunt, to seek, @deva[ग्रथ्] to take, to string together, @deva[तर्ज्] and @deva[तर्क्] to reprove, @deva[वद्] and @deva[गल्व्] to injure, to hurt, @deva[विच्छद्] to kill (@deva[विछ्द्] according to some), @deva[चित्र्] to measure, @deva[घृष्] to desire, to contract, @deva[मृत्] to kill, @deva[तुद्] to fear, @deva[सूच्] to praise, @deva[यज्] to worship, @deva[रभ्] to guess, @deva[मृष्] to strike, @deva[सुव्] to look at, to inspect, @deva[कुत्स्] to reproach, @deva[छुत्] (according to some @deva[खुट्]) to cut, @deva[भुल्] to drop down, @deva[भृट्] to see, to expound, @deva[खुद्] to abstain from giving, to muddle, @deva[खुट्] to cut, @deva[वज्ज्] to deceive, @deva[भूष्] to have the power of generation, to be eminent, @deva[उर्द्] to gratify, @deva[दिन्] to bewail, @deva[यु] to know, @deva[विद्] to know, to be conscious of, @deva[मुद्] to stop, @deva[हुष्] to censure, and @deva[हुत्स्] to smile improperly.
