## Genitive with Certain Indeclinables

The Genitive is used with indeclinables like @deva[मध्ये], @deva[परि], @deva[गते], &c.; @deva[गङ्गायाः मध्ये परि वा] in the middle or on the other side of the Ganges; @deva[अर्थः ग्रामस्य गते] for the sake of this life.
