[^1] When a question is asked and an answer is given to it, the Present is used in the sense of the Past tense when the particle @deva[नु] is used; @deva[कः अकार्षीत् किम्]—@deva[नु करोति सः] where @deva[करोति] is equivalent to @deva[अकरोत्]; when the particles @deva[नु] and @deva[ह] are used the Present may be optionally used; @deva[कोऽकार्षीत् किम्]—@deva[नु करोति] or @deva[चकार]—@deva[ह करोति] or @deva[न्यकार्षीत्].

[^1]: @deva[ननोर्लटः प्रश्ने। ननोर्हिभाषा।] Pāṇ. III. 2. 120, 121.
