Seṭ roots (subject to @ref[684]), and all derivative bases admit of @deva[इ]; roots of the Tenth Class and causals reject their final @deva[अय], Par. Frequentatives their final @deva[अ], and Ātम. Frequentatives their @deva[य]:—

| Roots. | P. P. Ptc. | | Roots. | P. P. Ptc. |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| @deva[शङ्क्] | @deva[शङ्कित] 'suspected.' | | @deva[कथ्] | @deva[कथित] 'told.' |
| @deva[उद्] | @deva[उदित] 'spoken.' | | @deva[प्रथ्] | @deva[प्रथित] 'spread.' |

| Roots. | P. P. Ptc. | | Roots. | P. P. Ptc. |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| @deva[एष्] | @deva[एषित] 'increased.' | | @deva[बोधय्] cau. Base of @deva[बुध्], @deva[बोधित] 'made to know.' |
| @deva[कम्प्] | @deva[कम्पित] 'shaken.' | | @deva[चिकीर्ष] des. b. of @deva[कृ]; @deva[चिकीर्षित] 'desired to do.' |
| @deva[चुर्] | @deva[चोरित] 'stolen, deceived.' | | @deva[बोबुध्] Atम. Fre. Base of @deva[बुध्], @deva[बोबुधित] 'frequently known.' |
| @deva[मृश्] | @deva[स्पृशित] 'taken.' | | @deva[बोभूय]—Par. Fre. Base of @deva[भू], @deva[बोभूत] &c. |

**Exceptions:—**@deva[इष्], @deva[उष्], @deva[चष्], 'to go, to till,' &c., @deva[चित्] 'to know to observe,' &c., @deva[विद्], @deva[मिद्], @deva[स्तृ], @deva[भृत्], @deva[भृष्], @deva[भी], @deva[पी], @deva[हीय], @deva[स्यै], @deva[प्रिह्य], @deva[चुष्], @deva[डूष्], @deva[दुष्], @deva[सुष्], @deva[रुष्].

N. B. There are many Seṭ roots which reject the intermediate @deva[इ] but as some of them form their P. P. participles in @deva[न] and others irregularly, they will be given in their proper places.
