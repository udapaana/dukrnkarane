## Monosyllabic Roots with Penultimate अ

Monosyllabic roots, having a short @deva[अ] for their penultimate, change it to @deva[इ] and drop the reduplicative syllable before the weak terminations and before @deva[व] when it takes @deva[इ], provided their initial letter undergoes no change in the reduplication, e. g. @deva[पत्] 1st dual @deva[पेतुव्]+@deva[इव]=@deva[पेत्]+@deva[इव]=@deva[पेतिव], @deva[पेतिव] 2 sing. @deva[शक्ष्व], @deva[शसक्ष्व] 2. sing. of @deva[शक्], but @deva[निनेश्व] 2nd dual of @deva[नन्द्] as the @deva[अ] here is prosodially long.

Exceptions:—Roots beginning with @deva[व] and the roots @deva[शास्] and @deva[ग्रस्].
