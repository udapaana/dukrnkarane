## I. Masculine

Verbal derivatives formed by the affixes @deva[अच्] and @deva[घञ्] are masculine, as @deva[पाकः] 'cooking', @deva[जयः] or @deva[विजयः] 'victory', @deva[भावः] 'being', &c.
