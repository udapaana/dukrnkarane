## List of Common Sanskrit Prepositions

The following are the prepositions (upasargas) commonly prefixed to roots:

### @deva[अति]

'beyond, over'; @deva[अतिक्रम] 'overstepping' or 'going beyond', @deva[अतिसर्जन] 'a gift', &c.

### @deva[अधि]

'over, above', &c.; @deva[अधिगम] 'going up, acquisition', @deva[अधिकार] 'high office or power', @deva[अधिक्षेप] 'censure', &c.

### @deva[अनु]

'after, behind, along', &c.; @deva[अनुक्रमण] 'following', @deva[अनुच्छोति] 'imitation', @deva[अनुग्रह] 'favour', &c.

### @deva[अप]

'away from, away'; @deva[अपनयन] 'the act of taking away', @deva[अपहृ] 'to plunder, seize', &c., @deva[अपकार] 'harm, wrong', &c.

### @deva[अपि]

(sometimes @deva[पि]) 'near to', 'over', 'taking to', &c.; @deva[अपिगम्] 'to be resolved into or reduced to', @deva[अपिधान] or @deva[पिधान] 'a covering', @deva[अपिधा] 'destruction', &c.[^1]

This prep. is more commonly used as an independent adverb having a cumulative force in classical Sanskrit.

### @deva[अभि]

'towards, near to', &c.; @deva[अभिगम्] 'to go towards', @deva[अभिजन] 'noble descent or family', @deva[अभिमान] 'self-respect', @deva[अभिरुच्] 'to defeat', &c.

### @deva[अव]

(sometimes @deva[व]; see @deva[अपि] and note) 'away, down', &c.; @deva[अव] or @deva[वगाह] 'to plunge into', @deva[अवतार] 'descent', @deva[अवगत] 'reproached', @deva[अवमन्] 'to disrespect', &c.

### @deva[आ]

'up to, towards, all round, a little', &c.; @deva[आच्छाद्] 'to cover all round', @deva[आकार] 'form or shape (within due bounds)', @deva[आकाश] 'that which shines all round' i.e. 'the ether'; @deva[आकम्प] 'to shake a little', &c.

### @deva[उद्]

'upon', &c.; @deva[उद्गम्] 'to go up', @deva[उद्यम] 'industry', @deva[उत्सर्ग] 'pouring out, hence a gift, a general rule', &c.

### @deva[उप]

'near to, towards, by the side of', &c.; @deva[उपगम्] 'to go near to', @deva[उपकृति] f. 'an obligation', @deva[उपरति] f. 'death', @deva[उपस्थान] 'praise, worship', @deva[उपमिति] f. 'comparison', &c.

### @deva[दुस्]

'bad, hard to be done', &c.; @deva[दुराचार] 'bad conduct', @deva[दुष्कर] 'hard to be done', @deva[दुःसह] 'difficult to be borne', &c.

### @deva[नि]

'in, into, great, opposed to', &c.; @deva[निड्] 'to insult', @deva[निकेत] 'a house', @deva[निचय] 'a heap, a great collection', @deva[निपीत] 'drunk in', @deva[निदेश] 'a command', &c.

### @deva[निस्]

'out of, away from, without', &c.; @deva[निःसृ] 'to issue out', @deva[निर्गम] 'a passage out', @deva[निर्दोष] 'out of i.e. free from blame', @deva[निःशङ्क] 'without doubt', &c.

### @deva[परा]

'away, back, opposed to', &c.; @deva[पराकृ] 'to reject, to despise', @deva[पराक्रम] 'to act bravely', @deva[परागत] 'gone away', @deva[पराञ्च] 'turn back', @deva[पराजय] 'what is opposed to victory, defeat', &c.

### @deva[परि]

'all round, about', &c.; @deva[परिधा] 'to place all round i.e. to put on or wear', @deva[परिधि] 'a wall &c. that surrounds', @deva[परिणाम] 'ripening, maturity', @deva[परिगणना] 'counting all round, i.e. a complete enumeration', &c.

### @deva[प्रति]

'towards, back, in return, in opposition to', &c.; @deva[प्रतिगम्] 'to go towards', @deva[प्रतिभाषण] 'a speech in return, an answer', @deva[प्रतिविकार] 'an act in opposition to i.e. a remedy', &c.

### @deva[वि]

'apart, separate from, reverse to', &c.; @deva[विचल्] 'to move apart', @deva[वियुज्] 'to be separated', @deva[विक्री] opp. of @deva[क्री] 'to sell', 'to buy', &c.; sometimes this has an intensive force.

### @deva[सम्]

'together with, excellent, full', &c.; @deva[संगम्] 'to be united', @deva[संस्कार] 'perfection', @deva[संस्कृति] 'refinement', @deva[संहार] 'destruction, contraction', &c.

### @deva[सु]

'well, thoroughly', &c. (in this sense it is opposed to @deva[दुस्]); @deva[सुकृत] 'done well', @deva[सुशासित] 'thoroughly trained, well governed', &c. It is also used in the sense of 'very, excessively': @deva[सुमहत्] 'very great'.

[^1]: Cf. @deva[कारणेन अपिगच्छति करण], &c. Śārīra Bhāṣya. According to the grammarian Bhāguri the prepositions @deva[अपि] and @deva[अव] may optionally lose their @deva[अ]—@deva[वावे भागुरिरहोगमवागोरुपसर्गेर्भे] (Sid. Kau.)
