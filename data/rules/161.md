The cardinal numbers between 100 and 200, 200 and 300, &c. are generally expressed by means of @deva[अधिक] added to the number showing the excess and then optionally prefixing the whole to 100, 200, &c, e. g. @deva[एकाधिक शतं] or @deva[एकाधिकशतं] 101, @deva[द्वादशाधिक शतं] or @deva[द्वादशाधिकशतं] 112, @deva[पञ्चाशदधिक शतं] 150. &c. After 1000 this @deva[अधिक] may be repeated with every multiple of ten or hundred; e. g. @deva[द्वि-द्वानवत्यधिकाष्टशताधिकसहस्र] 1892, @deva[एको-नचत्वारिंशदधिकषट्शताधिकसप्तसप्ततिसहस्राधिक लक्षय] 177639, &c. Similarly @deva[उत्तर] may also be used insted of @deva[अधिक] e. g. @deva[चतुःपञ्चा-शदुत्तरं सप्तशतम्] 754. Or these may also be, at times, expressed by the copulative particle @deva[च] as @deva[सप्त च शतानि विंशतिश्च] 720.
