@deva[अ] (@deva[शप्]) is added on to the roots of the First or @deva[भ्वादि] class[^1] before the terminations. Before this @deva[अ] the penultimate short and the final vowel of a root take their Guṇa substitute; e. g.

@deva[बुध्+ति=बुध्+अ+ति=बोधति]; @deva[जि+अ+ति=जे+अ+ति=जयति], &c.

[^1]: @deva[कर्तरि शप्] । @deva[दिवादिभ्यः श्यन्] । @deva[तुदादिभ्यः शः] । Pāṇ. III. 1. 68, 69, 77. Of about 2200 roots occurring in the Sanskṛt language nearly half (about 1076) belong to the first class.
