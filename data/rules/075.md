@deva[नदी] f. a river; @deva[वधू] f. a woman, a bride.

| L. | @deva[नद्याम्] | ,, | @deva[नदीषु] |

Decline all other fem. nouns in @deva[ई] similarly.

**(a)** The words @deva[अर्वी] a woman in her monthly course, @deva[लक्ष्मी] wealth, @deva[तरी] a boat, @deva[स्तरी] smoke, and @deva[तन्त्री] a lute, do not drop the @deva[ई] of the nom.[^1] e. g. @deva[अर्वीः]; @deva[लक्ष्मीः] &c.

Decline @deva[श्वश्रू] a mother-in-law, @deva[चमू] an army, @deva[कर्कन्धू] the jujube tree, @deva[कैतवहू] a phlegmatic woman, @deva[यवागू] rice-gruel, @deva[चर्चू] a class of compositions, and other feminine nouns in @deva[ऊ] similarly. @deva[अतिचमू] mas. and fem. is declined like @deva[चमू] except in the Acc. plu. mas. which is @deva[अतिचमून्].
