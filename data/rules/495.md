## Admission of Intermediate इ before Terminations

Special rules about the admission of the intermediate @deva[इ] before the terminations @deva[व], @deva[म], @deva[वहे], @deva[से], @deva[वहे], @deva[महे]; see (@ref[457]).

(a) All @deva[सेट्] (@[Seṭ]) roots admit it, whether @deva[सेट्] (@[seṭ]) or @deva[अनिट्] (@[aniṭ]), except @deva[ई], @deva[ऊ], @deva[ऋ], @deva[लृ], @deva[हु], @deva[वृ]; @deva[वृ], @deva[ग्रु], @deva[क्षु], permit @deva[इ] or @deva[अ]; But @deva[इ] with @deva[सम्], and @deva[वृ] admit it only as @deva[सचक्रिव], @deva[ववरिव].

(b) @deva[अनिट्] (@[Aniṭ]) roots ending in short @deva[अ], except @deva[अव्], reject it before @deva[व]; as @deva[तस्थिव] from @deva[स्था]; but @deva[आरिव] from @deva[ऋ].

(c) @deva[अनिट्] (@[Aniṭ]) roots with a final vowel or with a penultimate @deva[अ] admit it optionally before @deva[व].
