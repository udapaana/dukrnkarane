To feminine nouns ending in @deva[स्] simply add the terminations.

@deva[भास्] f. splendour.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. | @deva[भाः] | @deva[भासौ] | @deva[भासः] |
| I. | @deva[भासा] | @deva[भाभ्याम्] | @deva[भाभिः] |
| L. | @deva[भासि] | @deva[भासोः] | @deva[भासुः] |
