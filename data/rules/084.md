There are no words ending in @deva[कृ] or @deva[खृ]; therefore, the imitative sounds of the roots @deva[कृ] and @deva[खृ] and @deva[शक्कृ] and @deva[डक्खृ] are taken to show what the forms of such words will be, if there be need to use such words.

@deva[कृ] m.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. | @deva[किरः], @deva[कृः] | @deva[किरौ], @deva[कौ] | @deva[किरः], @deva[कृः] |
| A. | @deva[किरम्], @deva[कृम्] | ,, | @deva[किरः], @deva[कृन्] |
| I. | @deva[किरा], @deva[कृा] | @deva[कीर्भ्याम्], @deva[कृभ्याम्] | @deva[कीर्भिः], @deva[कृभिः] |
| D. | @deva[किरे], @deva[कृे] | ,, | @deva[कीर्भ्यः], @deva[कृभ्यः] |
| Ab. | @deva[किरः], @deva[कृः] | ,, | ,, |
| G. | ,, | @deva[किरोः], @deva[कोः] | @deva[किराम्], @deva[कृाम्] |
| L. | @deva[किरि], @deva[कृि] | ,, | @deva[कीर्षु], @deva[कृषु] |

Decline @deva[खृ] similarly.

| I. | @deva[शक्कृवा] | @deva[शक्कृभ्याम्] | @deva[शक्कृभिः] |

Decline @deva[डक्खृ] similarly.
