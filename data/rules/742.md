A few transitive roots take the affix @deva[एलिम्] having the same force as the affix @deva[यत्]; @deva[पचेलिमः] fr. @deva[पच्], 'fit to ripen or to be cooked; as @deva[पचेलिमा ओषधयः]; @deva[भिदेलिमाः] (fr. @deva[भिद्]) @deva[स्तम्बाः] 'the pine trees ought to be felled,' &c.
