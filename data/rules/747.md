Veṭ roots, except @deva[प्रच्छ्] which takes @deva[इ] necessarily, and @deva[रुच्], @deva[पूर्] which reject @deva[इ], the five roots given under @ref[472] and the roots marked with an indicatory @deva[उ][^1] admit of @deva[इ] optionally before @deva[क्त्वा] e. g.

| Roots | | Ind. P. Ptc. or Ger. |
|---|---|---|
| @deva[मृज्] | to cleanse | @deva[मृजित्वा], @deva[मृष्ट्वा] |
| @deva[गाह्] | to enter | @deva[गाहित्वा], @deva[गाढ्वा] |
| @deva[गुह्] | to conceal | @deva[गूहित्वा], @deva[गुहित्वा], @deva[गूढ्वा] |
| @deva[गुप्] | to protect | @deva[गोपायित्वा], @deva[गोपित्वा], @deva[गुप्त्वा], @deva[गुप्त्वा] |
| @deva[ईश्] | to desire | @deva[ईषित्वा] or @deva[ईष्ट्वा] |
| @deva[सह्] | to endure | @deva[सोढित्वा] or @deva[सोढ्वा] |
| @deva[हृष्] | to covet | @deva[हर्षित्वा] or @deva[हृष्ट्वा] |
| @deva[अञ्च्] | to go, to worship | @deva[अञ्चित्वा] having gone, @deva[अर्चित्वा] having worshipped |
| @deva[क्षण्] | to kill | @deva[क्षत्वा], @deva[क्षणित्वा] |
| @deva[खन्] | to dig | @deva[खनित्वा], @deva[खात्वा] |
| @deva[तन्] | to stretch | @deva[तनित्वा], @deva[तत्वा] |
| @deva[दम्] | to tame | @deva[दमित्वा], @deva[दान्त्वा] |
| @deva[शाम्] | to pacify | @deva[शामित्वा], @deva[शान्त्वा] |
| @deva[क्रम्] | to go &c. | @deva[क्रमित्वा], @deva[क्रान्त्वा][^2] |
| @deva[वृष्] 4. P. | to be straight, &c. | @deva[वर्षित्वा], @deva[वृष्ट्वा] |
| @deva[वह्] I. A. | to be | @deva[वहित्वा] or @deva[ऊढ्वा] &c. &c. |

But @deva[मृज्]—@deva[मार्जित्वा], @deva[रुच्]—@deva[रुष्ट्वा], @deva[पूर्]—@deva[पूर्त्वा], @deva[गृ]—@deva[गृहीत्वा].
