## Government: Influence of One Part of Speech Over Another

Government is that influence which one part of speech exerts over another, requiring it to be put in a particular case. It is mainly concerned with Cases, viz. "the Government of Nouns or of Cases by Verbs and Nouns" and "Government by Prepositions."
