The following is a list of such of the Taddhita affixes as are commonly to be met with.

## Section I. Miscellaneous Affixes

### @deva[अ]

is added in the sense of:—

**(1)** 'the son of'; as @deva[उपगोः अपत्यं उपगव्यः] 'the son of Upagu'; so @deva[वाह्नेयः] from @deva[बह्नेय]; @deva[पर्वतस्य अपत्यं स्त्री पार्वती] 'the daughter of the mountain,' &c.

**(2)** ' the descendant of'; as @deva[उत्सस्य गोत्रप्रापयं पुमान् औत्सः] ' a descendant of Utsa', @deva[उत्सस्य गोत्रप्रापयं स्त्री औत्सी] 'a female descendant of Utsa', ( vide @ref[311], @ref[318] );

**(3)** ' dyed with,' @deva[हरिद्रया रक्तं हारिद्रं वस्त्रं] ' a garment dyed with turmeric';

**(4)** 'made of,' @deva[देवदारोर्विकारः देवदारवः] 'made of the fir tree;'

**(5)** 'belonging to,' &c.; @deva[देवस्य अयं देवः] 'belonging to a god,' @deva[शर्कराया भवं शार्करं] 'of sand'; @deva[ऊर्णाया भवं और्णं वस्त्रं] 'a woolen garment,' @deva[ग्रैष्मः] 'belonging to the sultry season'; @deva[नैशः] 'nocturnal;' @deva[सांवत्सरः] 'yearly' &c.; when added to @deva[हैमन्त] the final @deva[त] is dropped, @deva[हैमनः] 'autumnal,' ( vide Sis. VI. 65; Kir. XVII. 12 ). @deva[हैमन्तः] means 'agreeable in the autumnal season;'

**(6)** 'lord of'; @deva[पृथिव्या ईश्वरः पार्थिवः] 'the lord of the earth': @deva[पञ्चालानां स्वामी पाञ्चालः] 'the king of the Panchālas'; @deva[ऐक्ष्वाकः][^1] 'the king of the Ikshvāku race',

**(7)** 'a collection of'; @deva[काकानां समूहः काकं]; @deva[बकानां समूहः बाकं] 'a flock of cranes'; similarly @deva[मायूरं] from @deva[मयूर] 'a peacock,' @deva[कापोतं] from @deva[कपोत] 'a pigeon', @deva[शिखिनां समूहो शैखिनं]; @deva[गर्भिणीनां समूहो गार्भिणं] &c

**(8)** 'knowing or studying'; @deva[व्याकरणं अधीते वेद वा वैयाकरणः] 'one who studies grammar, grammarian', &c.

**(9)** and to form abstract nouns; @deva[मुनेः भावः मौनं] 'silence', @deva[युवन्-यौवनं] 'youth,' @deva[सुहृद्-सौहार्दं] 'friendship'; @deva[पृथोर्भावः पार्थवं] 'greatness, width,' &c.

### @deva[क]

forms derivatives with various significations:—

**(1)** @deva[उष्ट्रे भवः औष्ट्रकः] 'produced from or relating to a camel;' @deva[ग्रीष्मे भवः ग्रैष्मकः] 'produced in the hot season,'

**(2)** @deva[कुलालेन कृतं कौलालकं] 'made by a potter' @deva[ब्रह्मणा कृतं ब्राह्मकं] 'made by Brahman,'

**(3)** @deva[आरण्यकः] 'a forester,' 'a wild man',

**(4)** @deva[राज्ञां योग्यं राजन्यकं] 'a place fit for kings to live in'; @deva[मानुष्यकं] 'a country fit for men to live in' @deva[कुरुषु जातः कौरवकः] (also @deva[कौरवः][^2]) 'an inhabitant of Kuru'; @deva[युगन्धरेषु जातः यौगन्धरकः] (also @deva[यौगन्धरः][^2]) 'inhabiting Yugandhara';

**(6)** @deva[पथि जातं पन्थ्यकं] 'grown on a way';

**(7)** @deva[पन्थानं गच्छतीति पथिकः] 'a traveller';

**(8)** @deva[पूर्वाह्णे भवः पूर्वाह्णिकः] 'happening in the forenoon'; similarly @deva[अपराह्णिकः] 'happening in the afternoon',

**(9)** is added to denote 'enmity' @deva[काकोलूकयोः वैरं काकोलूकिका][^3] 'the antipathy between the crow and the owl'; similarly @deva[कुत्सिकुटिलिका], &c.;

**(10)** when added to words ending in the Tad. affixes signifying 'the child or the descendant', and to the words @deva[उक्षन्], @deva[उष्ट्र], @deva[उरभ्र] 'a ram', @deva[राजन्], @deva[राजन्य], @deva[राजपुत्र], @deva[वत्स], @deva[मनुष्य] and @deva[अज], it has the sense of 'a collection of'; @deva[उपगुनां समूहः औपगवकं] 'a number of the descendants of Upagu'; @deva[औक्षकं] 'a herd of cattle'; @deva[राजकं] 'a collection of kings', @deva[राजन्यकं] 'an assemblage of kshatriyas'; @deva[वात्सकं] 'a number of calves'; @deva[मानुष्यकं], @deva[अजकं] &c.;

**(11)** it is also added to @deva[क्रम], @deva[पद], @deva[शिक्षा], and @deva[मीमांसा] in the sense of 'one who has studied them'; @deva[क्रमकः] 'a student who goes through a regular course of study, or one who has studied the krama arrangement of a sacred text', @deva[मीमांसकः] 'a student of the mīmāṃsā philosophy', &c.

### @deva[वह्]

is added to the words @deva[पितृ] and @deva[मातृ] in the sense of 'the father of'; @deva[पितुः पिता पितामहः] 'a paternal grandfather'; @deva[मातामहः] 'a maternal grandfather'.

**(1)** @deva[उल] is added to @deva[मातृ] in the sense of 'the brother of'; @deva[मातुर्भ्राता मातुलः] ' a maternal uncle ';

**(2)** and @deva[व्य] is added to @deva[पितृ] and @deva[भ्रातृ] in the sense of ' the brother and the son of ' respectively ; @deva[पितुः भ्राता पितृव्यः] ' a paternal uncle '; @deva[भ्रातुः पुत्रः भ्रातृव्यः] ' a nephew.'

### @deva[आयन], @deva[इ]

And @deva[आयन], @deva[इ] are added to patronymics formed by means of Tad. affixes in the sense of 'the child of,' @deva[दाक्षायणः-पिः] ' the son of Dākshi '; @deva[गार्ग्यायणः-णिः] ' the son of @deva[गार्ग्य], a descendant of Garga '. @deva[आयन] is also added to the word @deva[कापिशी] ' name of a town ' in the sense of 'produced in'— @deva[कापिशायनः] , and optionally to @deva[द्रोण]; @deva[द्रोणायनः] or @deva[द्रौणिः] son of Drona.

### @deva[इ]

in the meaning of ' a son or a descendant of '; @deva[दाक्षिः] ' the son of Daksha '; @deva[वैयासकिः] ' the son of Vyāsa,' &c. ( In the case of the words @deva[व्यास], @deva[बह्व] ' name of a low caste,' @deva[विवाह], @deva[चण्डाल] and @deva[लिङ्ग], the final @deva[अ] is changed to @deva[आह्] before this @deva[इ]. )

### @deva[इ], @deva[ठञ्], @deva[ठन्]

has various senses:—

**(1)** @deva[रैवत्याः अपत्यं पुमान् रैवतिकः][^4] ' the son of Revati;

**(2)** ' happening or given once a month, monthly, lasting for a month ', &c. @deva[मासेन क्रीयते इति मासिकं वेतनं पुस्तकं वा]; similarly @deva[वार्षिकः भाटः] &c.

**(3)** ' gathering together ', @deva[सैनिकः];

**(4)** ' asking; ' @deva[स्नातं पृच्छतीति सौस्नातिकः] ' one who asks another whether he had an auspicious ablution '; so @deva[सुखसुप्तं पृच्छतीति सौसुखसुप्तिकः] ' one who asks another whether he had comfortable sleep '. ( see Rag. VI. 61 X. 14.); @deva[सौसहायिकः] &c ;

**(5)** ' using an instrument ', @deva[असिः प्रहरणमस्य असिकः] ' one who strikes with a sword, a swordsman '; @deva[धानुष्कः] ' an archer, '

**(6)** ' mixed with, ' &c.; @deva[दध्ना संसृष्टं दाधिकं] 'mixed with curds', @deva[मारीचिकं] fr. @deva[मरीचि] 'black pepper';

**(7)** @deva[धर्मे रत इति धार्मिकः] 'pious, religious', similarly @deva[अधार्मिकः].

**(8)** @deva[उड्डुपेन तरतीति नौपिकः] 'a boatman'; @deva[नाविकः] &c.

**(9)** @deva[हेष्टिना क्षरतीति हास्तिकः] 'one who rides an elephant'; @deva[शाकटेन क्षरतीति शाकटिकः] 'one who drives in a carriage';

**(10)** @deva[दध्ना भक्षयतीति दाधिकः] 'one who eats with curds';

**(11)** 'living upon' @deva[वेतनेन जीवतीति वैतनिकः] 'one who lives upon wages', so @deva[वाहनिकः], @deva[भोजवेदिकः] &c.

**(12)** 'carrying upon'; @deva[कटवेण हरतीति कौत्सङ्गिकः]

**(13)** @deva[अस्तीति बुद्धिः अस्य आस्तिकः] 'a believer in God and sacred writings'; @deva[नास्तिकः] &c.

**(14)** it is added to @deva[लाक्षा], @deva[रोचना], @deva[शाकल] and @deva[कर्दम] in the sense of 'dyed with', @deva[लाक्षया रक्तं लाक्षिकं] 'dyed with lac'. @deva[रोचनिकः], @deva[शाकलिकः] 'chequered or spotted', @deva[कार्दमिकः]:
