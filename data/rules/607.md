## Nasal Insertion in Causals

The roots @deva[गृभ्], @deva[स्तभ्], @deva[स्तम्भ्], and @deva[लभ्] insert a nasal before their final.

**Examples:**
- @deva[गृभ्]—@deva[गृम्भयति-ते]
- @deva[रम्भयति-ते], &c.
