## Samprasāraṇa in Reduplicative Syllable

Before the strong terminations of the Perfect Samprasāraṇa takes place in the reduplicative syllable only. In this case the initial conjunct consonant of a root is reduplicated as it is; i. e. without Samprasāraṇa; as

(a) The vowel following the Samprasāraṇa is dropped.
