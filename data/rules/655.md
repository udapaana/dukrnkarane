## Special Uses of क्यच् Affix

The following are the different senses in which the affix @deva[क्यच्] is used in the particular cases given below:

### (a) Desirous of Committing Sin

It is added to @deva[पाप], @deva[कट], @deva[इर्ष्यु] and @deva[वृजन्] in the sense of 'desirous of committing sin':

@deva[पाप चिकीर्षति]—@deva[पापायते], @deva[कटायते], etc.

Added to @deva[कट] it has also the sense of 'prompt in': @deva[कटाय कर्मणि]—@deva[कटायते] (@deva[पाप कर्तुमुत्सह इत्यर्थः], Sid. Kau.)

### (b) Ruminating

To @deva[रोमन्थ] used objectively: @deva[रोमन्थायते] 'is ruminating'

### (c) Sending Forth, Vomiting

To @deva[वाष्प] 'tears', @deva[ऊष्मन्] 'heat', and @deva[फेन्], in the sense of 'sending forth, vomiting':

@deva[वाष्पायते] 'he sheds tears'

@deva[ऊष्मायते] 'gives out heat'

@deva[फेनायते] 'sends forth foam'

### (d) Experiencing or Enjoying

To @deva[सुख] and others in the sense of 'experiencing or enjoying':

@deva[सुखं वेदयते]—@deva[सुखायते] 'he enjoys happiness'

But @deva[परस्य सुखं वेदयते] 'he shows the happiness of another'

### (e) Making or Doing

To @deva[शब्द्], @deva[घेर्], @deva[कलङ], @deva[अभ्र], @deva[पुण्] 'sin', @deva[सुदिन्] 'a fair day', @deva[दुदिन्] 'a cloudy day' and @deva[नीहार] 'fog, heavy dew', in the sense of 'making or doing':

@deva[शब्दं करोति]—@deva[शब्दायते] 'he makes a sound' (also @deva[शब्दयति] cau.)

@deva[सुदिनायते], etc.

---

### (e) By means of the affix क्यष्य (य) and conjugated in Parasm. and Ātm.
