The Indeclinable Past Participle or gerund in @deva[क्त्वा] is formed of all roots or derivative verbal bases to which a preposition (nor a prepositional word) is prefixed. The affix @deva[क्त्वा] is of the same nature as the @deva[त्] of the Past Passive Participle, so

that all the changes that take place before @deva[त्] generally take place before @deva[क्त्वा]. In other words from the P. P. Ptc. in @deva[त्] or @deva[न] separate the @deva[त्] or @deva[न] from it, add @deva[क्त्वा] instead, and this will be the form of the gerund in @deva[क्त्वा]; e. g.
