## Optional Formation from Specific Words

Verbs may be formed optionally from the words @deva[स्फातयि], @deva[चयायति], @deva[भूरि], and @deva[अर्थ] by means of regular affixes, or without the help of any affixes as given in the last paragraph.

### Three Methods of Formation

The special affixes are @deva[क्यच्], @deva[चङ्कच्] or @deva[णिच्]; so these four words may be used in three ways:

1. As nominal verbs with the affixes added
2. As verbs used transitively without affixes
3. As nominal verbal bases not requiring any affixes for their use

### Examples

From @deva[स्फातयि]: @deva[स्फातयि], @deva[स्फातयति], @deva[स्फातययति] 'to use salt'

From @deva[आत्मन्]: @deva[आत्मनि], @deva[आत्मायति], @deva[आत्मायते] respectively

Similarly from @deva[अम्बस्], @deva[पयस्], @deva[शुचि]: @deva[अम्बयन्ति], @deva[पयस्यति], etc.

In all these cases the root affix may be taken to be @deva[णिच्] or @deva[णिच्].
