## First Aorist Exclusively Parasmaipada; Ātmanepada Alternatives

This Variety is exclusively Parasmaipadi. @deva[दा], @deva[धा], and @deva[स्था] take the fourth Variety in the Ātm. @deva[भू] when Ātm. takes the fifth; and @deva[इ] with @deva[आङ्] Ātm. the fourth.
