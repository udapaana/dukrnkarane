## Reduplicative इ and उ Changes

When initial @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] of a root takes @deva[गुण] (@[Guṇa]) or @deva[वृद्धि] (@[Vṛddhi]) substitute, the reduplicative @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] is changed to @deva[इय] or @deva[उव]; otherwise the two vowels combine to form long @deva[ई] or @deva[ऊ]; as @deva[इ]→@deva[इयेश]=@deva[इयेश], @deva[ऊ]→@deva[उवोच]=@deva[ऊचोच]; @deva[ईविध], &c.
