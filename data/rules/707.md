The P. P. Participles of roots meaning 'to think or to wish,' 'to know,' 'to adore,' and of the roots @deva[हन्], @deva[भू] etc. have the sense of the present Tense; @deva[राज्ञा मतः] 'he is honoured by the king;' @deva[सत्त्रैष आरुत्रेः द्युतः तथा] the root is kindled;' so @deva[भूतः] etc.
