This Participle is generally used at the end of compounds; @deva[अश्मन्यस्य हताम्] 'he was pelted to death,' @deva[बन्दिनीत्वं गृहीताम्] (Vīks. I. 1.) She was taken a captive;' @deva[प्रभङ्गं शत्रुघ्नम्] (Bhaṭṭi. I. 2.) 'He totally exterminated his enemies, &c.'
