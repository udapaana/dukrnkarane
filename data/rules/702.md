(a) The roots @deva[इ] and @deva[यज्] take the affix @deva[वत्] like @deva[त्] in an active sense; @deva[इत्वत्] 'one who extracted Soma juice;' @deva[यजत्वत्] 'one who has offered a sacrifice;' @deva[अत्] is added to @deva[जु] optionally in the same sense; @deva[जीर्णः] or @deva[जग्वतः] 'one who has become old' (also @deva[जग्मिवत्]).
