When @deva[ए] or @deva[ओ] at the end of a word is followed by @deva[अ] the latter merges into the former[^1] and the sign ऽ is sometimes written in its place; e.g. @deva[हरे] + @deva[अव] = @deva[हरेऽव] protect, Oh Hari; @deva[विष्णो] + @deva[अव] = @deva[विष्णोऽव].

**(a)** If the word @deva[गो], ending in @deva[ओ], be followed by @deva[अ], the @deva[अ] is optionally retained, and if by a vowel, @deva[अव] is optionally substituted for @deva[ओ];[^2] @deva[गो] + @deva[अग्र] = @deva[गोग्र], @deva[गोऽग्र] and @deva[गवाग्र] a multitude of cows or chief among cows; but if it be followed by @deva[इन्द्र] and @deva[अक्ष] it necessarily changes its @deva[ओ] to @deva[अव]; @deva[गो] + @deva[इन्द्रः] = @deva[गवेन्द्रः] an excellent bull; @deva[गवाक्षः] a lattice-window, similarly.

---

[^1]: @deva[पूर्वरूपम्]

[^2]: @deva[गोः प्रथमायाः क्वचित्] Vārt.
