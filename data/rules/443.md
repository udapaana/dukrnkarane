(a) The base is formed by reduplication of the root.

(b) The third personal plural termination loses its @deva[न].

(c) The Imperfect third person plural termination is @deva[उस्] (P.), before which the final @deva[आ] of roots is dropped and the final @deva[इ], @deva[उ] and @deva[ऋ], short or long, are guṇated.
