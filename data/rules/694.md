The roots @deva[मुद्], @deva[विद्य], 6. A., @deva[ज्ञद्], @deva[शा], @deva[हा] and @deva[हा] take @deva[न] or @deva[त्]; @deva[मुग्नः] or @deva[मुतः]; @deva[विनः] or @deva[वितः], @deva[ज्ञानः] or @deva[ज्ञातः], @deva[शानः] or @deva[शातः] and @deva[ह्रानः] or @deva[ह्रातः].
