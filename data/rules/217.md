Additional instances of the aluk of the Loc.

**(a)** Aluk is necessary in the case of the words—(1) @deva[गो] and @deva[तुर] followed by @deva[दिव्]; as @deva[गविदिव्]:, 'stationary in the sky', @deva[तुरदिव्]: firm in battle; (2) @deva[हृद्] and @deva[दिव्] followed by @deva[स्पृश्]; @deva[हृदिस्पृश्], @deva[दिविस्पृश्] (@deva[हृदयं दिवं च स्पृशतीति]); (3) @deva[अन्त] and @deva[मध्य] followed by @deva[गुरु]: @deva[मध्ये-गुरुः], @deva[अन्तेगुरुः] and (4) words expressive of parts of the body except @deva[कृषीव] and @deva[सक्थन्] and not followed by @deva[काम]; @deva[कण्ठेकाम]:, @deva[उरसिलोमा] (having the hair on the breast: Bah.), but @deva[मूर्धसक्थनि-शिरसि], @deva[मूर्धे कामो अस्य मुखकामः]·

**(b)**[^1] When a noun in the Loc. is compounded with a verbal derivative and the whole is a name, aluk takes place in most cases; e. g. @deva[स्तम्बेरमः] (@deva[स्तम्बे] a kind of grass @deva[रमते अत्र]) an elephant; @deva[कर्णेजपः] one who whispers into the ear, a spy; @deva[स्वर्चरः] a heavenly being; @deva[पङ्केरुह] a lotus; @deva[कुक्षिशय्य], @deva[जलेशयः] a fish; but @deva[कुशवसः], @deva[सतदिवस्] or @deva[सरोवस] &c.

**(c)** There is optional aluk of the Loc. of a word expressive of time when compounded with @deva[काल] and words ending in the affixed @deva[तर], @deva[तम] and @deva[तन]; @deva[पूर्वाह्णे-हे-काले]; @deva[पूर्वाह्णे-हे-तरः] &c. @deva[पूर्वाह्णे-हे तनः]·

**(d)**[^2] When the compound is not a name, aluk is necessary when @deva[ज] comes after @deva[प्राहृद्], @deva[शरद्], @deva[काल] and @deva[दिव्]; and optional when it comes after @deva[वर्ष], @deva[हर], @deva[शर] and @deva[धर]; as @deva[प्राहृदि-शरदि-काले-दिवि-जः]; @deva[वर्षेजः] or @deva[वर्षजः] produced in the rainy season, &c.

**Exceptions**[^3]:—No aluk takes place in the case of verbal derivatives ending in @deva[इन्] and the words @deva[सिद्ध], @deva[चन्द्र], and @deva[स्थ] in the ordinary language; as @deva[स्थलशायिन्] 'an ascetic,' @deva[साद्वद्यवसिद्धः]; @deva[चक्रचन्द्रः]; @deva[समस्थः]; but @deva[छुप्णोऽध्यासिनः] &c.

**(e)**[^4] When a noun in the Loc case ending in @deva[अ] or a consonant and not denoting time is compounded with @deva[शरण्य], @deva[शाय] and @deva[वासिन्] and with @deva[बन्ध] aluk is optional; @deva[वनेशरण्य]:, @deva[ग्रामेशवासः], @deva[ग्रामेवासी], @deva[कूलेसम्बन्धः], but @deva[स्थविवासः], @deva[मनिबन्धः] &c.
