## Vowel Changes in ऊर्ण्

The vowel of @deva[ऊर्ण्] takes Vṛddhi optionally in the Parasm, i. e. optionally takes Guṇa substitute; also it optionally remains unchanged before @deva[इ] (Vide @ref[466], @ref[518]).
