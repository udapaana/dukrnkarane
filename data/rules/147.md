@deva[अन्य] 'another,' @deva[अन्यतर] 'either of two,' @deva[इतर] 'other,' @deva[एकतम] 'one of many,' @deva[कतर] 'who or which of two,' @deva[कतम] 'who or which of many,' @deva[यतर] 'who or which of two,' @deva[यतम] 'who or which of many,' @deva[ततर] 'that one (of two),' @deva[ततम] 'that one (of many),' are declined in all the three genders like @deva[यद्] e. g.

mas.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. | @deva[कतरः] | @deva[कतरौ] | @deva[कतरे] |

fem.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. | @deva[कतरा] | @deva[कतरे] | @deva[कतराः] |
| &c. | | | |

neu.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. & A. | @deva[कतरत्] | @deva[कतरे] | @deva[कतराणि] |
| &c. | | | |

**N. B.** @deva[अन्यतम] is not a pronoun, as it is not mentioned in the group @deva[सर्वादि] (@deva[सर्वाद्यतमसहस्रस्य गणे पठनमावात् संज्ञा] Sid. Kau.). It is, therefore, declined like nouns ending in @deva[अ].
