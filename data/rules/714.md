The affix @deva[य] (@deva[यत्]) is added on to roots ending in a vowel in the sense of 'fit to be, or ought to be.' Before this @deva[य] the radical vowel undergoes Guṇa substitute and final @deva[आ] (@deva[इ], @deva[ऋ] and @deva[ओ] changeable to @deva[आ]) is changed to @deva[ए].

| | | |
|---|---|---|
| @deva[दा]— | @deva[देयः] | what is fit or ought to be given. |
| @deva[पा]— | @deva[पेयः] | „ „ sucked. |
| @deva[गै]— | @deva[गेयः] | „ „ sung. |
| @deva[भो]— | @deva[भोयः] | „ „ cut. |
| @deva[ग्रथ्]— | @deva[ग्रैयः] | „ „ collected. |
| @deva[नी]— | @deva[नेयः] | „ „ led or carried. |
