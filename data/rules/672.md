## Formation of Present Passive Participle

The participle of the Present Passive is formed by adding @deva[मान] to the Passive base in @deva[य]:

### Examples

| Root | Participle | Meaning |
|------|------------|---------|
| @deva[बुध्] | @deva[बुध्यमान] | who or what is known |
| @deva[अद्] | @deva[अद्यमान] | what is eaten |
| @deva[दा] | @deva[दीयमान] | who or what is given |
| @deva[चि] | @deva[चीयमान] | who or what is gathered |
| @deva[कृ] | @deva[क्रियमाण] | what is done |
| @deva[कृ] | @deva[कीर्यमाण] | what is scattered |
| @deva[चोर्] | @deva[चोर्यमाण] | who or what is stolen |

### Derived Forms

@deva[बुध्] cau. Pass.: @deva[बोध्यमान] 'who or what is caused to know'

@deva[बुध्] Des. Pass.: @deva[बुबोधिषयमाण] 'who or what is desired to know', etc.
