## पृथक्, विना, नाना with Ablative/Accusative/Instrumental

The indeclinables @deva[पृथक्], @deva[विना] and @deva[नाना] govern the Ablative, the Accusative and the Instrumental cases; @deva[पृथक् रामात् रामं रामेण वा] different from or without Rāma; so @deva[नाना रामात्]. &c. @deva[नाना नारीं निष्फलः लोकयात्रु] (Vop.) worldly life is vain without a woman (wife).[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. II. 3. 32
