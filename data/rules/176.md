@deva[इ] is substituted for the @deva[अ] (short) of a base preceded by a consonant before @deva[ईयस्], @deva[इष्ठ] and the Tad. aff. @deva[इमन्] e. g.[^1]

| | Comp. | Superl. |
|---|---|---|
| @deva[कृशु] 'thin' | @deva[कृशीयस्] | @deva[कृशिष्ठ] |
| @deva[दृढ] 'strong, firm' | @deva[दृढीयस्] | @deva[दृढिष्ठ] |
| @deva[परिवृढ] 'head, chief' | @deva[परिवृढीयस्] | @deva[परिवृढिष्ठ] |
| @deva[पृथु] 'broad' | @deva[प्रथीयस्] | @deva[प्रथिष्ठ] |
| @deva[भूरि] 'much' | @deva[भूयीयस्] | @deva[भूयिष्ठ] |
| @deva[श्लक्ष्ण] 'soft' | @deva[श्लद्यीयस्] | @deva[श्लद्यिष्ठ] |
