## Roots Taking First Aorist Variety

@deva[इ], @deva[स्था], @deva[रा], @deva[धा], and roots assuming the forms of @deva[धा] (Vide @ref[459]), @deva[पा] 'to drink' and @deva[भू] take this Variety.
