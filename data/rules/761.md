There is another Indeclinable Participle having the same sense as that in @deva[क्त्वा], formed with the affix @deva[अम्]. Before this the vowel of the derivative verbal base generally undergoes the same changes as it does before the @deva[इ] of the Pass. Aorist 3rd sing.; @deva[नी]—@deva[नयाम्] 'having carried or led,' @deva[दा]—@deva[दायाम्] 'having given,' @deva[भू]—@deva[भावाम्], @deva[भिद्]—@deva[भेदाम्], @deva[ब्रह्]—@deva[ब्राह्माम्], @deva[गम्]—@deva[गमाम्], &c.
