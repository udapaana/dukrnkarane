## Suffixes Conveying Reproach

By means of the suffixes @deva[कुङ्], @deva[किय], @deva[णिच्] and @deva[कुण] the sense of reproach is conveyed:

### Examples

@deva[स्तेनकुयति], @deva[स्तेनकियति] 'to call a person a thief'

@deva[भृत्येयति] 'to call a person a servant'

Similarly @deva[किंकरेयति]
