## Restrictions on Roots Ending in आ

The roots ending in @deva[आ] which are restricted to the First, Second and Third Varieties do not take this Variety.

### PARADIGMS

**@deva[यच्छ्].**

| | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[अयंसिषम्] | @deva[अयंसिष्व] | @deva[अयंसिष्म] |
| 2. | @deva[अयंसीः] | @deva[अयंसिष्टम्] | @deva[अयंसिष्ट] |
| 3. | @deva[अयंसीत्] | @deva[अयंसिष्टाम्] | @deva[अयंसिषुः] |

@deva[विरम्]—@deva[अरंसिषम्], @deva[व्यरंसिष्व], @deva[व्यरंसिष्म] &c; @deva[नम्]—@deva[अनंसिषम्], @deva[अनंसिष्व], @deva[अनंसिष्म], &c; @deva[शो]—@deva[अशासिषम्], &c; @deva[छो]—@deva[अछासिषम्], &c.; @deva[भि] or @deva[भी]—@deva[अभासिषम्], @deva[अभासिष्व], @deva[अभासिष्म], &c.; @deva[ली]—@deva[अलासिषम्] @deva[अलासिष्व], @deva[अलासिष्म], &c.

**SEVENTH VARIETY OR FORM**
(Parasam. and Ātm.)
