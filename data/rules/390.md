@deva[अ] is added on to the roots of the sixth or @deva[तुदादि] class before which the penultimate vowel remains unchanged, and the final @deva[इ], @deva[उ], short or long, @deva[ऋ] and @deva[ॠ] are changed to @deva[इय], @deva[उव], @deva[रिय] and @deva[रीय] respectively; e. g. @deva[क्षिप्+ति=क्षिप्+अ+ति=क्षिपति]; @deva[धु+ति=धुव+अ+ति=धुवति]; @deva[रि+अ+ति=रियति]; @deva[कृ+अ+ते=क्रियते]; @deva[तृ+अ+ति=तिर्+अ+ति=तिरति], &c.
