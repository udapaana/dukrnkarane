The augment @deva[अ] is prefixed to roots in this tense. This @deva[अ] is replaced by @deva[आ] in the case of roots beginning with a vowel[^1]; this @deva[आ] forms Vṛddhi with an initial vowel; e. g. @deva[अ+ईक्ष्+त्=आ+ईक्ष्+अ+त्=ऐक्षत्]; similarly @deva[ईक्ष्]—@deva[ऐक्षत्]; @deva[उक्ष्]—@deva[औक्षत्]; @deva[ऊह्]—@deva[औहत्]; @deva[ऋच्छ्]—@deva[आर्च्छत्] &c.

**(a)** When a preposition (@deva[उपसर्ग]) is prefixed to a root, the augment @deva[अ] or @deva[आ] comes between the prep. and the root; e. g. @deva[हृ] with @deva[प्र], @deva[प्राहरत्].
