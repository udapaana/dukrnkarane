## Dropping of Penultimate Nasal in Benedictive

A penultimate nasal is generally dropped. Some of the roots dropping their nasal thus are:

@deva[भञ्ज], @deva[अञ्ज], @deva[वञ्ज], @deva[रञ्ज], @deva[सञ्ज], @deva[रुञ्ज], @deva[युञ्ज], @deva[मुञ्ज], @deva[उञ्च], @deva[स्कन्द्], @deva[स्यन्द्], @deva[इञ्ज], @deva[वृञ्ज], @deva[स्तम्भ], @deva[सिञ्ज], @deva[शुङ्ग], @deva[शुम्भ], @deva[अञ्च] and @deva[तुङ्ग].
