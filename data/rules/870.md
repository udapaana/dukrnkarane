## Locative/Instrumental with Nakshatra Names (Time)

When the names of Nakshatras are used as showing a particular time the Locative or the Instrumental is used; @deva[पुङ्गेजातरोदेन भूपनम् विसर्जति] । @deva[पुङ्गे अग्नौ हूति वा] । (Sid. Kau.)[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. II. 3, 45
