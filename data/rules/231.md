A Dwigu compound is possible[^1] (a) under the circumstances mentioned in @ref[223] (a) i. e.-

(1) when a Taddhita affix is to be added to the compound so formed, @deva[षण्णां मातृणामपत्यं कार्त्तिकेयः]: 'the son of six mothers, Kārtikeya' (vide Ku. IX); @deva[पञ्चकपालः]: &c; or (2) when the compound itself becomes the first member of another compound, as @deva[पञ्चगावः पणं यस्य असौ पञ्चगवधनः]:, @deva[पञ्च: गतिषु:] &c

(b) And when the compound denotes an aggregate; in this case it is singular;[^2] as @deva[त्रयाणां भुवनानां समाहारः: त्रिभुवनम्] the three worlds (taken collectively); as @deva[पञ्चगवधम्], @deva[पञ्चगवधम्], &c.
