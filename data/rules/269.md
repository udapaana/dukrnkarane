In forming the Avyayībhāva compounds the following rules should be observed:—

**(a)** The final long vowel is shortened, and the ending @deva[इ] or @deva[ई] is changed to @deva[य] and @deva[ओ] to @deva[उ]; @deva[गोपावति भाः पातीति वा गोपः] । @deva[तस्मिन्निति भक्तियोगः], @deva[भगविष्णु] after Vishṇu, @deva[उपगु] near a cow, &c.

**(b)** The final @deva[त्] of mas. and fem. nouns in @deva[अत्] is dropped; and optionally that of neuter nouns ending in @deva[अत्];[^1] @deva[उपराजत्]; @deva[उपब्रह्मत्-सं].

**(c)** @deva[अ] is optionally substituted for the final of @deva[नदी], @deva[पौर्णमासती], @deva[आग्रहायणी] and @deva[गिरि];[^2] @deva[उपनक्षत्र-दि], @deva[उपर्पौर्णमासम्-सि], @deva[उपाग्रहायणम्-णि] 'near to the full-moon day of Mārgas'īrsha'; @deva[उपगिरम्-रि].

**(d)** It is also optionally added to all nouns ending in any of the first four letters of a class;[^3] @deva[उपक्षत्रियम्-यित्].

**(e)** @deva[अ] is attached to the final of the following nouns when compoundedin an Avyaya.[^4]:—@deva[हारद्], @deva[विपाश्], @deva[भगस्], @deva[मनस्], @deva[उपनह्], @deva[अनडुह्], @deva[द्विप्], @deva[हिमवत्], @deva[द्विगु्], @deva[हरि], @deva[विशु्], @deva[चेतस्], @deva[चतुर्] @deva[तड्], @deva[यड्], @deva[कियत्], @deva[जरस्] (substituted for @deva[जरा]) &c; @deva[हारदः समीपम् उपहारदम्], @deva[प्रतिवि-पाशम्], 'towards the Vipāśā,' @deva[दिशोर्मध्ये अपदिशम्] between two divisions of the compass, @deva[उपजरसम्] towards old age, @deva[अध्यात्मम्] on the soul, &c.; and to @deva[आदि] when preceded by @deva[प्रति], @deva[परि] changed to @deva[परो], @deva[सम्] and @deva[अनु], the @deva[इ] of @deva[आदि] being dropped; @deva[अक्षणः प्रति प्रत्यक्षम्] before one's eyes; @deva[अक्षण परे परोक्षम्] out of sight; @deva[समक्षम्], and @deva[अन्वक्षम्] afterwards, forthwith.
