## Prefixing Augment इ to Desiderative स्

To this @deva[स्] the augment @deva[इ] is to be prefixed after Seṭ roots, optionally after Veṭ ones and not after those that are Aniṭ, subject to the following exceptions:

### (1) Roots Ending in इ, ई, ऋ, ॠ

Roots ending in @deva[इ] or @deva[ई], @deva[ऋ] and @deva[ॠ] short, and the roots @deva[मृज्] and @deva[मृड्] do not take @deva[इ]; @deva[इ]—@deva[इयिष्] (Vide @ref[618]. (2)); @deva[ऋ]—@deva[ईरिष्], &c.

**Exceptions:**
@deva[ऋ] 'to go,' @deva[वृ] Ā. 'to repeat,' @deva[वृ] 6 Ā. 'to hold,' and @deva[वृ] Ā. 'to purify,' admit of @deva[इ] (see also 4 below)

### (2) Roots Taking इ Necessarily

@deva[स्मि], @deva[अञ्ज्], @deva[भञ्ज्], @deva[अर्च्] take @deva[इ] necessarily.

### (3) Roots Differing in Parasmaipada and Ātmanepada

@deva[ईड्], @deva[वृप्], @deva[वृध्], @deva[स्पृह्] and @deva[कृत्स्] do not admit of @deva[इ] in the Par. (see @ref[484]). In the Ātm. they admit @deva[इ], the last two do so optionally.

**Examples:**
- @deva[दाड्]—@deva[विदृक्षति], @deva[विवर्तिषते], &c.

### (4) Roots with Optional इ

Roots ending in long @deva[ऋ] and @deva[ॠ] and the roots @deva[हराष्], @deva[त्रिह्], @deva[जड्], @deva[द्], @deva[ष], @deva[ढ], @deva[स्], @deva[हू], @deva[कृष्] 'to prosper,' @deva[कृष्], @deva[भर्ज], @deva[इङ्] (i. e. @deva[गम्], of the 3rd cl. and optional Cau. Base of @deva[ज्ञा]), @deva[सृ] 'to give,' @deva[पन्], @deva[पुन्], @deva[टुन], @deva[चुट्], @deva[हुर्], @deva[दुर्], and @deva[युज्] (Vide @ref[485].) take @deva[इ] optionally.

**Exceptions:**
@deva[कृ] 'to scatter,' @deva[पृ] 'to swallow,' take @deva[इ] necessarily. The intermediate @deva[इ] is not lengthened in the case of these roots; @deva[चिकीर्षिष्], &c.

### (5) Roots Differing by Voice

The roots @deva[कृष्], @deva[गृह्] and @deva[ढुक्] take @deva[इ] in the Par. and reject it in the Ātm.
