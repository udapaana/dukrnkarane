## Classification of Derivative Verbs

The Derivative Verbs are divided into four classes:

1. **Causals or Nijantas** (@deva[णिजन्तः])
2. **Desideratives or Sanantas** (@deva[सन्नन्तः])
3. **Frequentatives or Yanantas** (@deva[यङन्तः])
4. **Denominatives or Nāmadhātus** (@deva[नामधातवः])

The formation and the conjugation of these will be given in the present section.
