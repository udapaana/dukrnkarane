## Root वस् with Prepositions Governs Accusative

The root @deva[वस्] preceded by the prepositions @deva[उप], @deva[अनु], @deva[अभि] and @deva[आ] governs the Accusative case; @deva[वैकुंठं हरिः वसति] Hari dwells in Vaikuṇṭha; @deva[सुनिर्जनं वनं वसति] he dwelt in a dreary forest; but @deva[वस्] with @deva[उप] meaning 'to abstain from food' is used with the Loc. @deva[अनुपवसति]; @deva[आवसति वनं रामः] Rāma dwelt in the forest.[^1]
