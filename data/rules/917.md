[^1] The infinitive with the final @deva[म्] dropped is joined with the nouns @deva[काम] and @deva[मनस्] to form an adjectival compound meaning 'wishing or having a mind to do any thing;' @deva[प्रत्यासि प्रतिगमिष्यकामं विद्याम् महर्षेः] &c. (Rag. V. 18) the disciple of the great sage was desirous of returning &c.; @deva[अहं जनः प्रष्टुमनाः तुभ्यम्] (Kum. V. 40) this person has a mind to ask you a question.
