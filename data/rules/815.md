## Instrumental with Characteristic Attributes

A characteristic attribute indicative of the existence of a particular state or condition is put in the Instrumental; @deva[जटाभिः तापसः] he is an ascetic (which is apparent) from his matted hair (@deva[जटाभिः=प्रकृतितः उद्भूतैर्जटाभिः] Sid. Kau.).
