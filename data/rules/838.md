## Ablative for Distance in Space

When the sense of an indeclinable participle is suppressed, it is a source of wonder; the distance in space being put in the Ablative case in the sense of 'after,' @deva[द्वारात् पश्यति] sees from the door, @deva[गोदावर्यात् गङ्गाम् द्वारं निष्ठा] (Sid. Kau.)[^1]
