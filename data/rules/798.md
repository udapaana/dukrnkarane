## Transitive Verbs Govern Accusative Case

All transitive verbs govern an Accusative; @deva[पुष्पाणि उपचिनोति] collects flowers; @deva[अपः एव सर्वं] (the creator) created water first, &c. Several transitive verbs govern what is called in English a factitive object besides a direct object; @deva[प्राकृतान् प्रकृतिं त्वां पुनः पुरुषं विदुः] (Kum. II. 13) they consider thee to be Prakṛti, they know thee to be Purusha; @deva[कुमारं नेतारं कृता] having made Kumāra the leader (of the forces); @deva[नाम तस्य अजं चक्रे] made his son Aja by name.
