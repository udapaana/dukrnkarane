# Chapter VIII. Formation of Feminine Bases

Feminine bases are derived from the masculine by the addition of the affixes @deva[आ] (@deva[टाप्], @deva[डाप्], @deva[चाप्]), @deva[ई] (@deva[ङीप्], @deva[ङीष्], @deva[ङीन्]), @deva[क] (@deva[कङ्]) and @deva[ति].
