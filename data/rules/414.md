The roots @deva[ली], @deva[री], @deva[ल्ली], @deva[श्री], @deva[द्रु], @deva[द्रू], @deva[द्रू], @deva[ध्रू], @deva[श्रु], @deva[ग्लु], @deva[स्नु], @deva[स्तू], @deva[क्रू], @deva[क्लू], @deva[प्लू], @deva[म] and @deva[भ्रू] have their finals shortened necessarily, and @deva[क्षी], @deva[भ्री], and @deva[श्री] optionally in the Special Tenses; as @deva[धुनाति]-@deva[नीते], @deva[पवुणाति]-@deva[णीते], @deva[मणाति]-@deva[णीते], &c. @deva[क्षीणाति], @deva[शिणाति], &c.
