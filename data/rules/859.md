## Genitive/Instrumental with Potential Passive Participles

In the case of Potential passive participles the agent of the action is put in the Genitive or in the Instrumental case; @deva[मया मम वा सेव्यो हरिः] Hari ought to be served by me; @deva[राक्षसानाम्यस्य नगरम् त्रातृः] (Bhaṭṭi VIII. 125) the forest together with the lord of the demons ought to preserve must be destroyed by me; @deva[गन्तव्या ते वसतिरलका हे.] (Meg.) Thou should go to Alakā.[^1]
