## Instrumental with Defective Limbs

Words expressing some defect in a limb of the body govern the Instrumental of the defective limb; @deva[अक्ष्णा काणः] blind of one eye; so @deva[पदेन खञ्जः] &c.[^1]

[^1]: Pāṇ. II. 3. 20
