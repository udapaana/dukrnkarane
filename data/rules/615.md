## Desiderative Forms Without Desiderative Sense

There are a few primitive roots, which, though they take a Desiderative form, do not convey a desiderative sense (See @ref[39]. b). As has been looked upon as primitive roots, the Desiderative bases may be derived from them according to the rules given below.

**Example:**
@deva[जुगुप्सिषत] 'he wishes to censure' from @deva[जुगुप्स्] &c.
