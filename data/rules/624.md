## Desiderative Passive and Complete Paradigms

In the Passive, the Aorist 3rd sing. is formed according to @ref[597]. (e). The forms of the remaining tenses are made up in the usual way.

### Paradigms: @deva[बुध्] deśi. (3rd sing.)

| Tense | Par. | Ātm. | Passive |
|-------|------|------|---------|
| Present | @deva[बुबोधिषति] | @deva[बुबोधिषते] | @deva[बुबोधिष्यते] |
