## Terminations of the Fifth Form

Terminations:—These are obtained by prefixing @deva[इष्] @deva[इ] to the terminations of the Fourth Variety[^1] except in the case of the 2nd and 3rd per. sing.; e. g.

**Parasmaipada:**

| | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[इषम्] | @deva[इष्व] | @deva[इष्म] |
| 2. | @deva[ईः] | @deva[इष्टम्] | @deva[इष्ट] |
| 3. | @deva[ईत्] | @deva[इष्टाम्] | @deva[इषुः] |

**Ātmanepada:**

| | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[इषि] | @deva[इष्वहि] | @deva[इष्महि] |
| 2. | @deva[इष्ठाः] | @deva[इषाथाम्] | @deva[इष्ध्वम्] |
| 3. | @deva[इष्ट] | @deva[इषाताम्] | @deva[इषत] |
