According to Vāmana, the roots @deva[स्तु], @deva[ह्व] and @deva[वह्] take @deva[इ]. @deva[यत्]. According to Pāṇini the roots @deva[स्तु]—@deva[स्तुत्यः] 'praiseworthy;' @deva[ह्वे]—@deva[ह्वेत्यः]; @deva[वह्]—@deva[वह्यः]; optionally they take the affix @deva[एयत्] (to be given hereafter); @deva[स्तव्यः], @deva[ह्वायः], @deva[वाह्यः].
