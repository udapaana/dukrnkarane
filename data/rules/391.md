Roots of the tenth or @deva[चुरादि] class[^1] add @deva[अय] before the personal terminations. Before @deva[अय] (1) the penultimate short vowel (except @deva[अ]) takes the Guṇa substitute; and the final vowel and the penultimate @deva[अ] not prosodially long, take their Vṛddhi substitute; e. g. @deva[चुर्+ति=चुर्+अय+ति=चोर्+अय+ति=चोरयति]; @deva[धू+अय+ति=धो+अय+ति=धाव+अय+ति=धावयति]; @deva[ताड्+अय+ति=ताड्+अय+ति=ताडयति]; but @deva[दण्ड्+अय+ति=दण्डयति], &c.

[^1]: This class contains a few primitive verbs, almost all the roots belonging to it being derivative; besides, all Causals and some Nominal verbs may be regarded as belonging to this class.
