(a) The following two words which are the names of two rivers are derived by means of this affix; @deva[भिवर्ति कुवं विषः] from @deva[छिद्+क्यप्]; @deva[जलपूर्णां नाम्ना:], @deva[उञ्जुद्+क्यप्]; see Rāg. XI. 8. In other cases these roots take the affix @deva[त्]; @deva[भेत्तुंः], @deva[उन्चिद्धता].

(b) Similarly the words @deva[पुष्य:] and @deva[विष्णुप्]; both the names of the constellation Pushya, are derived from @deva[एष्] and @deva[तिष्] respectively with the affixes @deva[क्यप्]; (verging towards, @deva[विष्णुः], @deva[तिष्ठेत्युच्यते])
