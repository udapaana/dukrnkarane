The root @deva[पद्] when preceded by @deva[अमा] 'with' takes the affix @deva[घ्यत्] before which the penultimate @deva[अ] is optionally changed to Vṛiddhi; @deva[अमा रुङु गुवातोऽव्या चन्द्रा:शो अवतिब्धुवा] or @deva[वाश्या] 'the day on which the sun and the moon are with each other' i. e. are in conjunction.'

(a) @deva[युज्] when preceded by @deva[पाणिं] or the preposition @deva[समत्] takes the affix @deva[घ्यत्]; as @deva[पाणिस्यो डुयुयोऽ पाणिगुम्योऽ यृज्यः]; similarly @deva[समवसुयुर्यं].
