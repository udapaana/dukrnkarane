The first vowel of a root together with the initial consonant, if any, is reduplicated i. e. doubled; as @deva[पत्]=@deva[पपत्], @deva[उत्]=@deva[उउत्], &c. after reduplication.

**Note**—The first syllable of a reduplicated root is called 'the reduplicative syllable; e. g. the first @deva[प] in @deva[पपत्], or first @deva[उ] in @deva[उउत्].
