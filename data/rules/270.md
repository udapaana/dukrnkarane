The indeclinables forming Avyayībhāva compounds have various senses[^1]; e. g. (1) the meaning of case; as @deva[गोपे इति अधिगोपम्] 'in the protector of the cowherd,' so @deva[हरादिति अधिहरि], @deva[अध्यात्मम्] &c.; (2) @deva[सामीप्य] or 'vicinity,' as @deva[कृष्णस्य समीपमष्टम्याम्] near Krishna; so @deva[उपहरम्] &c. (3) @deva[समृद्धि] or 'prosperity' as @deva[मद्राणां समृद्धिः समद्रम्], 'in a country wherein the Madras are in a prosperous condition,' (4) @deva[दुर्द्धि] (@deva[वि+ऋद्धिः]) or 'bad condition,' as @deva[यवनानां दुर्द्धिः दुर्यवनम्] 'when the Yavanas are in a bad condition,' (4) @deva[अभाव] or absence; as @deva[मक्षिकाणामभावः निर्मक्षिकम्] 'where flies are utterly absent' i. e. 'perfect loneliness', or 'quiet', so @deva[निर्जनम्] &c. ( 6 ) @deva[अत्यय] or 'the close or the transgression of' as @deva[हिमस्य अत्ययः आतिहिमम्], 'after the wintry season,' so @deva[आतिवसन्तम्], @deva[आति-शैवनम्], @deva[अतिमात्रम्] 'transgressing the proper limits', &c;( 7 ) @deva[असम्भति] or 'unfitness of time;' as @deva[निद्रा संप्रति न युज्यते इति आति-निद्रम्] 'past sleeping time;' as @deva[आतिनिद्रम् उत्तिष्ठति पुरुषः]; ( 8 ) @deva[आदुर्भीव] or 'manifestation' as @deva[हरिशब्दस्य प्रकाशः इतिहरि] in which the name Hari is manifested ( loudly uttered ); ( 9 ) @deva[पश्चात्] or 'after', as @deva[विष्णोः पश्चात् अनुविष्णुः]; ( 10 ) @deva[योग्यता][^2] or 'fitness' as @deva[रूपस्य योग्यमनुरूपम्] in a corresponding manner; so @deva[अनुरूपम्] agreeably or comfortably to, favourably; &c. ( 11 ) @deva[सीख्या] or 'repetition' as @deva[अर्थे अर्थे प्रति प्रत्यर्थम्] 'in every case,' @deva[अहन्यहनीति प्रत्यहम्-ह], 'every day'; so @deva[प्रतिपर्वम्], &c. ( 12 ) @deva[अनतिवृत्ति] or the 'non-violation of'; as @deva[शक्तिमनतिक्रम्य यथाशक्ति] 'not going beyond' i. e. 'according to one's power', so @deva[यथाविधि][^3] &c. ( 13 ) @deva[साहश्य] or 'similarity'; as @deva[हरेः साहश्ये सहरि] 'like Hari.' ( 14 ) @deva[आनुपूर्व्य] or 'succession in order, priority', as @deva[ज्येष्ठस्य आनुपूर्व्येण अनुज्येष्ठम्] 'according to seniority', so @deva[अनुक्रमम्] 'according to the proper order,' &c. ( 15 ) @deva[यौगपय] or 'simultaneousness', as @deva[चक्रेण युगपत् सचक्रम्] 'along with the wheel'.( 16 ) @deva[सम्पत्ति] or 'power, influence;' as @deva[क्षत्रणां सम्पत्तिः सक्षत्रम्] 'with the Kshatriyas in a satisfactory condition,' or 'very influential' or 'powerful.' ( 17 ) @deva[साकल्य] or 'entirety, fulness'; as @deva[तृणमपि अपरित्यज्य सतृणम्] 'in a manner not leaving out even grass' @deva[भक्षि]; and ( 18 ) @deva[अन्त] or 'end'; as @deva[अन्तेअग्निमन्थ्यपर्यन्तमधीते साग्नि] 'including the work on fire ( which is studied last of all )', so @deva[सक्षाध्यम्], &c.
