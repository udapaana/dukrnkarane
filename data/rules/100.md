The @deva[वा] of root-nouns ending in @deva[वाह्] is changed to @deva[ऊ] before the vowel terminations beginning with the Acc. plu.[^1]; e. g. @deva[विश्ववाह्] = the sustainer of the universe, a lord:—

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. | @deva[विभ्वाह्‌-इ] | @deva[विभ्वाही] | @deva[विभ्वाह्‌] |
| A. | @deva[विभ्वाहम्] | ,, | @deva[विभ्वोह्‌] |
| I. | @deva[विभ्वोहा] | @deva[विभ्वाड्भ्याम्] | @deva[विभ्वाहभिः] |
| D. | @deva[विभ्वोहे] | ,, | @deva[विभ्वाहभ्यः] |
| Ab. | @deva[विभ्वोह्‌] | ,, | ,, |
| G. | ,, | @deva[विभ्वोहोः] | @deva[विभ्वोहाम्] |
| L. | @deva[विभ्वोहि] | ,, | @deva[विभ्वाहषु] |

Decline similarly @deva[हञ्यवाह्‌], @deva[भारवाह्‌], @deva[भुवाह्‌], @deva[भेतवाह्‌] &c. Acc. plu., Inst. sing. &c. of @deva[भुवाह्‌], are @deva[भुहः], @deva[भुहा] &c.
