## Formation of Desiderative Forms

Any primitive root of the ten classes, and also the causal base, may optionally[^1] take a Desiderative form, which, like the causal base, is conjugated in all the ten Tenses and Moods of three voices.

---

[^1]: Or the notion of desire may be conveyed by means of a periphrase; i. e. @deva[पिपठिषति] or @deva[पठितुमिच्छति] 'he wishes to read.'
