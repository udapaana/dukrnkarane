## Roots Not Shortening Penultimate Vowel

The roots @deva[घ्राघ्], @deva[एघ्], @deva[काम्], @deva[कीड्], @deva[क्षीद्], @deva[स्वाद्], @deva[सेद्], @deva[राय्], @deva[राव्], @deva[स्पुव्], @deva[रुद्], @deva[ग्लेव्], @deva[स्वेद्], @deva[रोघ्], @deva[धाव्], @deva[रुज्], and @deva[रुष्] restore the @deva[छे], @deva[द्], @deva[लेव्], @deva[लौव्], @deva[शेष्], @deva[वेष्], @deva[ढ्लौष्], @deva[ब्लोष्], @deva[व्लौष्], @deva[रेष्], @deva[हेष्], and others less important, do not shorten their penultimate; 3 sing. @deva[अञ्जशाशत्त्], @deva[राजत्त्], @deva[अचकाशत्], @deva[अचिकीइत्], @deva[अचिक्षीत्त्], @deva[अचिक्षावत्], @deva[अचिसेत्त्], &c.
