@deva[नु] and @deva[उ] are added on to the roots of the 5th and 8th classes respectively before the terminations.[^1]

[^1]: @deva[स्वादिभ्यो नु:] । @deva[तनादिकृणोतः उ:] । Pāṇ. III. 1. 73, 79.
