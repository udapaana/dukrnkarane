## Vowel Changes Before य in Nominal Verbs

Before this @deva[य]:

### (1) Final अ and आ → ई

@deva[पुत्र आत्मजः इच्छति]—@deva[पुत्रीयति] (@deva[पुत्र] + @deva[य] = @deva[पुत्री] + @deva[य] + @deva[ति]) 'he wishes for a son'

### (2) Final इ and उ are Lengthened

@deva[कवि]—@deva[कवीयति] 'he wishes for a poet'

### (3) Final ऋ → री

@deva[कर्तृ]—@deva[कर्तीयति]

### (4) Final ओ and औ → अव् and आव्

@deva[गो]—@deva[गावयति]

@deva[नौ]—@deva[नावयति]

### (5) Final Nasal Dropped, Preceding Vowel Changed

A final nasal is dropped and the preceding vowel is changed like an original final vowel:

@deva[राजन्]—@deva[राजीयति] 'he wishes for a king'

### (6) Other Final Consonants Unchanged

In other cases the final consonant remains unchanged:

@deva[वाच्]—@deva[वाच्यति] 'he wishes for words'

@deva[दिव्]—@deva[दिव्यति] (@deva[द्युस्यति] according to some) 'he wishes for heaven'

@deva[सविद्]—@deva[सविद्यति] 'he wishes for holy sticks', etc.

### (7) Taddhita Affix Dropped

The Taddhita affix expressing 'a descendant of' is dropped and then the changes mentioned above take place:

@deva[गार्ग] (the son of @deva[गर्ग]) @deva[आत्मजः इच्छति]—@deva[गार्गीयति] (@deva[गार्ग] + @deva[य] + @deva[ति] = @deva[गार्गि] + @deva[य] + @deva[ति] = @deva[गार्गी] + @deva[य] + @deva[ति]), etc.
