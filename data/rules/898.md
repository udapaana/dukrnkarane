The past passive participles are often used impersonally, the agent being put in the Instrumental case; @deva[प्रकाशितं] or @deva[भासितं सूर्येण] it is shone by the sun; @deva[जितं पुत्रस्नेहेन] victorious (all powerful) is the affection for sons; @deva[पाण्डित्यं प्रकाशितं तेन] he showed his learning; @deva[परिहृतं पश्चात् विषयेन], &c.
