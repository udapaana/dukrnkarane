The roots @deva[इ] 1. 2. P. 'to go,' @deva[स्तु], @deva[शास्], @deva[इ] 5. P. A. @deva[ह्व], @deva[दुह्], and roots having @deva[ऋ] short for their penultimate, except @deva[कृष्] and @deva[वृत्], take the affix @deva[क्यप्] (@deva[य]) in the same sense as @deva[यत्]. When a root ends in a short vowel, @deva[त्] is inserted between the final vowel of the root; e. g. @deva[इ+य=ए+त्+य]='worthy of being served;' @deva[स्तुत्यः] 'deserving praise;' @deva[शास्]—@deva[शिष्यः] 'fit to be instructed;' @deva[दुह्]—@deva[दुह्यः]; @deva[इ]—@deva[आइत्यः]; @deva[दुह्]—@deva[दुह्यः] 'worthy of being served;' @deva[भृ]—@deva[भृत्यः]; @deva[दृश्]—@deva[दृश्यः] 'fit to be increased as wealth, &c.' but @deva[कल्पः] (@deva[कृष्+य]) 'able;' @deva[वद्]—@deva[वर्यः] (@deva[वृत्+एयत्]) 'that ought to be told or said.'
