## Additional Uses of इङ् Affix

@deva[इङ्] is further added in the following cases:

@deva[काणेन अभियान]—@deva[आभीकणाकिङ्गति]

@deva[लाता अदुभाट]—@deva[अदुभाकिङ्गति]

@deva[काणेन उपगायत]—@deva[उपकाणाकिङ्गति]

@deva[भूतिके शूपस्तोति]—@deva[उपभूतिकाकिङ्गति]

@deva[तट् यूव्वाति]—@deva[तट्यति]

@deva[पाणीज्]—@deva[पाणीजीकिङ्गति]

@deva[निवातवते]—@deva[निवाते] 'clears the air of vapours'

@deva[चूर्णाकिङ्गति]

@deva[एनीयाजेडे]—@deva[एनयते] 'declares her variegated', etc.

The various changes taking place here before the affix @deva[य] the student will easily notice as irregularities.
