Past Active participles are derived from the P. P. Passive Participle in @deva[त्] or @deva[न] by changing it to them the affix @deva[वत्]; e.g.

| Roots | | P. P. Ptc. | P. Ac. Ptc. | |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| @deva[भू] | to be | @deva[भूतः] | @deva[भूतवत्] | one or that has been |
| @deva[कृ] | to do | @deva[कृतः] | @deva[कृतवत्] | „ „ done |
| @deva[कृ] | to scatter | @deva[कीर्णः] | @deva[कीर्णवत्] | „ „ scattered |
| @deva[दिद्] | to cut | @deva[दिन्नः] | @deva[दिन्नवत्] | „ „ cut |
| etc. | | etc. | etc. | etc. |
