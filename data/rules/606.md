## Causals with प and ञ् Augments

@deva[ज्ञा] 'to pare, to sharpen,' @deva[हा] 'to cut,' @deva[हा] 'to finish,' @deva[हे] P. 'to give,' @deva[अभ्], @deva[कुश्], @deva[कृष्], @deva[गुह्], all P. and meaning 'to injure,' 'to kill,' @deva[चट्], 'to kill,' @deva[कृ] P. 'to honour,' @deva[ज्वद्], 'to shine,' @deva[हृद्], @deva[कृद्], 'to shake, to move' P., 'to break' (10 Â.),' @deva[हृष्], @deva[पच्] 'to cook, to boil,' @deva[हा] 'to kill, to gratify, to sharpen, to manifest,' @deva[चट्], 'to live or to be' (@deva[प्रापयति] in other senses), @deva[श्र] 'to sport, to toil the tongue,' @deva[भश्] 'to be poor, to reduce,' @deva[छद्], @deva[सद्], @deva[जद्], @deva[वृ], @deva[कह्] 'to be crooked, to shine,' @deva[पद्], @deva[स्पृश्] and @deva[ई] 1 P. 'to go,' 'to call,' @deva[ट्ये] 'to cover,' @deva[वे] 'to weave,' @deva[से] 'to waste away,' and @deva[पा] 'to drink' insert @deva[प] instead of @deva[व्].

**Examples:**
- @deva[ज्ञापयति]
- @deva[स्थापयति]
- @deva[दाशयति]
- @deva[पाययति], &c.

### (a) Special Augments

@deva[पा] 'to protect' and @deva[वे] when it means 'to shake,' insert @deva[ल्] and @deva[ञ्] respectively before @deva[अय्].

**Examples:**
- @deva[पालयति] 'he protects'
- @deva[वाञ्जयति] 'he shakes'
