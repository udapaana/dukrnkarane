The guttural of the reduplicative syllable is changed to the corresponding palatal (subject to the above rule) and @deva[ह] to @deva[ज]; e. g. @deva[कृ]=@deva[चकृ]; @deva[गम्]=@deva[जगम्]; @deva[पवि]=@deva[तिपवि]; @deva[सक्त्]=@deva[तसक्त्]=@deva[चसक्त्], @deva[हृणु] &c.
