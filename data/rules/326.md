If prātipadika end in @deva[वन्] it changes its @deva[न्] to @deva[र्] before @deva[ई];[^1] @deva[शक्वन्-शक्वरी] the strong one; @deva[पीवन्-पीवरी], @deva[शर्वन्-शर्वरी] the night, @deva[सुत्वानमतिक्रान्ता अतिशर्वरी], @deva[अतिपीवरी], &c.

**Exception.**— But if a base end in @deva[वन्] applied to roots ending in a soft consonant; or if a compound has such a base for its latter member, the fem. is formed by adding @deva[आ] only, before which the preceding @deva[अन्] is dropped,[^2] e. g. @deva[अवाबन्+आ=अवाबा] a Brāhmaṇa woman (@deva[ब्राह्मणी]) or a female thief; @deva[राजगुप्ता].

---

[^1]: @deva[वनोरच्] । Pāṇ. VI. 1. 77, 7.

[^2]: @deva[अनो ब्रह्मणोः] । @deva[अव इत्पातोप्यतोऽन्यतरस्याम्] Pāṇ. IV 1. 12. 28.
