@deva[हा] 'to abandon' assumes the forms @deva[जहि] or @deva[जहो] before consonantal weak terminations except in the Pot. and @deva[जह्] before vowel terminations and those of the Pot. The Imp. 2 sing. forms are @deva[जहाहि], @deva[जहिहि] and @deva[जहोहि].
