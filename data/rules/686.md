The following roots insert @deva[इ] optionally:—

(a) @deva[मुष्], @deva[सन्], @deva[पुष्], @deva[दुष्], @deva[षुष्], @deva[क्षुष्], @deva[जुष्], @deva[सुष्], @deva[अष्य], @deva[शुष्], with @deva[स्तु], @deva[स्वप्] with @deva[आ], and @deva[हृष्] 4. 1. Par. when used with @deva[लोमन्] 'the hair,' or when it means 'to be surprised or disappointed,' @deva[मुषितः], @deva[इत्यायः], (see @ref[696] a), @deva[चलितः], @deva[कुलितः], @deva[कुद्र]. Bh. VIII. 8; @deva[स्वै], @deva[शूष्] 'to do, 'wasted, perished,' @deva[द्रोषित]; @deva[स्पृष्ट], @deva[स्पृशित]; @deva[क्षुष्], @deva[क्षुषित]; @deva[शून्], @deva[ज्वरितः]; @deva[रुष्], @deva[रुषित]; @deva[आह्] (See @ref[696] a), @deva[मुघवत्] or @deva[हुषित]; @deva[आस्वाप्तः], @deva[आस्वपित]; @deva[हृष्ट]—@deva[हर्षित]—@deva[लोमन्] 'horripilated with joy,' @deva[हृष्ट]—@deva[हर्षितः लोमः] @deva[हृष्य्]—@deva[हृषितो धीवान्] 'disappointed.'

(b) @deva[क्षिप्] and @deva[भ] take @deva[इ] optionally before @deva[त्] and @deva[स्य]; @deva[क्षिप्तः], @deva[क्षिपित]; @deva[भूतः], @deva[भवित].

(c) The following roots admit of @deva[इ] optionally before @deva[त्] when the P. P. Participle is used impersonally, or when it conveys the sense of beginning to perform the action or to undergo the condition expressed by the root; @deva[मुद्], @deva[स्मर्], @deva[पुष्], @deva[फल्], @deva[शिक्ष्], @deva[वृत्], @deva[स्पृश्], I. 4. cl. @deva[स्मुर्]-@deva[स्पृष्ट], @deva[एज्]-@deva[स्फार्], @deva[क्षिद्] and @deva[स्विदु] I. 4. P. I. A.; @deva[स्मृद्]—@deva[प्रस्मृदितः] or @deva[प्रस्वेदितः चैत्रः] 'Chaitra has begun to perspire;' @deva[प्रस्वेदितं] or @deva[प्रस्वेदितमनेन] 'he has perspired,' &c.; @deva[स्वेदु] 4. cl. has @deva[स्वेदित] also.

**N. B.** When the participle is not used in the sense given above these roots reject @deva[इ]; @deva[स्पृष्टः]—@deva[स्पृष्ट]; 'perspired, sounded inarticulately,' &c.
