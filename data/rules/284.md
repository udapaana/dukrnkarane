@deva[न] is always added in the case of the following twentyfive compounds which are:—@deva[अतिवाणादि षट्काशादि त्रिशदादि श्रेयः तुर्याः]; @deva[अश्वचतुर्] destitute of four; so @deva[विपलचतुर्] and @deva[पुद्धचतुर्]: all three Bah. compounds; then eleven Dw. compounds for which see @ref[192] (b) the last two; and @ref[196](c); @deva[मूड्गाभ्यां अपरित्यज्य समृद्धिभवः] Avya. (@deva[सरजः] as a Bah.); @deva[निर्भीतं श्रेयो निःश्रेयसम्] 'sure, never failing bliss;' @deva[पुरुषस्य आयुः पुरुषायुषम्] 'the period of human life'; both Tat; @deva[द्वयोरायुषोः समाहारः द्व्यायुषम्] 'the period of two lives;' similarly, @deva[त्र्यायुषम्], both Dwigus; @deva[कृष्णशुक्लम्] Dw.; @deva[जातश्चासौ उक्षा च जातोक्षः] 'a young bull'; @deva[महोक्षः] 'a grown up bull,' @deva[वृद्धोक्षः] 'an old bull'; all Karma; @deva[कुनः समीपम् उपकुनम्] 'near the dog', Avya. @deva[गोष्ठे श्वा गोष्ठश्वः], 'a dog in a cowpen that barks at others'; hence figuratively 'a person who idly stays at home and slanders others'. Tat.
