In a few cases, the intermediate members retain their case endings in which case the compounds are called 'Aluk' (@deva[अलुक्]); e. g. @deva[देवानांप्रियः] a fool, @deva[युधिष्ठिरः] name of the eldest Pāṇḍava &c.
