**(a)** @deva[उदक] becomes @deva[उद्] necessarily (1) when it forms a proper name, also when it is final; or (2) when followed by the words @deva[पूर्], @deva[वास], @deva[वाहन], and @deva[धि]:[^1] @deva[उदमेघः] 'a particular cloud filled with water'; @deva[उदधिः], @deva[क्षीरोदः] the sea of milk; @deva[उडुपोदः] &c.; @deva[उदवर्षं तिष्ठति], @deva[उदवासः] standing in water; @deva[उदवाहनम्]; @deva[उदधिः] 'a vessel for holding water' @deva[घटः], @deva[संज्ञाये तु पूर्णेण सिद्धम्] Sid. Kau.;

**(b)** and optionally when followed by a word beginning with a simple consonant and denoting 'a vessel to be filled up with water', and by @deva[मन्थ], @deva[औदन], @deva[सक्तु], @deva[विन्दु], @deva[वज्र], @deva[मार], @deva[हार], @deva[शीर्षप्] 'a yoke with a pan at each end for carrying loads' (@deva[काठी] Maráthi) and @deva[गार]; @deva[उदकुम्भः] or @deva[उदककुम्भः]; but @deva[उदकस्थाली] (as @deva[स्थाली] begins with a conjunct consonant), @deva[उदकपाषम्]; @deva[उदमन्थम्] or @deva[उदकमन्थम्] barley-water; @deva[उदौदनः] or @deva[उदकौदनः] rice boiled with water, water-meal; @deva[उदवीवः] or @deva[उदकवीवः], 'a yoke for carrying water;' @deva[उदवागः] or @deva[उदकवागः] 'plunging or bathing in water' &c.
