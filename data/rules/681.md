The affix @deva[त्] is weak.

**Exceptions:—**

(a) The roots @deva[नी], @deva[विद्] 1 cl., @deva[दिश्], @deva[विष्टर्], @deva[प्रुष्] and @deva[भृष्] admit of the Guṇa change before @deva[त्] when @deva[इ] is prefixed to it; @deva[या] I. A also does the same when it takes @deva[इ] (see @ref[686]b),

(b) Such of the roots of the 1st class as have @deva[उ] for their penultimate change their vowel to Guṇa optionally, when the affix @deva[त्] is added with the Intermediate @deva[इ], and in P. P. Participle as used impersonally, or when it conveys the sense of beginning to perform the action or undergoing the condition expressed by the root; @deva[मुद्] 'to delight' @deva[मुदित], but 'to begin to delight' @deva[मुदित] or @deva[प्रमुदित]: @deva[म्हुदित] or @deva[प्रमोदित रामयुः]: @deva[प्रहुद्दितः] or @deva[प्रमोदितः मायुः], &c. similarly; @deva[बुद]-@deva[प्रबुदित] or @deva[प्रबोदित] &c.
