## Dropping of य in General Tenses

In the general tenses the affix @deva[य] (@deva[क्यच्] and @deva[क्यट्] to be given hereafter) is dropped when preceded by a consonant:

@deva[सविद्यांचकार] Perf. 1 sing.

@deva[सविद्यिता], @deva[सविद्यिष्यति] 1st and 2nd Fu. 3 sing. from @deva[सविद्यति]

But @deva[पुत्रीयांचकार] from @deva[पुत्रीयति]

---
