@deva[पञ्चन्]—@deva[नवदशन्]. These are also adjectives agreeing in case with the nouns to which they are applied. They are declined in the pl. and are uniform in all the three genders.

| | @deva[पञ्चन्] | @deva[षष्] | @deva[अष्टन्] |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. A. | @deva[पञ्च] | @deva[षट्-ष्] | @deva[अष्ट] |
| I. | @deva[पञ्चभिः] | @deva[षड्भिः] | @deva[अष्टभिः-अष्टाभिः] |
| Dat. & Ab. | @deva[पञ्चभ्यः] | @deva[षड्भ्यः] | @deva[अष्टभ्यः-अष्टाभ्यः] |
| G. | @deva[पञ्चानाम्] | @deva[षण्णाम्] | @deva[अष्टानाम्] |
| L. | @deva[पञ्चसु] | @deva[षट्सु] | @deva[अष्टसु-अष्टासु] |

@deva[सप्त], @deva[नवन्] and the following cardinals up to @deva[नवदशन्] ought to be declined like @deva[पञ्चन्].
