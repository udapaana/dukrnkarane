The particle @deva[कु] forms a Karm. compound with any other word, @deva[कुपुरुषः] ( @deva[कुत्सितः पुरुषः] ) a bad man; @deva[कुयवः] &c.

**(α)**[^1] @deva[कु] is changed to ( 1 ) @deva[कद्] when followed by a noun with an initial vowel in a Tat. by @deva[त्रि] and the nouns @deva[रथ] and @deva[वद] and by @deva[ऋण] ' denoting a species;' @deva[कुत्सितोऽश्वः कदश्वः]: ' bad horse;' so @deva[कदन्नं] 'coarse food;' but @deva[कुद्रः] ' having bad camels' (a Bah. comp.) @deva[राजा]; @deva[कुत्सिताः त्रयः कद्त्रयः]: ' three inferior things;' @deva[कद्रथः] ' a bad chariot,' @deva[कद्व्रः]: 'speaking ill, a bad speaker,' @deva[कनृणम्] ' a kind of fragrant grass.'

(2) To @deva[का] when followed by @deva[पश्चिम्] and @deva[अक्ष] and when meaning 'a little,' and optionally when prefixed to @deva[पुरुष]; @deva[कापश्यम्], @deva[काक्षः] ' a sidelong look or a frown of displeasure;' see Bh.V. 54 (@deva[अक्षसादेश्च तत्पुरुषः अधिक्शब्देन बहुत्रीहिर्या́] Sid- Kau. ). @deva[इंषज्जलं काजलं] 'a little water,' @deva[इषत्पुरुषः काषुरुषः], but @deva[कुत्सित:पुरुष: कुपुरुष:] or @deva[कपुरुष]:.

(3) And to @deva[का] or @deva[कब] when followed by @deva[उष्णः]; @deva[कोष्णं], @deva[कबोष्णं] and @deva[कडुष्णं] by ( 1 ), all meaning 'luke-warm'.

[^1]: @deva[कोः कत्पुरुषस्त्रि] ( '@deva[त्रौ च]' Vārt: ) @deva[रथवदयोश्च । दुर्णे च जातौ + कापश्चसयोः । ईषदर्थे । विभाषा पुरुषे । कर्वं चोक्तम्] Pāṇ. VI. 3. 101-107.
