The sense of the comparative and the superlative may also be expressed by the particular case used; @deva[अस्य हृदयं पाषाणादपि कठिनम्] his heart is harder than stone; @deva[छात्राणां छत्रेषु वा चैत्रः मेधः] Chaitra is the cleverest of all students.
