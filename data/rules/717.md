The roots @deva[गद्], @deva[पद्], @deva[वद्] and @deva[यम्], take the affix @deva[य][^1] when not preceded by a preposition; @deva[गद्]—@deva[गद्यः] 'what ought to be spoken or told;' @deva[पद्यः], @deva[वद्यः], @deva[यम्यः]. @deva[वद्] with @deva[आ] may take this affix, if it does not mean 'a preceptor;' in which case it takes the affix @deva[एयत्] (@deva[य]) @deva[आवद्यः देशः] 'a country fit to be gone to,' but @deva[आचार्यः] 'a preceptor.'

[^1]: @deva[अचे यत्]। Pāṇ. III. 1. 97.
