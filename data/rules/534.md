## Special Forms of भू in First Aorist

@deva[भू] takes @deva[अभू] instead of @deva[उस्] in the 3rd per. pl. and changes its vowel to @deva[ऊव्] before the vowel terminations; e. g. @deva[अभूवम्], @deva[अभूव], @deva[अभूव] 1st pers.; @deva[अभूतः], @deva[अभूताम्], @deva[अभूवन्] 3rd per.
