Words ending in @deva[उ] and those having @deva[क], @deva[ट], @deva[ण], @deva[थ], @deva[प], @deva[ष], @deva[ख], @deva[घ], @deva[र], or @deva[स], for their penultimate; as @deva[प्रभुः], @deva[भानुः], @deva[इक्षुः], @deva[स्तबकः] 'a bunch of flowers' &c; @deva[घटः], @deva[पाषाणः], @deva[शोथः] 'a swelling', @deva[फेनः]; @deva[दीपः]; @deva[स्तम्भः]; @deva[रोमः]; @deva[समयः]; @deva[क्षुरः] 'a razor', @deva[अङ्कुरः], @deva[धरः], @deva[वायसः] &c.

**Exceptions:**—Words ending in—

**(a) उ**—@deva[धेनु], @deva[रज्जु] (except when it ends a compound, in which case it is both mas. and fem.) @deva[कुहू]—@deva[हू] 'the last day of a lunar month on which the moon is invisible,' @deva[सरयु] 'name of a river,' @deva[तनु], @deva[करेणु] and @deva[विषेणु] 'a kind of creeper,' which are all fem; @deva[इक्ष्वु], @deva[जाठु], @deva[वसु] 'wealth', @deva[अश्रु], @deva[जतु] 'lac,' @deva[त्रपु] 'tin or lead', @deva[तालु], @deva[दारु], @deva[मधु] 'honey,' @deva[स्वादु] 'relish', @deva[वस्तु] and @deva[मस्तु] 'sour cream', which are all neuter.

**(b) क**—@deva[चिबुक] 'the chin', @deva[इत्लुक], @deva[प्रातिपदिक], @deva[अंशुक] 'a garment,' @deva[उल्मुक] 'a fire brand.'

**(c) ट and ण**—@deva[किरीट], @deva[इकुट], @deva[ललाट], @deva[चतुष्पट] 'a place where four roads meet'; @deva[कृण], @deva[लवण], @deva[पर्ण], @deva[उष्ण].

**(d) थ and त**—@deva[काष्ठ], @deva[पृष्ठ], @deva[रिक्थ] 'property left at death,' @deva[उक्थ] 'a hymn of the Sāmaveda,' 'a kind of sacrifice,' @deva[जघन], @deva[अजिन] 'the hairy skin of a black antelope', @deva[तुहिन] 'snow', @deva[कानन], @deva[विपिन], @deva[वन], @deva[चेतन], @deva[झासन], @deva[सोपान] 'a flight of steps', @deva[क्षिपुन], @deva[श्मशान], @deva[रव], @deva[चिह्न].

**(e) प, भ, म**—@deva[पाप] 'sin', @deva[स्तुप], @deva[त्रिल्प], @deva[पुष्प], @deva[शष्प] 'tender grass', @deva[अन्तरीप] 'an island', @deva[कुङ्कुम] 'saffron', @deva[रुच्म] 'gold, iron', @deva[सिध्म] 'a leprous spot', @deva[युध्म] 'a battle', @deva[इष्म], @deva[युग्म] (generally mas.), @deva[अध्यात्म] 'knowledge of the soul'.

**(f) य and र**—@deva[हृदय], @deva[इन्द्रिय], @deva[उत्तरीय] 'an upper garment', @deva[द्वार], @deva[अभ्र], @deva[तक्र], @deva[वज्र], @deva[वय] 'lead', @deva[छिद्र], @deva[नीर], @deva[कूर्च], @deva[श्वभ्र], @deva[अभ्र], @deva[तिमिर], @deva[विचित्र], @deva[केदार], @deva[उदर], @deva[शरीर], @deva[कन्दर] 'dry ginger', @deva[पञ्जर] 'a cage', @deva[जठर], @deva[अजिर] 'courtyard', @deva[वैर], @deva[चत्वर], @deva[पुष्कर], @deva[गह्वर], @deva[कुहर] 'a cave', @deva[कुटीर] 'a hut' (m. also), @deva[कुलीर] 'a crab', @deva[काश्मीर] 'name of a country', @deva[अम्बर], @deva[शिशिर], @deva[तन्त्र] 'a loom, the ritual &c.'; @deva[यन्त्र], @deva[भवत्र], @deva[क्षेत्र], @deva[मित्र], @deva[कलत्र], @deva[चित्र], @deva[क्षुद्र], @deva[नेत्र], @deva[गोत्र] 'a family', @deva[अङ्गुलित्र] 'a finger-armour', @deva[शस्त्र], @deva[शास्त्र], @deva[वस्त्र], @deva[पत्र], @deva[पात्र], @deva[शुक्र].

**(g) ष and स**—@deva[कुज्जीष] 'a frying pan', @deva[अम्बरीष] 'a frying pan', @deva[पीयूष], @deva[परीष], @deva[किल्बिष] 'sin, guilt', @deva[कल्मषः] 'sin, stain' (sometimes mas.), @deva[विष], @deva[मुष] 'chaff', @deva[साहस], which (i. e.) are all neuter.
