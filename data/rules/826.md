## Dative/Instrumental with परिक्री (Hire)

The root @deva[क्री] with @deva[परि] 'to hire, as a servant,' &c., optionally governs the Dative (and optionally the Inst.) of the price at which he is hired @deva[शतेन शताय वा परिक्रीतः] (Sid. Kau.)[^1]
