## Samprasāraṇa in Perfect Tense

The change of a semi-vowel to its corresponding vowel is called @deva[सम्प्रसारण] (@[Samprasāraṇa]). Samprasāraṇa generally takes place before weak terminations in the case of the following roots:—@deva[वद्], @deva[वस्], @deva[शप्], @deva[वच्], @deva[स्वप्], @deva[वह्], @deva[यज्], @deva[वञ्च्], @deva[वै], @deva[वष्], @deva[व्यध्], @deva[दो], @deva[स्मृ], @deva[वप्], @deva[भज्], @deva[स्वञ्ज्], @deva[भञ्ज्], @deva[ग्रह्], @deva[सह्] and @deva[स्वप्]. In the Perfect the roots @deva[मध्यू], @deva[द्रक्षु] and @deva[मध्य] form an exception.
