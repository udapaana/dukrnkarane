## Third Aorist of Tenth Class Roots and Causals

Roots of the tenth Class and Causals:—

(a) The @deva[अय्] of the base is dropped (the vowel changes taking place before it being reta'ned) and a short vowel substituted for the long one (@deva[ऋ] and @deva[ॠ] being shortened to @deva[इ], and @deva[औ] and @deva[ऐ] to @deva[उ]).

The base so modified is then reduplicated according to the general rules; @deva[शोच्] (Cau. B. of @deva[छु]) =@deva[शोच]-@deva[शुच]-@deva[शुशोच्] after reduplication; @deva[चकम्] (from @deva[कम्])=@deva[कम]-@deva[चकम्]-@deva[चचकम्].

(b) @deva[इ] is substituted for the @deva[अ] of the reduplicative syllable if the vowel following it is short, or of the base if the vowel long; the @deva[इ] of the reduplicative syllable is lengthened if not followed by a long syll. or a conjunct consonant; thus @deva[चकम्]-@deva[चिकम्]-@deva[चीकम्]; @deva[चिचित्]-@deva[चिचित्]; @deva[त्रकद्]-@deva[चत्रकद्]-@deva[चित्रकद्], the @deva[इ] is not lengthened as it is followed by a conjunct consonant; @deva[स्पुर्]-@deva[स्पुस्पुर्] only, as @deva[प] is followed by a syllable prosodially long.

(c) Roots having a penultimate @deva[ऋ], short or long, optionally preserve it, the long @deva[ऋ] te ng changed to the short one; @deva[वृत्] causal base @deva[वर्तय]-@deva[वर्तय] after cropping @deva[अय] and @deva[वृत्] by this: @deva[वर्त्]-@deva[चवृत्]-@deva[चवृत्]-@deva[चिवर्त्]-@deva[चीवृत्]; @deva[कृत्]-@deva[कोत्तय]-@deva[कोत्तद्] and @deva[कृत्] by this: @deva[कात्]-@deva[चिकोत्]; @deva[कूत्]-@deva[चाकद्].

(d) To the base so prepared the augment @deva[अ] is to be prefixed and the terminations added; @deva[अचीकमत्] from @deva[कम्]; @deva[अचीकमत्] from @deva[कम्]; @deva[अचीचित्] from @deva[चित्]; @deva[अचिवर्त्] or @deva[अचीवृत्त्] from @deva[वृत्]; @deva[अचिकोत्त्] from @deva[कृत्]; @deva[अस्पुस्पुरत्] from @deva[स्पुर्]; @deva[अचिवर्त्त्], @deva[अचीवृत्त्] from @deva[वृत्], @deva[अचिकोत्त्],@deva[अचीकृत्त्],@deva[वृत्];@deva[अस्पुस्पुद्],@deva[अचुछुदत्] from @deva[छुद्], &c.

@note[type=note]{N. B. The Ātm. form in @deva[इ] when not given should be supposed to bs understood.}
