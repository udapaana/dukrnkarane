@deva[भृ] I. cl. takes this affix when the participle so derived does not form a name; @deva[भृत्याः] 'those who ought to be nourished or maintained,' hence 'soldiers,' &c.; but @deva[भार्याः] (@deva[भृ+एयत्]) 'a class of Kshatriyas;' when @deva[सम्] precedes @deva[भृ], @deva[क्यप्] and @deva[एयत्] are added optionally; @deva[संभृत्याः] or @deva[संभार्याः].

**N. B.** The word @deva[भार्या] meaning 'a wife' ought to be derived from @deva[भृ] of the 3rd class and affix @deva[एयत्].
