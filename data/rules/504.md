VI. 1. 137—139. @deva[स] is prefixed to the root @deva[इ] after the prepositions @deva[सम्] and @deva[परि] when it means to ornament or collect together; and after the preposition @deva[उप] in the above sense, as well as in the sense of procuring agreeably to a supplicant a thing without destroying the thing itself (@deva[सनी प्रदानापानं मनिद्वहः] S. 33. on Pāṇ. [^2]: The @deva[इ] is changed to @deva[गुण] (@[Guṇa]) as it is preceded by a conjunct consonant now (Vide @ref[499] Sid. Kau. on Pāṇ. VII. 1. 10-11.)

[^3]: The @deva[व] of the terminations @deva[ध्वस्व], @deva[स्वम्], and @deva[महे] of 2nd per. pl. of the Benedictive, the Aorist and the Perfect, is changed to @deva[ढ्] when preceded by any vowel except @deva[अ] or @deva[आ] or by @deva[य], @deva[र], @deva[व], @deva[ल] or @deva[ह्]. When the aforesaid terminations are preceded by the vowel @deva[ई], it is changed to @deva[इ], if @deva[ई] be preceded by one of the consonants given above.
