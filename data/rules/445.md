If a conjunct consonant begins a root, the first consonant only with the following vowel is reduplicated; e. g. @deva[प्रच्छ्]=@deva[पप्रच्छ्].

**(:)** If the first member of a conjunct consonant, however, be a sibilant (@deva[श], @deva[ष] or @deva[स]) and the second a hard consonant, the hard consonant is reduplicated. @deva[स्तुभ्]=@deva[तुष्टुभ्]; @deva[स्कन्द्]=@deva[चस्कन्द्]; but @deva[स्वन्]=@deva[सुस्वन्] &c.
