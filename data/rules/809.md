## Causative Verbs: Accusative or Instrumental with दा, स्मृ, आह, दृश्

The primitive subject of the verbs @deva[दा] and @deva[स्मृ], of @deva[आह], and @deva[दृश्] when used in the Atm. is either put in the Accusative or the Instrumental; @deva[हारयति कारयति वा यर्स्यं यर्यं वा कटं] he causes the servant to take or weave a mat; @deva[अविमुवटिहुड्नो यरुयुरु नेतं नेतं वा] he makes the devotee bow down to, or see, God.[^1]
