@deva[युज्] in the sense of 'to be harnessed' takes the affix @deva[क्यप्] and changes its final to @deva[ग्]; @deva[युग्यः गौः] 'a bull to be harnessed to the yoke;' in other sense it takes the affix @deva[त्]; @deva[युक्तः].

---

**By means of the affix @deva[घ्यत्].**
