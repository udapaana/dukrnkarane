Vṛddhi (@deva[औ]) is substituted for the @deva[ओ] of words ending in @deva[ओ] in the first five inflections except the Acc. sing.; @deva[आ] is substituted for @deva[ओ] in the Acc. sing. and plural.[^1] Nouns ending in @deva[औ] are regularly declined.

@deva[गौ] m. f. a bull or a cow.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. | @deva[गौः] | @deva[गावौ] | @deva[गावः] |
| A. | @deva[गाम्] | ,, | @deva[गाः] |
| I. | @deva[गवा] | @deva[गोभ्याम्] | @deva[गोभिः] |
| D. | @deva[गवे] | ,, | @deva[गोभ्यः] |
| Ab. | @deva[गोः] | ,, | ,, |
| G. | ,, | @deva[गवोः] | @deva[गवाम्] |
| L. | @deva[गवि] | ,, | @deva[गोषु] |

Decline similarly @deva[स्थतो] (@deva[स्थतः उः शङ्करः येन]) and @deva[खो] f. the sky. The neu. @deva[प्रयो] (@deva[प्रष्ठत यो: यस्मिन् तत्]) changed to @deva[प्रयु] should be declined like @deva[मधु].

@deva[रक्षौ] m. the moon.

| | Sing. | Dual | Plural |
|---|---|---|---|
| N. V. | @deva[रक्षौः] | @deva[रक्षावौ] | @deva[रक्षावः] |
| A. | @deva[रक्षावम्] | ,, | ,, |
| I. | @deva[रक्षावा] | @deva[रक्षौभ्याम्] | @deva[रक्षौभिः] |
| D. | @deva[रक्षावे] | ,, | @deva[रक्षौभ्यः] |
| Ab. | @deva[रक्षावः] | ,, | ,, |
| G. | ,, | @deva[रक्षावोः] | @deva[रक्षावाम्] |
| L. | @deva[रक्षावि] | ,, | @deva[रक्षौषु] |

Decline similarly @deva[नौ] f. a boat, a ship. The neu. @deva[धुनौ] (@deva[धनु नौः यस्मिन्]) changed to @deva[धुनु] should be declined like @deva[मधु].
