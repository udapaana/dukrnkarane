@deva[अष्टन्] becomes @deva[अष्टा] when followed by @deva[कपाल] and @deva[गो] and the compound so formed conveys the sense of 'an oblation' and 'yoked to' respectively;[^1] @deva[अष्टाकपाल:] 'prepared or offered in eight pans' (@deva[पुरोडाश:]); @deva[अष्टागवं] (@deva[शकटम्]) 'a carriage with eight bullocks yoked to it'.

---

[^1]: @deva[अष्टन: कपाले हविषि । गावि च युक्ते] Vārtikas.
