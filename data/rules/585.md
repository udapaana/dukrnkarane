## Final Vowel Changes to ई in Ātmanepada Benedictive

@deva[दा], @deva[यम्] and roots assuming these forms, @deva[धा], @deva[स्था], @deva[पा], @deva[मा], 'to drink', @deva[पा], 'to abandon' and @deva[शो], change their final to @deva[ई].

Final @deva[या] (original or substituted; see @ref[459]), if it be preceded by a conjunct consonant, is changed to @deva[ई] optionally.

**Examples:**
- @deva[दा]—@deva[दीयात्]
- @deva[यम्]—@deva[यीयात्]
- @deva[पा]—@deva[पीयात्]
- @deva[निशा]—@deva[निशीयात्], @deva[निर्शीयात्] &c.

@note[type=exception]{@deva[पा] to 'protect, @deva[पायात्].}

**Ātmanepada.**
