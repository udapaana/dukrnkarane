## Formation of Ātmanepada Frequentative Base

The Ātm. Frequentative base is formed by adding @deva[य] to the root, before which it undergoes the same changes as before the @deva[य] of the Passive:

@deva[भू]—@deva[भूय], @deva[ऋ]—@deva[ईर्य], @deva[जि]—@deva[जीय], @deva[शी]—@deva[शीय], @deva[भू]—@deva[भूय], @deva[सू]—@deva[स्वय], @deva[ऋ]—@deva[अर्थ], @deva[दू]—@deva[कीर्य], @deva[पे]—@deva[धीय], etc.; @deva[निह्]—@deva[निष्य], @deva[तृ]—@deva[तूर्य], @deva[खण्ड्]—@deva[खन्दय], @deva[नस्त्र]—@deva[नश्य], etc.

### (a) Special Vowel Changes

@deva[ज्ञा] and @deva[स्मा] change their vowel to @deva[ई], and @deva[ऋ] preceded by one radical consonant is changed to @deva[ऊ], and not to @deva[ऋ]:

@deva[ज्ञा]—@deva[ज्ञीय], @deva[स्मा]—@deva[स्मीय], @deva[कृ]—@deva[कूर्य]

### (b) Samprasāraṇa and Substitutions

The roots @deva[सघुत्], @deva[समुद्], @deva[स्मर्], @deva[स्वप्], @deva[मृड्], @deva[मञ्चु], @deva[मस्त्र] and @deva[गुञ्ज्] take Samprasāraṇa.

@deva[हु] and @deva[हु] substitute @deva[ई] for their final vowel.

@deva[सरिट्] substitutes @deva[जिर्] and @deva[व्याद्] @deva[री]:

@deva[सघुत्]—@deva[निच्], @deva[स्पर्]—@deva[हन्य], @deva[मुड्]—@deva[युन्], @deva[हे]—@deva[इय], @deva[ज्ञा]—@deva[ज्ञीय], @deva[साद्]—@deva[मिल्य], @deva[व्याद्]—@deva[पीन्]
