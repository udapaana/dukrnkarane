## Locative/Instrumental with आसक्त and उत्सुक

With the words @deva[आसक्त] and @deva[उत्सुक], the Locative or the Instrumental is used; @deva[आसक्त उत्सुको वा हरिणा हरौ वा] intent upon Hari; @deva[अन्य आसक्तानं प्रयातं आसक्तते वा येरीयदकायानाम्], a woman gets anxious (or restless) when her husband goes out; @deva[तेजस्विभवत्कात्युट्] Kir. XVI. 7[^1]
