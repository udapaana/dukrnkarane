**(a)** The prepositions @deva[अप], @deva[परि] and @deva[बहिः] and words derived from the root @deva[अञ्च्] (@deva[प्राङ्], @deva[प्रत्यङ्], @deva[उदङ्], @deva[अवाङ्], @deva[तिर्यङ्], &c.), are optionally compounded with a noun in the Ablative;[^1] @deva[अपविष्णु] or @deva[अपविष्णोः] 'away from Vishṇu'; @deva[परिविष्णु] or @deva[परिविष्णोः]; @deva[बहिर्वनम्] or @deva[बहिर्वनात्]; @deva[प्राग्वनम्] or @deva[प्राग्वनात्] 'to the east of the forest' &c.

**(b)** @deva[आ] showing limit, inclusive and exclusive, is optionally compounded with a noun in the Ablative; and so are @deva[अनु], @deva[आधि], and @deva[प्रति] all meaning 'towards' with a noun in the Accusative;[^2] @deva[आमुक्ति] or @deva[आमुक्तेः] 'until final liberation' @deva[संसारः], @deva[आबालं] or @deva[आबाल-केभ्यः] up to the child; @deva[हरिमक्तिः]; @deva[अग्न्याधि] or @deva[अधिमग्नि] towards the fire @deva[इन्धनः]; @deva[पदान्नि]; @deva[प्रवाहि] or @deva[अहि प्रति]; @deva[अनुवनम्] or @deva[वनमनु].

**(c)** @deva[अनु] meaning 'towards' or 'alongside of,' 'lengthwise,' is also similarly compounded; @deva[अनुवनम्] ( @deva[वनस्य समीपे अशनिर्गतः] 'the thunderbolt passed ) towards the forest'; @deva[वङ्गया अनु अनुगङ्गम् वाराणसी] 'Varânasi is alongside the Ganges' ( @deva[भङ्गावेच्यंसहशब्दैच्यो] । @deva[षष्ठ्येतत् इत्यर्थः] S. K.)
