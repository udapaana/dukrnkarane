Root nouns in @deva[ई] or @deva[ऊ] M. F. N.

Rule of Sandhi:—**(a)** The ending @deva[इ] or @deva[उ], short or long, of nouns derived from roots with the affix @deva[क्विप्] (0) and of @deva[ङी], is changed to @deva[इय्] or @deva[उव्] before the vowel terminations[^1]; the fem. nouns of this description are optionally declined like @deva[नदी] in the Da. Ab. Gen. and Loc. singulars and the Gen. plu.

**(b)** But if the ending @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] of a many-voweled noun having a root at the end be not preceded by a radical conjunct consonant or the root noun has a preposition termed @deva[गति] (e. g. as added to the root) or a word governed by the root preceding it, it is changed to @deva[य्] or @deva[व्], except in the case of @deva[स्त्री] and nouns ending in @deva[क्षी].[^2]
