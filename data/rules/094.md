**(a)** @deva[च्] or @deva[ज्] is changed to @deva[क्] when followed by a hard consonant or by nothing, and to @deva[ग्] when followed by a soft consonant.[^1]

**(b)** The ending @deva[ष्] or @deva[छ्] of root-nouns and the final of @deva[त्रष्], @deva[भष्], @deva[वृष्], @deva[षष्], @deva[पज्], @deva[राज्], and @deva[झाष्] are changed to @deva[ट्] when followed by any consonant except a nasal or a semi-vowel or by nothing[^2]; the @deva[ट्] is changed to @deva[ड्] or @deva[ड्] when at the end of a word, and to @deva[ड] when followed by a soft consonant. The @deva[ष्] of @deva[परिव्राज्] is similarly changed to @deva[ड्] or @deva[ड्].

**(c)** But the @deva[ज्] of the root-nouns @deva[दिज्], @deva[दज्], @deva[स्थुज्] and @deva[सज्] is changed to @deva[क्] as also the @deva[ट्] of @deva[धृष्टुट्] 'a bold or impudent man' and the @deva[ष्] of such words as @deva[विष्टुष्] &c. The @deva[ज्] of @deva[नज्] is optionally changed to @deva[ट्] or @deva[क्], as also the @deva[ष्] of @deva[तष्] and @deva[गौरष्]. The @deva[ज्] of @deva[कृत्विज्] is changed to @deva[क्].

**(d)** @deva[न] may optionally be inserted between @deva[च्] and the loc. pl. term. @deva[इ].

**(e)** Before vowel terminations final @deva[इ] may optionally be changed to @deva[य्].
