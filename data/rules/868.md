## Locative with साधु and निपुण (adoration sense)

The words @deva[साधु] and @deva[निपुण], not preceded by the prepositions @deva[अनु], @deva[परि] and @deva[प्रति], are used with the Loc. when the sense of adoration is to be conveyed; @deva[मातरि तिष्ठपतिरति] the son is partially disposed towards his mother; but @deva[निपुणः राज्ञः भृत्यः] a clever servant of the king. When preceded by the prepositions @deva[अनु], @deva[परि] and @deva[प्रति] these are used with the Accusative; @deva[साधुयानुवधो भार्यायां प्रति युद्धं वा].[^1]
