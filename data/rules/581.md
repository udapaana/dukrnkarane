## Vowel Changes in Benedictive Before Parasmaipada Terminations

The final @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] is lengthened; final @deva[ऋ] short is changed to @deva[रि], and final @deva[ऋ] changed to @deva[ऋर्] or to @deva[ऊर्] when preceded by a labial or @deva[व्] before the Par. terminations of the Benedictive, and the @deva[य] of the Passive.

**Examples:**
- @deva[चि]—@deva[चीयात्]
- @deva[स्तु]—@deva[स्तुयात्]
- @deva[कृ]—@deva[कुरियात्]
- @deva[लृ]—@deva[लीरियात्]
- @deva[वृ]—@deva[वूरियात्], &c.
