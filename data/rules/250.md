The negative particle @deva[अ] or @deva[अन्] and prepositions, may, sometimes, form Bah. compounds with substantives; the verbal derivatives used to express their sense may be optionally retained;[^1] @deva[अधिगमानः गुरः यस्य स अगुरः]; @deva[प्रपतितानि पर्णानि यस्य स प्रपर्णः] a tree with its leaves dropped; @deva[निर्गता घृणा यस्य स निर्घृणः] ruthless; @deva[उद्धृता कन्धरा यस्य स उत्कन्धरः] with the neck uplifted; @deva[विगतं जीवितं यस्य स विजीवितः] dead &c. Also @deva[अधिगमानगुरः]; @deva[प्रपतितपर्णः] &c.; @deva[आस्ति क्षीरं यस्याः सा आस्तिक्षीरा गौः] a milk-cow (where @deva[आस्ति] is an indeclinable meaning 'having').

[^1]: Implied by context; no specific Pāṇini reference given in the text.
