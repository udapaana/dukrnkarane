## Vowel Changes in Second Aorist Variety

The preceding @deva[अ] is dropped before @deva[अम्], @deva[अव्], and @deva[अन्त] and lengthened before @deva[इ] and @deva[म]. The radical vowel except a final @deva[ऋ], short or long, and the @deva[ऋ] of @deva[गृत्], does not undergo @deva[गुण] (@[Guṇa]) or @deva[वृद्धि] (@[Vṛddhi]) substitute.
