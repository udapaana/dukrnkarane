## Genitive with हेतु (Purpose, Cause)

When the word @deva[हेतु] (cause, object) is used in a sentence, that which is the object and the word @deva[हेतु] are put in the Genitive case; @deva[नित्यस्य हेतोः] for the sake of ('with the sake of getting'); @deva[तस्य हेतोः दाय] for the sake of her, &c.
