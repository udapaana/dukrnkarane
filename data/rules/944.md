The third person singular of the Imperative in the passive voice is often used and sometimes as a courteous form of expression; @deva[आनीयतां राजदाः] the prince should be brought @deva[श्रूयतां संगो पञ्चितैः] may you hear, ye Pandits; @deva[एतत्स्यान्नं स्वाद्यताम्] sake and eat.
