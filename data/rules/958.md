[^1] When words having the sense of 'to wish,' such as @deva[इच्छ्], @deva[वृ], &c. are used, the Potential or Imperative is used @deva[इच्छामि प्रभुञ्जीत] or @deva[अश्नीयात्] I wish you should dine; @deva[इच्छामि सोमं पिबेत् वा भवान्] (Sid. Kau.) I wish Your Honour will drink Soma.

(a) But when the agents of both the actions are the same, the Potential alone is used in the sense of the Infinitive; @deva[भुञ्जीविच्छति] (Sid. Kau.) i. e. @deva[भोक्तुमिच्छति] wishes that he will eat (wishes to eat).

[^1]: @deva[इच्छार्थेषु लिट्लोट्युटौ च। लिङ् च] । Pāṇ III. 3. 157. 159.
