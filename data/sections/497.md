## § 497. The final @deva[ग्], short or long, is changed to @deva[सय] before a vowel weak termiation and @deva[इ] to @deva[इय] or @deva[य] as

§ 497.  The final @deva[ग्], short or long,  is  changed  to  @deva[सय] before a vowel weak termiation and @deva[इ] to @deva[इय] or @deva[य] as  it is  preceded  by  a conjunct consonant or not; as @deva[इयय्]+@deva[इव]=@deva[इयेयव] ! dual; @deva[तितिर्]+@deva[इव]=@deva[तितिर्]+@deva[इव]=@deva[तितिक्व], @deva[लिभिर्]+@deva[इरे]=@deva[लिभिरिरे], &c.
