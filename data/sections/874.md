## § 874. Verbs having the sense of 'love, regard for, attach-

§ 874.  Verbs  having  the  sense  of  'love,  regard  for,  attach-
ment  to'  such  as  @deva[स्निहु],  @deva[अनुरुञ्युजु],  @deva[चायुहुदु],  @deva[रयु],  &c.  and  their  deriva-
tives  generally  govern  the  Locative;  @deva[तेयुयु  पुत्रुनै  नुस्यु]:  the  father
loves  his  son;  @deva[अति  युनै  सोदुरुहुयोयुषि  युदुनुदु]  (S'ak.  I)  I  have  a
sisterly  affection for them also; @deva[न  संदुट्  निरुयुत्युकुन्युयुकुयुयुयुनों  मयुयुयुनैयुयुयुनुयुः]
I  do  not,  indeed,  love  the  ascetic's  daughter,  @deva[अङुयुयुदुयुयुयुयुत्युयो  रात्रु
जुनुतु  याहुद्युरुज्युनु]  people  do  not  love a  king  whose  ministers  are
corrupt;  @deva[मायुयुत्युद्युयु  निरुतिरै  निरुयुनुकुयु  क्तुयुर्युं]  (Manu.  S.  III.  179),
@deva[रहसि  रमुते]  (Māl.  Mād.  III.  2.)  takes  pleasure  in  solitude;  @deva[रत्युः
श्युयुयुतै]  (Bhattṛi.  I.)  devoted  to  his  welfare.

Note:—@deva[अनुरुञ्युजु]  and  @deva[भुनुदुट्युयु]  are  sometimes  used  with  the
Accusative  also;  @deva[रायुयुयुयुयुदुयुञ्युच्युन्ति]  (Rāma.);  @deva[माद्युयुनुयुनुयुनैदुयुञ्युन्तो]
(Bhattṭ.  IV.  29.  ).
