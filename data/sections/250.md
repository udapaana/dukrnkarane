## § 250. *The negative particle @deva[अ] or @deva[अन्] and prepositions, may, sometimes, form Bah. compounds with substanti

§ 250. *The negative particle @deva[अ] or @deva[अन्] and prepositions, may, sometimes, form Bah. compounds with substantives; the verbal derivatives used to express their sense may be optionally retained; @deva[अविद्यमानः पुत्रः यस्य स अपुत्रः], @deva[प्रपतितानि पर्णानि यस्य स प्रपर्णः] a tree with its leaves dropped; @deva[निर्गेता घूणा यस्य स निर्घूणः] ruthless; @deva[उद्धता कन्धरा यस्य स उत्कन्धरः] with the neck uplifted; @deva[विगतं जीवितं यस्य स विजीवितः] dead &c. Also @deva[अविद्यमानपुत्रः], @deva[प्रपतितपर्णः] &c.; @deva[अति शौरं यस्याः सा अतिशौरा गौः] a milk-cow (where @deva[अति] is an indeclinable meaning 'having').
