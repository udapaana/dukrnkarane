## § 920. Present for Past with @deva[नु] @[nu]

The Present used with @deva[स्म] @[sma] and @deva[हि] @[hi] in the sense of the Past is equivalent to @deva[अकरोत्] @[akarot].

When the particles @deva[नु] @[nu] and @deva[उत] @[uta] are used, the Present may be optionally used:

**Examples:**

- @deva[कट्युयुकुयूर्युनै: किंयु—नं करोति] @[kaṭyuyukuyūryunai: kiṃyu—naṃ karoti] or @deva[चकार] @[cakāra]
- @deva[उत करोति] @[uta karoti] or @deva[चकार] @[cakāra]
