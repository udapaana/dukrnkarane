## § 509. Roots Dropping Nasal in Perfect

The roots @deva[प्रच्य] @[pracya], @deva[मच्य] @[macya], @deva[क्रच्य] @[kracya], and @deva[स्वञ्ज्] @[svañj] drop their nasal optionally before the terminations of the Perfect.

@deva[श्रञ्च्] @[śrañc], @deva[ग्रञ्च्] @[grañc], @deva[दंश्] @[daṃś] obey § 500, even before the strong terminations when their nasal is dropped.

### @deva[प्रच्य] @[pracya]

| Person | With nasal | Without nasal |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| 1 sing. | @deva[प्रप्रच्य] @[papracya] | @deva[प्रेय] @[preya] |
| 1 dual | @deva[प्रप्रच्यव] @[papracyava] | @deva[प्रेयिव] @[preyiva] |
| 1 pl. | @deva[प्रप्रच्यम] @[papracyama] | @deva[प्रेयिम] @[preyima] |
| 2 sing. | @deva[प्रप्रच्यिय] @[papracyiya] | @deva[प्रेयिय] @[preyiya] |
| 2 dual | @deva[प्रप्रच्युः] @[papracyuḥ] | @deva[प्रेयुः] @[preyuḥ] |
| 2 pl. | @deva[प्रप्रच्य] @[papracya] | @deva[प्रेय] @[preya] |
| 3 sing. | @deva[प्रप्रच्य] @[papracya] | @deva[प्रेय] @[preya] |
| 3 dual | @deva[प्रप्रच्युः] @[papracyuḥ] | @deva[प्रेयतुः] @[preyatuḥ] |
| 3 pl. | @deva[प्रप्रच्यः] @[papracyaḥ] | @deva[प्रेयः] @[preyaḥ] |

### @deva[मच्य] @[macya]

Similarly conjugated: @deva[जमच्य] @[jamacya] / @deva[येय] @[yeya], @deva[जम्रच्यव] @[jamracyava] / @deva[येयिव] @[yeyiva], @deva[जम्रच्यम] @[jamracyama] / @deva[येयिम] @[yeyima], etc.

### @deva[दंश्] @[daṃś] P. and @deva[स्वञ्ज्] @[svañj] Ā.

Forms include @deva[दंश] @[daṃśa] / @deva[देम] @[dema], @deva[दंशिव] @[daṃśiva] / @deva[देमिव] @[demiva], @deva[दंशिम] @[daṃśima] / @deva[देमिम] @[demima], etc.

**N. B.**— The final @deva[ह्] @[h] of the roots @deva[दुह्] @[duh], @deva[मुह्] @[muh], @deva[स्निह्] @[snih], and @deva[स्नुह्] @[snuh] is changed to @deva[घ] @[gha] or @deva[व] @[va] when followed by any consonant except a nasal or a semi-vowel or by nothing.[^1]

---

[^1]: This is referenced in the text and applies to roots like @deva[दुह्] @[duh], @deva[मुह्] @[muh], @deva[स्निह्] @[snih], and @deva[स्नुह्] @[snuh].
