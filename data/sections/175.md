## § 175. The possessive affixes at the end of words and the

§ 175.  The possessive affixes at the end of words and the
affix @deva[न्]  are dropped before @deva[ईयस्] and @deva[ईष्]‡ and then these
terminations are applied subject to the above rule;  e. g. @deva[मतिमत्]
talented  @deva[मतीयस्] , @deva[मतिष्ठ]; @deva[मेधाविन्]-@deva[मेधीयस्] , @deva[मेधिष्ठ]; @deva[धानिन्]-@deva[धनीयस्]-
@deva[धनिष्ठ]. @deva[कृते]-@deva[करीयस्] , @deva[कारिष्] ( @deva[अतिशयेन कर्तो] ); @deva[स्तोढ]-@deva[स्तवीयस्] , @deva[स्तविष्ठ];
so @deva[स्रजीयस्] , @deva[स्राजिष्] from @deva[स्रग्विन्] one having a garland &c.
