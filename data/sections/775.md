## § 775. ( a ) ‡ The gerunds of @deva[अम्] and @deva[युष्] are used with nouns

§ 775.  ( a ) ‡ The gerunds of  @deva[अम्]  and @deva[युष्]  are used with  nouns
denoting a period of time intervening  between the repetition or
performance of the action; @deva[द्वुवुहुरुचायुहं] or @deva[द्वुवुहुभुवुवातुं  गां]: @deva[पायुयति] 'he
makes the cows drink once  in  every  two days  to intervene,
i. e  every third day'  @deva[अत् पायुवित्युवा द्युयुभुषूहुत्रुकुरुम्यु  पुन]: @deva[पायुयतीतोर्युर्यै]: @deva[।]
( Sid. Kau. ); similarly @deva[द्युहुत्युरुयु]  or @deva[द्युहुतुरुरु] .
