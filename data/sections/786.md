## § 786. When a substantive or an adjective is used predica-

§ 786.  When a substantive or an adjective is used  predica-
tively with verbs of incomplete predication  such as  @deva[युद्]  to grow,
to seem, to be, to appear,' &c.  the  substantive  or  adjective
used must agree with the subject in case;  @deva[एषं मे  विश्युयुयुरुः]  this is
my  resolve;  @deva[सः  श्युयुतोरुः  प्रजानरुह्नोः छुच्छुयुर्]  that  king  seems
wakened  through  wakefulness; @deva[नयुत्युड्युप्रुट्युड्युयुनयुरुयुयु]  desirous of
taking hold of the three worlds.
