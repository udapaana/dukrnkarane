## § 958. Potential with Verbs of Wishing

When words having the sense of "to wish," such as @deva[इच्छ] @[icch], @deva[वञ्च] @[vañc], etc. are used, the Potential or Imperative is used:

**Examples:**

- @deva[इच्छामि भवान् भुञ्जीत] @[icchāmi bhavān bhuñjīta] or @deva[भुङ्क्तु] @[bhuṅktu] I wish you should dine
- @deva[इच्छामि सोमं पिबेत् भवान्] @[icchāmi somaṃ pibet bhavān] (Sid. Kau.) I wish Your Honour will drink Soma

**(a)** But when the agents of both the actions are the same, the Potential alone is used in the sense of the Infinitive:

@deva[भुञ्जीविच्छति] @[bhuñjīvicchati] (Sid. Kau.) i.e. @deva[भोक्तुमिच्छति] @[bhoktumicchati] wishes that he will eat (wishes to eat)
