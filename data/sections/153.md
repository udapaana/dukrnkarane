## § 153. @deva[पूर्व] 'prior, eastern', @deva[अवर] 'posterior, western', @deva[दक्षिण]

§ 153. @deva[पूर्व] 'prior, eastern', @deva[अवर] 'posterior, western', @deva[दक्षिण]
'southern,' @deva[उत्तर] 'superior, northern, subsequent', @deva[अपर] 'other,'
and @deva[अधर] 'inferior, lower,' when they denote relation in space,
time or person with reference to a particular thing or point of
time &c. and are not a name, are pronouns† and are then declin-
ed like @deva[सर्व]; but they are optionally declined like @deva[राम] in the
Nom. plu. and Ab and Loc. sing. when mas; e. g. Nom. @deva[पूर्वे]:
@deva[पूर्वे पूर्वे] or @deva[पूर्वाः] Ab. @deva[पूर्वाद्]-@deva[पूर्वस्मात् पूर्वाभ्याम् पूर्वेभ्यः]; Loc. @deva[पूर्वे]-@deva[पूर्वस्मिन्];
&c.; but @deva[दक्षिणा]: @deva[गायकाः] ( skilful musicians ) only, and not @deva[दक्षिणे],
for @deva[दक्षिण] does not show any relation here; so @deva[उत्तराः कुरवः] and
not @deva[उत्तरे], as @deva[उत्तराः] is a name here.
