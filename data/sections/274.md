## § 274. @deva[उपसम्पन्नपदे च] 

§ 274. @deva[उपसम्पन्नपदे च ।]

When @deva[उप] is used, in the sense of @deva[सम्पत्ति] (as stated in the previous section), the Accusative is dropped.

Example:— @deva[उपकण्ठम्] 'near the throat', @deva[उपकूलम्] 'near the bank'.
