## § 872. † The prepositions @deva[उपु] in the sense of 'exceeding'

§ 872.  † The  prepositions  @deva[उपु]  in  the  sense  of  'exceeding'
and  @deva[आरैंङु]  in  that  of  'the  master  of'  govern  the  Locative;  @deva[उयुयुरुयुयुयुनै
हुरेण्युयुः]  the  merits of Hari exceed a pardruha; @deva[आरै  मुयुवि रामुः] or @deva[आपि
रायुनै  यू]:  Rāma  is  the  lord  of  the  earth;  in  other  senses  these
prepositions  are  used  with  the  Accusative,  for  which  see  § 806.
