## § 290. @deva[पञ्चम्याः कृच् ॥]

§ 290. @deva[पञ्चम्याः कृच् ॥]

After a word in the Ablative case, the affix @deva[क्त] (called @deva[कृत्] in technical grammar) is used.

Examples:— @deva[यथाक्रोशम्] 'within the distance of a @deva[क्रोश] (cry)', @deva[यथायोजनम्] 'within a @deva[योजन]'.
