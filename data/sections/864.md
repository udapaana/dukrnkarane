## § 864. * The place where an action takes place with re-

§ 864.  * The  place  where  an  action  takes  place  with  re-
ference to the subject or object is called Adhikaraṇa (@deva[अयुकुरुणु])
and is put in the Locative case; † @deva[स्युयुपुति  निरियुयुनै] (Bham. 1. 60);
@deva[भाक्तुयो  नन्दुनुकुयुनोनं]  (Ibid  64);  @deva[स्युयुत्युयुयु  औदुनं  पुयुयुते]  cooks  food  in a
cooking  utensil;  @deva[कुणे  कुयु  यवुति]  tells  (something)  into  the  ear;
@deva[युयुयुदु  इयुकुयु  अवि],  &c.  The  Locative  also  denotes  the  time  when
an action  takes  place;  @deva[तास्युयुच्  विबुयुड्युयुयुयुः  कुयुलं  दिवुनैकुयुः]  (Kum.  II.
1.)  the  gods  being  harassed  at  that  time;  @deva[दिवुयुन्युयुनै  निद्युयुयुयु  गन्तुदुङु]
(Rag.  II.  15.)
