## § 923. Particle @deva[स्म] @[sma] Converting Present to Past

The particle @deva[स्म] @[sma] when used with the Present converts it into a Past tense:

**Examples:**

- @deva[प्रतिच्छन्युर्णेति यविषायवुनों यवत्तुरायुयु तत्युयु भायत्युयादुरोयो निषुयू-] @[praticchanyurṇeti yaviṣāyavunoṃ yavatturrāyuyu tatyuyu bhāyatyuāduruyo niṣuyū] in a certain village there dwelt a Brāhmaṇa Mitraśarmā by name

- @deva[रोरु यायूनुयोभायूनुयुस्युरु] @[roru yāyūnuyobhāyūnuyusyuru] the citizens ran in hundreds

The participle @deva[स्म] @[sma] may not necessarily be joined with it:

- @deva[त्युनुयुं स्युयु वैच्युयु यहुयूरुयूज यस्युयूहं न विभीषुणुः नम्युनै स्युयु हितुयुयुच्युदुनै] @[tyunuyuṃ syuyu vaicyuyu yahuyūruyūja yasyuyūhaṃ na vibhīṣuṇuḥ namyunai syuyu hituyuyucyudunai]
