## § 35. Optional श → छ Change

@deva[श] @[ś] preceded by a word ending in any of the first four letters of a class and followed by a vowel, a semi-vowel, a nasal or @deva[ह्] @[h], is optionally changed to @deva[छ्] @[ch],‡ e.g. @deva[तद् + शिव: = तद् + छिव:] @[tad + śivaḥ = tad + chivaḥ]; and @deva[तद् + छिव:] @[tad + chivaḥ], and then @deva[तत् + छिव:] @[tat + chivaḥ], and @deva[तत् + छिव:] @[tat + chivaḥ] by the above, and finally @deva[तच्छिव:] @[tacchivaḥ] and @deva[तच्छिव:] @[tacchivaḥ] that Siva (see subsection a, § 28.). Similarly @deva[तच्छ्लोकेन, तच्छ्लोकेन] @[tacchlokena, tacchlokena]; but @deva[वाक्श्रोतृति] @[vākśrotṛti] speech falters (as @deva[श] @[ś] is not followed by any of the letters mentioned in the rule).
