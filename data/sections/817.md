## § 817. The Instrumental is used with words expressing the

§ 817  The Instrumental  is  used  with  words  expressing the
idea  of—

(a) excelling; @deva[हुनांच महाभाग  तुगुतिवेङुते]  Oh fortunate one,  you
excel  your  ancestors  in  that  (devotion);  @deva[भाराविङुत्युयुयुति  भारं
सहुद्युयुयुशुः]  (Mu  3. 17  )  he  surpasses.  by his lustre,  the  lustre
of the sun; @deva[दुहितुरु संदुट् युणेयुसुयुयुञ्यु वनह्नुगिः ।] S'ak. I.

(b) resemblance, likeness, equality; @deva[स्युरेणु पितरुयुट्युदुरुति]  resembles
his father in vo:ce;  @deva[देदुवुन्युयुनं  स्युरेणु  च  रायुयुदुयुच्युदुट्युरुति]  (Utta.  4.);
@deva[अस्य युयुं सादुट् हुनुनं होचुनं  वादुयुति]  his face resembles that of his mother;
@deva[विष्णुयुनुयु  पड्युछुर्नो  नो]  equal  to  Vishṇu  in  valour.
