## § 739. The following words are irregularly derived by

§ 739.  The  following  words  are  irregularly  derived  by
means of the affix @deva[घ्यत्] ; @deva[आधातुरु]: ' what ought  to be brought from
the Gārhapatya  i.  e.  the Dakshinā-gni;' ( fr.  @deva[नी]  with  @deva[आ]  )
@deva[विदिणामायिनौगै दुत्तुवुदु । य हि गाहुपुत्युयानुपात्रोउक्षिनौस्यु तत्सुतुप्रुत्र्यतोगाम् ।]
( Sid.  Kau.  );  but  @deva[वायुं]  in  other  senses  ' to  be  led,  &c.  ' @deva[नोपुनुमार्युं]
( fr. @deva[नी] with @deva[यु] ) @deva[चोरु]: @deva[यीनोवनुहै  डुर्यथुर्यै]: ( Sid. Kau  );  ' disgusted
with  worldly  pleasures;'  as @deva[प्रणायुग्नैवोचारो  विरक्तो  डुर्युथुर्यै]: @deva[।] but
@deva[प्रणेयुं] in other cases.
