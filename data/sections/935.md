## § 935. First Future - Observation

When the continuousness of an action or nearness of time (i.e. the non-intervention of the same period between the two points of time referred to) is to be expressed the First Future must not be used.

**Examples:**

- @deva[याबज्जीवयानं दास्यति] @[yābajjīvayānaṃ dāsyati] he will give food throughout his life; and not @deva[दातु] @[dātu]
- @deva[य इयं अन्यस्या आनित्यतो] @[ya iyaṃ anyasyā ānityato]
