## § 630. Insertion of म् in Ātmanepada Frequentative

When a root ends in a nasal preceded by @deva[अ] @[a] and the nasal is not dropped, @deva[म्] @[m] (changeable to an Anusvāra or the nasal of the class to which the letter following it belongs) is inserted between the reduplicative @deva[अ] @[a] and the first radical consonant; the @deva[अ] @[a] of the reduplicative syllable remains unchanged (against @ref[§ 629 b]).

@deva[यम्-यम्य, पयम्य=येयम्यते] @[yam-yamya, payamya=yeyamyate] or @deva[यैयम्यते] @[yaiyamyate], @deva[जन्-जन्य-जञ्जन्य=जेजन्यते] @[jan-janya-jañjanya=jejanyate] or @deva[जञ्जन्यते] @[jañjanyate], but when @deva[जन्=जाप्] @[jan=jāp], the A. Freq. base is @deva[जाजाय] @[jājāya], 3rd sing. @deva[जाजायते] @[jājāyate].

**(a)** The same rule applies to the roots @deva[चर्] @[car], @deva[फल्] @[phal], @deva[जप्] @[jap], @deva[जभ्] @[jabh], @deva[दह्] @[dah], @deva[दंश्] @[daṃś], @deva[भ्रम्] @[bhram] and @deva[पर्] @[par]. @deva[चर्] @[car] and @deva[फल्] @[phal] after inserting @deva[र्] @[r] change the @deva[अ] @[a] of the following syllable to @deva[उ] @[u]; @deva[चर्=चर्य-चचर्य=चेचुर्य] @[car=carya-cacarya=cecurya] or @deva[चञ्चुर्य=चेचुर्यते] @[cañcurya=cecuryate] or @deva[चञ्चुर्यते] @[cañcuryate] by @ref[§ 394]. @deva[फल्=फल्य=पफल्य=पेफुल्यते] @[phal=phalya=paphalya=pephul yate] or @deva[पम्फुल्यते] @[pamphul yate]; @deva[दह्=दह्य-ददह्य=देदह्यते] @[dah=dahya-dadahya=dedahyate] or @deva[दन्दह्यते] @[dandahyate] @deva[जप्—जेजप्यते] @[jap—jejapyate] or @deva[जञ्जप्यते] @[jañjapyate].
