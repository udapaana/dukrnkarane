## § 279. @deva[अव्ययीभावेऽधिकरणे ॥]

§ 279. @deva[अव्ययीभावेऽधिकरणे ॥]

In the Avyayībhāva the first member of the compound is in the Locative case, when expressing @deva[अधिकरण] (locus or position).

Examples:— @deva[उपकुम्भम्] 'near the jar', @deva[अधिहरि] 'over or regarding Hari', @deva[अनुवृक्षम्] 'along the tree'.
