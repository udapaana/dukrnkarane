## § 558. Roots Taking Sixth Variety

Roots ending in @deva[आ] (including those changing to @deva[आ]), the roots @deva[धा], @deva[स्था] P. and @deva[पा] take this Variety. @deva[पच्] with @deva[उप] or @deva[उद्], A. and @deva[रक्ष्] A. take the fourth Variety.
