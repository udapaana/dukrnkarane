## § 554. Preservation of Vowel Unchanged in Certain Roots

The roots given under § 400 preserve their vowel unchanged; i.e. do not substitute @deva[इ] in the reduplicative syllable.

Examples: @deva[कच्]—@deva[अचकचत्], @deva[वर्]—@deva[अववरत्], @deva[घाट्]—@deva[अघाघाटत्], @deva[रह्]—@deva[अररहत्], @deva[पत्]—@deva[अपपतत्], @deva[स्खल्]—@deva[अस्खस्खलत्], @deva[सृज्]—@deva[असुसृजत्].
