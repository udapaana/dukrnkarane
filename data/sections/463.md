## § 463. Neither Guṇa nor Vṛddhi is substituted for the vowel of a few roots of the 6th class even before a s

§ 463  Neither Guṇa nor Vṛddhi is substituted for the vowel of a few roots of the 6th class even before a strong termination except the @deva[स्य] of the 1st and 3rd person  sing.  of the Perfect, the @deva[सीय] of the causal and the @deva[स्य] of the 3rd pers. sing. of the Passive
