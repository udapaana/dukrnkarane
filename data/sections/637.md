## § 637. Conjugation of Parasmaipada Frequentative

In the Conjugational Tenses the Parasm. Frequentatives follow the conjugation of the special base of a root of the 3rd class. @deva[इ] @[i] is optionally prefixed to the terminations of the singulars of the Present, of the 2nd and 3rd singulars of the Imperf. and of the 3rd sing. of the Impera.; when @deva[इ] @[i] is prefixed to these, a penultimate short vowel does not take Guṇa; @deva[दा-दादाति] @[dā-dādāti] or @deva[दादेति] @[dādeti]; @deva[दुह्-दोहेति] @[duh-doheti] or @deva[दरिदोहेति] @[daridoheti] or @deva[दरीदोहेति] @[darīdoheti] or @deva[दोहूतेति] @[dohūteti] or @deva[दरिदोहूतीति] @[daridohūtīti] or @deva[दरीदुदोहतीति] @[darīdudohatīti], @deva[कृ-चर्केति] @[kṛ-carketi] or @deva[चर्केतीति] @[carketīti], @deva[चरिक्षेति] @[carikṣeti] or @deva[चरिक्षेतीति] @[carikṣetīti], @deva[चरीक्षति] @[carīkṣati] or @deva[चरीकृतीति] @[carīkṛtīti].
