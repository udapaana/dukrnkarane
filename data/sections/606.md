## § 606. Roots inserting इय् or च् or ज् instead of व्

@deva[ज्ञा] @[jñā] 'to pare, to sharpen,' @deva[स्था] @[sthā] 'to cut,' @deva[दो] @[do] 'to finish,' @deva[दे] @[de] P. 'to give,' @deva[अम्] @[am], @deva[कृष्] @[kṛṣ], @deva[क्षिप्] @[kṣip], @deva[ह्नुष्] @[hnuṣ], all P. and meaning 'to injure,' 'to kill,' @deva[चत्] @[cat] 'to kill,' @deva[कृत्] @[kṛt] P. 'to honour,' @deva[ज्वद्] @[jvad], 'to shine,' @deva[ह्रद्] @[hrad], @deva[क्षद्] @[kṣad], 'to shake, to move' P., 'to break' (10 Ā.), @deva[वष्] @[vaṣ], @deva[यो] @[yo] 'to cook, to boil,' @deva[ह्रा] @[hrā] 'to kill, to gratify, to sharpen, to manifest,' @deva[चट्] @[caṭ], 'to live or to be' (@deva[प्राचायति] @[prācāyati] in other senses), @deva[लट्] @[laṭ] 'to sport, to loll the tongue,' @deva[वह्] @[vah] 'to be poor, to reduce,' @deva[छत्] @[chat], @deva[लद्] @[lad], @deva[जद्] @[jad], @deva[पृच्] @[pṛc], @deva[कह्] @[kah] 'to be crooked, to shine,' @deva[यद्] @[yad], @deva[स्पञ्] @[spañ] and @deva[घट्] @[ghaṭ] 1 P. 'to go,' 'to call,' @deva[ट्ये] @[ṭye] 'to cover,' @deva[वे] @[ve] 'to weave,' @deva[से] @[se] 'to waste away,' and @deva[पा] @[pā] 'to drink' insert @deva[इय्] @[iy] instead of @deva[व्] @[v]:

@deva[ज्ञापयति] @[jñāpayati], @deva[स्तापयति] @[stāpayati], @deva[दाशयति] @[dāśayati], @deva[पाययति] @[pāyayati], &c.

(a) @deva[पा] @[pā] 'to protect' and @deva[वे] @[ve] when it means 'to shake,' insert @deva[च्] @[c] and @deva[ज्] @[j] respectively before @deva[अय्] @[ay]:

@deva[पालयति] @[pālayati] 'he protects,' @deva[वाञ्जयति] @[vāñjayati] 'he shakes.'
