## § 720. @deva[व] with @deva[जुम्] in the senses of 'being conceived' takes the

§ 720  @deva[व] with @deva[जुम्] in the senses of 'being conceived' takes the
affix @deva[य]; as @deva[उपुराप्नो गौ]:  @deva[संभौणुमानां दुमुरेण  ङुम्पोङुयतै]: ( Sid.
Kau. ) but @deva[उप्सुरार्थों] ( @deva[उपुनु]+@deva[एयद्] ) @deva[कादुहों माहुस्य्या इपुक्षे]: (Sid. Kau.).
