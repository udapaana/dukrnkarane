## § 523. पी Substituted for प्यै in Perfect

@deva[पी] @[pī] is substituted for @deva[प्यै] @[pyai] 'to grow fat' in the Perfect and in the Frequentative; @deva[पिपये] @[pipaye] 1 sing., @deva[पिपियध्वे-ढे] @[pipiyadhve-ḍhe] 2 pl.
