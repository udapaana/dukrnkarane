## § 249. Obs —The Bah. compounds are also further divided into two kinds; @deva[तद्गुणसंविज्ञानबहुव्रीहि]: and @deva[अतद्ग]

§ 249 Obs —The Bah. compounds are also further divided into two kinds; @deva[तद्गुणसंविज्ञानबहुव्रीहि]: and @deva[अतद्गुणसंविज्ञानबहुव्रीहि]:. That in which the sense of the attributive member is also present is called Tadgunasamvijnāna. @deva[पीताम्बरं हरिमाह्म] is an instance of the first; here such a @deva[हरि] is meant as has the yellow silk garment actually on. @deva[चित्रयं गोषां आनय] is of the other;
