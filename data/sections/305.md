## § 305. Doubling in Avyayībhāva

@deva[अव्ययीभावे च] @[avyayībhāve ca]

And this doubling occurs in Avyayībhāva compounds.

e.g. @deva[यथायथा] @[yathāyathā]: according to each

e.g. @deva[सर्वसर्व] @[sarvasarva]: every single one
