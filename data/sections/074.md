§ 74  Obs. ( a ) @deva[ओडुलोमि] a descendant of Udūloman ( @deva[उडुलोमः अपत्यं एषमात्] ) is declined like @deva[हरि] in the sing, and dual and like @deva[राम] in the plural, the original word @deva[उडुलोम] being substituted.* N. @deva[ओडुलोमिः औडुलोमौ], @deva[उडुलोमाः] &c.

   ( b ) Words of similar derivation ( see Pā n.. II. 4, 62. 63. 65. 66 and IV. 1. 105 ) also substitute their originals in the plural, e. g. @deva[भार्गेस्य अपत्यं गार्ग्यः] N. @deva[भार्ग्यः भार्ग्यौ भार्गाः] &c.

        Words ending in @deva[ई] and @deva[ऊ] mas. and fem.
