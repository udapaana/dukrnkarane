## [^5] 24. Aya-Ādi Substitution

@deva[ए, ओ, ऐ] @[e, o, ai] and @deva[औ] @[au], when followed by a vowel, are changed to @deva[अय, अव, आय] @[aya, ava, āya], and @deva[आव] @[āva] respectively[^3]; e.g. @deva[हरे + ए = हरय] @[hare + e = haraya] for Hari; @deva[विष्णो + ए = विष्णव] @[viṣṇo + e = viṣṇava] for Viṣṇu; @deva[ने + अक: = नायक:] @[ne + akaḥ = nāyakaḥ] A leader; @deva[पो + अक: = पावक:] @[po + akaḥ = pāvakaḥ] the purifier i.e. fire.

### (a) Optional Dropping of य/व

@deva[य] @[ya] or @deva[व] @[va] at the end of a word and preceded by @deva[अ] @[a] or @deva[आ] @[ā] is optionally dropped when followed by a vowel or a soft consonant[^1]; e.g. @deva[हरे + एहि = हरयेहि] @[hare + ehi = harayehi] or @deva[हर एहि] @[hara ehi]; @deva[विष्णो + इह = विष्णविह] @[viṣṇo + iha = viṣṇaviha] or @deva[विष्णु इह] @[viṣṇu iha]; @deva[श्रिये + उपत: = श्रियायुपत:] @[śriye + upataḥ = śriyāyupataḥ] or @deva[श्रिया उपत:] @[śriyā upataḥ] intent on getting money; @deva[गुरो + उत्क: = गुरावत्क:] @[guro + utkaḥ = gurāvatkaḥ] or @deva[गुरा उत्क:] @[gurā utkaḥ] longing to see the preceptor.


### (b) Before य Termination

@deva[ओ] @[o] and @deva[औ] @[au], when followed by a termination beginning with @deva[य] @[ya], become @deva[अव्] @[av] and @deva[आव्] @[āv] respectively[^4]; e.g. @deva[गो + य = गव्यम्] @[go + ya = gavyam] produced from a cow as milk, ghee &c. @deva[नौ + य = नाव्यम्] @[nau + ya = nāvyam] accessible by a boat, navigable.


### (c) गो before Consonants

The @deva[ओ] @[o] of the word @deva[गो] @[go] is also changed to @deva[अव्] @[av] when followed by [consonants].
