## § 841. * The ablative is used with—

§ 841.  * The ablative is used with—

(a)  The  prepositions  @deva[अयु]  and  @deva[परिंङु]  meaning  'away  from,
without' and  meaning  'as  far  as'  or 'including,  comprehend-
ing'; @deva[यदुनुनुयुयु हरिकिभुयो द्युंङुयुते रतोरुतुयुरुतुः] (Bām. I.) now  that  he
dwelt  in  Laṅkā,  in  terror,  away  from  the worlds;  @deva[अयु  हरेः
सत्तुरुः]  Samsāra  exists  outside  Hari;  @deva[अयु  ङुतिगुर्तुयो  द्युयुयुं  देयुः]  it
rained everywhere, except in the country of  Trigarṭa. Similarly
@deva[परिंङु  हरेः  सीमुयुयुयुः  परिङु  युट्युदुयुयुष्युंङु  द्युयु]:  (Vop.  )  &c.  @deva[आयुकुनं  संकुयु
आरुकुछुयुयुद्युट् ब्रद्युयु] Brahman pervades every thing; @deva[आ यतोयुयु शुत्रोयुर्णो]
until the learned are satisfied.
