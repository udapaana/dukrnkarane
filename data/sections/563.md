## § 563. Dropping @deva[स]/@deva[सा] in Atmanepada

The roots @deva[दुह्], @deva[लिह्], @deva[मिह्], and @deva[गुह्] when Atmanepadi drop the initial @deva[स] or @deva[सा] of the terminations of the 1st dual, 3rd sing. and 2nd sing. and pl. optionally.
