## § 234. The @deva[स्वि] formations (see Chap. XI.) are also classed as prepositional Tatpurusha compounds; as @deva[शुडीं]

§ 234. The @deva[स्वि] formations (see Chap. XI.) are also classed as prepositional Tatpurusha compounds; as @deva[शुडींछत्य] having turned white what was not so before.
