## § 392. ( a )† The preceding @deva[अ] is lengthened before a conjugational termination beginning with a semivowel,

§ 392.  ( a )† The preceding @deva[अ] is lengthened before a conjugational termination beginning with a semivowel, a nasal or @deva[म्] or @deva[य]; e.  g. @deva[नयामि] &c.
