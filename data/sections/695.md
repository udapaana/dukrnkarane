## § 695. @deva[प्यास्] has @deva[पूत] when it is used with 'one's own limbs.'

§ 695.  @deva[प्यास्] has @deva[पूत] when it is used with 'one's own limbs.'
@deva[पूतं छुवं], and @deva[प्यास] or @deva[पून] in other cases,  as @deva[प्याव]: 02 @deva[पून]: @deva[रोक्य]:
when it is preceded by a preposition, @deva[इ] is not substituted; and the
vowel; @deva[प्रस्यात्र]; but when it is used with  @deva[अङ्क्षु] or @deva[ज्चृषुत्] with the
preposition @deva[आ], @deva[इ] is necessarily substituted;  @deva[आपीत]: @deva[अभूत]; @deva[भारनौ
ऊसै]:.
