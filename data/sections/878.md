## § 878. * When the action done or suffered by a person or

§ 878.  * When  the  action  done  or  suffered  by  a  person  or
thing  indicates  another  action  i.  e.  when  the  time  of  the  happen-
ing  of  the  one  action  which  is  known  indicates  that  of  the  second
action,  the  Locative  Absolute  be  used;  @deva[गोयुयु  दुयुयुयुनुयुयुयुङु  गतुः]
he  went  away  while  the  cows  were  being  milked;  @deva[अवुरुच्युयुयुयो]
