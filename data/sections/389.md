## § 389. @deva[य] ( @deva[इयत्] ) is added on to the final of a root of the Fourth or @deva[दिवादि] class before the terminations;

§ 389.  @deva[य] ( @deva[इयत्] ) is added on to the final of a root of the Fourth or @deva[दिवादि] class before the terminations; the radical vowel remains unchanged; e. g. @deva[कुर्]+@deva[ति]=@deva[कुर्]+@deva[य]+@deva[ति]=@deva[कुप्यति].
