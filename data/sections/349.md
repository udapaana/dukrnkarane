## § 349. Remaining Taddhita Affixes - General Note

There are numerous other Taddhita affixes used in Sanskrit for forming secondary nominal bases. The affixes described in the preceding sections (§§ 337-348) represent the most commonly encountered forms.

For a complete enumeration of all Taddhita affixes and their specific uses, students should consult comprehensive works on Pāṇinian grammar and the specialized treatises on Sanskrit word formation.
