## § 532. Roots इ, स्था, दा, धा in First Variety

The roots @deva[इ] @[i], @deva[स्था] @[sthā], @deva[दा] @[dā], @deva[धा] @[dhā], and roots assuming the forms of @deva[दा] @[dā] and @deva[धा] @[dhā] [belong to this variety].
