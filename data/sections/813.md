## § 813. ‡ When the accomplishment of an object is to be

§ 813  ‡ When  the  accomplishment  of  an  object  is  to  be
expressed, the Instrumental  of  words  expressing  the  length of
time or space is used; @deva[अहुनु कोणेनं  भुङ्युत्युकुट्युवीतुः] the  section  (of
the Veda)  was  studied  by  him  in a  day,  or a Koṣa  (i. e  by
going over it );  but  @deva[यरुयुमुर्योरुयु  नारायुयुः]  as  the  accomplishment
does not take place.
