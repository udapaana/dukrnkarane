## § 712. The affixes @deva[तव्य] or @deva[अनीय] are added to roots or

§ 712.  The  affixes  @deva[तव्य]  or  @deva[अनीय]  are added  to  roots  or
derivative bases in the sense of ' must be fit to be,' &c.   Before
these the ending  vowel  and  the  penultimate  short  of a root
take their Guṇa substitute,   Before @deva[तव्य]  Set roots take @deva[इ], Anit
roots do not, and Wet roots  take  it optionally.  Before @deva[अनीय]
penultimate @deva[ऋ] is always  changed  to @deva[अर्]  and  not  to  @deva[र] ( as it
