## § 269. In forming the Avyayibhāva compounds the following rules should be observed:—

§ 269. In forming the Avyayibhāva compounds the following rules should be observed:—

(a) The final long vowel is shortened, and the ending @deva[ण] or @deva[र्] is changed to @deva[इ] and @deva[औ] to @deva[उ]; @deva[गोपायति गां]: @deva[पातीति वा गोपाः । तत्स्मिन्नति अधिगोषम्], @deva[अनविष्णु] after Vishnu, @deva[उपय] near a cow, &c.
(b) †The final @deva[न] of mas. and fem. nouns in @deva[अन्] is dropped; and optionally that of neuter nouns ending in @deva[अन्]; @deva[उपराज्ञम्]; @deva[उपचम्भर्म]-@deva[र्मं].

* @deva[क्षौत्त्रविवक्षायां तु इहाभक्तिः ।] Sid. Kau. @deva[लिइकूविशेषविवक्षायां तु इहाभक्तिरित्यादि]-@deva[सिद्धच्चै पियादिछु भक्तिशब्दभाट]: @deva[।] Tb.
† @deva[अनश्र । नपुंसकादन्यतरस्याम् ।] Pān. V. 4. 108, 109.
