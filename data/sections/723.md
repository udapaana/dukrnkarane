## § 723. The roots @deva[इ] 1 2. P. 'to go,' @deva[स्तु], @deva[शाष्] , @deva[इ] 5. P. A. @deva[हु],

§ 723.  The roots @deva[इ] 1  2. P.  'to go,' @deva[स्तु], @deva[शाष्] , @deva[इ] 5. P. A. @deva[हु],
@deva[डुष्] , and roots having @deva[ऋ] short for their penultimate, except @deva[कृष्]
and @deva[वृत्] , take the affix @deva[क्यप्] ( @deva[य] ) in the same sense as @deva[यत्] . When
a root  ends  in  a  short  vowel,  @deva[त्]  is inserted between the final
vowel of the root; e. g. @deva[इ]+@deva[य]=@deva[ए]+@deva[य]='worthy of being served ;'
@deva[स्तुय्] 'deserving praise;' @deva[शाष्]-@deva[शिष्य] 'fit to be instructed;' @deva[वुद्]-@deva[वुद्द्य]:
@deva[इ]-@deva[आइय]; @deva[डुष्]-@deva[डुष्य] 'worthy of being served;' @deva[भृत्]-@deva[भृत्य]; @deva[दृष्]-@deva[दृश्य]
'fit to be increased as wealth, &c. ' but  @deva[कल्प] ( @deva[कृष्]+@deva[य] )  'able;'
@deva[वुद्]-@deva[वर्य] ( @deva[वृद्]+@deva[एयत्] ) 'that ought to be told or said.'
