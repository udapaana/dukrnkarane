## § 357. The words @deva[इसत], @deva[नयन], @deva[लौह], @deva[धन], @deva[मांस], @deva[कर्धर], @deva[काष्टक] ' a

§ 357. The words @deva[इसत], @deva[नयन], @deva[लौह], @deva[धन], @deva[मांस], @deva[कर्धर], @deva[काष्टक] ' a
bow ', &c., @deva[विवर], @deva[जल], @deva[इल], @deva[धन], @deva[चल], @deva[अज्ञ], @deva[इडुस्], @deva[झुल्व] ' copper ',
@deva[पत्तन], @deva[रण], and their synonyms.

    Exception:—@deva[सीर], ' a plough ', @deva[अर्थं] ' wealth ', @deva[ओदनं] ' cooked
rice ', @deva[आहव] ' a battle ', @deva[संग्राम] · a battle ', all mas., and @deva[आचि]
' war ' and @deva[अटवी] ' a forest ', both fem.
