## § 161. The cardinal numbers between 100 and 200, 200 and

§ 161. The cardinal numbers between 100 and 200, 200 and
300, &c are generally expressed by means of @deva[अधिक] added to
the number showing the excess and then optionally prefixing
the whole to 100, 200, &c, e. g @deva[एकाधिक शतं] or @deva[एकाधिकशतं] 101,
@deva[द्वादशाधिक शतं] or @deva[द्वादशाधिकशतं] 112, @deva[पञ्चाशदधिकं शतं] 150. &c.
After 1000 this @deva[अधिक] may be repeated with every multiple of
ten er hundred; e g. @deva[द्वि]-@deva[द्वानवत्याधिकाष्टशतोत्ताधिकसहस्रम्] 1892, @deva[एकोन]-
@deva[नचत्वारिंशदधिकषट्शतोत्ताधिकसप्तसहस्राधिक लक्षम्] 177639, &c.
Similarly @deva[उत्तर] may also be used instead of @deva[अधिक] e. g @deva[चतुःपञ्चा]-
@deva[शदुत्तरं सप्तशतम्] 754. Or these may also be, at times expressed
by the copulative particle @deva[च] as @deva[सप्त च शतानि विंशतिश्च] 720.
