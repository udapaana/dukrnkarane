## § 674. The terminations of the Perf. and the Past parti-

§ 674.  The terminations of the Perf. and the Past parti-
ciples are weak, and therefore the radical vowel does not take
its Guṇa substitute before these.  The penultimate nasal is
generally dropped before these ( See § 534 )
