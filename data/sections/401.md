## § 401. 10th Class Roots Taking Exclusively Ātmanepada

Some roots of the 10th class exclusively take the Ātmanepada, i.e., even when the fruit of the action expressed by them does not accrue to the agent. These are:

@deva[अर्त्] @[art] to request, to desire; @deva[कुह्] @[kuh] to astonish, to deceive; @deva[चिन्त्] @[cint] to be conscious of, to think; @deva[दंश्] @[daṃś] to bite; @deva[दृश्] @[dṛś] (or @deva[दश्] @[daś] according to some) to see, to bite; @deva[दुप्] @[dup] or @deva[दिप्] @[dip] to accumulate; @deva[तञ्ज्] @[tañj] to support a family; @deva[मन्त्र्] @[mantr] to counsel secretly; @deva[युष्] @[yuṣ] to search, to hunt, to seek; @deva[स्त्यू] @[styū] to take, to string together; @deva[तर्ज्] @[tarj] and @deva[तर्ष्] @[tarṣ] to reprove; @deva[बध्] @[badh] and @deva[गल्भ्] @[galbh] to injure, to hurt; @deva[विच्छद्] @[vicchad] to kill (@deva[विच्छद्] @[vicchad] according to some); @deva[किच्छ्] @[kicch] to measure; @deva[घृष्] @[ghṛṣ] to desire, to contract; @deva[मत्] @[mat] to kill; @deva[शुत्] @[śut] to fear; @deva[सूत्] @[sūt] to praise; @deva[यज्] @[yaj] to worship; @deva[रभ्] @[rabh] to guess; @deva[युर्] @[yur] to strike; @deva[शुम्] @[śum] to look at, to inspect; @deva[कूत्स्] @[kūts] to reproach; @deva[चुट्] @[cuṭ] (according to some @deva[कूट्] @[kūṭ]) to cut; @deva[भुल्] @[bhul] to drop down; @deva[भुट्] @[bhuṭ] to see, to expound; @deva[कूद्] @[kūd] to abstain from giving, to muddle; @deva[कुट्] @[kuṭ] to cut; @deva[बज्ज्] @[bajj] to deceive; @deva[भ्रूश्] @[bhrūś] to have the power of generation, to be eminent; @deva[उद्] @[ud] to gratify; @deva[दिन्] @[din] to bewail; @deva[ज्ञा] @[jñā] to know; @deva[विद्] @[vid] to know, to be conscious of; @deva[मुद्] @[mud] to stop; @deva[यु] @[yu] to censure; and @deva[कूत्स्य्] @[kūtsy] to smile improperly.
