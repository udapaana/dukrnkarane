## § 835. † In the case of @deva[जि] with @deva[पत्युर] the thing unbearable is

§ 835.  † In the case  of  @deva[जि]  with  @deva[पत्युर]  the  thing  unbearable  is
put in the Ablative and the context alone  shows  the  acceptable
or  difficult;  @deva[तां  यरुजुयुयुयुनों  न  मीते]:  (Bhatti.  VIII.  71,)  who  got
disgusted  with  the  love  (of  Rāvaṇa);  but  @deva[वाचुद्  पराजुयुते  ।]
