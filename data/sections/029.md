## § 29. Exception Before क्

If a letter of the group @deva[स्] @[s] be followed by @deva[क्] @[k] it does not substitute its corresponding lingual; @deva[सत् + क: = सत्क:] @[sat + kaḥ = satkaḥ] sixth good man.
