## § 820. † The roots @deva[रुच्] and others having the same sense

§ 820  † The  roots  @deva[रुच्]  and  others  having  the  same  sense
govern  the  Dative  of  the  person  or  thing  pleased  or  satisfied;
@deva[हुरै  रोचुते  कृष्ण]: Kṛiṣhṇa  likes devotion;  @deva[अस्मै न स्पृहुर्  न  सतिस्युयुहुः
स्युयुङुः इगुणियुः स्युदुते तुयुषुयुरु] ' a  stream of sweet water,  cool  and  per-
fumed, is not liked by one who has already drunk  enough of  water.'
