## § 725. @deva[शुच्] also takes this affix optionally; @deva[शुय्य] 'what is fit

§ 725.  @deva[शुच्] also takes  this affix optionally;  @deva[शुय्य] 'what is fit
or ought to be cleansed;'  optionally  it takes @deva[एयत्] before which
the final @deva[य] is changed to @deva[प्र]; @deva[शोच्य].
