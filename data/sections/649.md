## § 649. No Restriction on काम्य Affix

The restriction given at @ref[§ 644] does not hold good in the case of this affix, @deva[किंकाम्यति] @[kiṃkāmyati], @deva[स्वः काम्यति] @[svaḥ kāmyati]
