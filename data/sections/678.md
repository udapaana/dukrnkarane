## § 678. The participles of the Periphrastic Perfect Par, and

§ 678.  The participles of the Periphrastic Perfect Par, and
Atm. are formed by the addition of the Perf.  Participal forms
of the auxiliary  verbs  @deva[कृ],  @deva[असु]  or  @deva[भू] to  the base in  @deva[आम्] e. g.
@deva[दयालासिवसु], @deva[ञ्चत्रिषिवसु]. @deva[गण्]-@deva[गणयामासिवसु] , @deva[गणयामचक्रुवसु] &c.

            (c) Past Passive Participles.
