## § 144. The affix @deva[अति] is added to @deva[तद्] , @deva[यद्] and @deva[किम्] to show

§ 144. The affix @deva[अति] is added to @deva[तद्] , @deva[यद्] and @deva[किम्] to show
number or measure e. g  @deva[तति] 'so many', @deva[यति] 'as many,' and
@deva[कति] ' how many'. These are declined in the plural only, and
take no termination in the Nom and Acc. ; e. g. Nom. and
Acc. @deva[कति] Inst. @deva[कतिभिः] the rest like @deva[हरि].

        8—Reciprocal Pronouns.
