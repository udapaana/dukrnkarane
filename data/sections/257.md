## § 257. The following words lose their final vowel when at the end of a Bahuvrihi:—

§ 257. The following words lose their final vowel when at the end of a Bahuvrihi:—

(a) ‡ The word @deva[पाद्] when it is preceded by a numeral or @deva[ङ] or when the compound involves comparison and it is not preceded by a word of the @deva[हस्त्यादि] group (@deva[हस्तिन्], @deva[अज], @deva[कूरुल्], @deva[अश्व], @deva[कषोत], @deva[जाल], @deva[गण्ड], @deva[हासी], @deva[गणिका] and some others), @deva[द्वौ पादौ यस्य द्विपाद्] a biped; @deva[दुपाद्] fair-footed; (@deva[द्वाद्रास्य इव पादौ यस्य].) &c. but @deva[हस्तिपादः], @deva[कूदुल्पादः], &c.

(b) @deva[पाद्] becomes @deva[पद्] and the comp, takes @deva[ई] in the fem. in the case of the words @deva[कुम्भपदी], and others (i. e. @deva[कुम्भपदी], @deva[एकपदी], @deva[जाल्पदी], @deva[दडैपदी], @deva[शोतपदी], @deva[विपदी], @deva[द्विपदी], @deva[त्रिपदी], @deva[दासिंपदी], @deva[विष्णुपदी], @deva[घुपदी], &c.;) § but @deva[कुम्भपादः] mas.

* @deva[अत्र नासिकाया]: @deva[संज्ञायां नसं चारसुल्लाद् । उपसर्गोच्च ।] Pān. V 4. 118. 119 @deva[वेभ्यो चकृष्ण । स्थश्र] Vārtikas.
† @deva[पूर्वेपदात्सेज्ञायामयः । ड]
