## § 120. @deva[शी] is a suffix which is added to roots and crude

§ 120. @deva[शी] is a suffix which is added to roots and crude
nominal bases to form nouns and adjectives. ( @deva[द्वी०] V. 4. 60, 65,
66 ; Pan. V. 2. 115, 118, 119 &c. ). Thus we have @deva[प्रजा]-@deva[ईशी] = @deva[प्रजा]-
@deva[ईश] = nom. @deva[प्रजेशः] ( derived form praja; a lord of creatures );
@deva[काङ्क्षी] ( craving for = @deva[काङ्क्षा]-@deva[ईशी], derived from @deva[काङ्क्षा] ); @deva[लोहित्यशी]
( being desirous of @deva[लोहित्या] ). Before the case-affixes @deva[शी] becomes
@deva[इन्], thus @deva[काङ्क्षिन्]; and in the fem., @deva[न्] of @deva[शीन्] being dropped, @deva[शी] takes
the fem. affix @deva[ङीष्], ( @deva[वा०] III. 2. 7 ; Pan. IV. 1. 6 ) and is declin-
ed after the analogy of @deva[नदी]. Thus @deva[काङ्क्षी] ( fem. ), @deva[काङ्क्षिणी],
@deva[काङ्क्षिणम्] &c. The neuter forms are @deva[काङ्क्षि] ( nom. voc. and acc.
sing. ), @deva[काङ्क्षीनि] ( nom. voc. and acc. pl. ) &c. This declension
is similar to that of @deva[शी] ( the pos. suf. of the mas. pres. part.
active in the @deva[शी] conjugation ), @deva[शीन्] making its nom. @deva[इ]; and
will be subsequently explained along with the part. of this con-
jugation.
