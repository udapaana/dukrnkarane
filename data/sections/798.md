## § 798. All transitive verbs govern an Accusative; @deva[पुष्पाण्युप]-

§ 798.  All transitive  verbs  govern an  Accusative;  @deva[पुष्पाण्युप]-
@deva[चिनोति]  collects  flowers;  @deva[अप  एव  सर्वजन्त्युयुनो]  (the creator )  created
water first, &c.   Several  transitive  verbs  govern  what is called
in English a  factitive object besides a  direct  object;  @deva[प्राकृतानि]:
@deva[प्रकृतिं  त्युक्तो नं  पुनरुङु  विदुःु]  Kum. II. 13  they  consider  thee to  be
Prakṛti, they know thee to be Purusha; @deva[कुमारं नेतारं कृता] having
made Kumāra the leader ( of the forces ); @deva[नामु ततावुत्युजनामु अजं
चक्रार]  made his son Aja by name.
