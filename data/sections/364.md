## § 364. Besides these there are in Sanskrit a few nouns

§ 364. Besides these there are in Sanskrit a few nouns
having one inflection only which are treated as indeclinable
( nipātas ), e. g. @deva[अन्यद्] another ( reason ), @deva[अस्तम्] setting, @deva[अस्ति]·
that exists, @deva[ओम्] the well-known sacred syllable om, @deva[चनस्] satis-
faction, food, @deva[चाहु] coaxing, @deva[नमस्] a bow, @deva[नास्ति] non-existence @deva[धुर्]
the earth, @deva[धुवर्] the sky, @deva[वदि] the dark fortnight, @deva[ज्ञशु] happiness,
@deva[सुदि] or @deva[सुदि] the bright fortnight, @deva[संवत्] a year, @deva[स्वाहा] food offered
to gods, @deva[स्वधा] food offered to the manes, @deva[स्वर्] heaven, @deva[स्वस्ति]
happiness &c.
