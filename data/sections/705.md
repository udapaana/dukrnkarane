## § 705. Active Sense P. P. Participles - Motion and Intransitive Roots

(a) The P. P. Participles of roots implying motion, of intransitive roots, and of the roots @deva[श्लिष्] @[śliṣ], @deva[वृत्] @[vṛt], @deva[स्था] @[sthā], @deva[आस्] @[ās], @deva[वस्] @[vas] 'to dwell,' @deva[जन्] @[jan], @deva[रुह्] @[ruh], and @deva[जृ] @[jṛ] 4. cl. have an active sense:

- @deva[गतोऽहं मद्रासपुरम्] @[gato'haṃ madrāsapuram]: 'I went to Madras'
- @deva[स्थानगतः बालः] @[sthāna-gataḥ bālaḥ]: 'the boy has become languid'
- @deva[लक्ष्मीमालिङ्गितः हरिः] @[lakṣmīmāliṅgitaḥ hariḥ]: 'Hari embraced Lakshmi'
- @deva[शेषशयितः] @[śeṣaśayitaḥ]: 'slept on Sesha'
- @deva[वैकुण्ठवसितः] @[vaikuṇṭhavasitaḥ]: 'dwelt in Vaikuntha'
- @deva[शिवसेवितः] @[śivaseivitaḥ]: 'served Shiva'
- @deva[हरिदिनोदितः] @[haridino-ditaḥ]: 'observed a fast on the day sacred to Hari'
- @deva[रामजात] @[rāmajāta] @[jātaḥ]: 'was born after Rāma'
- @deva[गरुडासस्थः] @[garuḍāsasthaḥ]: 'sat on Garuḍa'
- @deva[विच्चुन्नरुद्धः] @[viccunnaruddhaḥ]: 'grown after this', etc.
