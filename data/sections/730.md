## § 730. ( a ) The following two words which are the names of

§ 730. ( a ) The following  two words which are the names of
two rivers are derived by means of  this affix;  @deva[तितर्ति  कुवं  विषु]:
from @deva[शिद्]+@deva[क्यप्] ; @deva[ऊम्ज्चुद्युकै  उञ्चुप्य]:, @deva[उञ्चुद्]+@deva[क्यप्]; see  Rāg  XI. 8.
In other cases these roots take the affix @deva[त्]; @deva[भेत्त्री], @deva[उन्चिद्धता].

( b ) Similarly the words @deva[पुषुय]: and  @deva[विष्ठुप्]; both the names of
the constellation Pushya,  are derived from @deva[एष्] and @deva[तिषु] respecti-
vely with the affixes @deva[क्यद्य]: ; ( verging towards, @deva[विष्ठुय]:, @deva[तिष्ठेत्यौ]: )
