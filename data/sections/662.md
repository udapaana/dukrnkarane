## § 662. The notion of ' desiring ' is expressed by adding

§ 662.  The notion of ' desiring ' is expressed by adding
a root to a root; e. g. ' to eat salt,' @deva[विष्कुप्यति] = @deva[विप]+@deva[यु]. @deva[यु]
meaning ' to take ' or ' to desire ', so @deva[पुष्पयति], @deva[विषगर्भयति] =
@deva[विप]+@deva[गृध्], similarly @deva[मुष्कर्षयति], &c.
