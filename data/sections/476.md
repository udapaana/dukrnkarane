## § 476. @deva[त] is inserted before the ending consonant of the root @deva[मसु] before which @deva[स] is dropped, and after th

§ 476.  @deva[त] is inserted before the ending consonant of the root @deva[मसु] before which @deva[स] is dropped,  and  after  the  vowel  of  @deva[नसु], @deva[पोरु] and @deva[दिवु] followed by any  consonant  except  a  nasal or a semi-vowel: @deva[मग्रु] &c.; @deva[वंद्र],  @deva[मशिद्र].  The  @deva[स]  of @deva[मसु]  is  changed to @deva[य] when not  dropped
