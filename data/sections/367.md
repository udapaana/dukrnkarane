## § 367. Two or more of these prepositions may also be

§ 367.    Two or more of these prepositions may also be
combined and prefixed; e. g. @deva[अभिनिविद्चः] ' to enter into with
resolution,' @deva[संमुपगम्] 'to come in close contact with,' &c.
