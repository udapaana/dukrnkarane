## § 907. भविष्यम् and भाव्यम् Used Impersonally

The neuter forms @deva[भविष्यम्] @[bhaviṣyam] and @deva[भाव्यम्] @[bhāvyam] are used impersonally in the sense of 'being,' 'not being' this can be on all probability i. e ' the noun denoting the agent being put in the Instrumental case: @deva[अवश्यतपि कारणेन भविष्यम्] @[avaśyatapi kāraṇena bhaviṣyam] there must be some cause; @deva[नदया संन्यासारम्भा भविष्यम्] @[nadayā saṃnyāsārambhā bhaviṣyam] or @deva[भाव्यम्] @[bhāvyam] in all probability his strength must be corresponding to his sound; @deva[आरोप भवदुपरिष्वरा वैदेहीं] @[āropa bhavadupariṣvarā vaidehīṃ] (in all likelihood) be seated in the carriage, &c.
