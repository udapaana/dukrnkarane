## § 693. Rule 693

§ 693 )

Exceptions .—

(a) @deva[शिङ्] has @deva[शित] when it means 'a part,  a portion,' @deva[शिङ] in
other senses

(b) @deva[विङ्] 6 P. A. takes @deva[त्] in the sense of 'flt for enjoyment or
famous,' @deva[विक्त] ' wealth, property,' @deva[विक्] ' famous '; @deva[विङ्] in other cases.

(c) @deva[भृङ्] , @deva[पूर्] and @deva[चुच्छु] ; @deva[मत्], @deva[पुर्ण] 'filled,' ( also @deva[पुण] when it means
the same as @deva[यु] ), @deva[यूत्].

§ 689. Roots ending in @deva[ऋ]† ( @deva[ऋ], @deva[लृ] and @deva[ओ] changeable to @deva[आ] )
and beginning with a conjunct consonant containing a semi-
vowel also substitute @deva[न्] for @deva[त्]; @deva[हु] ' to run, to sleep,' @deva[प्राण];  @deva[मुळे] ' to
fade,' @deva[मुळात्]; @deva[स्पृशे]-@deva[स्पृशान] 'collected to a mass,' &c

Exceptions :—@deva[ग्या] 'to name,' &c,  @deva[स्तै]  'to contemplate,' @deva[स्तै] and
@deva[द्रै]; @deva[स्थितु], @deva[स्थाण],  @deva[चात] and @deva[हूत].

§ 690.  *The roots given at § 414 and @deva[ज्या] substitute @deva[न्] for @deva[त्].
