## § 656. The affix @deva[य] is added to @deva[लोहित] and some other words

§ 656  The affix @deva[य] is added to @deva[लोहित] and some other words
and to words ending in the affix @deva[आ] ( @deva[शट्] ) and the nominal
base thus formed is conjugated in the Parasm. and Ātmane.;
e g @deva[लोहित]—@deva[लोहितायति]-@deva[ते] ' becomes red. ' @deva[पटवायते] ' utters the
sound Paṭpaṭ.'

     (f) By means of the affix @deva[इङ्] ( @deva[गिङ्] and @deva[गाङ्] )
             and conjuga'ed in Parasm. and Atm.
