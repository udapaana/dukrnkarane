## § 397. There are a few roots which add a penultimate nasal in the Special Tenses and Moods necessarily; e g

§ 397.  There are a few roots which add a penultimate nasal in the Special Tenses and Moods necessarily; e g  @deva[क्षिद्] to cut @deva[चिन्दति], @deva[अह्] to go @deva[अहते], @deva[पिष्] to  roll into a ball @deva[पिण्डति], @deva[छार्] to purify, to go @deva[शुञ्जति] &c; and a few more which do so optionally, e. g  @deva[दुम्] to be firm @deva[चदुति]-@deva[दुदुति]; @deva[घुत्] or @deva[म्लुत्] to go @deva[झागति]-@deva[घुचति], @deva[उपसर्ग]-@deva[रहित]-@deva[मुञ्जति]; @deva[छुत्] to pluck @deva[छुञ्जति], @deva[लुञ्जति] all P; @deva[शु] A. to hum @deva[गुञ्जते], @deva[गुनति]; @deva[यत्]‌ P. to roar @deva[गर्जति], @deva[गजति] and others less important
