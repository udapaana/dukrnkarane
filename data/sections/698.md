## § 698. A radical @deva[इ] preceding or following a vowel is some-

§ 698.  A radical @deva[इ] preceding or following a vowel is some-
times changed to @deva[ऊ] before @deva[त्] or @deva[न्];  when it is preceded by @deva[य] it is
dropped; @deva[इङ्]-@deva[गत], @deva[वुय]; @deva[पुरीत्], @deva[पुर्ङ]-@deva[पूर्ण], @deva[तिर]:-@deva[एप्त], @deva[पि]:-@deva[पूत],
@deva[यून] ( See § 693, c. ).
