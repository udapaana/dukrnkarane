## § 191. The names of fruits, parts of an army, herbs, deer,

§ 191.  The names of fruits, parts of an army, herbs, deer,
birds, insects, corn and grass, form a Samāhāra-Dwandwa,
only when they are taken in the plural;† as @deva[बदराणि च
आमलकानि च बदरामलकम्], but @deva[बदरौ च आमलकौ च बदरामलकै];
@deva[रथिकश्च अश्वारोहश्च रथिकाश्वारोहौ], &c.
