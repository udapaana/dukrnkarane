## § 273. @deva[विभक्त्यलोपे सम्पन्नार्थे लुक्] 

§ 273. @deva[विभक्त्यलोपे सम्पन्नार्थे लुक् ।]

When the case-ending is not dropped, and the compound has the sense of 'accomplishment' or 'completion', the Accusative sign is dropped.

Example:— @deva[सजलम्] 'together with water', @deva[समुद्रम्] 'along with the ocean'.
