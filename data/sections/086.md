§ 86. Vṛddhi ( @deva[औ] ) is substituted for the @deva[औ] of words ending in @deva[औ] in the first five inflections except the Acc. sing ; @deva[आ] is substituted for @deva[औ] in the Acc. sing. and plural * Nouns ending in @deva[ऐ] are regularly declined.
