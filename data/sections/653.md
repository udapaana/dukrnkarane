## § 653. Before this affix the final @deva[अ] of a nominal base is

§ 653.  Before this affix the final @deva[अ] of a nominal base is
lengthened, @deva[अ] remains unchanged, other final letters undergo
the same changes as they do before the other @deva[य] ( @deva[क्यच्] ).  The
final @deva[अ] of a noun is changed to @deva[आ] optionally and that of @deva[अन्तवर्]
