## § 481. @deva[इ] is to be prefixed, or not, or optionally to the terminations given above, according as the root

§ 481.  @deva[इ]  is to be prefixed, or not, or optionally to the terminations given above, according as the  root  is  Set or  Anit  or Wet.  Before the terminations the final vowel and the penultimate short take their Guṇa substitute.
