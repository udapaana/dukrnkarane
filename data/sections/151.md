## § 151. @deva[अन्तर] is a pronoun ( and therefore declined like @deva[सर्व]* m.

§ 151. @deva[अन्तर] is a pronoun ( and therefore declined like @deva[सर्व]* m.
n f ) when it means " exterior, outer; or what is to be worn
out-side, as a garment"* except when followed by @deva[पर] ; it is
optionally declined like nouns ending in @deva[अ] in the Nom. pl. and
Ab. and Loc. sing. of the mas, e. g @deva[अन्तरे अन्तरा वा यदः];
@deva[अन्तरे]-@deva[अन्तरा वा शाट्कः] ( garments ). But @deva[अन्तरायां पुरे].
