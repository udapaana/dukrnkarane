## § 391. Roots of the tenth or @deva[चुरादि] class* add @deva[अय] before the personal terminations. Before @deva[अय] ( I ) the pe

§ 391.  Roots of the tenth or @deva[चुरादि] class* add @deva[अय] before the personal terminations.  Before @deva[अय] ( I ) the penultimate short vowel ( except @deva[अ] ) takes the Guṇa substitute; and the final vowel and the penultimate @deva[अ] not prosodially long, take their Vṛddhi substitute; e. g. @deva[चुर्]+@deva[ति]=@deva[चुर्]+@deva[अय]+@deva[ति]=@deva[चोर्]+@deva[अय]+@deva[ति]=@deva[चोरयति]; @deva[धू]+@deva[अय]+@deva[ति]=@deva[धो]+@deva[अय]+@deva[ति]=@deva[धाव]+@deva[अय]+@deva[ति]=@deva[धावयति]; @deva[ताड्]+@deva[अय]+@deva[ति]=@deva[ताड्]+@deva[अय] +@deva[ति]=@deva[ताडयति]; but @deva[दण्ड्]+@deva[अय]+@deva[ति]=@deva[दण्डयति], &c.
