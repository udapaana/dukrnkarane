## § 141. Indefinite pronouns are formed by the addition

§ 141. Indefinite pronouns are formed by the addition
of @deva[चित्] or @deva[चन] or @deva[अपि] and sometimes of @deva[किंत्] to the various
cases of the pronoun @deva[किम्] in all the genders; e. g. @deva[क]:@deva[श्चित्], @deva[कश्चन]
a certain one, @deva[कोऽपि], @deva[केनापि], @deva[कयाचन], @deva[कर्थापि], @deva[ख्यापित], &c.
