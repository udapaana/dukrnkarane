## § 264. But if the last word of a Bah. compound be a fem. noun ending in @deva[ई] or @deva[ऊ] not capable of being changed

§ 264. But if the last word of a Bah. compound be a fem. noun ending in @deva[ई] or @deva[ऊ] not capable of being changed into @deva[इय] or @deva[उव] before the vowel case termination, or if it ends in @deva[ङ], @deva[क] is necessarily affixed; @deva[ईश्वरः कर्त्र यस्य तद् ईश्वरकर्तृकं जगत्], @deva[बहुनदीको देशः], @deva[रूपवती वधू यस्य स रूपवद्धूकः], &c.; but @deva[सुधी]: @deva[स्त्री] is an exception; @deva[बहुस्त्रीकः], @deva[सस्त्रीकः] &c.
