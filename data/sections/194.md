## § 194. ( a ) When the names of deities well known to be

§ 194.  ( a ) When the names of deities well known to be
in constant association with each other are compounded, @deva[आ] is
substituted for the final vowel of the preceding word, except
in the case of the word @deva[वायु] × ; e. g. @deva[मित्राबरुणौ], @deva[सुर्याचन्द्रमसौ],
@deva[अभ्रामरूतौ], &o. but @deva[आद्रिवायु] or @deva[बाग्मयी].
