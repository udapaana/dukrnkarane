## § 291. @deva[कर्मणि स्थानिनः] 

§ 291. @deva[कर्मणि स्थानिनः ।]

When the object or @deva[कर्मन्] is the first member, @deva[स्थानिन्] (that which occupies a place) is understood.

Example:— @deva[अधिशयनम्] 'in or upon the bed'.
