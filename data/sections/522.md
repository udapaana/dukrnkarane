## § 522. युज् Assumes Form दियुज् After Reduplication

@deva[युज्] @[yuj] after reduplication assumes the form @deva[दियुज्] @[diyuj]; @deva[दियुते] @[diyute], 1 sing., @deva[दियुतिषे] @[diyutiṣe] 2 sing.
