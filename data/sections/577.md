## § 577. Vṛddhi in @deva[ऊर्णु]

The vowel of @deva[ऊर्णु] takes Vṛddhi optionally in the Parasm. i.e. optionally takes Guṇa substitute; also it optionally remains unchanged before @deva[ह].
