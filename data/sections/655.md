# § 655

The following are the different senses in which the affix @deva[क्यच्] is used in the particular cases given below.

## (a) Desirous of Committing Sin

It is added to @deva[सत्र], @deva[कट], @deva[इच्छु] and @deva[नहन्] in the sense of 'desirous of committing sin':

- @deva[पाप चिकीर्षति—सत्रायते], @deva[कटायते], &c.

Added to @deva[कट] it has also the sense of 'prompt in': @deva[कटाय फमौ—कटायते] (@deva[पाप कदुयुस्तह इत्यर्थः], Sid. Kan.).

## (b) Ruminating

To @deva[रोसन्य] used objectively: @deva[रोसन्यायते] 'is ruminating.'

## (c) Sending Forth, Vomiting

To @deva[वाष्] 'tears,' @deva[ऊष्मन्] 'heat,' and @deva[फेन्], in the sense of 'sending forth, vomiting':

- @deva[वाष्पायते] 'he sheds tears'
- @deva[उष्मायते] 'gives out heat'
- @deva[फेनायते] 'sends forth foam'
