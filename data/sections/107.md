## § 107. The neuter forms are @deva[प्राक्] in nom., voc., and acc.

§ 107. The neuter forms are @deva[प्राक्] in nom., voc., and acc.
sing., like @deva[स्वदृष्ट] and @deva[प्राञ्ची] in nom., voc., and acc. pl. like
@deva[वारि] ( @deva[वा०] II. 4. 4 ; Pan. VI. 1. 96, VII. 1. 70 ). Other forms
are similar to those of the masculine. The forms of fem. and
neuter of @deva[सम्यञ्च्] &c. can be formed by the student himself.

                Bases ending in @deva[त्] or @deva[द् ॥]
