## § 681. The affix @deva[त्] is weak.

§ 681.  The affix @deva[त्] is weak.

Exceptions:—

(a) The roots @deva[नी], @deva[विद्य] 1 cl., @deva[दिश्] , @deva[विष्टर्] , @deva[प्रुष्] and @deva[भृष्] admit of
the Guṇa change before @deva[त्] when @deva[इ] is prefixed to it; @deva[यु ।]. A  also
