## § 932. Particle @deva[स्म] @[sma] with Different Tenses

With the particle @deva[स्म] @[sma] not joined with @deva[स्यु] @[syu], the Aorist, the Imperfect, the Perfect or the Present may be used:

**Example:**

- @deva[युनुयुङुनुयू युयूयु यवूदूरूयूदूयूर्युयुर्युयुनै] @[yunuyuṅunuyū yuyūyu yavūdūrūyūdūyūryuyuryuyunai] (Sid. Kau.) here formerly dwelt pupils

But when @deva[स्म] @[sma] is used with @deva[स्यु] @[syu], the Present alone can be used:

- @deva[युयूनुनै स्यु पुयुरु] @[yuyūnunai syu puyuru] he formerly sacrificed
