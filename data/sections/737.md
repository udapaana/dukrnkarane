## § 737. ( a ) The roots @deva[यञ्च्] , @deva[याच्] , @deva[रुच्] , @deva[प्रवुच्] , @deva[स्पृश्] , and @deva[एच्]

§ 737.  ( a ) The roots  @deva[यञ्च्] ,  @deva[याच्] ,  @deva[रुच्] ,  @deva[प्रवुच्] , @deva[स्पृश्] ,  and @deva[एच्]
do not change  their  @deva[चु]  or  @deva[ऋ]  to  @deva[ऋ]  or  @deva[यु]  before  @deva[घ्यत्] ;  @deva[शाय्युं
यात्युं]; @deva[रोच्यं] , @deva[प्रवत्युयुं] ( @deva[प्रत्यशितोर्य]: ), @deva[ज्ञेस्युं] , @deva[स्पश्युं] , @deva[गात्युं].

( b ) @deva[इन्धु] does not change  its  @deva[चु]  to  @deva[ऋ]  before  @deva[घ्यत्] when the
meaning is ' what ought  to  be spoken out,  speech, '  @deva[वाच्युं]; but
@deva[वाक्युं]; a sentence.'

( c ) @deva[पञ्च्] when it means 'to go' does not change @deva[चु] its to
@deva[ऋ] :  @deva[पञ्चुर्युं] ;  when it  means  ' to bend'  the @deva[चु]  is  changed  to
@deva[ऋ] :  @deva[पञ्कुवुं शरीरुम्]

( d ) @deva[सू युज्] when it is preceded by @deva[इु]  and  @deva[हि],  takes  the affix @deva[एयत्]
in  the sense of  'what  is  possible or capable of;'  and  does
not  change  its  @deva[ऋ]  to  @deva[यु] ;  @deva[प्रयुङ्कुर्   शक्युं  प्रयोज्युं];  @deva[तिवौर्ङुकुर्   शक्युं]:
@deva[तिवौज्युं]: @deva[शुङुय]: !

( e ) @deva[भुज्] has  @deva[भोजुं]  meaning  ' food, '  and  @deva[भोज्युं]  'what is fit
to be enjoyed.'
