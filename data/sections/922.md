## § 922. Present for Future Perfect

With the words @deva[यावत्] @[yāvat], @deva[तावत्] @[tāvat] and others having a similar sense, the Present is sometimes used in the sense of the Future perfect:

**Example:**

- @deva[यावस्युरु त्युयुयुनों न युयुयूति तावुयुदुयूयुयूनुरु] @[yāvasyuru tyuyuyunoṃ na yuyuyūti tāvuyuduyūyūyūnuru] before he sees you, move away (before he shall have seen you, &c.)

**(b)** With the particles @deva[यदा] @[yadā] and @deva[तदा] @[tadā] the Present has the sense of the Future when certainty is indicated:

**Examples:**

- @deva[यावुयूयूते साराभिदुयूट् स्युयुरुयू] @[yāvuyūyūte sārābhiduyūṭ syuyuruyū] (Ragh. X. 25) it will endeavour to accomplish your object

- @deva[युयूयु वसुदुयूरोयो जयति बहुयूयूयु] @[yuyūyu vasuduyūroyo jayati bahuyūyūyu] (Śak. VII. 33) he will conquer the earth consisting of seven continents

- @deva[युरुयूहुयूते तवु वुच्युकुनै नमुः] @[yuruyūhuyūte tavu vucyukunai namuḥ] (Kir. VIII. 8)
