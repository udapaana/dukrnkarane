## § 651. The penultimate @deva[अ] of a noun ending in a nasal is

§ 651.  The penultimate @deva[अ]  of a noun ending in a nasal is
lengthened,  The base is treated like a root of the last gana-
nation , its last vowel only taking Guṇa before @deva[त] &c ; ( termina-
