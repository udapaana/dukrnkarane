## § 773. *A root may be used in its gerundive form in @deva[अम्]

§ 773.  *A  root may  be used  in its gerundive  form  in  @deva[अम्]
with its object prefixed to it if that object be a part of the body
which can be severed without fatal effects;  @deva[भुहुनोर्युं  कुषुयति] ' nar-
rates contracting the eyebrows, but  @deva[हिमुरं नीदुदुयुरु] ( not @deva[हिंस्राहुयुरु] )
as the severance of the head would cause death.

( a ) Similarly when a part of  the  body  is  completely  hurt
or pressed in the action,  the  gerund  in  @deva[अम्]  is  used  with  that
part; @deva[डुर]-@deva[गुतितुर्युं  युष्युन्नै]  i.  e.  @deva[कुलुसुयुदुर]:  @deva[पीडुयुन्नै]:  ' afflicting  the
whole bosom;' @deva[उद्गीनिंहिंसुवातं त्रुन्हिरिषु ग्राह्युं अनुर्युं]:
