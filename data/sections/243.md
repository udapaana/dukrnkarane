§ 243. † @deva[अष्टन्] becomes @deva[अष्टा] when followed by @deva[कपाल] and @deva[गौ] and the compound so formed conveys the sense of 'an oblation' and 'yoked to' respectively; @deva[अष्टाकपालः] 'prepared or offered in eight pans' (@deva[पुरोडाशः]); @deva[अष्टागवं] (@deva[शकटम्]) 'a carriage with eight bullocks yoked to it'.
