## § 413. Root गृह् - न Changed to ह्

The @deva[न] @[na] of @deva[गृह्] @[gṛh] is changed to @deva[ह्] @[h].

e.g. @deva[गृह्णाति] @[gṛhṇāti]; @deva[अगृह्णाम्] @[agṛhṇām], @deva[अगृह्णीत] @[agṛhṇīta], @deva[अगृह्णीम] @[agṛhṇīma], &c. Imperf. 1st. pers.
