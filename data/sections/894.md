## § 894. The roots @deva[शायु] and @deva[स्युयु] are generally used with present

§ 894. The roots @deva[शायु] and @deva[स्युयु] are generally used with present
participles to show the continuity of the action denoted by
them; @deva[पशून् वधं कुन्युच् अायुसे] used to kill (always kept on killing)
animals; @deva[तं युतियुयूट्युयुट् तस्युयुयो] remained waiting for him.
