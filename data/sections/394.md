§ 394. * The @deva[ऋ] ( long ) of a root, penultimate or final, when it does not take Guṇa or Vṛddhi, is changed to @deva[इर्], and to @deva[उर्] if a labial or @deva[र्] precedes; and the @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] is lengthened when @deva[इर्] or @deva[उर्] is followed by a consonant; e. g. @deva[कृ] 4. P. 'to become old,' @deva[कीर्यति]; @deva[भ्रंशते], &c; @deva[हृ] 6. P  @deva[क्षिपति], @deva[भिक्षते], &c; here @deva[क्रि] does not become @deva[दीर्घ] as @deva[क्रि] is followed by @deva[अ]; @deva[चुर्] 10. P. As. @deva[कोरयति]; @deva[अकोरयत्], @deva[अकोरीत्], &c
