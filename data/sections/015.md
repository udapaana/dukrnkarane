## § 15. @deva[गुण] @[guṇa] and @deva[वृद्धि] @[vṛddhi]

The vowels @deva[अ, ए, ओ] @[a, e, o] and the syllables @deva[अर] @[ar] and @deva[अल्] @[al] are called @deva[गुण] @[guṇa]; the vowels @deva[आ, ऐ, औ] @[ā, ai, au] and the syllables @deva[आर] @[ār] and @deva[आल्] @[āl] are called @deva[वृद्धि] @[vṛddhi].[^1] The @deva[गुण] @[guṇa] and @deva[वृद्धि] @[vṛddhi] vowels and syllables that are substituted for the simple vowels will appear from the following table:

| Simple V. | @deva[अ] @[a] | @deva[इ] @[i] & @deva[ई] @[ī] | @deva[उ] @[u] & @deva[ऊ] @[ū] | @deva[ऋ] @[ṛ] & @deva[ॠ] @[ṝ] | @deva[ऌ] @[ḷ] |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|---------------|
| @deva[गुण] @[guṇa] | @deva[अ] @[a] | @deva[ए] @[e] | @deva[ओ] @[o] | @deva[अर] @[ar] | @deva[अल्] @[al] |
| @deva[वृद्धि] @[vṛddhi] | @deva[आ] @[ā] | @deva[ऐ] @[ai] | @deva[औ] @[au] | @deva[आर] @[ār] | @deva[आल्] @[āl] |

[^1]: @deva[वृद्धिरादैच् । अदेङ्गुणः । उरण् रपरः] @[vṛddhirādaic. adeṅguṇaḥ. uraṇ raparaḥ] Pāṇ. I. 1. 2; I. 1. 51.
