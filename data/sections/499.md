## § 499. Guṇa is substituted for the final vowel of roots ending @deva[इ अ] preceded by a conjunct consonant,

§ 499.  Guṇa is substituted for the final vowel of roots ending  @deva[इ अ] preceded  by  a  conjunct  consonant,  and  in  long  @deva[अ] and of the roots @deva[अ]. @deva[चच्छु]  and  @deva[जाग्]; as @deva[तस्तरिव] 1 dual of @deva[स्यु], &c. @deva[इ], 1 dual of @deva[गा]; @deva[विधविव] 2 sing.  of @deva[विध्]; @deva[रुरुव] 1st dual of @deva[रु]; @deva[सूषुप्व], @deva[शुश्रुविव] 1 dual of @deva[सू], &c weak terminations. and shorten their vowel  when  they do not; as @deva[शासरिव], @deva[सासविव] 1 dual of @deva[शु], &c.
