## § 889. The sense of the comparative and the superlative may

§ 889. The sense of the comparative and the superlative may
also be expressed by the particular case used; @deva[अस्युयु दुदुयुं यायुयुयुयुयुनुकु]-
@deva[ठिनं] his heart is harder than stone; @deva[छुयुयुयूयुर्णों छुयुनैदुट् वा चैदुयु]: @deva[युयुयुदुट्]:
Chaitra is the cleverest of all students.
