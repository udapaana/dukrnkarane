## § 821. ‡The roots @deva[स्तुयुयुष्] 'to praise,' @deva[गुट्] 'to hide,' @deva[स्था] 'to stand,'

§ 821.  ‡The roots @deva[स्तुयुयुष्]  'to  praise,'  @deva[गुट्]  'to hide,'  @deva[स्था]  'to stand,'
and @deva[मुयु]  'to  swear,'  govern  the  Dative  of the person to whom
some  object  or  feeling  is to  be conveyed,  @deva[गोर्नों  स्युसुयुद्  कृयुयुयुयु
श्युयुरुते]-@deva[नूनं]-@deva[कृष्णुं]-@deva[स्तुरीयु] ' a  cowherd;  else  Kṛiṣhṇa  instilled  courage  into
flatters  'Kṛṣhṇa  or  apparently  conceals  her  feeling  from  him
or  waits  for  him  or  swears  before  him;'  (Vide  Bhatti.  VII.  73.
74,  );  but @deva[रानानं स्तुयुयुते  मन्त्रो]  'a  minister  praises his  king.'
