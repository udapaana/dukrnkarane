## § 585. Final @deva[आ] Changed to @deva[य

वा], @deva[धा] and roots assuming these forms, @deva[सा], @deva[स्था], @deva[यै], @deva[पा] 'to drink', @deva[हा] 'to abandon' and @deva[सौ], change their final to @deva[य].
