## § 726. ( a ) @deva[भू] when it has a Subanta prefixed to it and is

§ 726.  ( a ) @deva[भू] when it has  a  Subanta  prefixed  to  it and is
without a preposition takes the affix  @deva[क्यप्] impersonally;  @deva[ब्रह्मणौ
भाव]: @deva[ब्रह्मभूयं] 'identity with Brahman.' When no Subanta is used
prepositionally with it, it takes the affix @deva[यत्] ; @deva[भव्य]  or @deva[भूयम्].
