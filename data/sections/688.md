## § 688. @deva[नु]*is substituted for @deva[त्] when it immediately follows a

§ 688  @deva[नु]*is substituted for @deva[त्] when it immediately follows a
final @deva[इ] or @deva[ऋ] ; this final @deva[इ] is also changed to @deva[ञ]: @deva[शिङ्]-@deva[निग], @deva[यू]-@deva[शुनौ];
@deva[पुर्ङ]-@deva[पूर्ण] ( See § 693 )

Exceptions .—

(a) @deva[शिङ्] has @deva[शित] when it means 'a part,  a portion,' @deva[शिङ] in
other senses

(b) @deva[विङ्] 6 P. A. takes @deva[त्] in the sense of 'flt for enjoyment or
famous,' @deva[विक्त] ' wealth, property,' @deva[विक्] ' famous '; @deva[विङ्] in other cases.

(c) @deva[भृङ्] , @deva[पूर्] and @deva[चुच्छु] ; @deva[मत्], @deva[पुर्ण] 'filled,' ( also @deva[पुण] when it means
the same as @deva[यु] ), @deva[यूत्].
