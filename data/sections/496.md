## § 496. When initial @deva[इ] or @deva[य] of a root takes Guṇa or Vṛddhi substitute, the reduplicative @deva[इ] or @deva[य] is change

§ 496.  When initial @deva[इ] or @deva[य] of a root takes  Guṇa or Vṛddhi substitute, the reduplicative @deva[इ] or @deva[य]  is  changed  to  @deva[इय]  or  @deva[उय]; otherwise the two  vowels  combine  to  form  long  @deva[इ]  or  @deva[उ]; as @deva[इयय्]+@deva[अ]=@deva[इयेय], @deva[उच्चोय्]+@deva[अ]=@deva[उचोय]; @deva[इविध्], &c.
