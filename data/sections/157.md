## § 157. The pronominal adverbs that are most commonly

§ 157. The pronominal adverbs that are most commonly
met with are derived from the pronouns @deva[तद्] , @deva[एतद्] , @deva[यद्] , @deva[इदम्] ,
@deva[किम्] , and @deva[सर्व] and the pronominal adjectives @deva[पूर्व], @deva[पर] &c. by means
of such affixes as @deva[तस्] , @deva[त्र], *@deva[द], @deva[क्] &c. added in the sense of the
Ab. or the Loc., @deva[द्वा] , @deva[द्वानीम्] , @deva[द्वि] &c. showing time, @deva[ताद्] , † showing
point of time, place or direction, @deva[आ], @deva[आत् आहि] &c. expressing
direction, @deva[था], @deva[थ] + &c. expressing way or manner &c. ; e g.
