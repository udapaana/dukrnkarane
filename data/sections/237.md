## § 237. Some upapadas such as @deva[उञ्छे], @deva[नीचै], @deva[तिर्यक्], @deva[दृसतः], &c. also optionally form compounds with the verba

§ 237. Some upapadas such as @deva[उञ्छे], @deva[नीचै], @deva[तिर्यक्], @deva[दृसतः], &c. also optionally form compounds with the verbal indeclinable in @deva[त्वा] (changeable to @deva[य]); as @deva[उञ्छेःछत्य] (or @deva[उञ्छेः कृत्वा]), @deva[तिर्यक्छत्य], @deva[दृसतोयस्य], @deva[मानाछत्य], @deva[एकथासूरः], &c. For further particulars vide the Chapter on verbal derivatives
