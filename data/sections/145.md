## § 145. Reciprocity is expressed by the repetition of the

§ 145. Reciprocity is expressed by the repetition of the
pronominal adjectives @deva[अन्य], @deva[इतर] and @deva[पर]; e. g. @deva[अन्योन्य], @deva[इतरेतर],
and @deva[परस्पर]. These are generally used in the sing. and also
advertially e. g. @deva[परस्परेण स्पृहणीयदोभर्म] &c. ( Rag. VII. 14);
@deva[परस्परं विबद्धन्ते] &c. They are more commonly used as the first
members of compounds; as @deva[अन्योन्यमोभाजनवाद् बुधुत] &c. (Ku.
I, 44, @deva[इतरेतरयोगाः] (Sis. X. 24.) &c.

        9- -Possessive Pronouns.
