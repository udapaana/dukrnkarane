§ 261. *The words @deva[उरस्], @deva[सर्पिस्], @deva[डुपानह्], @deva[दाधि], @deva[मधु], @deva[ज्ञालि]-@deva[ही] and @deva[धूर्], @deva[अनडुह्], @deva[पयस्], @deva[नौ] and @deva[लुरुसी] when used in the singular, have a @deva[क] added to them, when they form the latter member of a Bah. comp.; @deva[उध्वं] (expanded, well-developed) @deva[डुरः यस्य उध्वोरस्कः]; @deva[प्रियसर्पिष्कः] 'one fond of ghee', &c. @deva[एकः एषाद् यस्य अर्तौ एकाषुर्कः] 'having one man only', &c; when @deva[धूर्] and the words following it are used in the dual and the plural they take @deva[क] optionally. @deva[द्विड्माद्] or @deva[द्विड्मकः] &c.

(a) @deva[अर्थ] takes the final @deva[क], necessarily when preceded by @deva[अन्] and optionally in any other case; @deva[अनर्थकः] 'useless'; but @deva[अपृथंयर्थं] 'meaningless' @deva[वच].
