## § 765. This gerund or the one in @deva[स्या] is used with the words

§ 765.  This gerund or the one in @deva[स्या]  is used with the  words
@deva[अचे], @deva[पुयुं] and  @deva[पुञ्चं],  used  as prepositions, although the notion of
repetition is not  present in  these compounds; @deva[अत्रुभैसुख्वुव्युत्वा हा
स्त्रौयौ] ' having taken to goes out;' so @deva[यथायुगोन] or @deva[पुञ्चवा यृडु]-
@deva[भोरिते] or @deva[पुञ्चवा बुद्धुते].
