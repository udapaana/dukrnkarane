## § 570. @deva[गम्], @deva[यम्] Dropping Nasal

@deva[गम्], and @deva[यम्] with @deva[उप] 'to marry,' optionally drop their nasal, when they take the Atm. terminations. @deva[यम्] when it means 'to give out' necessarily drops its nasal.
