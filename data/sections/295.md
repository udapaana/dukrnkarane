## § 295. @deva[उत्तमाधममध्यमाः सुदुःकुल्युत्तरपदे] 

§ 295. @deva[उत्तमाधममध्यमाः सुदुःकुल्युत्तरपदे ।]

The words @deva[उत्तम] 'best', @deva[अधम] 'lowest', and @deva[मध्यम] 'middle' become @deva[सु], @deva[दुः], @deva[कु], and @deva[उत्तर] when used as the latter member of a compound.

Examples:— @deva[सत्सुः] 'very good', @deva[सद्दुः] 'very bad', @deva[सत्कुः] 'middling', @deva[सदुत्तरः] 'better'.

Obs. These substitutions occur only in special contexts, and @deva[उत्तर] substitutes for @deva[उत्तम] in comparative sense.
