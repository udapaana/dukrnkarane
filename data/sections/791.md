## § 791. When an adjective is preceded by a word used in

§ 791.  When an adjective is preceded  by  a  word  used  in
the sense  of 'a certain' or when it qualifies an  adjective  used
as a noun, it is treated as a substantive and generally put in the
neuter;  @deva[किंचिद्रूपम्] something beautiful,  @deva[किंचिद् गम्भीरम्] some-
thing deep, @deva[अल्पं गुरु] something important,  @deva[दृढतरम्] something very
hard.

(a) But such expressions as  @deva[सुरूपोऽयं],  @deva[दृढरूपोऽयं]  are also
used.
