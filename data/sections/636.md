## § 636. Insertion of र् or ल् in Parasmaipada Frequentative

@deva[र्] @[r] or the syllable @deva[रि] @[ri] or @deva[री] @[rī] is inserted between the @deva[अ] @[a] of the reduplicative syllable and the radical consonant of roots ending in short @deva[अ] @[a] or having it for their penultimate; similarly @deva[ल्] @[l] or @deva[लि] @[li] or @deva[ली] @[lī] is inserted in the case of @deva[कृप्] @[kṛp]; @deva[उत्=उरुत्-वरुत्] @[ut=urut-varut] or @deva[वरिवृत्] @[varivṛt] or @deva[वरीवृत्] @[varīvṛt]; @deva[छद्-चछद्] @[chad-cacad], or @deva[चरिछद्] @[carichchad] or @deva[चरीछद्] @[carīchad]; @deva[कृप्-चकृप्] @[kṛp-cakṛp], or @deva[चलिकृप्] @[calikṛp] @deva[चलीकृप्] @[calīkṛp]; @deva[टुप्-दुटुप्] @[ṭup-duṭup], @deva[दरिटुप्] @[dariṭup] or @deva[दरीटुप्] @[darīṭup].
