## § 815. * A characteristic attribute indicative of the existence

§ 815.  * A  characteristic  attribute indicative of the existence
of a particular  ste'e  or  condition  is  put  in  the  Instrumental;
@deva[जटाभिः तापसः] he is an  ascetic  (which is  apparent)  from  his
matted hair ( @deva[जटाभिः]=@deva[प्रकृतितोद्भूतुर्यैजुट्युभिः] Sid. Kan. ).
