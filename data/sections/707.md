## § 707. Present Tense Sense of P. P. Participles

The P. P. Participles of roots meaning 'to think or to wish,' 'to know,' 'to adore,' and of the roots @deva[मान्] @[mān], @deva[भा] @[bhā], etc. have the sense of the present Tense:

- @deva[राज्ञा मतः] @[rājñā mataḥ]: 'he is honoured by the king'
- @deva[सरित् इध्यते अग्निः पदम्] @[sarit idhyate agniḥ padam]: 'the root is kindled'

So also @deva[दीप्त] @[dīpta], etc.
