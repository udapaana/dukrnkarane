## § 296. @deva[न त्रिषु सप्तमीप्रतिरूपकाधिकरणे ॥]

§ 296. @deva[न त्रिषु सप्तमीप्रतिरूपकाधिकरणे ॥]

These substitutions (@deva[सु], @deva[दुः], @deva[कु]) do not occur when the first member is in the Locative case denoting @deva[प्रतिरूप] (resemblance) or @deva[अधिकरण] (locus).

Example:— @deva[यथाक्षौद्रम्] 'like honey', @deva[अधिहरि] 'over Hari' - here we do not substitute @deva[सु] etc.
