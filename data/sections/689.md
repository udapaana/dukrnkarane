## § 689. Roots ending in @deva[ऋ]† ( @deva[ऋ], @deva[लृ] and @deva[ओ] changeable to @deva[आ] )

§ 689. Roots ending in @deva[ऋ]† ( @deva[ऋ], @deva[लृ] and @deva[ओ] changeable to @deva[आ] )
and beginning with a conjunct consonant containing a semi-
vowel also substitute @deva[न्] for @deva[त्]; @deva[हु] ' to run, to sleep,' @deva[प्राण];  @deva[मुळे] ' to
fade,' @deva[मुळात्]; @deva[स्पृशे]-@deva[स्पृशान] 'collected to a mass,' &c

Exceptions :—@deva[ग्या] 'to name,' &c,  @deva[स्तै]  'to contemplate,' @deva[स्तै] and
@deva[द्रै]; @deva[स्थितु], @deva[स्थाण],  @deva[चात] and @deva[हूत].
