## § 588. Irregular Benedictives

@deva[ई] 'to go'—@deva[ईयासम्]; but shortens its @deva[ई] after a preposition; @deva[अयियासम्]; Atm. @deva[एषीय]. @deva[ऊह्] also, when joined with prepositions shortens its @deva[ऊ] before weak terminations beginning with @deva[य]; @deva[समुह्यासम्].
