## § 590. Three Kinds of Passive Verbs

There are three kinds of Passive Verbs: (1) the Passive properly so called (@deva[कर्मणि प्र०]), (2) an Impersonal Passive (@deva[भावे प्र०]), and (3) a Reflexive (@deva[कर्मकर्तरि प्र०]).
