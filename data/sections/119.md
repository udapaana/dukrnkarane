## § 119. ( a ) In the nom. sing. of the fem. bases ending in

§ 119. ( a ) In the nom. sing. of the fem. bases ending in
@deva[ऋ] the substitution of @deva[सवर्णदीर्घ] is optional, so @deva[स्वसृ] makes @deva[स्वसा] or
@deva[स्वसृ]; and in the acc., nom., voc., and acc. dual @deva[र्] is changed to
anusvāra regularly, and we get @deva[स्वसारम्], @deva[स्वसारौ]; @deva[मातरम्], @deva[मातरौ] &c.
So also before the case-termination of the gen. and loc. dual @deva[र्]
is changed to anusvāra, and we get @deva[पित्रोः], @deva[मात्रोः], @deva[स्वस्रोः ॥]
