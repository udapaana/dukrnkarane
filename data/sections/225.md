## § 225. Double Adjective and Participle Karmadhāraya

Two adjectives may also be compounded into what is called the @deva[विशेषणोभयपदकर्मधारयः] @[viśeṣaṇobhayapadakarmadhārayaḥ] as @deva[युवकृष्णः] @[yuvakṛṣṇaḥ], @deva[सुरुणसरस्वरः] @[suruṇasarasvaraḥ].

(a) Two participles expressive of two consecutive actions of the same individual may be compounded together, the one expressive of the previous action being placed first;[^1] @deva[आदौ स्नातः पश्चात् अनुलिप्तः] @[ādau snātaḥ paścāt anuliptaḥ] @deva[स्नातानुलिप्तः] @[snātānuliptaḥ] 'first bathed and then anointed'; similarly @deva[पीतोद्वान्तम्] @[pītodvāntam] 'first drunk and afterwards vomited out', @deva[पीतप्रतिबद्धः] @[pītapratibaddhaḥ]; @deva[यथावितप्रतिष्ठक्तः] @[yathāvitapratiṣṭhaktaḥ] (Rag. II.1, IV.43), etc.

(b) In the case of the two sets of words given above (§ 222, g.) forming compounds with one another those beginning with @deva[पूर्व] @[pūrva] are placed first. In the case of @deva[एक] @[eka] compounded with @deva[वीर] @[vīra] it may precede or follow it; as @deva[वीरैकः] @[vīraikaḥ] or @deva[एकवीरः] @[ekavīraḥ]; the former, however, is preferable. In the case of the first set of words viz. @deva[एक] @[eka]...@deva[केवल] @[kevala] the one succeeding precedes the one preceding when compounded together; as @deva[पुराणजरद्] @[purāṇajarad], @deva[केवलसर्वम्] @[kevalasarvam], etc.

(c) A past participle is compounded with another with the negative particle prefixed to it;[^2] '@deva[कृताकृतम्] @[kṛtākṛtam],' what is done and not done, i.e. 'partially done'.

(d) The word @deva[युवन्] @[yuvan] (m. f.) enters into a Karm. comp. with the words @deva[खलित] @[khalita], @deva[पलित] @[palita], @deva[वलित] @[valita] having wrinkles, and @deva[जरती] @[jaratī], and is placed first;[^3] i.e. @deva[युवा] @[yuvā] + @deva[खलितः] @[khalitaḥ] @deva[युवखलितः] @[yuvakhalitaḥ] 'a young bald-headed man'; @deva[स्त्री] @[strī] 'a young bald-headed woman'; @deva[युवजरती] @[yuvajaratī] 'a young woman looking old'; @deva[युवपलितः] @[yuvpalitaḥ] 'a young man whose hair is grey', etc.

[^2]: @deva[येन नञ्चतुरितैर् अन्त्र] @[yena nañcaturitair antra] Pāṇ. II.1.60.
[^3]: @deva[युवाखलितपलितवलितजरतीभिः] @[yuvākhalitapalitavalitajaratībhiḥ] Pāṇ. II.1.67.
