## § 568. Irregular Aorist - @deva[दा], @deva[धा], @deva[स्था

दा], @deva[धा], and roots assuming the forms of @deva[दा] and @deva[धा] and @deva[स्था] substitute @deva[इ] for their final vowel in the Atm. This @deva[इ] does not take its Guṇa substitute.
