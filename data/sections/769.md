## § 769. ¶ With the words @deva[वुसुद्], @deva[अक्षत] and @deva[जीव] are used res-

§ 769.  ¶ With the words  @deva[वुसुद्],  @deva[अक्षत]  and @deva[जीव] are used res-
pectively the gerunds in @deva[अम्] of the roots @deva[हुन्] , @deva[छु] and  @deva[शुह्] in a cog-
nate sense;  @deva[वुसुच्छुवातं इत्युते] 'kills destroying the roots i  e  totally,'
@deva[अक्षुतकुरुते करोति]  'does  what  ought not to have been done;'  @deva[जीवुचाहै
यहाति] ' captures him so as to preserve him i  e. alive.'
