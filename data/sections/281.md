## § 281. @deva[विशेषणञ्च] 

§ 281. @deva[विशेषणञ्च ।]

And an adjective (used as the first member) must also be in the Singular.

Example:— @deva[यथामहत्] 'according to greatness' (not @deva[यथामहान्ति]).
