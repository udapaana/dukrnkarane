615. There are a few primitive roots, which, though they take a Desiderative form, do not convey a desiderative sense (See § 39. b). As has been looked upon as primitive roots, the Desiderative bases may be derived from them according to the rules given below; as @deva[जुगुप्सिषत] 'he wishes to censure' from @deva[जुगुप्स्] &c.
