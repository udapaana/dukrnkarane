## § 618. Vowel Changes before the Desiderative Affix

The radical vowels undergo the following changes before @deva[स] @[sa]—

**(a)** The @deva[स] @[sa] with @deva[इ] @[i] is strong and without @deva[इ] @[i] weak.

The usual rule as to Guṇa substitute holds good in the one case and does not in the other; @deva[इष्-विदत्सिते] @[iṣ-vidatsite], @deva[विदत्स] @[vidatsa]; @deva[इ-विपत्सि] @[i-vipatsi], &c.

**(b)** The final @deva[ऋ] @[ṛ] and @deva[उ] @[u] and the penultimate @deva[अ] @[a] of @deva[इष्] @[iṣ] and @deva[मृ] @[mṛ] (substituted for @deva[म्] @[m] 2 Par. 'to go,' and for @deva[स्] @[s] with @deva[अधि] @[adhi] 'to remember' or 'to study') are lengthened and final @deva[अ] @[a], short or long, changed to @deva[ई] @[ī], or to @deva[उ] @[u] after labials or @deva[य] @[ya] when the @deva[स] @[sa] is unaugmented; @deva[जि-जिगीष] @[ji-jigīṣ], @deva[इ-इयुष] @[i-iyuṣ], @deva[छ-चिकीर्ष] @[cha-cikīrṣ], @deva[नृ-तितीर्ष] @[nṛ-titīrṣ], @deva[प्र-धरर्ष] @[pra-dhararṣ], @deva[प्र-उपर्ष] @[pra-uparṣ], &c.

**(c)** The roots @deva[रुह्] @[ruh], @deva[विद्] @[vid] and @deva[द्रप्] @[drap] do not change their vowel to Guṇa; and the roots @deva[ग्रह्] @[grah], @deva[स्वप्] @[svap] and @deva[प्रच्छ] @[prachcha] take Samprasāraṇa; @deva[रुरुहिष] @[ruruhiṣ], @deva[विविदिष] @[vividiṣ], @deva[दुद्रपिष] @[dudrapiṣ], @deva[जिघृष] @[jighṛṣ], @deva[सुषुप्स] @[suṣups], @deva[पिपृच्छिष] @[pipṛcchiṣ].

**(d)** Roots with an initial consonant, and having @deva[इ] @[i] or @deva[उ] @[u] short for their penultimate, and ending in any consonant, except @deva[न्] @[n] or @deva[म्] @[m], change their vowel to Guṇa optionally when @deva[इ] @[i] is prefixed to @deva[स] @[sa]; @deva[उप-दिघृतिष] @[upa-dighṛtiṣ], @deva[दिघोतिष] @[dighotiṣ]; @deva[उद्-दुद्रविष] @[ud-dudraviṣ] or @deva[दुद्रोविष] @[duddroviṣ] &c.
