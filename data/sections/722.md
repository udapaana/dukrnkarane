§ 722.  @deva[हत्] takes the affix @deva[यत्]  optionally,  before which @deva[युष्] is
substituted  for  it;  @deva[हत्]+@deva[य]=@deva[यष्य]='what  ought  to  be  killed,'
it  optionally  takes  @deva[एयत्] ,  in  which  case  @deva[यात्]  is  substituted
