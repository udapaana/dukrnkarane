## § 486. In the case of @deva[इ] with @deva[आग्नि], @deva[गा] is optionally substituted for @deva[इ] in the Conditional and the Aoris

§ 486.  In the case of @deva[इ] with @deva[आग्नि], @deva[गा] is  optionally  substituted for @deva[इ] in the Conditional  and  the Aorist. @deva[इ] is substituted for the final vowel of the verbs @deva[चा] 3 P. A  1 P, @deva[प्रा], @deva[दो], @deva[ते], @deva[घे], @deva[मा], @deva[स्या], @deva[भा] ( substituted for @deva[इ] 2 P  and @deva[स्था] with @deva[आग्नि] , ) @deva[म्रा], @deva[मा], and @deva[को] before a  consonantal  weak  termination.  All  terminations added to @deva[मा] substituted for @deva[इ] are weak

Paradigms.

1. @deva[अध्येषे      अध्येयाव्हि      अध्येयामहि]
2. @deva[अध्येयथाः   अध्येयाथाम्     अध्येयध्वम्]
3. @deva[अध्येष्ट     अध्येयाताम्     अध्येष्यन्त]
1. @deva[अध्यागीष्ये  अध्यागीष्याव्हि   अध्यागीष्यामहि]
2. @deva[अध्यागीष्यथाः अध्यागीष्याथाम्  अध्यागीष्यध्वम्]
3. @deva[अध्यागीष्यत  अध्यागीष्याताम्  अध्यागीष्यन्त]
