## § 140. The sense of the reflexive pronoun is, in Sanskrit,

§ 140. The sense of the reflexive pronoun is, in Sanskrit,
expressed by the word @deva[आत्मन्] 'self' which is invariably used
in the masculine gender and singular number, and by the
reflexive adverb @deva[स्वयम्] e. g. @deva[यत्र दुर्योगात्मानं मया स्वयं वाह्मने]:
"they all (the wives of Das'aratha ) saw in dreams that they
were guarded by dwarfs;" so @deva[म] or @deva[सा छलाप्राप्तिम् आत्मानम्]-
@deva[गच्छति । राजा स्वयं समरमुपि जगाम], &c
