§ 498.  Roots ending in @deva[आ] take @deva[ओ]  instead  of  @deva[अ]  in  the  1st and 3rd per. sing and drop their final  @deva[आ]  before  vowel  weak terminations, and before such as  take the  augment  @deva[इ]; as  @deva[ददौ]. @deva[द्विव], @deva[द्विव] 1st Per. @deva[ददौ]; @deva[द्विव] 2. Per. sing. of @deva[दा].
