## § 402. Roots Belonging to Both 1st and 10th Classes

The following roots belong to the 1st and the 10th classes:

@deva[युज्] @[yuj], @deva[धूञ्] @[dhūñ] to unite, to restrain; @deva[अर्च्] @[arc] to worship; @deva[ईर्] @[īr] to throw; @deva[ली] @[lī] to melt; @deva[हुञ्ज्] @[huñj] to abandon, to avoid; @deva[चु] @[cu] to cover; @deva[जॄ] @[jṝ], @deva[जि] @[ji] to grow old; @deva[तिच्] @[tic] to separate, to join; @deva[शिण्] @[śiṇ] to leave a residue; @deva[तण्] @[taṇ] to burn; @deva[चुष्] @[cuṣ] to be pleased or satisfied; @deva[चुञ्च्] @[cuñc] to kindle; @deva[चुण्] @[cuṇ], @deva[छुण्] @[chuṇ], @deva[धूम्] @[dhūm] to kindle; @deva[धूष्] @[dhūṣ] to fear; @deva[श्रथ्] @[śrath] to release, to kill; @deva[मी] @[mī] to go; @deva[ग्रन्थ्] @[granth] to string together; @deva[कील्क्] @[kīlk], @deva[चील्क्] @[cīlk] to endure; @deva[अर्द्] @[ard] to kill; @deva[हिंस्] @[hiṃs] to kill; @deva[अर्द्] @[ard] to worship; @deva[सड्] @[saḍ] with @deva[आ] @[ā] to go, to assail; @deva[शुच्] @[śuc] to purify, to cleanse; @deva[छुद्] @[chud] to cover; @deva[जुष्] @[juṣ] to satisfy, to guess, to kill; @deva[यी] @[yī] to please; @deva[श्रन्थ्] @[śranth], @deva[ग्रन्थ्] @[granth] to compose, arrange; @deva[घ्राण्] @[ghrāṇ] to obtain; @deva[तम्] @[tam] to confide, to stretch; @deva[चन्] @[can] to confide, to hurt; @deva[बद्] @[bad] to inform; @deva[वच्] @[vac] to speak; @deva[मान्] @[mān] to honour, worship; @deva[भू] @[bhū] A. to obtain (@deva[भवति] @[bhavati] also according to some); @deva[गर्ह्] @[garh] to censure; @deva[मार्ग्] @[mārg] to seek; @deva[कुण्ठ्] @[kuṇṭh] to grieve, to remember with regret; @deva[युज्] @[yuj] to clean; @deva[घुष्] @[ghuṣ] to endure; @deva[धूष्] @[dhūṣ] to brave, to overcome; @deva[जस्] @[jas] to hurt, to injure; @deva[त्रिभ्] @[tribh] 10 A, 1. P, to torment, to beg; @deva[ढुक्ष्] @[ḍhukṣ] to aver, and some others.
