## § 294. Rule 294

§ 294. @deva[सुषु च ॥]

The substitution of @deva[सु] is also not made when a @deva[कृत्] affix is elided.

Example:— @deva[सुदर्शम्] 'easy to see', @deva[सुकरम्] 'easy to do'.
