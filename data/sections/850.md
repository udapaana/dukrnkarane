## § 850. * The root @deva[ज्ञा] meaning 'to have an incorrect know-

§  850.  * The  root  @deva[ज्ञा]  meaning  'to  have  an  incorrect  know-
ledge of' governs the Genitive;  @deva[तेलं  सर्पिषो  जानीते]  supposed  oil  to
be  ghee;  but  @deva[सर्पिर्युजुयुनोति].

(a)  Verbs  implying  'to  think  of,  to  remember,'  such  as  @deva[स्युट्],  @deva[ट्]
with  @deva[अवि],  'to be master of,'  such as  @deva[हुशुट्],  @deva[यु]  with  @deva[विवु],  &c.  and to  ha·e
compassion  upon  such  as  @deva[दुयु],  &c,  govern  the  Genitive  as  to  the
object;  @deva[वाटिचुट्युदुङुः  स्युरुति]  (Meg.  50)  dost  thou  remember  the  lord?
@deva[स्युयुरुट्  राजुवुवुस्युयुणुयुति  विवुयुयुयुङु  राजुवुनुयुयुस्युयुः]  Rām.  VI.  60.  3.  @deva[अत्युयुनुदि  तवु
छुट्युयुणुः]  (Bhattṛi  VIII.  119.)  Lakshmana.  remembers  thee;  @deva[यभुवुते
मित्रुस्युयु  हुन्युयुवुकुयुयुनुयुयु  मद्युयुनुयुयुः]  (Mal.  Mād.  I.)  the  great  king  now
master  over  his  daughters;  @deva[युदि  ते  पेयुयुयुनुयु  आत्युयुनो  युयुयुविछुयुयुयुयु]
(Uttar.)  if  after  I  see  him  I  shall  have  control  overmyself;
@deva[मायुराम्यो  अङ्गुयुनोऽस्युयु  चुङुतुनुः]  (S'ak.  II.)  I  have  lost  all  power  over
my  limbs;  @deva[कुष्युचुचुरीवुयु  मनसो  वुचुदुट्]:  (Kum.  III.  64.)  with  great
difficulty they very  well  control  their  minds;  @deva[कैयुदुयुस्युर्नों  निषुयुयु  न
येर्णों  ब्रुजुन्ति  तेषो  दुयुरुनं  न  प्रुस्युयुयुनु]  (Bhattṭ.  II.  33.)  why  dost  thou
feel  no  compassion  for  those  whose  wealth  does  not  see
the  morrow  ?  @deva[रामुस्युयु  दुयुयुयुनुनुः]  (Ibid.  VIII  119)  taking  pity
on  Rāmā.

(b) @deva[कुच्]  meaning  'to  impart  additional  properties'  governs  the
Genitive;  @deva[युट्युयुयुयुयुङु  स्युवुयुभुयोयुहु]  'fuel  imparts  heat  to  water,'  @deva[युयु
कुरुयुचिन्युयुयुयुकुच्युयुयुयुः]  Bhatti.  VIII.  119.
