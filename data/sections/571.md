## § 571. 3rd Person Singular Forms

The third person sing. of @deva[पद्] is @deva[अपादि]. @deva[कुष्] 4. A. takes the termination @deva[इ] optionally in the 3rd sing. before which the penultimate @deva[र्] takes Guṇa.
