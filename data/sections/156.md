## § 156. @deva[प्रथम], @deva[चरम], @deva[अल्प], @deva[अर्थ], @deva[कतिपय], and words ending in

§ 156. @deva[प्रथम], @deva[चरम], @deva[अल्प], @deva[अर्थ], @deva[कतिपय], and words ending in
the termination @deva[तय] optionally form their Nom. plu. like that
of pronouns; e. g. @deva[प्रथमेः]-@deva[प्रथमाः], @deva[कोतपये]-@deva[कतिपयाः], @deva[द्वितयेः]-@deva[द्वितयाः] &c.

11—Pronominal Adverbs.
