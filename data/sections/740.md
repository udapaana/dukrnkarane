# § 740

This page contains content for §740-743, covering Future Passive Participles formed with different affixes.

## § 740 (Continuation from previous page)

Examples of formations:

- @deva[तिवुद्धुत्युं:] @deva[तिवुदुत:] (from @deva[वि] with @deva[हि])
- @deva[शुसीदेत अनेन संत्रिदूयुते घाश्या] (from @deva[धा]) @deva[चरुख:]
- @deva[कुपुष्यन पाषुते] (@deva[अनिमुत्र सोम:]) @deva[कुपुष्यायुख:] @deva[कष्टु:]
- @deva[संद्याषैविरोर्य:] @deva[संज्यास्यु:] (a sacrifice)
- @deva[परिज्ञात्युं], @deva[उपुत्युत्युं]
- @deva[स्वयूत्युं:] @deva[पुविष्पाषैविकायिनौकुयुं ते] (rites for depositing the sacrificial fire)
- @deva[पनिचुत्युत्], @deva[डुयुचुयुद्], @deva[तंवूत्युद्] in other cases
- @deva[चौवते अतीं चिस्युत:] @deva[आद्रुते]; @deva[अतीरे चयनं] @deva[आश्ङाचिस्युत्]

## § 741

The roots @deva[भू] and @deva[भै] take the affix @deva[यत्]; @deva[स्था] takes @deva[अनीय]; and @deva[गै], @deva[यु] and @deva[पच्] take @deva[घ्यत्], in an active sense:

- @deva[अदुर्तोतो भव्युं] (also @deva[भव्युभुनुनं])
- @deva[भायुवातो गेव:] 'one who sings' (also @deva[गेयुं राम अनेनै])
- @deva[प्रवुचनौय:] 'one who speaks' @deva[उरु]
- @deva[वानौय:] 'one who stands for', @deva[स्तुयु]
- @deva[पुचोय युरितिर्यै:], @deva[पात्युयुं] 'food'

## (3) By means of @deva[एलिमुर्] (@deva[एलिम्])

## § 742

A few transitive roots take the affix @deva[एलिमुर्] having the same force as the affix @deva[यत्]:

- @deva[पचेलिमु] from @deva[पच्], 'fit to ripen or to be cooked'; as @deva[पचेलिमा भावान:]
- @deva[भिदेलिमाः] (from @deva[भिद्]) @deva[स्तृम्बा:] 'the pine trees ought to be felled,' &c.

## § 743

The declension of these Participles follows that of nouns ending in @deva[अ].
