## § 277. @deva[सर्वनाम्नः] 

§ 277. @deva[सर्वनाम्नः ।]

When the first member is a pronoun, the Accusative is dropped (even when denoting an animate being).

Examples:— @deva[सत्वम्] 'with thee', @deva[समाम्] 'with me', @deva[सयत् किञ्चित्] 'with whatever'.
