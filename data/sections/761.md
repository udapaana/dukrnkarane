## § 761. There is another Indeclinable Participle having the

§ 761.  There is another  Indeclinable Participle having the
same sense as that in @deva[स्या],  formed  with  the affix @deva[अम्] .  Before
this the vowel of the derivative verbal  base  generally  undergoes
the same changes as it does before the @deva[इु] of the  Pass.  Aorist
3rd sing.; @deva[नी]-@deva[नयाम्] 'having  carried  or  led,'  @deva[दा]-@deva[दायाम्]  'having
given,' @deva[यू]-@deva[भावाम्], @deva[पिपु]-@deva[भेदाम्] , @deva[मह्]-@deva[भाह्राम्] , @deva[गम्]-@deva[गमाम्] , &c.
