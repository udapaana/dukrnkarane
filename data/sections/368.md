## § 368. When used in a compound the verb may be omitted

§ 368.    When used in a compound the verb may be omitted
after the prepositions @deva[अति], @deva[अधि], @deva[अनु], @deva[अप], @deva[अव], @deva[अभि], @deva[उप], @deva[परि] and
@deva[प्रति]; @deva[अतिक्रान्तो मालां अतिमाळः] &c. Vide § 232.
