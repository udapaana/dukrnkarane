## § 500. Monosyllabic roots, having a short @deva[अ] for their penultimate, change it to @deva[य] and drop the red

§ 500.  Monosyllabic  roots,  having  a  short  @deva[अ]  for  their penultimate, change it to @deva[य] and drop  the  reduplicaive  syllable before the weak  terminations  and  before  @deva[य]  when  it  takes  @deva[इ], provided  their  initial  letter  undergoes  no  change  in  the reduplication, e. g   @deva[पत्] 1st dual  @deva[पेतुव्]+@deva[इव]=@deva[पेत्]+@deva[इव]=@deva[पेतिव], @deva[पेरिव] 2 sing. @deva[शोक्ष्व], @deva[शसक्ष्व] 2. sing. of @deva[शक्], but @deva[निनेश्व]  2nd  dual of @deva[नन्द्] as the @deva[अ] here is prosodially long,

Exceptions:—Roots  beginning  with  @deva[व]  and  the  roots  @deva[शासु] and @deva[ग्रस्].
