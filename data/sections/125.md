## § 125. With regard to the roots ending in @deva[ह्] which form

§ 125. With regard to the roots ending in @deva[ह्] which form
nouns in @deva[ह्] ( fem. ) with affix @deva[क्विप्], such as @deva[मुह्] ( a moment from
the root @deva[मुह्] ) we have the nom. sing. @deva[मूढा] or @deva[मूट्]; or by VIII.
3. 41 @deva[मूढा]; acc. sing. @deva[मूढम्], ins. @deva[मूढा], dat. @deva[मूढे], gen. @deva[मूढः], loc.
@deva[मूढि] or @deva[मूढिं] or @deva[मूढिस् ॥] Similarly we have @deva[अप्स्वाही] ( @deva[नी० उह्]-
@deva[व्याइ] = @deva[वाही] ) @deva[॥] Similarly the forms of @deva[दुह्] and of @deva[राह्] ( lightning )
can be formed. They occur in the @deva[वेदे] only.

                    Bases ending in @deva[श्व ॥]
