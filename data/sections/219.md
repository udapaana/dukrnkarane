## § 219. Exceptions to Nañ Transformation

The @deva[न] @[na] is not changed to @deva[अ] @[a] or @deva[अन्] @[an] in the following cases[^1]:—@deva[नभ्र] @[nabhra] 'that which does not shine,' 'a cloud;' @deva[नपात्] @[napāt] (pre. p.) 'one who does not protect;' @deva[नवेद्यः] @[navedyaḥ] 'one who does not know,' @deva[नासत्याः] @[nāsatyāḥ] [@deva[न सत्याः असत्याः न असत्याः नासत्याः] @[na satyāḥ asatyāḥ na asatyāḥ nāsatyāḥ]][^2] the two Aśvins, the physicians of the gods. @deva[नहुष] @[nahuṣa] [@deva[न हि श्वेतीति] @[na hi śvetīti]] name of a demon slain by Indra; @deva[नकुलः] @[nakulaḥ] [@deva[न कुलम् अस्य] @[na kulam asya], for the mungoose is supposed to belong to no particular species of animals]; @deva[नसद्] @[nasad] [@deva[न स्वम् अस्य] @[na svam asya] that which leaves no cavity, or does not go to heaven being burnt down with the dead body]; @deva[नक्षत्रम्] @[nakṣatram] [@deva[न क्षरति इति] @[na kṣarati iti]] that which does not move from its proper place, hence a star; @deva[नक्रः] @[nakraḥ] [@deva[न क्रामति इति] @[na krāmati iti] that which does not go far off from water]; @deva[नाकः] @[nākaḥ] [@deva[न कं अकं न अकं अक्रम्] @[na kaṃ akaṃ na akaṃ akram]]; @deva[नग] @[naga] when it does not qualify an animal retains its @deva[न] @[na] optionally as @deva[नगः] @[nagaḥ] or @deva[अगः] @[agaḥ] 'a mountain or a tree,' but @deva[अगः शूद्रः] @[agaḥ śūdraḥ] 'a Śūdra that cannot move.'

**N.B.** It should be noted that some of these are Bahuvrihi compounds.

[^2]: @deva[अत्र बहुव्वचनसविवक्षिता । तेन 'नासत्यावश्विनौ मुक्तो' इति सिद्धम्] @[atra bahuvvacanasavivakṣitā | tena 'nāsatyāvaśvinau mukto' iti siddham] Tatvabodhini.
