## § 147. @deva[अन्य] 'another,' @deva[अन्यतर] 'either of two,' @deva[इतर] 'other,'

§ 147. @deva[अन्य] 'another,' @deva[अन्यतर] 'either of two,' @deva[इतर] 'other,'
@deva[एकतम] 'one of many,' @deva[कतर] 'who or which of two,' @deva[कतम] 'who or
which of many,' @deva[यतर] 'who or which of two,' @deva[यतम] 'who or which
of many,' @deva[ततर] 'that one ( of two ),' @deva[ततम] 'that one ( of many ),'
are declined in all the three genders like @deva[यद्] e. g.

                                mas.
N.        @deva[कतरः        कतरौ        कतरे]
        &c.            fem.
N.        @deva[कतरा        कतरे        कतराः]
        &c.            neu.
N. & A.    @deva[कतरत्    कतरे        कतराणि]
        &c.
N. B. @deva[अन्यतम] is not a pronoun, as it is not mentioned in the
group @deva[सर्वादि] ( @deva[तत्रान्यतमशब्दस्य गणे पाठ्माबाचं संज्ञा] Sid. Kau. ). It
is, therefore, declined like nouns ending in @deva[अ].
