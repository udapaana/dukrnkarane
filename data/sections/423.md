## § 423. Augment इ in अन्, जक्ष्, रुद्, श्वस्, स्वप्

@deva[अन्] @[an] P. 'to breathe,' @deva[जक्ष्] @[jakṣ] 'to eat,' @deva[रुद्] @[rud] P. 'to weep,' @deva[श्वस्] @[śvas] P. 'to sigh' and @deva[स्वप्] @[svap] P. 'to sleep,' insert the augment @deva[इ] @[i] between their final and the terminations beginning with any consonant except @deva[य] @[y]; in the case of 2 and 3 sing. Imperf. they insert @deva[ई] @[ī] or @deva[आ] @[ā]; e.g.
