## § 895. The use of the perfect participles is very limited; it

§ 895. The use of the perfect participles is very limited; it
is used in the sense of 'who or what has done &c.'; @deva[तं तास्युयुयुरुयुनं
नगुरीयुकुयुयुयुङु] (Rag. V. 61.) him who had halted in the vicinity
of the city; @deva[तस्युयु] (S'ak V. II. 9.) @deva[समुयुत्युयुयुरुहस्युरे] (Mag. V. 34.) of thee who
hast obtained all good things, @deva[स शुयुयुनुर्तोयुत्युच्युच्युनं] (Bhattṛi. I. 20)
when he heard his words, &c.
