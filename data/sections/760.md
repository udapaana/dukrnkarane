## § 760. Desiderative bases add @deva[य] immediately and Freq.

§ 760.  Desiderative  bases  add  @deva[य]  immediately  and  Freq.
bases  add  it  after dropping  their  @deva[यु],  when it is proceded by a
consonant and dropping the final @deva[अ]  when  it is preceded  by  a
vowel; @deva[बुभु]-@deva[यबुबुषिष्य]; Fre. @deva[यबोबुघुय]; @deva[भू] Fre. @deva[यबोभुय] &c.
