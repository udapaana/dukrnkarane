## § 736. The root @deva[वुत्] when preceded by @deva[अमृ] ' with' takes the

§ 736.   The root @deva[वुत्] when preceded by @deva[अमृ] ' with' takes the
affix @deva[घ्यत्] before which the penultimate  @deva[अ] is optionally  changed
to Vṛiddhi; @deva[अमृ रुङु छुवातोउत्वा चन्ना]:@deva[छो अधुवुद्धुवा] or @deva[वाञुया] 'the day
on which the sun and the moon are with each other' i. e.  are in
conjunction.'
