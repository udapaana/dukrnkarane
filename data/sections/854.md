## § 854. ‡ The roots @deva[युयुयुयुट्] (i. e. @deva[यु] with @deva[नि] and @deva[अत्]) , @deva[युयुयु] and

§ 854.  ‡ The  roots  @deva[युयुयुयुट्]  (i.  e.  @deva[यु]  with  @deva[नि]  and  @deva[अत्])  ,  @deva[युयुयु]  and
@deva[विवु]  when  they  all  mean  to  transact  business,  or  to  stake  in
gambling,  govern  the  Genitive  of  their  object;  @deva[शुतुस्युरु  युयुवुदुयुति]
invests a hundred  rupees  in  business;  @deva[गोभुयुयुयुणुयुत्युयुयुसो]  he  staked
his  life;  @deva[अदेवोत्  भुयुयुगुयुनोपुयुनों]  lost  his  brothers  and  pleasures  in
gambling,  &c.;  but  when  @deva[विवु]  is  preceded  by  a  preposition,
the  Accusative  may  also  be  used;  @deva[शुतुस्युरु  शुतं  वा  प्रतिदुशुयुति]
(Sid.  Kau.)
