## § 756. ( a ), @deva[च्युत्] and @deva[छुयु] do not take Samprasāraṇa, @deva[प्रत्युव्य];

§ 756.  ( a ), @deva[च्युत्]  and  @deva[छुयु]  do  not  take Samprasāraṇa,  @deva[प्रत्युव्य];
@deva[उत्युत्य] 'having become  old,'  @deva[उप्युत्य]  'having  covered,'  but @deva[ऋ]
with @deva[परि]  or  @deva[संत्] ,  takes Samprasāraṇa  optionally,  @deva[पुरिस्युत्य]  or
@deva[परिदुर्य]; @deva[संस्युत्य] or @deva[संदीर्य].
