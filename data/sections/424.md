## § 424. अस् 'to be' - Complete Paradigm

@deva[अस्] @[as] P. (Ā.[^1] rare) 'to be'—drops its @deva[अ] @[a] before weak terminations and @deva[त्] @[t] before a termination beginning with @deva[स्] @[s] and @deva[ध्व्] @[dhv]. Before 2 and 3 Imperf. it takes the augment @deva[ई] @[ī]. It is irregular in many respects.

### Present

| | Parasmaipada | | | Ātmanepada | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1. | @deva[अस्मि] @[asmi] | @deva[स्वः] @[svaḥ] | @deva[स्मः] @[smaḥ] | @deva[हे] @[he] | @deva[स्वहे] @[svahe] | @deva[स्महे] @[smahe] |
| 2. | @deva[असि] @[asi] | @deva[स्थः] @[sthaḥ] | @deva[स्थ] @[stha] | @deva[से] @[se] | @deva[साथे] @[sāthe] | @deva[ध्वे] @[dhve] |
| 3. | @deva[अस्ति] @[asti] | @deva[स्तः] @[staḥ] | @deva[सन्ति] @[santi] | @deva[स्ते] @[ste] | @deva[स्थाते] @[sthāte] | @deva[सते] @[sate] |
