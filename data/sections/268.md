## § 268. An Avyayibhāva comp. consists of two members the first of which is, in most cases, an indeclinable (

§ 268. An Avyayibhāva comp. consists of two members the first of which is, in most cases, an indeclinable (a preposition or an adverb) and the second a noun, the whole being treated like the Nom. sing. of neu. nouns. the comp so formed is indeclinable; e. g. @deva[अधिहरि] 'in Hari'; @deva[अन्तार्गिरि] 'in a mountain,' &c.
