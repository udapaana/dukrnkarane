## § 501. Insertion of त After Reduplicative Syllable

@deva[त] @[t] is inserted after the reduplicative syllable in the case of roots beginning with @deva[अ] @[a] and ending in a conjunct consonant, in that of @deva[अह्] @[ah] 'to pervade' and @deva[आस्] @[ās] 'to go'; consequently @deva[अ] @[a] after @deva[त] @[t] is changed to @deva[आ] @[ā].

e.g. @deva[अञ्ज्] @[añj]: @deva[अञ्ज् + अ = आञ्ज् + अ + अ = आनञ्ज] @[añj + a = āñj + a + a = ānañja]

Similarly @deva[आनन्द्] @[ānand] 1 sing. of @deva[अन्द्] @[and], @deva[आनर्च्] @[ānarc] of @deva[अर्च्] @[arc], etc.
