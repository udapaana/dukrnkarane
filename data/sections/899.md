## § 899. The past passive participles of the roots @deva[यनु], @deva[युनु]

§ 899. The past passive participles of the roots @deva[यनु], @deva[युनु]
and @deva[यस्] and their synonyms are used in the sense of the Present
tense and are construed with the Genitive. (See § 857.)

For further particulars vide §§ 705—707.
