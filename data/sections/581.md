## § 581. Lengthening and Vowel Changes

The final @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] is lengthened; final @deva[अ] short is changed to @deva[इ], and final @deva[ऋ] changed to @deva[ईर्] or to @deva[ऊर्] when preceded by a labial or @deva[व].
