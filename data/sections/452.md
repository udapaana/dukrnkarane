## § 452. @deva[दा] and @deva[धा] drop their @deva[आ] after reduplication before weak terminations; @deva[दध्] becomes @deva[घद्] before @deva[स्],

§ 452.  @deva[दा] and @deva[धा] drop  their  @deva[आ]  after  reduplication  before weak terminations; @deva[दध्] becomes @deva[घद्] before @deva[स्], @deva[ध्व], @deva[त]  and @deva[थ].  The Par. Imperative 2 per. singulars are @deva[देहि] and @deva[घेहि] respectively.
