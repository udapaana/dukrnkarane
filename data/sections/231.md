## § 231. A Dwigu compound is possible (a) under the circumstances mentioned in § 223 (a) i. e.—

§ 231. A Dwigu compound is possible (a) under the circumstances mentioned in § 223 (a) i. e.—

(1) when a Taddhita affix is to be added to the compound so formed; @deva[षण्णां मातृणामपत्यं षाण्मातुरः] 'the son of six mothers, Kārtikeya' (vide Ku. IX); @deva[पञ्चकपालः] &c; or (2) when the compound itself becomes the first member of another compound, as @deva[पञ्चगावः पर्वं यस्यो पञ्चगवघनः]; @deva[पञ्चनार्धिमयः] &c.

(b) And when the compound denotes an aggregate; in this case it is singular; ‡ as @deva[त्रयाणां भुवनानां समाहारः त्रिभुवनम्] the three worlds (taken collectively); as @deva[पञ्चपात्रम्], @deva[पञ्चगवम्], &c.

* @deva[शाकपाधिवादीनां सिद्धं उत्तरपदलोपस्योयसंख्यानम् ।] Vārt on Pān. II. 1. 60.
† @deva[संख्यापूर्वो द्विगुः ।] Pān. II. 1. 52.
‡ @deva[द्विगुरेकवचनम् ।] Pān. II. 4. I.
