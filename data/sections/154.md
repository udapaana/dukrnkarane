## § 154. @deva[एक] when a numeral is declined in the sing. and @deva[द्वि]

§ 154. @deva[एक] when a numeral is declined in the sing. and @deva[द्वि]
in the dual only, @deva[द्वि]. is considered as @deva[द्व], and they are both
declined like @deva[सर्व] in all the three genders; o g
