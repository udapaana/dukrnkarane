## § 293. @deva[अव्ययीभावे कृत्यलुकि न च ॥]

§ 293. @deva[अव्ययीभावे कृत्यलुकि न च ॥]

When a @deva[कृत्] affix is elided in an Avyayībhāva compound, the substitution (of @deva[दुर्] etc.) does not take place.

Example:— @deva[दुर्दर्शम्] 'difficult to see' (here @deva[दुर्] is not replacing @deva[स]).
