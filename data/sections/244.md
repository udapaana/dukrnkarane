## § 244. ‡All the rules given above for the change of the finals of the Tat. compounds do not hold good in th

§ 244. ‡All the rules given above for the change of the finals of the Tat. compounds do not hold good in the case of the Neg. Tat; e. g. @deva[न राजा अराजा] one not a king; @deva[न सस्या असस्या], &c.
