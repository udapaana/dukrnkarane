## § 772. § The gerund in @deva[अम्] of roots having the sense of @deva[हिंस]

§ 772.  § The gerund in  @deva[अम्]  of roots having the sense of @deva[हिंस]
'to strike,' &c.  are used  with  words  denoting  the  instrument
when the object of the gerund and of  the  principal  verb is the
same; @deva[दुण्डुचायुवातं  गां]:  @deva[काढुयति]  i.  e.  @deva[दुण्डुनैगाचायुवातं]  'he collects to-
gether cows beating ( them ) with a stick;' @deva[प्रपुरवुत्तुं हिंसति] ; but @deva[यकोश्चन
चोरुयाहुस्य  गां]: @deva[काढुयति].
