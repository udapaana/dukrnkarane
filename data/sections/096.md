## § 96. When a mono-syllabic root (or root-noun) begins with @deva[ब्] , @deva[ग्] or @deva[ध्] and ends with @deva[प्], @deva[ट्], @deva[ध्], @deva[भ्] or @deva[ह]

§ 96. When a mono-syllabic root (or root-noun) begins with @deva[ब्] , @deva[ग्] or @deva[ध्] and ends with @deva[प्], @deva[ट्], @deva[ध्], @deva[भ्] or @deva[ह्], the @deva[प्], @deva[ग्] or @deva[ध्] is respecti-vely changed to @deva[प्], @deva[द्] or @deva[प्]  before @deva[सु] or @deva[ष्व्] or when followed by nothing or when the final soft aspirate is changed to an unaspi-rate letter†.
