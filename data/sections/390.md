## § 390. @deva[अ] is added on to the roots of the sixth or @deva[तुदादि] class before which the penultimate vowel remains u

§ 390.  @deva[अ] is added on to the roots of the sixth or @deva[तुदादि] class before which the penultimate vowel remains unchanged, and the final @deva[इ], @deva[उ], short or long, @deva[ऋ] and @deva[ॠ] are changed to @deva[इय], @deva[उय], @deva[रिय]
