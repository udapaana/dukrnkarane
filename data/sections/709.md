## § 709. Participle of the Future Tense

The Participle of the Simple Future, Active as well as Passive, is formed from the third person singular form of the Second Future of a root. The Parasm. Ptc. is formed simply by dropping the final @deva[इ] @[i], and the Atm. and the Passive one by substituting @deva[मान] @[māna] for the final @deva[इ] @[i]:

### Participles

| Roots | Par. | Atm. | Passive |
|-------|------|------|---------|
| @deva[दा] @[dā] | @deva[दास्यत्] @[dāsyat] | @deva[दास्यमाण] @[dāsyamāṇa] | @deva[दास्यमाण] @[dāsyamāṇa] or @deva[दायिष्यमाण] @[dāyiṣyamāṇa] |
| @deva[भू] @[bhū] | @deva[भविष्यत्] @[bhaviṣyat] | @deva[भविष्यमाण] @[bhaviṣyamāṇa] | @deva[भविष्यमाण] @[bhaviṣyamāṇa] or @deva[भाविष्यमाण] @[bhāviṣyamāṇa] |
| @deva[चुर्] @[cur] | @deva[चोरयिष्यत्] @[corayiṣyat] | @deva[चारयिष्यमाण] @[cārayiṣyamāṇa] | @deva[चोरयिष्यमाण] @[corayiṣyamāṇa] or @deva[चोरिष्यमाण] @[coriṣyamāṇa] |
| @deva[गम्] @[gam] | @deva[गमिष्यत्] @[gamiṣyat] | @deva[गमिष्यमाण] @[gamiṣyamāṇa] | @deva[गम्यमाण] @[gamyamāṇa] |
| @deva[जि] @[ji] | @deva[जेष्यत्] @[jeṣyat] | @deva[विजेष्यमाण] @[vijeṣyamāṇa] | @deva[जेष्यमाण] @[jeṣyamāṇa], @deva[जायिष्यमाण] @[jāyiṣyamāṇa] |
| @deva[कृ] @[kṛ] | @deva[करिष्यत्] @[kariṣyat] | @deva[करिष्यमाण] @[kariṣyamāṇa] | @deva[कारिष्यमाण] @[kāriṣyamāṇa], @deva[कारिष्यमाण] @[kāriṣyamāṇa] |
| @deva[श्रु] @[śru] | @deva[श्रोष्यत्] @[śroṣyat] | @deva[श्रोष्यमाण] @[śroṣyamāṇa] | @deva[श्रोष्यमाण] @[śroṣyamāṇa], @deva[श्राविष्यमाण] @[śrāviṣyamāṇa] |
| @deva[एष्] @[eṣ] A. | @deva[एषिष्यमाण] @[eṣiṣyamāṇa] | @deva[एषिष्यमाण] @[eṣiṣyamāṇa] |
| @deva[वद्] @[vad] | @deva[वक्ष्यत्] @[vakṣyat] | @deva[वक्ष्यमान] @[vakṣyamāna] | @deva[वक्ष्यमान] @[vakṣyamāna] |

Similarly:

- @deva[पिपाठिष्] @[pipāṭhiṣ] Desi. Base of @deva[पठ्] @[paṭh]: @deva[पिपाठिष्यत्] @[pipāṭhiṣyat], @deva[पिपाठिष्यमाण] @[pipāṭhiṣyamāṇa], etc.
- @deva[बोबुध्] @[bobudh] Fre. B. of @deva[बुध्] @[budh]: @deva[बोबुधिष्यत्] @[bobudhiṣyat], @deva[बोबुधिष्यमाण] @[bobudhiṣyamāṇa], etc.
