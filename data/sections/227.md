## § 227. Potentials and Similars in Karmadhāraya

Words ending in the terminations @deva[तव्य] @[tavya], @deva[अनीय] @[anīya], @deva[य] @[ya] and @deva[तुल्य] @[tulya] and words having a similar meaning, may be compounded with any subanta not importing a particular kind;[^1] e.g. @deva[भोज्यम् अन्नम्] @[bhojyam annam] 'any hot eatable,' @deva[तुल्यश्वेतः] @[tulyaśvetaḥ] 'of a similar white colour,' @deva[सदृशश्वेतः] @[sadṛśaśvetaḥ], etc.; but @deva[भोज्यः ओदनः] @[bhojyaḥ odanaḥ] as the latter word refers to a kind of food here.

---

[^1]: @deva[कृत्यतृत्यार्थ्या अजात्या] @[kṛtyatṛtyārthyā ajātyā] Pāṇ. II.1.59.
