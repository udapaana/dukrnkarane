## § 298. @deva[स्वरादिनि च] 

§ 298. @deva[स्वरादिनि च ।]

And @deva[स्थानिन्] is understood when the compound begins with a vowel.

Example:— @deva[अध्यृषि] 'in or regarding the ṛṣi'.
