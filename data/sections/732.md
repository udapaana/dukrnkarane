## § 732. The following words are derived from the root @deva[यहु]

§ 732.  The following  words  are  derived from  the  root  @deva[यहु]
with the affix @deva[क्यच्]:—@deva[अवुयहो]; @deva[मुयुहो],  @deva[पदै] 'two  technical  terms  in
grammar;' @deva[मुडुकु]: ' birds restrained from free motion, such as
parrots, &c. '    @deva[प्रवायुदेवायेय    परोत्तभ्युक्ता   इपुक्षे ।] Sid  Kau,
@deva[ग्रामयुद्धा सेना] ' an army  stationed  outside  a village' ; @deva[आर्यूदुहुद्धुते
आयुयुद्धै]: @deva[तिस्वामुद्धिव इत्यर्थै]: ( Sid.  Kau. ) ' siding with the noble. '
See Rāg. II. 43.
