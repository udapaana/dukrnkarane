## § 724. According to Vāmana, the roots @deva[सोत्] , @deva[इकु] and @deva[युह्] take

§ 724   According to Vāmana, the roots @deva[सोत्] , @deva[इकु] and @deva[युह्] take
@deva[इ यत्]   According to Pāṇini the roots @deva[स्तु]*-@deva[स्तु]:@deva[य] 'praiseworthy;' @deva[हकु]-@deva[ह्कु]:@deva[य]; @deva[युह्]-@deva[उह्य];
optionally they take the affix @deva[एयत्] ( to be given hereafter ); @deva[सोत्य],
@deva[ह्वेत], @deva[यौहार्].
