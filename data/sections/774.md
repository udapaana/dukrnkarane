## § 774. †The roots @deva[विश्रु] , @deva[युष्] , @deva[पश्] and @deva[स्कुन्द] are used in their

§ 774.  †The roots @deva[विश्रु] , @deva[युष्] , @deva[पश्]  and  @deva[स्कुन्द]  are used in their
gerundive forms in @deva[अम्] in the sense of ' complete  occupation or
pervasion or repetition'  with nouns  which  if not  compounded
would have stood in the Acc.;  @deva[गेहुगृहुगुर्युयायुते  गेहं  गेहुयुयुमुभुयुम् ।
स्कुन्दुम्युगौयुते  ।]  so  @deva[गेहुदुयुयुर्युयुरु] ,  @deva[गेहुदुयुनायुरु] ,  @deva[गेहुदुयु]-
@deva[स्कुन्दुम्] , &c.
