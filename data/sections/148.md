## § 148. Decline @deva[सर्व], @deva[विश्व], @deva[सम] and @deva[सिम] all having the same

§ 148. Decline @deva[सर्व], @deva[विश्व], @deva[सम] and @deva[सिम] all having the same
meaning, @deva[उभ] ( declined in the dual only ) and @deva[उभय] ( which has
no dual according to Kaiyata and other grammarians ) both
meaning 'both,' @deva[इतर] and @deva[एकतर] 'one of two,' like @deva[यद्] except in the
