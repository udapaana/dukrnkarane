## § 664. By means of the suffixes @deva[कुय], @deva[क्रिय], @deva[णिङ्] and @deva[कौय]

§ 664.  By means of the suffixes @deva[कुय], @deva[क्रिय], @deva[णिङ्] and @deva[कौय]
the sense of reproach is conveyed; e. g. @deva[स्तेनङ्कुयति], @deva[स्तेनङ्क्रियति]
means ' to call a person a thief, ' @deva[भृत्येयति] means ' to call a
person a servant;' so @deva[किंकरेयति].
