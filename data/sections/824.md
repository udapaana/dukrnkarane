## § 824. † The roots @deva[भुयु] and @deva[प्रुच्छु] both meaning 'to detemine

§ 824  † The  roots  @deva[भुयु]  and  @deva[प्रुच्छु]  both  meaning  'to  detemine
the  good  or  bad  fortune  of,'  govern  the  Dative  o:  the  person
with reference to whom the inquiry is made; @deva[कुलुयुयुयु राष्युयुति  हंदुते
वा । पृच्छो गर्नः अयुयुयुरुक्तो पयुट्युटुत्युच्युयुरुस्युयुयुः ।] Sid. Kau.
