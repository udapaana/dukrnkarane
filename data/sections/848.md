## § 848. † Words ending in the termination @deva[युयुनं] and showing

§ 848.  † Words  ending  in  the  termination  @deva[युयुनं]  and  showing
distance  and  also  having  the  same  sense,  @deva[युयुयुङु  युयुयुयु],  @deva[युयुयु],
@deva[युयुयुयुयुयुर],  @deva[युयुट्],  @deva[युयुयुयुयुर],  @deva[युयु].  @deva[युयुयुयुयुट्],  @deva[युयुयुट्],  @deva[युयुयु],  &c.  are  used
with the Genitive; @deva[युयुयुयु दुयुयुयुयुयुयुयु].  @deva[युयुयुयुयु]  &c.  in  the  north  or  to
the  north  of  Devadatta;  these  are  sometimes  used  in  the  Locative
Also  place;  @deva[युयुयुयुयुयुयु]  (S'ak.  I.)  under  the  tree;  @deva[युयुनं  विचुयुयु]
river  or  place  or  thing  is  to  be  found  @deva[युयुयुयुयुयुयु  विटुयुयुयुयुयुयु]  the  town
Pāṭaliputra is to  the  north  or  near  the  town  of  Bihār;  be
used with the Genitive or the Accusative.  @deva[युरुयुयुयुनं  युयुट्  युयुयुयुनं  युयु]
or the north of the river; @deva[युयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयु] (Bhatti. VIII.108. ),
to  be  north  of  the  river, @deva[युयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयुयु] (Bhatti. VIII.108. ),
@deva[युयुयुयुयुरुतुयुर्णों  युयुरुतुयुयु  युयुयुयुट्  युयुत्युयुयु  विदुयुयुनं]  (Māl.  Mād.  IX.  24.)
