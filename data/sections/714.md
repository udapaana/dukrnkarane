## § 714. The affix @deva[य] ( @deva[यत्] )* is added on to roots ending in a

§ 714   The affix @deva[य] ( @deva[यत्] )* is  added on to  roots ending in a
vowel in the sense of fit to be, or ought to be.' Before
this @deva[य] the radical vowel  undergoes  Guṇa  substitute and final
@deva[आ] ( @deva[य], @deva[ऋ] and @deva[ओ] changeable to @deva[आ] ) is changed to @deva[ए].

    @deva[दा]—@deva[देय] what is fit or ought to be given.
    @deva[पे]—@deva[पेय]       „       „       sucked.
    @deva[ने]—@deva[नेय]       „       „       sung.
    @deva[भो]—@deva[भोय]      „       „       cut.
    @deva[विर्]-@deva[वैय]      „       „       collected.
    @deva[नी]—@deva[नेय]       „       „       led or carried.
