## § 876. The root @deva[युयुयु] with @deva[अयु] 'to offend' is generally construed

§ 876.  The  root @deva[युयुयु]  with  @deva[अयु]  'to  offend'  is  generally  construed
with  the  Locative  and  sometimes  with  the  Genitive;  @deva[कुस्युयुयुच्युनुति
पुयुनुयुनुनै  अयुरुयुयुट्युयु  शुकुन्तुलुयु]  Ś'akuntala  has  offended  some  one  deserving
respect; @deva[न तु युयूस्युस्युयुनै हुयुयुनुयुयुरुयुदुट् युयुयूहोदुयु] (S'ak. III. 9.), @deva[विड्युयुनुरु]-
@deva[इरुयुकुदुनैनं  भवुतोनुयुयुयुरुयुयुदुट्  ।] Vik.  I.

THE GENITIVE AND THE LOCATIVE  ABSOLUTES.
