## § 842. * When a word expressive of 'a debt,' is merely

§ 842.  * When  a  word  expressive  of  'a  debt,'  is  merely
intended to be stated as a  cause,  it  is  put in the Ablative case;
@deva[शुतात्युदुवुदुङु  द्युयु]  a  thing  mortgaged for a hundred rupees,  @deva[कुट्युणुयुदुवुदुङु
इयु] tied down as it were by the debt he owes.
