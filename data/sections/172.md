## § 172. Before these the final @deva[ई] and @deva[ऊ] of a prātipadika are

§ 172.  Before these the final @deva[ई] and @deva[ऊ] of a prātipadika are
optionally shortened, e g  @deva[श्रीतरा] or @deva[श्रित्रा], @deva[श्रीतमा] or @deva[श्रित्मा];
@deva[घेष्ठरा] ( more lame ) or @deva[घेष्ठुरा], @deva[घेष्ठतमा], @deva[घेष्ठुतमा], &c.
