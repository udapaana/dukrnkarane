## § 859. * In the case of Potential passive participles the agent

§ 859.  * In the case of  Potential  passive  participles  the  agent
of the action is put in the Genitive or in the Instrumental case;
@deva[मया मम वा हेयुयो हरिः] Hari ought to be served by me; @deva[राक्तुयुसुनुयु]-@deva[स्युयु
नुयुयुरुमु  तृयुतुरुयुः]  (Bhattṛi  VIII.  125)  the  forest  together  with  the
lord of the demons  ought  to  preserve  must  be  destroyed  by  me;
@deva[गन्तव्युयु  ते  वसुतिरुटुकुयु  हे].  (Meg.)  Thou  should  go  to  Alakā.
