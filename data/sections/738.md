## § 738. Roots ending in @deva[उ], short or long, take the affix @deva[एलिम्]

§ 738.  Roots ending in @deva[उ], short or long,  take the affix @deva[एलिम्]
in the sense of  'what ought  or  must necessarily be done;'
@deva[छु]—@deva[छातुं]  'what  must  necessarily  be  cut  off,'  @deva[पाक्षु]  'what
must  necessarily  be  cooked,'  @deva[दा] to  be  with  @deva[आ]—@deva[आत्तुम्] ;  @deva[इु]  ' to
mix,' @deva[यात्], &c.

( a ) The roots @deva[वत्] ,  @deva[र्तु] ,  @deva[छुयु] ,  @deva[भृत्]  and  @deva[वत्]  also  do the same,
@deva[वाच्युं]  'what must necessarily be on;' @deva[प्रवत्तुं]  'what must be
spoken of distinctly;' @deva[छुत्युत्] , @deva[भात्युत्] , @deva[चात्युत्] .
