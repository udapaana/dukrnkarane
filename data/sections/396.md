# § 396

The following seven roots form their bases like the Desiderative in the senses indicated. These are:

- @deva[क्षिद्] 'to admonish medicine, to treat as a patient' @deva[चिकीषति]
- @deva[शाङ्ख्] 'to censure' @deva[जुगुप्साते]
- @deva[तिअ] 'to bear, to forgive' @deva[तितिक्षति]
- @deva[वृष्] 'to abhor, to act loathsomely' @deva[विद्राति]
- @deva[दाग्] 'to make straight' @deva[दीदाषाति-ते]
- @deva[माग्] 'to reason, to think' @deva[मीमांसति]
- @deva[घाण्] 'to sharpen' @deva[शीषाष्टाति-ते]

But @deva[किद्] 'to desire' @deva[केकति]; 'to dwell' @deva[केदाति]; @deva[दाग्] 'to cut' @deva[दादायति-ते] &c.
