## § 205. Instrumental Tatpurusha

A noun in the Instrumental may be compounded—

(a) with another expressive of an effect produced by what is denoted by the noun in the Instrumental case, and with @deva[अर्थ] @[artha];[^1] as @deva[शङ्कुलेन सलितं] @[śaṅkulena salitaṃ] @deva[शङ्कुशलितम्] @[śaṅkuśalitam] severed by means of a knife; @deva[धान्येन अर्थः] @[dhānyena arthaḥ] @deva[धान्यार्थः] @[dhānyārthaḥ] wealth obtained by means of grain.

(b) with verbal derivatives when the Instrumental has the sense of the agent or the instrument or means of the action;[^2] as @deva[हरिणा त्रातः] @[hariṇā trātaḥ] @deva[हरित्रातः] @[haritrātaḥ] saved by Hari; @deva[नखैर्भिन्नः] @[nakhairbhinnaḥ] @deva[नखभिन्नः] @[nakhabhinnaḥ] torn with the nails etc.

(c) with the words[^3] @deva[पूर्व] @[pūrva], @deva[सह] @[saha], @deva[सम] @[sama], @deva[ऊन] @[ūna] and words having the sense of @deva[ऊन] @[ūna], @deva[कलह] @[kalaha], @deva[निपुण] @[nipuṇa], @deva[मिश्र] @[miśra], @deva[स्पृश] @[spṛśa] and @deva[अवर] @[avara]; as @deva[मात्रा पूर्वः] @[mātrā pūrvaḥ] @deva[मातृपूर्वः] @[mātṛpūrvaḥ]; @deva[मात्रा सहः] @[mātrā sahaḥ] @deva[मातृसहः] @[mātṛsahaḥ] like his mother; @deva[पितृसमः] @[pitṛsamaḥ] equal to his father, @deva[मात्रा ऊनं] @[mātrā ūnaṃ] @deva[मातॄणः] @[mātṝṇaḥ]; so @deva[माषविकलः] @[māṣavikalaḥ] (less by one māsha, a particular weight); @deva[वाचा कलहः] @[vācā kalahaḥ] @deva[वाक्कलहः] @[vākkalahaḥ] a quarrel (in words); @deva[आचारनिपुणः] @[ācāranipuṇaḥ]; @deva[दधिमिश्रः] @[dadhimiśraḥ]; @deva[आचारस्पृश्यः] @[ācāraspṛśyaḥ] 'thin by the discharge of religious duties;' @deva[मात्रेण अवरः] @[mātreṇa avaraḥ] @deva[मासावरः] @[māsāvaraḥ] 'younger by a month.'

(d) With another noun signifying an article of food or something eatable, the noun in the Instrumental case denoting some kind of condiment used to season it or another article of food mixed with it;[^4] as @deva[दध्ना औदनः] @[dadhyā audanaḥ] @deva[दध्योदनः] @[dadhyodanaḥ] rice mixed with curds; @deva[घृतेन धानाः] @[ghṛtena dhānāḥ] @deva[घृतधानाः] @[ghṛtadhānāḥ] fried rice mixed with treacle.

(e) Sometimes @deva[स्वयं] @[svayaṃ] may take the place of the Instrumental as @deva[स्वयंकृतः] @[svayaṃkṛtaḥ] done by one's self.
