## § 248. A Vyadhikarana Bah. is that whose members are not in apposition to each other i. e. are in different

§ 248. A Vyadhikarana Bah. is that whose members are not in apposition to each other i. e. are in different cases when dissolved. A Vyadhikarana Bah. is not allowed in any case except in the Genitive and the Locative;* as @deva[चक्रै पाणौ यस्य स चक्रपाणिः हरिः], @deva[चन्द्रस्य इव कान्तिपदेयं स चन्द्रकान्तिः] †; so @deva[पद्मगन्धः], @deva[शेक्षपाणिः], &c. @deva[झूषी शेखरे यस्य अर्तौ शूशिशेखरः] &c.; but @deva[पक्रभिश्छुकूमस्य पक्रश्छुक्तः] is not allowed.
