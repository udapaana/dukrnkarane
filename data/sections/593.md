## § 593. Optional Changes in Passive

The roots @deva[स्वप्], @deva[सप्], @deva[तप्], and @deva[क्षप्] optionally drop their @deva[प्] and lengthen their @deva[अ]. @deva[शी] and @deva[श्रि] form bases as @deva[शय्य] and @deva[श्रय्य]. @deva[कुह्] shortens its @deva[ऊ] when prefixed. @deva[य्] and @deva[अस्] substitute @deva[वच्] and @deva[भू].
