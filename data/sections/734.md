## § 734. @deva[यञ्च्] in the sense of ' to be harnessed ' takes the affix

§ 734. @deva[यञ्च्] in the sense of ' to  be harnessed ' takes the  affix
@deva[क्यप्] and changes its final to @deva[री] ; @deva[पुयुय]: @deva[गो]: ' a bull to be harnessed
to the yoke;' in other sense it takes the affix @deva[त्]; @deva[पुङो].

               By means of the affix @deva[घ्यत्].
