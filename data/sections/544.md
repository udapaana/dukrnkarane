## § 544. 25 Ātmanepadi Roots in Parasmaipada

The following 25 roots, which are all Ātmanepadi, are conjugated in the Parasmaipada when they belong to this Variety. In the Ātmanepada they take the Fourth or Fifth Variety according as they are Anit or Set.
