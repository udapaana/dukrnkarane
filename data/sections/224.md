## § 224. Ku Prefix in Karmadhāraya

The particle @deva[कु] @[ku] forms a Karm. compound with any other word, @deva[कुपुरुषः] @[kupuruṣaḥ] (@deva[कुत्सितः पुरुषः] @[kutsitaḥ puruṣaḥ]) a bad man; @deva[कुपुत्रः] @[kuputraḥ] etc.

(a) @deva[कु] @[ku] is changed to[^1] (1) @deva[कद्] @[kad] when followed by a noun with an initial vowel in a Tat. by @deva[त्रि] @[tri] and the nouns @deva[रथ] @[ratha] and @deva[वद्] @[vad] and by @deva[हन] @[hana] 'denoting a species;' @deva[कुत्सितः अश्वः] @[kutsitaḥ aśvaḥ] @deva[कदश्वः] @[kadaśvaḥ] 'bad horse;' so @deva[कदन्नम्] @[kadannam] 'coarse food;' but @deva[कूदुः] @[kūduḥ] 'having bad camels' (a Bah. comp.) @deva[राज्ञा] @[rājñā]; @deva[कुत्सिताः त्रयः] @[kutsitāḥ trayaḥ] @deva[कत्रयः] @[katrayaḥ] 'three inferior things;' @deva[कद्रथः] @[kadrathaḥ] 'a bad chariot,' @deva[कद्वदः] @[kadvadaḥ] 'speaking ill, a bad speaker;' @deva[कदूषम्] @[kadūṣam] 'a kind of fragrant grass.'

(2) To @deva[का] @[kā] when followed by @deva[पृथु] @[pṛthu] and @deva[अक्ष] @[akṣa] and when meaning 'a little;' and optionally when prefixed to @deva[पुरुष] @[puruṣa], @deva[कापृथु] @[kāpṛthu], @deva[काक्षः] @[kākṣaḥ] 'a sidelong look or a frown of displeasure,' see Bh.V. 54 (@deva[अक्षिभ्यामुद्देशेन बहुनोक्तित्वम्] @[akṣibhyāmuddeśena bahunoktitvam] Sid. Kau.). @deva[ईषज्जलम्] @[īṣajjalam] @deva[काजलम्] @[kājalam] 'a little water,' @deva[ईषत् पुरुषः] @[īṣat puruṣaḥ] @deva[कापुरुषः] @[kāpuruṣaḥ]; but @deva[कुत्सितः पुरुषः] @[kutsitaḥ puruṣaḥ] @deva[कुपुरुषः] @[kupuruṣaḥ] or @deva[कापुरुषः] @[kāpuruṣaḥ].

(3) And to @deva[का] @[kā] or @deva[कव्] @[kav] when followed by @deva[उष्ण] @[uṣṇa]; @deva[कौष्णम्] @[kauṣṇam], @deva[कवोष्णम्] @[kavoṣṇam] and @deva[कदुष्णम्] @[kaduṣṇam] by (1), all meaning 'luke-warm'.
