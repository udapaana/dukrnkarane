## § 926. The Imperfect Tense

The Imperfect according to Pāṇini denotes past action not done to-day, i.e. done at some time prior to the current day:

**Example:**

- @deva[तुयूभुषुनं पोलुस्युयु] @[tuyūbhuṣunaṃ polusyuyu] (Bhaṭṭi) Bibhīshana spoke to them

---
