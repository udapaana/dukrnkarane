## § 575. Optional @deva[इ] for @deva[ईट्] in Certain Roots

The roots @deva[दीप्], @deva[जन्], @deva[पूर्], @deva[तान्] and @deva[व्याध्] optionally substitute @deva[इ] for @deva[ईट्] (third person sing. Atm.).
