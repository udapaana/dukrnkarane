## § 77. Root nouns in @deva[ई] or @deva[ऊ] :M. F. N.

§ 77. Root nouns in @deva[ई] or @deva[ऊ] :M. F. N.

Rule of Sandhi—(a) The ending @deva[ई] or @deva[उ], short or long, of nouns derived from roots with the affix @deva[किप्].(o) and of @deva[ऋ], is changed to @deva[इय्] or @deva[उव्] before the vowel terminations*: the fem. nouns of this description are optionally declined like @deva[नदी] in the Da. Ab. Gen. and Loc. singulars and the Gen. plu,

  (b) But if the ending @deva[ई] or @deva[उ] of a many-voweled noun having a root at the end be not preceded by a radical conjunct conso-nant or the root noun has a preposition termed @deva[गति] (e. g. as added to the root ) or a word governed by the root preceding it, it is changed to @deva[ण] or @deva[झ], except in the case of @deva[सुधी] and nouns ending in @deva[स्त]†.
