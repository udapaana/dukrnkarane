## § 477. @deva[वी] is substituted for @deva[अच्] 1 P. ' to go ' necessarily before any non-conjugational termination

§ 477.  @deva[वी] is substituted  for  @deva[अच्]  1 P. ' to go '  necessarily before  any  non-conjugational  termination,  and  optionally before such as begin with any consonant except @deva[य] , @deva[चेता],  @deva[अचिता];
@deva[चेयाम],  @deva[अचियाम] &c.

        ( 2 ) Second Future ( @deva[लुट्] ) and  ( 3 )  Conditional ( @deva[लुड्]_ ).
