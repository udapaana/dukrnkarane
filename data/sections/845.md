## § 845. ~ The words @deva[स्तोकं] 'a little' @deva[अल्युयु] 'a little,' @deva[ङुच्छु] 'diffi-

§  845  ~ The words  @deva[स्तोकं]  'a  little'  @deva[अल्युयु]  'a  little,'  @deva[ङुच्छु]  'diffi-
culty,'  and  @deva[कुतिपुयु]  'some'  when  used  in  an  adverbial sense with
verbs, are  used  in  the  Ablative  with  a  sense  of  'beginning
@deva[कुतिपुयुनं  युयुदु]  let off with little; similarly  @deva[अल्युनुतु  अलुयुयुन्युच्युयुच्युः  ङुच्युयुयुनुयु
कुयुष्टुयुहुयो छुत्तुं] dose  with  difficulty;  @deva[कुतिपुयुनं  कुतिपुयुयुयुयु  मायुयु].  but
@deva[स्तोकुनं  विषुयुनं  हतुः]  killed  with  a  little  poison;  when  used  adver-
bially they take the Accusative also; @deva[स्तोकं  गच्छुति]  goes a little.
