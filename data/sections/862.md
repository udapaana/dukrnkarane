## § 862. The Genitive is used with· indeclinables like @deva[मुयुध्युयुनै],

§ 862.  The  Genitive  is  used  with·  indeclinables  like  @deva[मुयुध्युयुनै],
@deva[परिरै], @deva[छुतै], &c.; @deva[गङ्गुयुयुयु यस्युयुनै  यरै वा] in  the  middle  or  on  the  other
side of the Ganges; @deva[अर्युनों ग्रायुयुनों छुते] for the sake of this life.
