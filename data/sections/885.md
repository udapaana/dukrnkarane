## § 885. The pronouns @deva[एकुच्] and @deva[अयुयुर] or @deva[अन्युयु] are used in the

§ 885. The pronouns @deva[एकुच्] and @deva[अयुयुर] or @deva[अन्युयु] are used in the
plural in the sense of 'some—others;' @deva[विपुवुयुयुयुः पुनुयुयुयुयुयुः सदुयूकुयु
इयुनैकं शाशुयुयुयूतिरुयुच्युद् द्युयुन्युयुनै कुलो निरुयुदुट् द्रुयुयुयुरु] some think that widow-
remarriage is sanctioned by the Ś'astras, some say that it is pro-
hibited by them, while others hold that it was allowed in the
Kali age. @deva[कोऽयुट्] may take the place of @deva[एकुनै].
