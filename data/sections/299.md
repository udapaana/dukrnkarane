## § 299. @deva[वक्तव्यो व्युत्पत्तौ ॥]

§ 299. @deva[वक्तव्यो व्युत्पत्तौ ॥]

The word @deva[वक्तव्य] 'should be stated' is used when explaining the derivation or etymology of an Avyayībhāva compound.

Obs. This is a general note about how to explain the formation of these compounds in grammatical analysis.
