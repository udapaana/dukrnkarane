## § 884. The pronoun @deva[तद्] has often the sense of 'well known,

§ 884. The pronoun @deva[तद्] has often the sense of 'well known,
renowned,' &c. @deva[तौ यार्वुतीपुरुदैनुभुदो] those (well-known) Pārvatī and
Parameśvara; @deva[त्युयुच्युनैयु वनुस्युयूलुयुति] those well-known forest sites.

(a) When repeated, this pronoun has the sense of 'various,
several;' @deva[तैयु तैयु रयुयुनीयुयु स्युयुनैयु] in those various highly delightful
spots; @deva[छुटोयुति तैतैः युयुत्युनै]: notwithstanding several efforts–@deva[कुयूहेतैः
स्त्युदुटुतुयुत्युन]: &c (Bhag. VII. 20).
