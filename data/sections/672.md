## § 672. The participle of the Pre, Passive is formed by adding

§ 672. The participle of the Pre, Passive is formed by adding
@deva[मान] to the Passive base in @deva[यु]; e g. @deva[श्रुष्यमान] ' who or what is
known, ' @deva[अयुमान] ' what is eaten, ' @deva[दीयुमान] who or what is given,
@deva[जीयुमान] ' who or what is gathered, ' @deva[किंयुमान] ' what is done, '
@deva[कू]—@deva[कीर्युमाण] ' what is scattered, ' @deva[चोर्युमाण] ' who or what is
stolen ' @deva[शुय्]—cau. Pass. @deva[चोष्युमान] ' who or what is caused to
know, ' Des.   Pass.  @deva[वुवोषियुमाण] ' who  or  what  is  desired
to know, ' &c.
