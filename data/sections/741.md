## § 741. The roots @deva[भू] and @deva[भै] take the affix @deva[यत्] ; @deva[भव्य] and @deva[स्था]

§ 741.  The  roots  @deva[भू]  and @deva[भै] take  the  affix  @deva[यत्] ;  @deva[भव्य] and @deva[स्था]
take @deva[अनीय];  and @deva[चत्] , @deva[यु]  and  @deva[पच्]  take  @deva[घ्यत्] ,  in an  active  sense;
@deva[अदुर्तोतो भव्युं] ( also  @deva[भव्युभुनुनं] );  @deva[भायुवातो  गेव]: ' one who sings'
( also @deva[गेयुं राम अनेनै] ); @deva[प्रवुचनौय]: 'one who speaks' @deva[उरु] , @deva[वानौय]: ' one
who stands for,' @deva[स्तुयु] , @deva[पुचोय युरितिर्यै]:, @deva[पात्युयुं] ' food.'

                ( 3 ) By means of @deva[के]'@deva[लिमुर्] ( @deva[एलिम्] ).
