## § 670. ( a ) The @deva[आ] of @deva[आन्] is changed to @deva[इ] in the case of

§ 670.   ( a ) The @deva[आ] of @deva[आन्] is  changed to @deva[इ]  in  the  case of
the root @deva[आस्] ( 2. cl. ) ' to sit;' Pre. P. @deva[आसीन].

. ( b ) @deva[आन] added to the roots  @deva[यु]  and  @deva[वज्]  forms  nouns;  e.  g.
@deva[पवमान]: ' that which purifies, hence, the wind' (cf. Rāg. VIII. 9,)
also, 'one of the sacred fires;' @deva[प्रजमान]: 'one who sacrifices.'
