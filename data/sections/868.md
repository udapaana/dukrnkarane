## § 868. † The words @deva[सायुयु] and @deva[निपुयुणु], not preceded by the pre-

§  868.  † The  words  @deva[सायुयु]  and  @deva[निपुयुणु],  not  preceded  by  the  pre-
positions  @deva[अनु],  @deva[परिंङु]  and  @deva[यति],  are  used  with  the  Loc.  when  the
sense of adoration is to be conveyed; @deva[यायुयुरिंङु तिष्युपुरितुयोरुयुति] the son is par-
tially disposed  towards  his  mother;  but  @deva[निपुयुणुः  रात्युयुः  भूयुयुः]  a  clever
servant of the king.  When  preceded  by  the  prepositions  @deva[अनु],  @deva[परिंङु]
and @deva[यति] these are used  with  the  Accusative;  @deva[सायुयुयुनुवुयुणो  भायुयुतुरुयुं
यति  युयुदुट् वा].
