## § 892. The present participle is to be used when contem-

§ 892. The present participle is to be used when contem-
poraneity of action is to be indicated. It is often idiomatically
used to express the sense of 'while' or 'whilst' in English; @deva[अरुयुयुयुनै
चुरुट्] while wandering in the forest; @deva[विवुयुहुड्युयोदुकुयुं विभुनुत एवु] while
he yet wore the marriage string
Vide 670 (b).
