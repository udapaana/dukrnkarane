## § 946. Imperative with Particle @deva[स्म] @[sma]

The Imperative with the particle @deva[स्म] @[sma] is used when a request is courteously expressed:

**Example:**

@deva[बालंयायतु स्म] @[bālaṃyāyatu sma] please teach the child.
