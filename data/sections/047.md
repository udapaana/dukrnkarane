## § 47. Visarga to ओ Before Short अ or Soft Consonants

Visarga (substituted for @deva[स्] @[s] and not @deva[र्] @[r]) preceded by short @deva[अ] @[a] and followed by short @deva[अ] @[a] or a soft consonant is changed to @deva[ओ] @[o]; @deva[शिवः + अच्युतः = शिव + ओ + अच्युतः = शिवोऽच्युतः] @[śivaḥ + acyutaḥ = śiva + o + acyutaḥ = śivo'cyutaḥ] Śiva is adorable (see [§ 25](rule_025.md)); @deva[देवः + वन्द्यः = देवो वन्द्यः] @[devaḥ + vandyaḥ = devo vandyaḥ] God should be saluted; but @deva[तिष्ठतु पयः अत्र त्रिदश] @[tiṣṭhatu payaḥ atra tridaśa] (where the @deva[अ] @[a] after @deva[पयः] @[payaḥ] is pluta); @deva[प्रातः + अत्र = प्रातरत्र] @[prātaḥ + atra = prātaratra] (for here the visarga is substituted for @deva[र्] @[r]); so @deva[प्रातरगच्छ] @[prātaragaccha] etc.
