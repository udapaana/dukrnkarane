## § 490. The Periphrastic Perfect is formed of all roots beginning with any vowel except @deva[अ] or @deva[आ] which i

§ 490.  The Periphrastic  Perfect  is  formed  of  all  roots beginning with any vowel except @deva[अ] or @deva[आ] which is  naturally or prosodially long, and of roots of more than one syllable  ( roots of the 10th class and other  derivative  roots  included ). Exceptions:—@deva[ऊण्] and @deva[चच्छ्] which take the Reduplicative Perfects.
