## § 143. Correlatives are formed by the affixes @deva[यत्] added

§ 143. Correlatives are formed by the affixes @deva[यत्] added
to @deva[यद्], @deva[तद्] and @deva[एतद्] , and @deva[यत्] added to @deva[इदम्] and @deva[किम्] in the sense
of 'as much as;' and @deva[ईश] or @deva[इश] in the sense of 'similar to';
@deva[तद्], @deva[एतद्] and @deva[यद्] become @deva[ता], @deva[एता] and @deva[या] respectively before
these, and @deva[इदम्] and @deva[किम् इ] and @deva[कि] before @deva[यत्] and @deva[ई] and @deva[की]
before @deva[ईश] or @deva[इश] ; e. g. @deva[तावत्] ( @deva[तःपरिमाणमस्य] ), @deva[ईयत्] ( @deva[ईद् परिमाण]-
@deva[मस्य] ), @deva[ताइश] like that, @deva[ईइश], like this, @deva[कियद्] how much, &c.
