## § 162. The Tad. affix @deva[अ]* may be used, instead of @deva[अधिक],

§ 162. The Tad. affix @deva[अ]* may be used, instead of @deva[अधिक],
in the case of the cardinal numbers ending in @deva[दशन्] , the numeral
@deva[विंशति] and those ending in @deva[शत्] i. e. in the case of the numerals
111-159, 211-259, 311-359, &c. and qualifying the numerals
@deva[शत] and @deva[सहस्र] before which the final @deva[अत्] , @deva[अति] and @deva[अत्] are dropped
e. g. @deva[एकादशं शतम्] 111, @deva[विंशं शतं] 120, @deva[पञ्चाशं शतं] 150, @deva[सप्तदशं द्विशतं]
217, @deva[त्रिंशं त्रिशतं] 330 and so on.
