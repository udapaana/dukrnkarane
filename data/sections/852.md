## § 852. * @deva[वाञु] meaning 'to wish' governs the Genitive when

§ 852.  * @deva[वाञु]  meaning  'to  wish'  governs  the  Genitive  when
the idea of a  benediction  is  implied;  @deva[युयुत्तुयु  नायुस्युयु]  wish  to  have
patience; @deva[युत्तुस्युयु मायुयुनं] desires to have wealth. So @deva[सर्पियुः मायुयुनुयुस्युयु] .
