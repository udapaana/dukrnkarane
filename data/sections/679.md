## § 679. The Past Passive Participle is formed by the addition

§ 679. The Past Passive Participle is formed by  the addition
of the affix @deva[त्] to the root, e. g. @deva[स्ना]–@deva[स्नात] ' bathed, ' @deva[शिं]–@deva[शित] ' con-
quered, ' @deva[नी]–@deva[नीत] ' carried,' @deva[श्रु]-@deva[श्रुत] 'heard,' @deva[स्वु]-@deva[रुब्ध] ' become,' @deva[हृ]-@deva[हृत]
'taken away,' @deva[त्यज्]-@deva[त्यक्त] 'abandoned,' @deva[चिन्त्]-@deva[चिन्त्], ' thought or me-
ditated upon,' &c.
