## § 286. @deva[इष्टप्राप्त्योः] 

§ 286. @deva[इष्टप्राप्त्योः ।

सा] also enters into compounds when the sense is 'wish' or 'attainment'.

Examples:— @deva[सकामम्] 'according to wish', @deva[साधिपम्] 'unto the sovereign', @deva[सालम्बम्] 'with support'.
