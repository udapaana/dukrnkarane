## § 587. Guṇa Before Strong Terminations

The Atm. terminations are strong. Before these radical vowel takes its Guṇa substitute, but when the intermediate @deva[इ] is not prefixed, the final @deva[ऋ] remains unchanged and @deva[उ] is changed to @deva[इट्] or @deva[उट्].
