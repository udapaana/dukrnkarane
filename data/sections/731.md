## § 731. The roots @deva[यु] , @deva[नी] and @deva[जि], preceded by @deva[हि], take this

§ 731.  The roots @deva[यु] , @deva[नी] and @deva[जि],  preceded by  @deva[हि],  take  this
affix, when they are connected with the words @deva[मुञ्ज], @deva[यट्यकु]  and  @deva[इळु]
respectively, @deva[विपुर्णो हुञ्ज]:' The Munja grass to  be  prepared  for
weaving into  a  rope ' ( @deva[मुञ्जात्रुकुर्णाय शोधनीयस्य  इत्यर्थ]: Sid.
Kau.  ); @deva[तिवीदै]: @deva[कट्यकु]: ' the girdle ought  to be destroyed;' @deva[शिहुविंटौ]
