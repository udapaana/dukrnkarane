## § 256. @deva[नासिका] (Nose) - Transformations in Bahuvrihi Compounds

### Main Text

(d) * @deva[नासिका] becomes @deva[नास] when it is preceded by a preposition or when the compound is used as a distinguishing name and it is preceded by any word except @deva[स्फूल]; @deva[उद्धता नासिका यस्य स उन्नासः] 'one having a prominent nose,' @deva[सुनसः] 'one having a good nose' @deva[द्रुतिव नासिका यस्य द्रुनसः] † 'one whose nose is like a tree,' 'large-nosed;' but @deva[स्फुलनासिकः]. @deva[नास] becomes @deva[नष्] optionally when preceded by @deva[खुर] or @deva[खर]; as @deva[खुरनसः]. or @deva[खुरनाः] 'having a nose like a horse's hoof i. e. flat;' @deva[खरनसः-नाः] 'sharp-nosed' @deva[नासिका] when preceded by @deva[वि] becomes @deva[य] or @deva[यस्], as @deva[विगमा नासिका यस्य स विघ्] or @deva[विघ्नः] 'one having a deformed nose.'
