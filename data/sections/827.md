## § 827. ‡ (a) A noun expressive of the purpose for which

§827.  ‡ (a) A  noun  expressive  of  the  purpose  for  which
anything  is  done,  or  the  result  to  which  anything  pertains,  or
action  for  which  anything  exists,  is  put  in  the  Dative  case;
@deva[छुकुरुयु  हरिं  भजुति]  'worships  Hari  for  final beatitude;'  @deva[भकितुयुगुयुयु
कुरुनं घस्युयुनं जायुतं वो] ' devotion  leads  to  knowledge;'  @deva[सुड्युयु
कुरुनं जायुतं सन्युबुनं  युयुयुयुरुः]  (Mb.  )  'water  gruel  tends  to
(produce)  fire,'  @deva[सुयुर्णुयुयु  विरुयुयुर्णों  हिरुण्युयुं]  (Mah.  Bhā.)  'gold  for
Kuṇḍala  (a  kind  of  ornament  );'  @deva[सुयुयुयु  दुयुरुयु]  '  wood  for  a
sacrificial post,' &c.
