## § 800. Roots having the sense of motion, real or metaphori-

§ 800  Roots having the sense of motion,  real  or  metaphori-
cal  govern  the Accusative  of  the  place  to  which it is directed.
@deva[ग्रामं  गच्छुति]  goes to a village; @deva[अजवुयुचुरुयुङुयु  विच्युचुत्युरु  दाव्युदं]  with his
bow strung he roamed all over the forest; @deva[आनन्दुस्य परां काष्ठुमुष्टु]-
@deva[गच्छुत]  they  reached  the  highest  point  of  joy;  @deva[वनस्य  कृष्णुयुङूतिं]
goes  to  Kṛiṣṇa (discus  of him) mentally;  @deva[अतः  प्रभृति  चिन्तुयुङोद्युच्  स  निद्रां
यथो]  while thus pondering he sank into sleep.
