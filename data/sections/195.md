## § 195. When a Samāhāra-Dwandwa. ends in a consonant

§ 195. When a Samāhāra-Dwandwa. ends in a consonant
of the palatal class or @deva[च], @deva[ष] or @deva[ङ], @deva[अ] is added to it*, @deva[ज्ञाक् च त्वक्
च वाक्त्वचम्] speech and skin, @deva[त्वक्कञ्चम्] a bark and a garland,
@deva[ज्ञाभीङ्वदम्], @deva[वाक्स्वङ्गम्], @deva[छत्रोपानहम्] an umbrella and shoes, &c,
But @deva[ज्ञाट्टुशारदौ] ( as it is not a Sam. Dwa. ).
