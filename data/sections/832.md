## § 832. * The object of roots implying motion (i. e. the

§ 832  * The  object  of  roots  implying  motion  (i.  e.  the
place  to  which  the  motion is directed),  if  it  be not a road,  is
put in  the  Accusative  or  the  Dative,  when  actual  motion  is
meant;  @deva[ग्रामं  मायुयु  वा  गच्छुति]  'goes to a village;'  but  @deva[मनसा  हरिं
भुजुति],  @deva[पुरुस्युयुं  गच्छुति].
