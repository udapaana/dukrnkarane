## § 340. List of Common Taddhita Affixes - Section I: Miscellaneous Affixes

The following is a list of such of the Taddhita affixes as are commonly to be met with.

### SECTION I. MISCELLANEOUS AFFIXES

**@deva[अ] @[a]** is added in the sense of:

(1) 'the son of':

e.g. @deva[उपगोः अपत्यं पुमान् औपगवः] @[upagoḥ apatyaṃ pumān aupagovaḥ]: the son of Upagu

e.g. @deva[वासुदेवः] @[vāsudevaḥ] from @deva[वसुदेव] @[vasudeva]

e.g. @deva[पर्वतस्य अपत्यं स्त्री पार्वती] @[parvatasya apatyaṃ strī pārvatī]: the daughter of the mountain, etc.

(2) 'the descendant of':

e.g. @deva[उत्सस्य गोत्रापत्यं पुमान् औत्सः] @[utsasya gotrāpatyaṃ pumān autsaḥ]: a descendant of Utsa

e.g. @deva[उत्सस्य गोत्रापत्यं स्त्री औत्सी] @[utsasya gotrāpatyaṃ strī autsī]: a female descendant of Utsa (vide § 311, 313)

(3) 'dyed with':

e.g. @deva[हरिद्रया रक्तं हारिद्रं वसनं] @[haridrayā raktaṃ hāridraṃ vasanaṃ]: a garment dyed with turmeric

(4) 'made of':

e.g. @deva[देवदारुविकारः दैवदारवः] @[devadāruvikāraḥ daidādaravaḥ]: made of the fir tree

(5) 'belonging to, etc.':

e.g. @deva[देवस्य अयं देवः] @[devasya ayaṃ devaḥ]: belonging to a god

e.g. @deva[शर्कराया इदं शार्करं] @[śarkarāyā idaṃ śārkaraṃ]: of sand

e.g. @deva[ऊर्णाया इदं और्णं वस्त्रं] @[ūrṇāyā idaṃ aurṇaṃ vastraṃ]: a woolen garment

e.g. @deva[ग्रैष्मः] @[graiṣmaḥ]: belonging to the sultry season

e.g. @deva[नैशः] @[naiśaḥ]: nocturnal

e.g. @deva[सांवत्सरः] @[sāṃvatsaraḥ]: yearly, etc.

When added to @deva[हेमन्त] @[hemanta] the final @deva[त] @[ta] is dropped, @deva[हेमनः] @[hemanaḥ]: autumnal (vide Śiśupālavadha VI.65; Kirātārjunīya XVII.12). @deva[हैमन्तः] @[haimantaḥ] means 'agreeable in the autumnal season'.

(6) 'lord of':

e.g. @deva[पृथिव्या ईश्वरः पार्थिवः] @[pṛthivyā īśvaraḥ pārthivaḥ]: the lord of the earth

e.g. @deva[पञ्चालानां नृपतिः पाञ्चालः] @[pañcālānāṃ nṛpatiḥ pāñcālaḥ]: the king of the Pañcālas

e.g. @deva[ऐक्ष्वाकः] @[aikṣvākaḥ]: the king of the Ikṣvāku race

(7) 'a collection of':

e.g. @deva[काकानां समूहः काकं] @[kākānāṃ samūhaḥ kākaṃ]

e.g. @deva[बकानां समूहः बाकं] @[bakānāṃ samūhaḥ bākaṃ]: a flock of cranes

Similarly @deva[मायूरं] @[māyūraṃ] from @deva[मयूर] @[mayūra] 'a peacock', @deva[कापोतं] @[kāpotaṃ] from @deva[कपोत] @[kapota] 'a pigeon', @deva[भैक्षाणां समूहो भैक्षं] @[bhaikṣāṇāṃ samūho bhaikṣaṃ]; @deva[गर्भिणीनां समूहो गार्भिणं] @[garbhiṇīnāṃ samūho gārbhiṇaṃ], etc.

(8) 'knowing or studying':

e.g. @deva[व्याकरणं अधीते वेद वा वैयाकरणः] @[vyākaraṇaṃ adhīte veda vā vaiyākaraṇaḥ]: one who studies grammar, grammarian, etc.

(9) and to form abstract nouns:

e.g. @deva[मुनेः भावः मौनं] @[muneḥ bhāvaḥ maunaṃ]: silence

e.g. @deva[युवन्] @[yuvan] → @deva[यौवनं] @[yauvanaṃ]: youth

e.g. @deva[सुहृद्] @[suhṛd] → @deva[सौहार्दं] @[sauhārdaṃ]: friendship

e.g. @deva[पथोर्भावः पार्थवं] @[pathorbhāvaḥ pārthavaṃ]: greatness, width, etc.
