## § 174. @deva[ईयस्] is another termination of the comparative degree

§ 174.  @deva[ईयस्] is another termination of the comparative degree
and @deva[ईष्] of the superlative.  These are added  to adjectives of
quality only †   Before these the last vowel of a word with the
following consonant if any is dropped; e g. @deva[लघु]-@deva[लघीयस्] , @deva[लघिष्ठ] ;
@deva[यट्], @deva[पटीयस्] , @deva[पटिष्ठ]; @deva[महत् महीयस्] , @deva[महिष्ठ]; &c. but @deva[पाच्कतर], @deva[पाच्कतम]
only.
