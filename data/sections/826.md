## § 826. † T'e root @deva[क्री] with @deva[परि] 'to hire, as a servent,' &c.,

§ 826  † T'e root @deva[क्री] with @deva[परि]  'to  hire,  as a  servent,'  &c.,
optionally  governs  the  Dative  (and  optionally  the  Inst  )
of  the  price  at  which  he  is  hired  @deva[शुतेनं  शुत्युयु  वा  परिक्रीतुः  ।]
Sid.  Kau.
