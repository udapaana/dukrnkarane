## § 393. Imperfect.

§ 393

                          Imperfect.

§ 293.  The augment @deva[अ] is prefixed to roots in this tense. This @deva[अ] is replaced by @deva[आ] in the case of roots beginning with a vowel*; this @deva[आ] forms Vṛddhi with an initial vowel; e. g. @deva[अ]+@deva[ह्रत्]+@deva[त्]=@deva[आ]+@deva[ह्रत्]+@deva[अ]+@deva[त्]=@deva[ऐह्रत्]; similarly @deva[ईक्ष्]-@deva[ऐक्षत्]; @deva[उक्ष्]-@deva[औक्षत्]; @deva[ऊह्]-@deva[औहत्]; @deva[ऋच्छ्]-@deva[आर्च्छत्] &c.

    ( a )  When a preposition ( @deva[उपसर्ग] ) is prefixed to a root, the augment @deva[अ] or @deva[आ] comes between the prep.  and the roo:s; e. g. @deva[इ] with @deva[प्र], @deva[प्राहरत्].
