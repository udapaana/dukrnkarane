## § 285. @deva[दिक्शब्देभ्यः सस्य] 

§ 285. @deva[दिक्शब्देभ्यः सस्य ।]

After words denoting quarters or directions, @deva[सा] (originally @deva[सह] 'with') enters into an Avyayībhāva compound.

Examples:— @deva[सपूर्वम्] 'from the east', @deva[सपश्चात्] 'from the west', @deva[सदक्षिणम्] 'from the south', @deva[सोत्तरम्] 'from the north'.
