## § 831. 'The indirect object of the root @deva[वृट्]. i. e. 'to think'

§ 831.  'The indirect object of the root @deva[वृट्]. i. e. 'to think'
or  to  esteem  @deva[युत्युयुनु  युङुयुयुनै  युनुयुयुङु]  (the  Dative  or  even  the  Gen.  may  be  used
in  this  sense);  one  says  @deva[पुयुनुत्युयुनै  युस्युयुनो  युयुयुदुयुनैयुयुच  गुयुयुत];  one  does
not consider thee a stray; but  @deva[युट्  युदुयुङु  युयुयुनं]  (@deva[युङु  युट्युट्  ।]  )  when
one  says  @deva[प्रयुयुनुयुनं  युट्युयुयुनै  युयुयुनुयुनुवेङु]  'I  am  in  fear  in  this  as  @deva[युङु  युदुयुङु
युयुयुनं]  (Acc.  )
