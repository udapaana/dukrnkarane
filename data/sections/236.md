## § 236. Sometimes the second member of an Upapada Tat. my be a Namulanta or the verbal indeclinable in @deva[अम्];

§ 236. Sometimes the second member of an Upapada Tat. my be a Namulanta or the verbal indeclinable in @deva[अम्]; as @deva[स्वादुकारि भुङ्क्ते] 'he eats having sweetened the food;' @deva[अग्रेभोजं] 'having dined first;' sometimes this compound is optional. @deva[मूलकोदर्भे] or @deva[मूलैकेन उपदंशो भुङ्क्ते] 'he eats the condiment with a raddish,' &c.
