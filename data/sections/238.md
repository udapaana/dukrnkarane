## § 238. *The word @deva[अकुलि] at the end of a Tatpurusha compound changes its final to @deva[अं] when preceded by a numer

§ 238. *The word @deva[अकुलि] at the end of a Tatpurusha compound changes its final to @deva[अं] when preceded by a numeral or an indeclinable, as @deva[द्वै अङ्गुली प्रमाणमस्य द्वयङ्गुलं दारु] 'a piece of wood measuring two fingers,' @deva[निर्भेतमङ्गुलिभ्यो निरङ्गुलम्] &c.
