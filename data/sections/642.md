## § 642. Changes Before क्यच् Affix

Before this @deva[य] @[ya],

**(1)** final @deva[अ] @[a] and @deva[आ] @[ā] are changed to @deva[ई] @[ī]; @deva[पुत्र आत्मनः इच्छति—पुत्रीयति] @[putra ātmanaḥ icchati—putrīyati] (@deva[पुत्र+य=पुत्री+य+ति] @[putra+ya=putrī+ya+ti]) 'he wishes for a son.'

**(2)** final @deva[इ] @[i] and @deva[उ] @[u] are lengthened; @deva[कवि—कवीयति] @[kavi—kavīyati] 'he wishes for a poet.'

**(3)** final @deva[ऋ] @[ṛ] is changed to @deva[री] @[rī]; @deva[कर्तृ-कर्त्रीयति] @[kartṛ-kartrīyati]

**(4)** final @deva[ओ] @[o] and @deva[औ] @[au] are changed to @deva[अव्] @[av] and @deva[आव्] @[āv]; @deva[गो-गव्यति] @[go-gavyati]; @deva[नौ-नाव्यति] @[nau-nāvyati].

**(5)** a final nasal is dropped and the preceding vowel is changed like an original final vowel; @deva[राजन्-राजीयति] @[rājan-rājīyati] 'he wishes for a king.'

**(6)** in other cases the final consonant remains unchanged; @deva[वाच्-वाच्यति] @[vāc-vācyati] 'he wishes for words,' @deva[दिव्-दिव्यति] @[div-divyati] (@deva[दीव्यति] @[dīvyati] according to some) 'he wishes for heaven,' @deva[समिध्-समिध्यति] @[samidh-samidhyati] 'he wishes for holy sticks,' &c.

**(7)** The Tad. affix expressing 'a descendant of' is dropped and then the changes mentioned above take place, @deva[गार्ग्य] @[gārgya] (the son of @deva[गर्ग] @[garga]) @deva[आत्मनः इच्छति गार्गीयति] @[ātmanaḥ icchati gārgīyati] (@deva[गार्ग्य+य+ति=गार्ग्+य+ति=गार्गी+य+ति] @[gārgya+ya+ti=gārg+ya+ti=gārgī+ya+ti]), &c.
