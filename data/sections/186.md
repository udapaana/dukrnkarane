## § 186. A Dwandwa compound consists of two or more nouns

§ 186.  A Dwandwa compound consists of two or more nouns
which, if not compounded, would be connected by the copulative
particle @deva[च] (and), † as @deva[रामकृष्णौ] which is equal to @deva[रामः च कृष्णः च];
@deva[पानिपादौ] which is the same as @deva[पाणी च पादौ च].  There are three
species of the Dwandwa, viz. @deva[इतरेतरद्वन्द्व], @deva[समाहारद्वन्द्व] and @deva[एकशेष]. ‡
