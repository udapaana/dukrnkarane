§ 388.  @deva[अ] ( @deva[भण्] ) is added on to the roots of the First or @deva[भ्वादि] class* before the terminations.  Before this @deva[अ] the penultimate short and the final vowel of a root take their Guṇa substitute; e. g.

    @deva[बुध्]+@deva[ति]=@deva[बुध्]+@deva[अ] + @deva[ति]=@deva[बोधति]; @deva[जि]+@deva[अ]+@deva[ति] = @deva[जे]+@deva[अ]+@deva[तिः]=@deva[जयति], &c.
