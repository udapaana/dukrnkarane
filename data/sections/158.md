## § 158. When a pronominal word is used as the subordinate

§ 158. When a pronominal word is used as the subordinate
mber of a compound, or is at the end of an Inst. Tat , or of
entence having the sense of the Inst. Tat, or at the end of a
vandwa, it is not declined like a pronoun*; e. g. @deva[अतिक्रान्तः]
