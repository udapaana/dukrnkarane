## § 890. The words @deva[युरु] and @deva[यवुरु] when used in the sense of the

§ 890. The words @deva[युरु] and @deva[यवुरु] when used in the sense of the
superlative govern the Genitive or the Locative; @deva[पुत्रु सर्वुनैत्तुयो युरुः]
'a son is the best of things possessed of touch;' @deva[चतुयुयुयुदुयुनों गोः युवुरु
होहुरुयुनुः कुयुयुर्युनों वरु] 'the cow is the best of quadrupeds and gold of
metals'; the neu. sing. of @deva[युरु] is used (with a word expressive
of negation) in the sense of 'better and not, or but not;' @deva[अकुरुत्युयुयु]-
@deva[न्युनुयुदुयुरुयुनै युर्] doing any thing slowly is better than not doing
