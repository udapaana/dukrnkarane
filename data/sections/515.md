## § 515. अद्, षद् and श्वे Admit इ Before य

The roots @deva[अद्] @[ad], @deva[षद्] @[ṣad] and @deva[श्वे] @[śve] admit @deva[इ] @[i] necessarily before @deva[य] @[ya].
