## § 742. A few transitive roots take the affix @deva[एलिमुर्] having

§ 742.  A  few  transitive  roots  take  the  affix  @deva[एलिमुर्]  having
the same force as the affix @deva[यु]; @deva[पचेलिमु] fr.  @deva[पच्] , 'fit to  ripen or to
be cooked; as @deva[पचेलिमा भावान]:; @deva[भिदेलिमाः] ( fr. @deva[भिद्] ) @deva[स्तृम्बा]: ' the pine
tress ought  to be felled,' &c.
