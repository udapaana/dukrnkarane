## § 643. Insertion of स् and अस् Before क्यच्

The consonant @deva[स्] @[s] and the syllable @deva[अस्] @[as] are inserted between any nominal stem and the affix @deva[य] @[ya]; @deva[मधु आत्मनः इच्छति मधुस्यति] @[madhu ātmanaḥ icchati madhusyati] or @deva[मधस्यति] @[madhasyati] 'he wishes for honey'; so @deva[दृतिस्यति] @[dṛtisyati], @deva[दृष्यस्यति] @[dṛṣyasyati], &c. Final @deva[अ्] @[a] is dropped before @deva[अस्] @[as]; @deva[पुत्रस्यति] @[putrasyati].

**(a)** @deva[अस्] @[as] is inserted after @deva[वृष] @[vṛṣa] and @deva[अश्व] @[aśva] when the sense to be conveyed is that of longing for them, after @deva[क्षीर] @[kṣīra] and @deva[लवण] @[lavaṇa] when the one is desired to be drunk and the other to be licked; @deva[वृषस्यति गौः] @[vṛṣasyati gauḥ] 'the cow longs for the ox,' @deva[अश्वस्यति वडवा] @[aśvasyati vaḍavā] 'the mare longs for the horse,' @deva[क्षीरस्यति बालः] @[kṣīrasyati bālaḥ] 'the child desires to drink,' @deva[लवणस्यति उष्ट्रः] @[lavaṇasyati uṣṭraḥ] 'the camel wishes for licking the salt.' But @deva[वृषीयति] @[vṛṣīyati] 'he desires to have a bull,' @deva[अश्वीयति] @[aśvīyati] 'he wishes for a horse,' @deva[क्षीरीयति] @[kṣīrīyati], @deva[लवणीयति] @[lavaṇīyati].
