§ 395   The penultimate @deva[इ],  @deva[उ]. @deva[ऋ]  or @deva[ऌ]  of a root, followed by @deva[र्] or @deva[व्] is lengthened when a consonant follows; † e. g. @deva[जृम्भ्] 1. A. to measure out, @deva[जृम्भते]; @deva[प्रुष्] to sprinkle @deva[फुः], to act dishonestly, to be crooked, @deva[हुम्भ्]  to faint, @deva[फुषम्भ्] to spread, to forget; @deva[दुर्व्]‌ to thunder, to shine; @deva[जुर्व्], @deva[तुर्व्], @deva[पुर्व्], @deva[दुर्व्], @deva[फुर्व्] all meaning to kill, @deva[रुम्भ्] to try, @deva[हुम्], &c , all Parasm.  and belonging to the 1st class, lengthen their penultimate vowel; @deva[दिह्र] 4   P  @deva[दीह्यति]; similarly @deva[किर्] has @deva[सीव्यति], @deva[दिइ], @deva[शीव्यति], &c.
