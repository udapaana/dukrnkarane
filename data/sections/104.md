## § 104. Bases derived from the root अञ्च् (to go, to worship)

Nouns derived from the root @deva[अञ्च्] @[añc] ('to go', 'to worship') by means of such affixes as @deva[क्विप्] @[kvip], @deva[किप्] @[kip] etc., such as:

- @deva[प्राञ्च्] @[prāñc] (@deva[प्र-अञ्च्] @[pra-añc]): eastern
- @deva[प्रत्यञ्च्] @[pratyañc] (@deva[प्रति-अञ्च्] @[prati-añc]): western
- @deva[सम्यञ्च्] @[samyañc] (@deva[सम्-अञ्च्] @[sam-añc]): a companion
- @deva[तिर्यञ्च्] @[tiryañc] (@deva[तिरद्-अञ्च्] @[tirad-añc]): going away
- @deva[सम्यञ्च्] @[samyañc] (@deva[सम्-अञ्च्] @[sam-añc]): going with or well
- @deva[विष्वञ्च्] @[viṣvañc] (@deva[विष्वब्-अञ्च्] @[viṣvab-añc]): following
- @deva[देवयञ्च्] @[devayañc] (@deva[देव-अञ्च्] @[deva-añc]): adoring a deity
- @deva[सर्वयञ्च्] @[sarvayañc] (@deva[सर्वे-अञ्च्] @[sarve-añc]): going everywhere
- @deva[उदञ्च्] @[udañc]: northern
- @deva[अन्वञ्च्] @[anvañc]: following

### Declension Rules

(a) In the Nom. sing., @deva[अञ्च्] @[añc] becomes @deva[अङ्] @[aṅ]. The nasal of @deva[अञ्च्] @[añc] is dropped before all terminations beginning with the Acc. pl., and the preceding semi-vowel is changed to its corresponding long vowel before the vowel terminations, after which @deva[अ] @[a] is dropped. When @deva[अञ्च्] @[añc] is not preceded by a semi-vowel, its @deva[अ] @[a] is changed to @deva[ई] @[ī] if not preceded by a long vowel; otherwise it remains unchanged. @deva[तिर्यञ्च्] @[tiryañc] becomes @deva[तिरश्च्] @[tiraśc] under similar circumstances. In other respects these words are declined like words ending in @deva[च्] @[c].
