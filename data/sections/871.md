## § 871. * Words expressive of the interval of time or space

§ 871.  * Words  expressive  of  the  interval  of  time or  space
are  used  with  the  Ablative  or  the  Locative;  @deva[अयु  युयुक्तुयुयुयुयुयुं  द्विकुच्]-
@deva[दुयुयुहुयुयु  भोक्तुयु]  having  dined  to-day  he  will  dine  again  after  two
days; @deva[समुयुद्युयुयुनं  शुनुत  द्विकुच्]  or  @deva[द्वयुनं]  frozen  standing  here  as  a  river
a  mark  two  miles  distant.
