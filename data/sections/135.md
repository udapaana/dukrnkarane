## § 135. The pronouns @deva[तद्] 'that or he, sha, it,' @deva[एतद्] 'this,'

§ 135. The pronouns @deva[तद्] 'that or he, sha, it,' @deva[एतद्] 'this,'
@deva[इदम्] 'this' and @deva[अदस्] 'that'. The Nom. sing of the first two is
respectively @deva[सः] and @deva[एषः] mas. and @deva[सा] and @deva[एषा] fem. In other
cases they become @deva[त] and @deva[एत] and are declined like nouns in @deva[अ]
except in the Nom. pl., D. Ab. sings , G. pl. and L. sing. the
terminations of which are @deva[ई], @deva[स्मै], @deva[स्मात्], @deva[इषाम्] and @deva[स्मिन्] mas.
and @deva[तौ], @deva[स्यै] , @deva[स्यास्] , @deva[सास्] and @deva[स्याम्] fem. respectively. All
pronouns ending in @deva[अ] are similarly declined.
