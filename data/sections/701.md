## § 701. Irregular P. P. Participles

The following roots form their P. P. Participles irregularly:

| Roots | P. P. Ptc. | Roots | P. P. Ptc. |
|-------|------------|-------|------------|
| @deva[अनुड्] @[anuḍ] with @deva[अव] @[ava] | @deva[अवनुड] @[avanuḍ], @deva[अनूड] @[anūḍ] | @deva[ध्या] @[dhyā] 'to instruct' | @deva[ध्यात] @[dhyāta] |
| with @deva[सम्] @[sam] | @deva[सनुड] @[sanuḍ], @deva[सनूट] @[sanūṭ] | @deva[दो] @[do] 'to cut' | @deva[दुन्न] @[dunna] |
| with @deva[वि] @[vi] or @deva[नि] @[ni] | @deva[व्युण] @[vyuṇa] | @deva[दुह्] @[duh] 'to be bulky or strong' | @deva[दुह] @[dugdha] |
| with @deva[अभि] @[abhi] in the sense of 'coming near' | @deva[अभ्युण्ण] @[abhyuṇṇa] | otherwise | @deva[दोहित] @[dohita] |
| in other cases | @deva[अबिद्धत] @[abiddhata] | @deva[अद्] @[ad] 'to eat' | @deva[अन्न] @[anna] |
| @deva[ऊर्ण्] @[ūrṇ] 1. A. 'to weave' | @deva[ऊत] @[ūta] | @deva[पाच्] @[pāc] 'to cleanse' | @deva[पीत] @[pīta] or @deva[पाचित] @[pācita] |
| @deva[कृच्छ्] @[kṛcch] 'to be difficult or painful' | @deva[कृच] @[kṛca], @deva[कृछ] @[kṛcha], @deva[कृट] @[kṛṭa], @deva[कृत] @[kṛta], @deva[कृच्छ्राण] @[kṛcchrāṇa]. The grammar of this root is very difficult; @deva[कृच] @[kṛca], @deva[वर्ण] @[varṇa], etc. | @deva[पे] @[pe] 'to suck' | @deva[पीत] @[pīta] |
| but @deva[कृचित] @[kṛcita], @deva[महीमाम्] @[mahīmām] 'gold rubbed on the touchstone' | | @deva[पच्] @[pac] 'to cook' | @deva[पक्व] @[pakva] |
| @deva[कुल्य] @[kulya] 'to be lean' | @deva[कृत] @[kṛta] | @deva[पा] @[pā] 'to drink' | @deva[पीत] @[pīta] |
| @deva[क्षिव्] @[kṣiv] 'to be intoxicated' | @deva[क्षीव] @[kṣīva] | @deva[पूय्] @[pūy] 'to stink, to putrify' | @deva[पूत] @[pūta] |
| @deva[ज्ञाप्] @[jñāp] 'to stink' | @deva[क्षाव] @[kṣāva] | @deva[फल्] @[phal] 'to expand' | @deva[फुल्ल] @[phulla] |
| @deva[चात्] @[cāt] 'to shake' | @deva[चाग] @[cāga] | @deva[बन्ध्] @[bandh] 'to bind' | @deva[बद्ध] @[baddha] |
| @deva[छो] @[cho] 'to be thin' | @deva[छाम] @[chāma] | @deva[मा] @[mā] 'to measure' | @deva[मित] @[mita] |
| @deva[गै] @[gai] 'to sing' | @deva[गीत] @[gīta] | @deva[मृछ] @[mṛch] 'to barter' | @deva[मीत] @[mīta] |
| @deva[छा] @[chā] 'to split' | @deva[छात] @[chāta] or @deva[छिन] @[china] | @deva[मुर्च्छ्] @[mūrcch] 'to faint' | @deva[मूत] @[mūta] or @deva[मूर्च्छित] @[mūrcchita] |
| @deva[त्रिह्] @[trih] 'to go, to become dry' | @deva[त्रुट] @[truṭa] | @deva[स्मृ] @[smṛ] with @deva[उत्] @[ut] | @deva[उद्धृत] @[uddhṛta] 'convalescent' |
| @deva[ह्राद्] @[hrād] 'to delight' | @deva[ह्रेष्ट] @[hreṣṭa] | @deva[स्था] @[sthā] with @deva[प्र] @[pra] or @deva[वि] @[vi] 'a master' | @deva[प्रस्थीत] @[prasthīta] or @deva[प्रस्तीत] @[prastīta] |
| @deva[व्या] @[vyā] 'to cook' (also @deva[व्यञ्] @[vyañ] cau. of @deva[वृत्] @[vṛt]) | @deva[बत] @[bata] 'boiled' (when it qualifies @deva[क्षीर] @[kṣīra] or @deva[पयस्] @[payas]) @deva[व्यञ्जन] @[vyañjana], @deva[अपक्त] @[apakta] | with @deva[परि] @[pari] | @deva[परिद्र] @[paridra] or @deva[परिद्रुहित] @[parudruhita], etc. 'grown, increased' |
| @deva[स्मर्] @[smar] with @deva[प्रति] @[prati] and @deva[वि] @[vi] | @deva[प्रतिस्मुर] @[pratismura], @deva[विस्मर्] @[vismar] (where the @deva[र] @[ra] is not changed to @deva[ऋ] @[ṛ]) | @deva[स्ञा] @[sñā] with @deva[नि] @[ni] | @deva[निङ्गीत] @[niṅgīta] 'clever' |
| @deva[स्फाय्] @[sphāy] 'to grow' | @deva[स्फीत] @[sphīta] | with @deva[नव] @[nava] | @deva[नवीष्ठा] @[navīṣṭhā] 'skilful, experienced, clever' (lit. one who knows the dangerous spots in a river) |
| @deva[स्पृद्] @[spṛd] with @deva[प्र] @[pra] | @deva[प्रस्पीत] @[praspīta] or @deva[प्रस्तीत] @[prastīta] 'sounded' | | but @deva[निवेवात] @[nivevāta], @deva[नवीस्मात] @[navīsmāta] in other cases |
| @deva[शो] @[śo] 'to sharpen' | @deva[शात] @[śāta] or @deva[शित] @[śita] | | |
