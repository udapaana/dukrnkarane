## § 173. @deva[तर] and @deva[तम] when attached to verbs, and indeclinables

§ 173.  @deva[तर] and @deva[तम] when attached to verbs, and indeclinables
forming adverbs, assume the forms of @deva[तराम्] and @deva[तमाम्]*; @deva[पत्तरितराम्], @deva[पत्तितत्तमाम्] ; @deva[उच्चैस्तराम्] ; @deva[उच्चैस्तमाम्] ; @deva[नितराम् नितमाम्] ; or @deva[सुतराम्]
&c ; but @deva[उच्चैस्तरः] adj. ( higher )
