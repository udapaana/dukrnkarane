## § 280. Rule 280

§ 280. @deva[एकवचनम् ।]

The first member of an Avyayībhāva compound must be in the Singular number.

Example:— @deva[यथाशक्ति] 'according to power' (not @deva[यथाशक्तयः]), @deva[अनुरथम्] 'after the chariot' (not @deva[अनुरथान्]).
