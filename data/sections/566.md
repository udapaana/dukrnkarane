## § 566. Vowel Changes in Fourth Form

In Parasmaipada the radical vowel takes Vṛddhi. In Atmanepada, Guṇa is substituted for final @deva[ऋ] or @deva[उ]. Penultimate @deva[ऋ] of Anit roots optionally changed to @deva[र].
