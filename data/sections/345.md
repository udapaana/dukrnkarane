## § 345. Affix I in the Meaning of 'A Son or Descendant'

**@deva[इ] @[i]** is added in the meaning of 'a son or a descendant of':

e.g. @deva[दाक्षिः] @[dākṣiḥ]: the son of Dakṣa

e.g. @deva[व्यासिः] @[vyāsiḥ]: the son of Vyāsa, etc.

In the case of the words @deva[निषाद] @[niṣāda], @deva[चण्डाल] @[caṇḍāla] 'name of a low caste', and @deva[बिम्ब] @[bimba], the final @deva[अ] @[a] is changed to @deva[अक्] @[ak] before this @deva[इ] @[i].
