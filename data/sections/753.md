## § 753. Roots of the 8th class ending in a nasal, except @deva[वत्]

§ 753.  Roots of the 8th class ending in a  nasal,  except @deva[वत्]
and the roots @deva[मन्] , @deva[वन्] and @deva[हुन्] , drop their nasal necessarily; @deva[शुक्] ,
@deva[तन्य] ,  @deva[यत्य] and  @deva[रुयु] do it  optionally;  @deva[तन्]  with  @deva[वि]-@deva[विन्तर्य];  @deva[भुस्] with
@deva[प्रति]-@deva[प्रतिभुष्य]; @deva[तन्य] with @deva[वि]-@deva[विन्तर्य] or @deva[तिवुत्त्यु]; @deva[रुय] with @deva[वि]—@deva[विरुय्य] or
@deva[विरुत्य]; @deva[मत्] with @deva[यू]—@deva[प्रणम्य] or @deva[प्रणत्य], &c
