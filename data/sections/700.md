## § 700. @deva[दा] 'to give,' and @deva[दे], form their P. P. Ptc. as @deva[दत्त]; the

§ 700.  @deva[दा] 'to give,' and @deva[दे],  form their P. P. Ptc. as @deva[दत्त];  the
@deva[व] of @deva[दत्त] is dropped  after a preposition ending in a vowel ; @deva[वर्]-
@deva[वस्त]; but in this latter case the preceding @deva[इ] or @deva[उ] is lengthened ;
@deva[नीत्], @deva[दत्त], &c.; @deva[इ] may optionally be retained also ; @deva[मदत्], @deva[अवदत्],
@deva[डुदत्],* &c.
