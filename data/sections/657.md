## § 657. Verbal bases are formed from the nouns given below

§ 657.  Verbal bases are formed from the nouns given below
by the addition of the affixes @deva[इङ्] ( @deva[गिङ्] ), and @deva[इङ्] ( @deva[गाङ्] ) with vari-
ous senses; those derived by means of @deva[गिङ्] are conjugated in
the Atm. and those by @deva[गाङ्] in the Par; e. g @deva[पुङ्क्ष्]  with @deva[हि], @deva[देत्],
and @deva[घट्]-@deva[शपुष्युङ्यते] 'raises the tail,' @deva[विवुष्युंगति] 'he raises the load:'
@deva[घटे] 'collects together the vessels;' @deva[सुयेश्वरते विष्ठु]. 'drinks @deva[सुरा]
cooked in pots,' @deva[कनीसकरति ङर्] 'causes to sprinkle,' &c
