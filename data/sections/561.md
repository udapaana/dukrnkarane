## § 561. Anit Roots Taking Seventh Form

Anit roots ending in @deva[आ], @deva[इ], @deva[ष] and @deva[ह] and having @deva[इ], @deva[उ], @deva[ऋ] or @deva[ॠ] for their penultimate take this Form. @deva[हृष्] is an exception; it therefore takes the Fourth Variety.
