## § 108. ( 1 ) Consonantal bases ending in @deva[त्] or @deva[द्] drop

§ 108. ( 1 ) Consonantal bases ending in @deva[त्] or @deva[द्] drop
this final letter in the nom. sing, either as being followed by
the special @deva[स्] which is dropped, but produces the dropping of
@deva[त्] or @deva[द्] by the rule @deva[नाम्यपरे] ( Pan. VIII. 2. 7 ), or, @deva[द्] or @deva[त्] is
dropped by the rule @deva[हलन्त्यम्] ( Pan. VIII. 2. 23 ), their place being
supplied ( optionally when initial, and necessarily when non-
initial ) by the @deva[सवर्ण] of the preceding vowel ( Pan. VI. 1. 68 )
if it be short ( @deva[ह्रस्व] ); thus @deva[भगवत्] ( happy or adorable ) takes the
nom. ending @deva[स्]; @deva[द्] becomes @deva[त्] before @deva[स्] by virtue of the rule
