## § 258. || @deva[हृदय] is changed to @deva[हृद्] when preceded by @deva[सु] or @deva[दुश्] meaning 'a friend', or an 'enemy' respectivel

§ 258. || @deva[हृदय] is changed to @deva[हृद्] when preceded by @deva[सु] or @deva[दुश्] meaning 'a friend', or an 'enemy' respectively; @deva[सुर्भं हृदयं यस्य स सुहृद्] 'a friend', @deva[दुष्टं हृ दुर्हृद्] 'an enemy'; but @deva[सुहृदयः] 'one who has got a good heart' i. e. 'one who is able to appreciate merits', &c; @deva[दुर्हृदयः] 'a man of wicked intentions.'
