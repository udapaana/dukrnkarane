§ 10. There are 26 classes of @deva[समवृत्त] @[samavṛtta] or regular metres according to the number of syllables in a @deva[पाद] @[pāda]. A metre with one syllable in each @deva[पाद] @[pāda] is called @deva[एकाक्षरा] @[ekākṣarā]; one with 2, @deva[द्व्यक्षरा] @[dvyakṣarā]; one with 3, @deva[त्र्यक्षरा] @[tryakṣarā], and so on up to 26.
