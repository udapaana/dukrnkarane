## § 283. @deva[उपसर्गादसंज्ञायाम्] 

§ 283. @deva[उपसर्गादसंज्ञायाम् ।]

When the @deva[उपसर्ग] does not receive a technical designation (i.e. when it is not termed @deva[गति] or @deva[कर्मप्रवचनीय]), it is not used in an Avyayībhāva compound.

Example:— We cannot say @deva[प्रभवम्], but should say @deva[प्रभवः], because @deva[प्र] in @deva[प्रभव] is not @deva[गति] or @deva[कर्मप्रवचनीय].
