# § 728

This section appears at the end of page 449. The rule §728 itself is not fully visible on this page, but it introduces content related to irregular formations with the @deva[क्यप्] affix that continues into §729-730.

**Note**: The page shows content for §729-731, which discusses irregular formations with @deva[क्यप्]:

## § 729

The following seven words are irregularly formed with the affix @deva[क्यप्] in the sense given in each case:

- @deva[राजवुद्ः] (@deva[राजन्+ऋ×क्यप्]) @deva[राजा शोतुस्य जनिभुवुत्तारा तिन्यपुन्युक्तिव:य । यङा छुतुम्हुकु: सोम: राजा यु हु: त कल्प्यते लेशः इच्छतुङ्यिक्षङोषौ कच्चु।विषयकैयोसो-संतुर्तैं सेवन द्रुव्यगुणो उत्योचुनैगोषोप्रत्यति:]

- @deva[मुषिय] 'falsehood' (from @deva[मुष्+क्यर्+क्यप्])

- @deva[कुल्प] (any base metal) from @deva[कूप+क्यप्]. Cf. Kir. I.35, Manu. VII.96.

@deva[कूष्] in other cases takes the affix @deva[एयच्]: @deva[कौत्युं] 'what ought to be concealed' @deva[क्षट्युं स्पूयुवेय पुल्मुन छुरुयुष्यात: छुघुकुरोंदि । छुयुक्ष् ङु कर्मोण छुर्णोगीयात्र:] 'growing in cultivated ground'; @deva[इ क्यद्रै अनुल्पेता:] 'too feeble verb'

## § 730

### (a) River Names

The following two words which are the names of two rivers are derived by means of this affix:

- @deva[तितर्ति कुवं विषु:] from @deva[शिद्+क्यप्]
- @deva[ऊम्ज्चुद्युकै उञ्चुप्य:], @deva[उञ्चुद्+क्यप्]; see Rāg XI.8.

In other cases these roots take the affix @deva[त्]: @deva[भेत्त्री], @deva[उन्चिद्धता].

### (b) Constellation Names

Similarly the words @deva[पुषुय:] and @deva[विष्ठुप्]; both the names of the constellation Pushya, are derived from @deva[पुष्] and @deva[तिषु] respectively with the affixes @deva[क्यच्]: (verging towards, @deva[विष्ठुय:], @deva[तिष्ठेत्यौ:])

## § 731

The roots @deva[यु], @deva[नी] and @deva[जि], preceded by @deva[हि], take this affix, when they are connected with the words @deva[मुञ्ज], @deva[यट्यकु] and @deva[इळु] respectively:

- @deva[विपुर्णो हुञ्ज:] 'The Munja grass to be prepared for weaving into a rope' (@deva[मुञ्जात्रुकुर्णाय शोधनीयस्य इत्यर्थ:] Sid. Kau.)
- @deva[तिवीदै: कट्यकु:] 'the girdle ought to be destroyed'
- @deva[शिहुविंटौ]
