## § 936. Second Future for Immediate Action

When the close proximity of a future action is intended, the Second Future or the Present may be used:

**Examples:**

- @deva[कु गमिष्यासि] @[ku gamiṣyāsi] when will you go?
- @deva[एव गच्छामि] @[eva gacchāmi] or @deva[गमिष्यामि] @[gamiṣyāmi] I shall just go
