## § 718. The roots @deva[वक्] implying reproach, @deva[पण्] meaning ' to

§ 718. The roots  @deva[वक्]  implying  reproach,  @deva[पण्]  meaning  ' to
transact business,' and @deva[पु] 9  Atm. not denoting limit or restraint,
take the affix @deva[य]; @deva[इः य] is prefixed to @deva[कृ] if it 'hints reproachingly or
demnation;' but @deva[अनुपु]  ( @deva[अन]+@deva[कु]+@deva[क्यप्।]i e  @deva[य] )  @deva[छुकत्नां] 'the name
of a preceptor  ought  not to be uttered ( out of respect ),' @deva[पण्या
गौ]: 'a cow is saleable;'  but @deva[पाण्यत]: ( @deva[पण्]+@deva[एयत्] i e  @deva[य] )  @deva[ब्राह्मण]: ' a
Brāhmaṇa deserving praise;' @deva[युत्] ( that can be chosen  or sought
after )' a title of @deva[विष्णु कुत्त्रा] ' hundred men ( i. e. any one )  can seek
the hand of a girl;' but @deva[वुत्र्या] ( @deva[इु]+@deva[क्यप्] i.e @deva[य] ) @deva[कन्या] ' to be married
by a particular man.
