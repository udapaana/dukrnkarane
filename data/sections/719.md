## § 719. The root @deva[युह्] not denoting an instrument for carrying,

§ 719.  The root @deva[युह्] not denoting an instrument  for  carrying,
and @deva[ऋ] in the sense of ' a master or a  Vaishya' take @deva[य];  @deva[वहं]  ' a
carriage or a vehicle;' but @deva[बाह] ( @deva[युह्]+@deva[एयत्] ) 'what can be borne,'
@deva[आर्य]: ' a master or ' Vaishya ;' but @deva[आदुर्] ( @deva[ऋ]+@deva[एयत्] ) ' fit to be
approached or adored '
