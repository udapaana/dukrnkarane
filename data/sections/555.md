## § 555. Non-Shortening of Penultimate in Certain Roots

The roots @deva[शास्], @deva[एज्], @deva[कास्], @deva[क्रीड्], @deva[क्षीव्], @deva[खाद्], @deva[सेव्], @deva[दीक्ष्], @deva[ताय्], @deva[दाय्], @deva[दृश्], @deva[नाय्], @deva[पाय्], @deva[वाय्], @deva[याच्], @deva[योध्], @deva[राध्], @deva[राज्], @deva[लाष्], @deva[लेप्], @deva[लोक्], @deva[लोच्], @deva[वेप्], @deva[वञ्च्], @deva[म्लाय्], @deva[म्लोच्], @deva[सेव्], @deva[सेध्], @deva[हेड्] and others do not shorten their penultimate.
