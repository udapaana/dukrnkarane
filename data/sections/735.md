## § 735. Roots ending in @deva[ऋ], and those ending in a consonant

§ 735.  Roots ending in @deva[ऋ], and those ending  in  a  consonant
take the affix @deva[घ्यत्] (@deva[य]) in the same sense  as @deva[क्यद्] . Before this  affix
the ending @deva[चु] and @deva[व] of a root are changed to @deva[वृ] and  @deva[यु]  respecti-
vely and the  final vowel and  the penultimate @deva[अ] take Vṛiddhi
substitute; any other penultimate vowel generally takes  Guṇa.

@deva[छु]—@deva[कार्युं] 'what ought to be done,'  @deva[पू]—@deva[पार्युं] ' what ought to be
worn,' @deva[दा]-@deva[देय]: @deva[हृ]-@deva[हार्युं]; @deva[पद्]-@deva[दुरु]-@deva[पार्यू]: ' what is to be thought of,' &c.;
@deva[वत्]-@deva[वाच्युं] ' what is meant in a sentence, ' @deva[पच्]—@deva[पाच्युं]  'what is  to
be cooked,' @deva[वुज्]—@deva[भोज्युं] ' what is t₫ be purified,' &c.
