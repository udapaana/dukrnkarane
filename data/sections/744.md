## § 744. The Indeclinable Past Participles are of the nature

§ 744.  The Indeclinable  Past Participles are  of  the  nature
of gerunds.  They  fall under two  heads:—( 1 ) Those  derived
by affixing  @deva[स्या] to the simple  root,  and ( 2 )  Those  derived by
means of @deva[य] affixed to the  root  compounded with prepositions or
words used prepositionally;  @deva[गस्]-@deva[गत्वा]  'having  gone,'  @deva[भू]  with
@deva[यश्यस्त्र] ' having ' having served;' &c.

        I  Indeclinable Participles formed with @deva[स्या].
