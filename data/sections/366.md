## § 366. The following are the prepositions ( upasargas ) com-

§ 366.    The following are the prepositions ( upasargas ) com-
monly prefixed to roots:—

@deva[अति]—' beyond, over'; @deva[अतिक्रम]: ' overstepping ' or ' going beyond,
        @deva[अतिसर्जन] ' a gift, &c.'

@deva[अधि]—' over, above, &c ;' @deva[अधिगम]: 'going up, acquisition,'@deva[अधिकार]
        ' high office or power,' @deva[अधिक्षेप]: ' censure,' &c.

@deva[अनु]—' after, behind, along, &c.' @deva[अनुक्रमण] ' following,' @deva[अनुच्छोति]·
        ' imitation,' @deva[अनुग्रह]: ' favour,' &c.

@deva[अप]—' away from, away.' @deva[अपनयनं] ' the act of taking away,' @deva[अपह]·
        ' to plunder, seize ' &c , @deva[अपकार]: ' harm, wrong' &c.

@deva[आपि] ( sometimes @deva[पि] )—'near to,' ' over,' ' taking to,' &c.; @deva[अपिगम्]
        ' to be resolved into or reduced to, * , @deva[अपिधानं] or @deva[पिधानं]
        ' a covering,' @deva[अध्यय]: ' destruction,' &c.

This prep. is more commonly used as an independent adverb
