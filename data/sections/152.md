## § 152. @deva[नेम] meaning 'half' is a pronoun, but it is optionally

§ 152. @deva[नेम] meaning 'half' is a pronoun, but it is optionally
declined like @deva[राम] in the Nom. plu. mas ; in other respects it is
declined like @deva[सर्व].
