§ 87. Bases ending in any of the first four letters of a class and in @deva[थ्], @deva[र्], @deva[ष्], @deva[श्], @deva[ष्], @deva[प्], @deva[स्] and @deva[ह्]. The declension of these bases consists, for the most part, in adding the general case termi nations and observing the rules of sandhi.
