## § 783. (a) When two or more subjects differing in number

§ 783. (a) When two or more  subjects  differing  in  number
are connected by  'and'  the  verb  must  be  plural;  @deva[नरो वा
राजा च भीष्मस्य सङ्गो मन्त्रयिषुः] ' @deva[इदं  आत्म देवा पशुकैः] &c. Mah. Bha.
Sometimes the verb agrees with the  nearest  subject  in  number,
@deva[स वे सेत्युद्युद् देवो रामत्याः च यथास्वमा । राजहरिः  पविदेवो  गान्धारो
चापि निर्दुर्वो ॥] Mah.  Bhā.  @deva[अहुश्च रविमिश्र पेतं  च  सन्ध्ये  घनोऽपि
बाहुतिश्च  भुयुत्युर पात ॥]

(b) But when they are  connected  by 'or'  and are all  sing.
the  verb  will  be  singular,  and  when  the  subject  differ  in
number the verb  will  agree  with  the  one  nearest  to it; @deva[रामः
गोविन्दो वा वनेतु] 'Let Rāma or Govinda  go,' @deva[स  वा  भुमे  वाट्युक्त वा
आदं यह्नन्तु] 'let him or these boys take  the  mango fruit.'
