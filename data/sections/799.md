## § 799. † Intransitive roots govern the Accusative nouns

§ 799.  † Intransitive  roots  govern  the  Accusative  nouns
denoting  space or country,  or  duration  of  time  and  distance;
@deva[इच्युच्युच्  युरिङु  वसति  युरुतुदं]  he  stays  in  the  country  of  the  Kurus;  @deva[तत्रु  कुति]-
@deva[पुयुयुम्  दिवुसं  अवसत्]  there  he dwelt  for  some  days,  @deva[गोदोहिकुयुयुल्युयु]
he  sits  down  till a  cow  is  milked;  @deva[कोशं  प्रतिष्यु]  he walks for a
Koṣa, @deva[कोशो  कुट्युटुटुला  नदी]  the  course of  the  river  is winding for a
Koṣa; but sometimes factive studies whose length @deva[दीर्घुकोशं सत्युच्युयुङुयुदे नरुरु]
the hill is situated at a  part of a Koṣa.
