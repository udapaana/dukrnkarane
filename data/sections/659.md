## § 659. @deva[इङ्] is further added in the following cases;—@deva[कैनपा अभि]-

§ 659.  @deva[इङ्] is further added in the following cases;—@deva[कैनपा अभि]-
@deva[यात]—@deva[आभीषेशयात]; @deva[लाताप अदुभाट]—@deva[अदुभाषयात]; @deva[काणया उपगायत]
—@deva[उपकाणायति], @deva[भूतीके दूपस्तोति]—@deva[उपभूतीकयति]; @deva[तट्ड यूद्वाति]—@deva[तड्यति],
@deva[पाणीज्]-@deva[पाणीजीगति]; @deva[निवायते]-@deva[निवाते] 'clears the air of vapours.';
@deva[चूर्णायति]; @deva[एनीयाञ्चडे]—@deva[एनयते] ( declares her variegated ); &c., &c.

The various changes taking place here before the affix @deva[य] the
student will easily notice as irregularities.

        ( g ) By means of the affix @deva[य] ( @deva[यङ्] ).
