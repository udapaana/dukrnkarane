## § 721. @deva[यु] takes this affix with the negative particle prefixed

§ 721  @deva[यु] takes this affix with the negative particle  prefixed
to  it and  forms  @deva[अजुवं]  'what cannot grow old' but this must
qualify @deva[संगत] 'friendship;' cf  @deva[तैन  संगदायेण रामाच्चयं कुळे द्युतुन ।]
Bhaț̤ṭi. VI. 53. In @deva[युगीरजुवं जरस्तोदुद्युमुदेवुन्याय उत्तसक्षम् ।] Aṅg.
XV III. 7, the word @deva[अजुवं] can be construed as an adverb; but it
does not qualify @deva[संगत], it will take the affix @deva[य]; as @deva[अजरिता कुम्बल्].
