## § 149. Obs :—@deva[उद] and @deva[उद] ( the 10th and 11th @deva[सर्वादि०] ) the one

§ 149. Obs :—@deva[उद] and @deva[उद] ( the 10th and 11th @deva[सर्वादि०] ) the one
@deva[उदात्त] ( udātta ), the other @deva[अनुदात्त] ( anudātta ), both synonyms of
@deva[अन्य] are declined like @deva[सर्व]. The former is of the form @deva[उत्त]
according to some and then it is declined regularly by adding
the terminations; e. g. @deva[स्वत् स्वतौ स्वत्] Nom. &c.
