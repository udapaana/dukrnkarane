## § 792. When an adjective qualifies a vocative noun, it

§ 792.  When  an  adjective  qualifies  a  vocative  noun,  it
takes the case  of  the  antecedent  noun;  or  it  agrees  with  its
noun in the vocative case.  @deva[गुरोऽस्य भोऽग्रज] (@deva[गुरो  भोऽग्रज] )  O  you
that are eldest brother,  &c.
