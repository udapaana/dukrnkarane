## § 165. @deva[एक] is declined in the sing. ( though it may have the

§ 165.  @deva[एक] is declined in the sing. ( though it may have the
dual and plural also ), and @deva[द्वि] in the dual alone. For further
particulars see § 154.  @deva[त्रि] and @deva[चतुर्] are declined in the plu
alone. E. g.
