## § 788. An adjective, participial or qualitative, must agree

§  788.   An adjective, participial  or  qualitative,  must  agree
with the  substantive  it  qualifies  in  gender,  number  and  case;
@deva[रुपत्युर युद्युरुः]  a  handsome  man; @deva[रुपत्युर्ती  स्त्री]  a  beautiful woman;
@deva[गतदर्शुनं च]  a  great  calamity;  @deva[एते  मर्त्यराः  गति  पुन्त्युक्तानि];  @deva[गच्छुन्तो
नरोः], &c.

But numeral adjectives of fixed  gender  and  number  remain
unchanged:—@deva[शतं  ब्राह्मणाः]  a  hundred  Drāhmaṇas;  @deva[शतं  स्त्रियः]  a
hundred women, @deva[विविंशति]:  @deva[वाट्युकुनि]  twenty children.
