## § 837. (a) § The teacher from whom something is learnt

§ 837  (a) § The  teacher  from  whom  something  is  learnt
regularly  is  put in the Ablative case;  @deva[उग्रुयुयुयुयुयुदुनेत]  learns  from
the  preceptor;  but  @deva[नट्युस्य  गायुर्थों  युयुणोति].
