## § 968. Conjunctions @deva[तु] @[tu], @deva[हि] @[hi], and @deva[वा] @[vā]

The particles @deva[तु] @[tu] "but", @deva[हि] @[hi] "for, because", and @deva[वा] @[vā] "or" are also excluded from the first place in a sentence.

**Examples:**

- @deva[आत्मीयः पुत्रः स्वा भार्या शच्चार्थो तु इदानीं किला] @[ātmīyaḥ putraḥ svā bhāryā śaccārtho tu idānīṃ kilā] the son is one's own son, the wife one's friend, but the daughter a source of anxiety

- Example with @deva[हि] @[hi]: (showing cause or reason)

- Example with @deva[वा] @[vā]: (showing alternative)
