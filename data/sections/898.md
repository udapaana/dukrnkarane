## § 898. The past passive participles are often used imperson-

§ 898. The past passive participles are often used imperson-
ally, the agent being put in the Instrumental case; @deva[प्रयुनूतुनं] or
@deva[युयुयोतितुनं यूरुयुनै] it is shone by the sun; @deva[जितं युयुयुवुनैयुयु] victorious (all
powerful) is the affection for sons; @deva[पाण्डुयूयुयुनितं तयुयुयुतुयु] he
showed his learning; @deva[परिहृतुयुं युयुस्युयूनं विषुयुनै], &c.
