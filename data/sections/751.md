## § 751. When a root is compounded with one or more pre-

§ 751.  When a root is  compounded  with one or more pre-
positions or  words  prefixed  to  verbs  like  prepositions,  the
indeclinable  participle  is formed  by  affixing @deva[य] immediately to
it ; the @deva[य] is  changed  to  @deva[त्य]  after  a  short  radical vowel ( even
when  it  combines  with the final vowel  of a  preposition  to a
long vowel ) ; e g.

    @deva[दा] with @deva[आ]—@deva[आदाय              इु] with @deva[यू]—@deva[प्रेत्य
    वि] with @deva[विस्]-@deva[विज्ञस्य           छु] with @deva[स्तु]—@deva[संस्कृत्य
    जि] with @deva[परा]—@deva[पराजित्य          छु] with @deva[विशु]—@deva[विभाछुत्य
    नी] with @deva[वि]—@deva[विनीय             पिपु] with @deva[तिपु]—@deva[तिवाच्य
    भू] with @deva[अनु]—@deva[अनुभूय            प्युदु] with @deva[उद्]-@deva[उत्पद्य
    इु] with @deva[अभि]—@deva[अभीत्य]           &c.    &c.    &c.
