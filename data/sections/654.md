## § 654. The suffix @deva[य] ( @deva[णयर्] ) is added to a few nouns such as @deva[शूर]

§ 654   The suffix @deva[य] ( @deva[णयर्] ) is added to a few nouns such as @deva[शूर]
'male,' @deva[भृत्] 'slow,' @deva[भूतिहं] 'learned,' @deva[शुयस्] 'famous,' in the sense of
@deva[उत्मनेष्] 'agitated,' &c. in the sense of ' becoming what it was not
before,' or ' what it was not like to before,' @deva[शूर]: @deva[भवति यूषायते] 'what
was not mrch now becomes much,' @deva[उत्मनायति] ' he ( who was not
agitated before ) becomes agitated,' similarly @deva[शुयनायते] &c.
