## § 897. In the case of past passive participles of intransitive

§ 897. In the case of past passive participles of intransitive
roots the agent is put in the Nominative case; @deva[तवुयु युयुवितुयो राजुयु
रहुतु] then the king of demons wept; @deva[युयुयुयुं भवुयुति] &c.
