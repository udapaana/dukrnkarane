## Three Types of Vṛtta

A Vṛtta again is of three kinds:[^1]

1. **@deva[समवृत्त]** (samavṛtta) or that in which all the quarters are similar
2. **@deva[अर्धसमवृत्त]** (ardhasamavṛtta) or that in which the alternate quarters are similar
3. **@deva[विषम]** (viṣama) or that in which the quarters are all dissimilar

[^1]: @deva[समसर्वसमं तथा विषमं च तथा त्रिधा]
