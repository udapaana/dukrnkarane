## Light and Heavy Syllables

A syllable is @deva[लघु] 'light,' or @deva[गुरु] 'heavy' according as its vowel is short or long.

### (a) Short and Long Vowels

The vowels @deva[अ], @deva[इ], @deva[उ], @deva[ऋ] and @deva[लृ] are short; and the vowels @deva[आ], @deva[ई], @deva[ऊ], @deva[ॠ], @deva[ए], @deva[ऐ], @deva[ओ], and @deva[औ] are long.[^1]

When a short vowel is followed by an anusvāra or Visarga or by a conjunct consonant, it is converted into what is called 'a prosodially long vowel;' as @deva[अंश], @deva[अच्छ], &c.

[^1]: @deva[ह्रस्वदीर्घगुरूरौ दीर्घाणां गुरुवद् विसर्गसंयुक्तानां च दीर्घत्वं भवति]
